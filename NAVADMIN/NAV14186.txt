RMKS/1. In accordance with reference (a), the navy recognizes outstanding 
community service projects each year. Awards are presented in five flagship 
sponsored categories: Personal excellence partnership; health, safety and 
fitness; project good neighbor; campaign drug free; and environmental 
stewardship. Awards are presented to shore, sea, and overseas commands.

2. It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2013 Personal Excellence 
Partnership Flagship Award winning commands:

   a. Small Shore Winner: Center for Naval Aviation Technical  Training Unit, 
Whidbey Island, Honorable Mention: Naval Support Activity Lakehurst.

   b. Medium Shore Winner: Naval Air Station Whiting Field, Honorable 
Mention: Navy and Marine Corps Intelligence Training Center.

   c. Large Shore Winner: Joint Expeditionary Base Little Creek-Fort Story, 
Honorable Mention: Naval Submarine Base Kings Bay.

   d. Small Sea Winner: Fleet Ballistic Missile Operational Test Support Unit 
TWO.

   e. Medium Sea Winner: USS WHIDBEY ISLAND (LSD 41).

   f. Large Sea Winner: Assault Craft Unit FOUR, Honorable Mention: USS 
NIMITZ (CVN 68).

   g. Small Overseas Winner: Navy Munitions Command, East Asia Division, Unit 
Guam.

   h. Medium Overseas Winner: USS ANTIETAM (CG 54).

   i. Large Overseas Winner: USS GEORGE WASHINGTON (CVN 73), Honorable 
Mention: U.S. Naval Hospital Guam.     

3. Congratulations to all and the communities that benefitted from the 
efforts of our dedicated and caring Sailors and civilians. Your participation 
and continued support of our Navy's communities are sincerely appreciated and 
make a difference. 

5. Released by Vice Admiral S. H. Swift, Director, Navy Staff.//
