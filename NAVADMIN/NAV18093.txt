RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP) 
eligibility for Navy Interactive ON-NET (ION) Operators.

2.  Effective immediately, qualified members with Navy enlisted 
classification (NEC) 9308 that are filling authorized Navy ION Operator 
billets are entitled to SD level-2, and qualified members with NECs 9326, 
9327 and 9328 that are filling authorized Navy ION Operator billets are 
entitled to SD level-4.

3.  Additional information on SDAP is located at
http://www.public.navy.mil/bupers-npc/career/enlistedcareeradmin/
Pages/SRB.aspx.  However, skill specific questions should be directed to the 
appropriate enlisted community manager, which can be found on the Navy 
Personnel Command (NPC) website.  Commands and units unable to access the 
listed website should contact any of the listed points of contact to obtain 
the revised SDAP rates.  In addition, as NEC codes change under the new NEC 
construct, updates to the SDAP matrix will be reflected on the NPC website.

4.  Points of contact:
    a.  Program manager:  MMNCM James Lucky, BUPERS-328, at
(901) 874-3215/DSN 882, or via e-mail at james.lucky1(at)navy.mil
    b.  Non-nuclear:  PSC Stannie Gregoire, PERS-81, at
(901) 874-3260/DSN 882, or via e-mail at stannie.gregoire(at)navy.mil
    c.  Nuclear:  ETNCM Jeremiah Ledda, OPNAV N133D2A, at
(703) 604-5492/DSN 664, or via e-mail at jeremiah.ledda(at)navy.mil or 
bullnuke(at)navy.mil

5.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or canceled, 
whichever occurs first.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral R. P. Burke, N1.//
