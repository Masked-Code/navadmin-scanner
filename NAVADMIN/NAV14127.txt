RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the schedule for advancement examination 
readiness reviews (AERR) from July 2014 to December 2014.  

2.  Per ref (a), this NAVADMIN supersedes ref (b) and should be read in its 
entirety.  AERR events provide an opportunity for selected rating subject 
matter experts (SME) from fleet and shore-based commands to impact 
sailorization by affecting the careers of every test-taker in the Navy.  
Selected chief, senior chief, and master chief petty officers on active duty, 
full-time support (FTS), and reservists currently on active-duty for special 
work (ADSW) may take part in the process that enables the Navy to advance the 
most qualified and rating-knowledgeable Sailors.  

3.  The readiness reviews are held at Naval Education and Training 
Professional Development and Technology Center (NETPDTC) on board Saufley 
Field, Pensacola, FL.  Temporary additional duty (TAD) travel in support of 
AERR events is considered mission critical and is flag approved.  All costs 
pertaining to TAD are funded by NETPDTC.

4.  The following ratings are scheduled for AERR events between July 2014 and 
December 2014:  ABE, ABF, AM, CTR, EA, EM (Conventional), ET (Nuclear), ETR, 
FT, GSM, IC, MA, MC, MM (Conventional), MN, NC (Counselor), ND, PS, RP, STS, 
UT.

5.  To take part in the AERR process, visit 
https://www.nko.navy.mil/group/navy-advancement-center/advancement-
examination-readiness-review-aerr.  This link provides the scheduled dates 
for the ratings listed above, eligibility and nomination package 
requirements, and other pertinent information regarding AERR events.

6.  Point of contact is Mr. Peter Pappas, NETPDTC N33A, at(850) 473-6121, DSN 
753, Fax (850) 473-6124 or via e-mail at peter.pappas(at)navy.mil.

7.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or canceled, 
whichever occurs first.
