SUBJ/NOVEMBER 2013 COMNAVAIRSYSCOM ACQUISITION CORPS ASSIGNMENT 
SLATING PANEL//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the November 2013 Commander, Naval Air 
Systems Command Acquisition Corps Assignment Slating Panel.  A slating panel 
will convene 6 November 2013 to select individuals for major program 
positions (O-6, GS-15), and major acquisition shore commands (O-6).

2.  Eligible military officers and civilians who desire to be considered must 
submit a signed application.  Application deadline is 7 October 2013.  
Application forms and additional slating panel information is available at:  
http://www.navair.navy.mil (select acquisition selection boards on the 
careers menu tab).

3.  The preferred method of application is via encrypted e-mail.  Please note 
the application will contain Personally Identifiable Information once 
completed by the applicant.  Applications will also be accepted via mail.
    a. Signed scanned applications should be submitted via the e-mail address 
provided in para 4.
b. Applications submitted via mail should use the following address: 
 
    Commander, Naval Air Systems Command (Air-7.3.6)
        Acquisition Corps Assignment Slating Panel
        22095 Fortin Circle
        BLDG 1489 RM 206
        Patuxent River MD 20670-1549

4.  Point of contact is LCDR Mark Angelo, AEDO Community Manager, at
(301) 757-8480/DSN 757 or via email at mark.angelo(a)navy.mil.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
