RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE AVAILABILITY OF THE COMMAND MASTER 
CHIEF/COMMAND SENIOR CHIEF/CHIEF OF THE BOAT (CMC/CSC/COB) LEARNING AND 
DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP (LADR) ON NAVY KNOWLEDGE ONLINE (NKO).  THE LADR IS A 
SAILOR-FOCUSED TOOL THAT OUTLINES TRAINING AND EDUCATION MILESTONES AT EACH 
PAY GRADE AND PROVIDES GUIDANCE OF ADVANCED EDUCATIONAL OPPORTUNITIES AND 
PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATIONS.

2.  THE CMC/CSC/COB LADR PROVIDES A PROFESSIONAL PATH FOR CURRENT LEADERS AND 
A TOOL FOR INTERESTED SAILORS TO CHART THEIR COURSE TOWARD OBTAINING THESE 
REWARDING LEADERSHIP POSITIONS.

3.  LADRS ARE UPDATED ANNUALLY AND SUPPORT THE CNO'S SAILING DIRECTIONS BY 
PROVIDING A VALUABLE RATE-SPECIFIC GUIDE FOR MENTORING AND DEVELOPING SAILORS.  
LADRS GUIDE SAILORS AND LEADERS BY IDENTIFYING CURRENT AND PROJECTED ENLISTED 
TRAINING AND EDUCATION OPPORTUNITIES INCLUDING RATE TRAINING, PROFESSIONAL 
MILITARY EDUCATION, ADVANCED EDUCATION INITIATIVES AND PROFESSIONAL 
CERTIFICATIONS.  PER REF A, FLEET COMMANDERS/TYPE COMMANDERS/TRAINING 
COMMANDERS SHOULD ENSURE DISTRIBUTION OF LADR'S TO EVERY ENLISTED PAY GRADE AT 
ALL COMMANDS AND USE THEM DURING CAREER DEVELOPMENT BOARDS.

4.  LADR FAMILIARIZATION BEGINS AT RECRUIT TRAINING WITH FOLLOW ON DISCUSSIONS 
DURING "A" SCHOOL.  COMMAND INDOCTRINATION PROVIDES AN ADDITIONAL OPPORTUNITY 
FOR LEADERSHIP TO REINFORCE THE VALUE OF LADRS IN INDIVIDUAL SAILOR 
DEVELOPMENT.  INTRODUCING LADRS EARLY IN A  SAILORS CAREER WILL RESULT IN 
EVERY SAILOR GAINING BASELINE KNOWLEDGE OF HIS  OR HER CAREER PROGRESSION AND 
ASSIST THEM IN SETTING REALISTIC GOALS  TOWARD UPWARD CAREER MOBILITY.  LADRS 
CAN BE ACCESSED THROUGH NKO'S HOME  PAGE BY SELECTING THE CAREER MANAGEMENT 
TAB AND THEN NAVIGATING ALONG THE  BLUE SIDE BANNER TO SELECT THE ENLISTED 
LADR HYPERLINK.
