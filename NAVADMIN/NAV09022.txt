MEDICINE AND SURGERY (BUMED) OR DESIGNEE TO EXECUTE SERVICE AGREEMENTS, 
EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY.  
2.  THIS ISP IS DESIGNED TO ENCOURAGE NURSES WITH CRITICAL SKILLS AND 
VALUABLE EXPERIENCE TO STAY NAVY.  ADDITIONALLY, ISP IS AN INCENTIVE 
WHICH ALLOWS NURSE COMMUNITY MANAGERS TO STRATEGICALLY AWARD PERSONNEL 
AS RETENTION NEEDS DICTATE.

READINESS TOMORROW.
