SUBJ/PAY AND PERSONNEL ADMINISTRATIVE SUPPORT SYSTEM TRANSFER AND ALIGNMENT 
OF NAVY PAY AND PERSONNEL SERVICES// 

REF/A/VCNO LTR/16DEC10/NOTAL// 

AMPN/REF A DESIGNATED THE DEPUTY CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS FOR MANPOWER, 
PERSONNEL, TRAINING AND EDUCATION (DCNO (N1)) AS NAVY'S END-TO-END BUSINESS 
PROCESS OWNER FOR PAY AND PERSONNEL MATTERS.// 

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the functional transfer of the Pay and 
Personnel Administrative Support System (PASS) Program from Commander, Navy 
Installations Command to Deputy Chief of Naval Operations (Manpower, 
Personnel, Training and Education) (DCNO) (N1), effective 1 October 2013.  
The transfer will unify policy, process, and execution under DCNO (N1) as the 
Navy's end-to-end business process owner for pay and personnel matters per 
ref (a).  

2.  Management and execution of the Navy's Pay and Personnel Service delivery 
is being aligned under the cognizance of Navy Personnel Command (NAVPERSCOM).  
The mission of NAVPERSCOM Pay and Personnel Management Department (PERS-2) is 
to deliver pay and personnel services to our uniformed personnel.  Personnel 
Support Detachments (PERSUPPDETs) will be aligned under NAVPERSCOM with a 
primary duty to Commanding Officer, Navy Pay and Personnel Support Center 
(NPPSC) and additional duty to their respective installation commanding 
officers.

3.  Delivery of pay and personnel services will not be impacted by the 
transition to NAVPERSCOM.  PERSUPPDETs will continue to ensure timely and 
accurate pay, personnel, and passenger transportation services to our active 
and reserve Sailors and their families.  

4.  Points of contact.  For questions related to PASS transition, contact the 
Assistant Commander Navy Personnel Command for Pay and Personnel Management 
(PERS-2) at 901-874-2361, DSN 882-2361, or via e-mail at ann.stewart@navy.mil 
or NAVPERSCOM, Personnel Services Operations Director (PERS-23) at 901-874-
4639, DSN 882-4639, or via e-mail at karen.vigneron@navy.mil.  The NPC 
Customer Service Center is available by calling 1-866-U-ASK-NPC (1-866-827-
5672/DSN: 882-5672).  

5.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
