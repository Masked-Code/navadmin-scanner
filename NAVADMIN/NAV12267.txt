RMKS/1.  THE PURPOSE OF THIS NAVADMIN IS TO SOLICIT NOMINATIONS FOR THE FISCAL 
YEAR 2012 HUMAN RESOURCES (HR) COMMUNITY JUNIOR OFFICER OF THE YEAR (JOOY) 
AWARD.  THE CHIEF OF NAVAL PERSONNEL IS THE SPONSOR FOR THE ANNUAL HR JOOY 
AWARD.

2.  THE HR COMMUNITY WILL RECOGNIZE ONE JUNIOR OFFICER, O4 AND BELOW, WHO HAS 
MADE SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE READINESS OF THE NAVY.

3.  ALL HR JUNIOR OFFICERS ARE ELIGIBLE (1200, 1205, 1207).

4.  NOMINATIONS SHALL BE SUBMITTED PER REF A.

5.  ALL NOMINATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED BY THE MEMBER'S COMMANDING OFFICER OR 
OFFICER-IN-CHARGE.

6.  ENDORSED NOMINATIONS WILL BE SUBMITTED TO:  DIRECTOR, HR CENTER OF 
EXCELLENCE VIA E-MAIL AT GSBPPHRCOE(AT)NPS.EDU OR BY MAIL
TO:
    DIRECTOR
    HR CENTER OF EXCELLENCE
    GRADUATE SCHOOL OF BUSINESS AND PUBLIC POLICY NPS
    555 DYER ROAD, IN-307
    MONTEREY, CA 93943

7.  THE NOMINATION DEADLINE FOR THE HR JOOY AWARD IS
14 SEPTEMBER 2012.

8.  THIS IS A FISCAL YEAR AWARD AND WILL RECOGNIZE CONTRIBUTIONS FROM FISCAL 
YEAR 2012.

9.  NOMINATIONS WILL BE EVALUATED BY A BOARD OF SIX VOTING MEMBERS (EQUALLY 
SPLIT BETWEEN 1200 AND 1205/1207 OFFICERS).

10.  AWARD WINNER WILL BE RECOGNIZED WITH:
    A.  NAVY AND MARINE CORPS COMMENDATION MEDAL;
    B.  AWARD PRESENTATION; AND
    C.  POSTING OF THE AWARD WINNER'S CONTRIBUTIONS ON THE HR RESOURCES 
WEBSITE.

11.  POINT OF CONTACT:
