RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL 
SELECTION BOARD SCHEDULED TO CONVENE 18 FEBRUARY 2009.  THIS BOARD WILL 
SELECT HIGHLY QUALIFIED NAVAL AVIATORS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS FOR 
THE U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USNTPS), AIR FORCE INSTITUTE OF 
TECHNOLOGY/U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL (AFIT/USNTPS) COOPERATIVE 
PROGRAM (COOP), AND THE U.S. AIR FORCE TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USAFTPS). 
2.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION, A COMPLETE APPLICATION 
LETTER AND COMMAND ENDORSEMENT MUST BE RECEIVED AT THE NAVY PERSONNEL 
COMMAND CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER (CSC) NOT LATER THAN 12 JANUARY 2009.  
THE ENDORSEMENT SHOULD CONSIDER WHETHER THE APPLICANT, IF SELECTED, IS 
AVAILABLE TO REPORT FOR INITIAL TRAINING AS EARLY AS APRIL 2009.  THE 
SERVICE MEMBER'S EMAIL ADDRESS(ES), PHONE NUMBER(S), COLLEGE 
TRANSCRIPTS AND POST-TPS TEST SQUADRON ASSIGNMENT PREFERENCES MUST BE 
INCLUDED WITH APPLICATION.  SUBMISSION OF THE APPLICATION AHEAD OF THE 
DEADLINE IS ENCOURAGED.

ALL CORRESPONDENCE BY SELECTING THE CUSTOMER SERVICE LINK AT

5672).  ADDITIONALLY, ENSURE MOST RECENT FITREP, SUCH AS 31JAN09 FITREP 
FOR LIEUTENANTS IS RECEIVED AT NPC CSC NOT LATER THAN 13 FEBRUARY 2009.  
5.  ELIGIBILITY:
    A.  UNRESTRICTED LINE (13XX) AVIATORS MUST BE DUE COURSE JUNIOR 
OFFICERS (O-2 OR O-3) WITH FORECASTED TIMING TO COMPLETE THE SELECTED 
TPS PROGRAM - A MINIMUM 24-MONTH TEST TOUR AND RETURN FOR SQUADRON DEPT 
HEAD TOUR NOT LATER THAN SCREEN GROUP PLUS 11 YEARS.
    B.  AEROSPACE ENGINEERING DUTY OFFICER (1510) AVIATORS, THROUGH THE 
GRADE OF LIEUTENANT COMMANDER, ARE ELIGIBLE TO APPLY IF ABLE TO MEET 
CAREER MILESTONES AND ARE ABLE TO SERVE A MINIMUM 24-MONTH FLIGHT TEST 
TOUR PRIOR TO FORECASTED IN-ZONE ELIGIBILITY FOR PROMOTION TO COMMANDER.    
6.  APPLICANTS SHOULD PERIODICALLY CHECK FOR UPDATES AND ADDITIONAL 
INFORMATION AT THE TPS BOARD WEBSITE:  WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/ 
ADMINISTRATIVE/TESTPILOT.
