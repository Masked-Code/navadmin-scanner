SUBJ/2008 NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM (NCSP)/PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR 
COMMUNITY SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS// 
RMKS/1.  EACH YEAR THE NAVY RECOGNIZES SHORE, SEA AND OVERSEAS COMMANDS 
FOR OUTSTANDING COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECTS BY PRESENTING AWARDS IN FIVE 
FLAGSHIP CATEGORIES: PERSONAL EXCELLENCE PARTNERSHIP; HEALTH, SAFETY, 
AND FITNESS; CAMPAIGN DRUG FREE; PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR; AND 
ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP.
2.  THE PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR AWARD RECOGNIZES THE BEST YEAR-ROUND 
VOLUNTEER-SUPPORTED PROGRAM OR SPECIAL PROJECT THAT PROMOTES OUTREACH 
ACTIVITIES THROUGHOUT THE YEAR TO ESTABLISH AND RESTORE HOPE TO THE 
COMMUNITY.
3.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE I ANNOUNCE THE 2008 NAVY PROJECT GOOD 
NEIGHBOR COMMUNITY SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS:
    A.  SMALL COMMANDS CATEGORY:
        - SHORE-TRANSIENT PERSONNEL UNIT PUGET SOUND, SILVERDALE, WA
        - SEA-USS NORTH CAROLINA (SSN 777), NEW LONDON, CT
        - OVERSEAS-LOGISTICS GROUP, WESTERN PACIFIC, SINGAPORE
    B.  MEDIUM COMMANDS CATEGORY:
        - SHORE-NAVY INFORMATION OPERATIONS COMMAND, SUGAR GROVE, WV
        - SEA-USS MESA VERDE (LPD 19), NORFOLK, VA
        - OVERSEAS-US NAVAL SUPPORT ACTIVITY, SOUDA BAY, GREECE
    C.  LARGE COMMANDS CATEGORY:
        - SHORE-NAVAL SUBMARINE SCHOOL, GROTON, CT
        - HONORABLE MENTION-NAVAL AIR STATION, PENSACOLA, FL
        - SEA-NO COMPETITORS RECOMMENDED
        - OVERSEAS-NO PACKAGES SUBMITTED 4.  MY SINCEREST THANKS TO ALL 
FOR YOUR CONTINUED SUPPORT OF THE NAVY'S COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM.  
PLEASE CONVEY MY PERSONAL APPRECIATION TO THE CARING AND DEDICATED 
SAILORS, CIVILIANS, AND FAMILIES WHO SELFLESSLY VOLUNTEERED AND 
CONTRIBUTED IN IMPROVING THE QUALITY OF LIFE IN THEIR COMMUNITIES.
5.  RELEASED BY MS. S. J. BOURBEAU, N1B.//
