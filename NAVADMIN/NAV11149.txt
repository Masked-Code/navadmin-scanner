RMKS/1.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE SELECTION OF JOINT 
BASE PEARL HARBOR-HICKAM (NOMINATED AS NAVAL STATION PEARL HARBOR (NSPH)) AS 
THE NAVY'S WINNER OF THE 2011 COMMANDER IN CHIEF'S ANNUAL AWARD FOR 
INSTALLATION EXCELLENCE.  IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF A, THIS AWARD RECOGNIZES ONE 
INSTALLATION FROM EACH SERVICE THAT HAS MADE BEST USE OF AVAILABLE RESOURCES 
TO ACCOMPLISH ITS ASSIGNED MISSION, AND HAS FOCUSED ON IMAGINATIVE AND 
INNOVATIVE MANAGEMENT ACTIONS THAT HAVE INCREASED WORK FORCE PRODUCTIVITY 
WHILE ENHANCING THE QUALITY OF LIFE FOR ITS PERSONNEL.  THIS OUTSTANDING 
INSTALLATION, REPRESENTED BY CAPTAIN RICHARD W. KITCHENS (CO), WILL BE 
HONORED IN A JOINT SERVICE CEREMONY ON 04 MAY 2011 IN THE PENTAGON.

2.  THE SELECTION WAS HIGHLY COMPETITIVE WITH THE WINNER CHOSEN FROM A FIELD 
OF ELEVEN (11) OUTSTANDING NOMINEES.  EACH NOMINEE IS A WINNER WITHIN ITS OWN 
REGION.  THE TOP THREE NOMINEES WERE EXTREMELY CLOSE, AND I EXTEND A SPECIAL 
"WELL DONE" TO THE SECOND PLACE WINNER: NAVAL BASE SAN DIEGO, CAPTAIN RICKY 
WILLIAMSON (CO), AND THE THIRD PLACE
WINNER: NAVAL AIR STATION WHIDBEY ISLAND, CAPTAIN JAY JOHNSTON (CO).
THE TOP THREE NAVY WINNERS WILL RECEIVE A NAVY MONETARY AWARD FOR THEIR 
SELECTION.
