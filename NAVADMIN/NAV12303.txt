SUBJ/WORLDWIDE NAVY BIRTHDAY ALL HANDS CALL//

RMKS/1. IN CELEBRATION OF THE NAVY'S 237TH BIRTHDAY, THE CHIEF OF NAVAL 
OPERATIONS (CNO) AND MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICER OF THE NAVY (MCPON) WILL HOLD 
A WORLDWIDE ALL HANDS CALL ON 11 OCTOBER 2012 AT 1300 EST. THE CALL WILL BE 
TELEVISED ON DIRECT-TO-SAILOR (DTS), THE PENTAGON CHANNEL AND ONLINE WITHOUT 
THE CHAT FUNCTION AT WWW.NAVY.MIL, AND WITH THE CHAT FUNCTION AT 
WWW.LIVESTREAM.COM/USNAVY .

2. QUESTIONS MAY BE SUBMITTED IN ADVANCE FOR CNO AND MCPON TO ANSWER LIVE AT 
THE ALL HANDS CALL. SUBMISSIONS SHOULD BE EMAILED TO USNTOWNHALL(AT)GMAIL.COM 
PRIOR TO AND DURING THE ALL HANDS CALL.  QUESTIONS CAN ALSO BE POSED DURING 
THE ALL HANDS CALL VIA WWW.LIVESTREAM.COM/USNAVY. PERSONNEL SUBMITING 
QUESTIONS SHOULD INCLUDE THEIR NAME, RANK, RATE AND COMMAND.

3. OCTOBER IS THE MONTH OF OUR NAVY'S BIRTH AND AS SUCH IT IS AN APPROPRIATE 
TIME TO REFLECT ON THE INTEGRAL ROLE WE PLAY IN ACCOMPLISHING THE NAVY'S 
WARFIGHTING MISSION.

4. RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL R. W. HUNT, DIRECTOR, NAVY STAFF.//
