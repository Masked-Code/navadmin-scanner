REF/A/DOC/BUPERS/13OCT2010// AMPN/BUPERSINST 1133.29H, APPLICATION PROCEDURES 
FOR CAREER RECRUITER FORCE (CRF)// REMARKS/1.  THE CAREER RECRUITER FORCE 
(CRF) WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1978 TO DEVELOP A CADRE OF EXCEPTIONAL RECRUITING 
MANAGERS TO PROVIDE CONSISTENCY AND LEADERSHIP TO THE RECRUITING EFFORT.  THE 
CRF PROGRAM IS MADE UP OF PROVEN, VOLUNTEER RECRUITERS WHO FILL KEY MIDDLE 
MANAGEMENT BILLETS.  THE SELECTION CRITERIA FOCUSES ON PROVEN RECRUITING 
PERFORMANCE WITH AN EMPHASIS ON EFFECTIVE LEADERSHIP, MANAGEMENT, COUNSELING 
AND TRAINING ABILITIES.

2.  THE CRF PROGRAM IS OPEN TO PERSONNEL WHO ARE CURRENTLY ASSIGNED OR HAVE 
SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED A TOUR IN RECRUITING DUTY WITH NEC CODES 9585, 9586 OR 
9587.  FURTHER ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND APPLICATION PROCEDURES ARE OUTLINED IN 
REF A.

3.  THE CONVENING DATES FOR THE NEXT FOUR SELECTION BOARDS ARE AS
FOLLOWS:
     A.  3 NOVEMBER 2011
     B.  16 FEBRUARY 2012
     C.  17 MAY 2012
     D.  31 JULY 2012

4.  ALL APPLICATIONS ARE REQUIRED TO BE RECEIVED NOT LATER THAN THREE WEEKS 
PRIOR TO THE CONVENING DATE OF A SCHEDULED SELECTION BOARD.  FORWARD PACKAGES 
TO:
    COMMANDER, NAVY RECRUITING COMMAND
    ATTN:  ASSISTANT CRF PROGRAM MANAGER, N11151
    5722 INTEGRITY DRIVE, BLDG 784
    MILLINGTON, TN 38054-5057
OR SCAN AND E-MAIL DIRECTLY TO MR. RICHARD THOMPSON AT 
RICHARD.E.THOMPSON2(AT)NAVY.MIL.

5.  ALL CANDIDATES WILL BE NOTIFIED VIA OFFICIAL MESSAGE OF THEIR SELECTION OR 
NON-SELECTION.

6.  SELECTEES CURRENTLY ON RECRUITING DUTY WILL BE CONVERTED IMMEDIATELY TO 
NAVY COUNSELOR (RECRUITER) (NEC-2186).  SELECTEES NOT ON RECRUITING DUTY WILL 
BE IMMEDIATELY ASSIGNED TEMPORARY NEC 2199.
FLEET PERSONNEL SELECTED WILL TAKE CURRENT RATING NAVY-WIDE ADVANCEMENT 
EXAMINATION UNTIL SUCCESSFULLY GRADUATING FROM THE CRF ACADEMY.  ALL SELECTEES 
WILL ATTEND THE CAREER RECRUITER FORCE ACADEMY IN PENSACOLA, FL IN CONJUNCTION 
WITH PERMANENT CHANGE OF STATION (PCS) ORDERS AT THEIR CURRENT PROJECTED 
ROTATION DATE (PRD).
