TECHNOLOGY (IT) AND DATA, TO INCLUDE SUPPORTING OPERATIONAL TECHNOLOGY 
NETWORKS AND SYSTEMS THAT ENABLE NEARLY EVERY ASPECT OF THE NAVY'S MISSION.  
PER REFERENCE (A), INDUSTRIAL CONTROL SYSTEMS SUPPORTING CRITICAL OPERATIONAL 
INFRASTRUCTURE ON NAVY INSTALLATIONS ARE OF PARTICULAR INTEREST. THIS 
NAVADMIN DIRECTS ALL SHORE ACTIVITIES, INCLUDING NAVY AND JOINT TENANTS THAT 
ARE OCCUPYING FACILITIES AND INFRASTRUCTURE ON NAVY INSTALLATIONS, TO SUBMIT 
TO INSPECTION AND ASSESSMENT OF INDUSTRIAL CONTROL SYSTEMS (ICS).

2. COMMANDER, NAVY INSTALLATIONS COMMAND (CNIC), SUPPORTED BY NAVFAC, WILL 
CONDUCT THE ASSESSMENTS AND IMPLEMENT CYBER MITIGATIONS PER REFERENCES (A), 
(B) AND (C).

3.  CYBERSECURITY DISCIPLINE SHOULD BE PART OF EACH COMMANDS WARFIGHTING 
CULTURE TO PROTECT THE NAVY SHORE ENTERPRISE FROM PERSISTENT CYBER THREAT.
