RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES PROMULGATION OF NAVY'S FOREIGN      
LANGUAGE TESTING POLICIES CONTAINED IN REF A.  FOREIGN LANGUAGE      
TESTING IS CRITICAL TO THE NAVY'S ABILITY TO CAPTURE AND MEASURE THE 
LANGUAGE CAPABILITIES AVAILABLE THROUGHOUT THE FLEET.  AS SUCH, REF  
A ESTABLISHES POLICIES AND ASSIGNS RESPONSIBILTIES FOR THE NAVY      
FOREIGN LANGUAGE TESTING PROGRAM AND SHOULD BE REVIEWED IN ITS       
ENTIRETY.
