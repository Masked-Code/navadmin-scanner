RMKS/1.  THE ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP SELECTION BOARD IS SCHEDULED 
TO CONVENE ON 15 SEPTEMBER 2008.  ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP IS A 
PREREQUISITE FOR ASSIGNMENT TO ONE OF APPROXIMATELY 700 DEPARTMENT OF 
THE NAVY (DON) CRITICALLY CODED ACQUISITION BILLETS.
2.  POLICY AND GUIDANCE ON ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP PROCEDURES FOR 
DON OFFICERS MAY BE FOUND AT THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE: 
WWW.ACQUISITION.NAVY.MIL/NAVYAOS/CONTENT/VIEW/FULL/61 (ALL LOWER CASE).

LATER THAN 8 AUGUST 2008 (NO ENDORSEMENTS REQUIRED).  THE REQUIRED 
APPLICATION COVER LETTER AND DATA SHEET ARE AVAILABLE FROM THE NAVY 
PERSONNEL COMMAND WEBSITE: WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/ACQUISITION.  ALL 
APPLICANTS ARE REQUIRED TO PROVIDE INFORMATION AS DELINEATED IN THE 
SAMPLE DATA SHEET.
4.  THE PREFERRED METHOD OF SUBMITTING APPLICATIONS IS BY MAIL.
