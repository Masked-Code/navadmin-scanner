RMKS/1.  Per reference (a), the Navy annually recognizes commands engaged in 
exemplary voluntary community service activities that promote academics, life 
skills and civic education for youth.

2.  For the 2017 Personal Excellence Partnership Flagship awards competition, 
nominations were received in the small, medium and large shore categories; 
small, medium and large sea categories; and small, medium and large overseas 
categories.

3.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the winners of the 2017 
Personal Excellence Partnership Flagship Awards competition.
    a. Small Shore Winner:  Surface Combat Systems Center
    b. Medium Shore Winner:  Navy Medicine Professional Development Center
    c. Large Shore Winner:  Naval Undersea Warfare Center Division, Newport
    d. Small Sea Winner:  Commander, Carrier Strike Group TWELVE
    e. Medium Sea Winner:  Helicopter Sea Combat Squadron TWO THREE
    f. Large Sea Winner:  Naval Mobile Construction Battalion THREE
    g. Small Overeas Winner:  Commander, Fleet Activities Chinhae
    h. Medium Overseas Winner:  No Award Given
    I. Large Overseas Winner:  Naval Hospital Guam

4.  Congratulations to all our deserving award winners.  Thank you for your 
partnership with the surrounding communities and ongoing support of the Navy 
Community Service Program. 

5.  Released by Vice Admiral K. M. Donegan, Director, Navy Staff.//
