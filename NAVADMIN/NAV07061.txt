RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT 
SCHOOL SELECTION BOARD (BOARD #380) SCHEDULED TO CONVENE 24 JUL 07.
THIS BOARD WILL SELECT HIGHLY QUALIFIED NAVAL AVIATORS AND NAVAL FLIGHT 
OFFICERS FOR THE U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USNTPS), AIR FORCE 
INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY/NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL COOPERATIVE PROGRAM 
(AFIT/USNTPS COOP), U.S. AIR FORCE TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USAFTPS), BRITISH 
EMPIRE TEST PILOT SCHOOL (ETPS), AND FRENCH EPNER TEST PILOT SCHOOL 
(FTPS).
2.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION, A COMPLETE APPLICATION 
LETTER (REQUIREMENTS IN REF A) AND COMMAND ENDORSEMENT MUST BE RECEIVED 
AT THE NPC CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER (CSC) NOT LATER THAN 11 JUN 07.  THE 
SERVICE MEMBER S EMAIL ADDRESS(ES), PHONE NUMBER(S), AND COLLEGE 
TRANSCRIPTS MUST BE INCLUDED WITH THE APPLICATION.
