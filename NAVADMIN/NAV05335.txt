SUBJ/FY-06 AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY (ACCP)//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  A VITAL PART OF DEVELOPING TOTAL FORCE STRATEGY
IS TO PROVIDE APPROPRIATE INCENTIVES FOR SKILLED PERSONNEL TO FILL
CRITICAL BILLETS REQUIRED FOR MAINTAINING COMBAT READINESS.  THE
AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY (ACCP) PROGRAM PROVIDES SELECTED
BONUSES AS AN INCENTIVE TO ELIGIBLE PILOTS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS
THROUGH DEPARTMENT HEAD, SEA DUTY, AND COMMAND BILLETS.  THE FY-06
PROGRAM IS RETROACTIVELY EFFECTIVE TO 1 OCT 05.
2.  FY-06 ACCP PROGRAM WILL INCREASE THE NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICER (NFO)
LONG-TERM BONUS FROM $15,000 TO $25,000 PER YEAR.  THE FY-06 PROGRAM
WILL PROVIDE LONG-TERM (5-YEAR) AND SHORT-TERM AVIATION CAREER
CONTINUATION PAY FOR VARIOUS SEA DUTY AND COMMAND BILLETS.  PAYMENT
OF ACCP 1-YEAR PRIOR TO COMPLETION OF ACTIVE DUTY SERVICE OBLIGATION
IS AUTHORIZED.  LONG-TERM APPLICANTS MAY APPLY AS LONG AS THE
MEMBER'S ACTIVE DUTY SERVICE OBLIGATION EXPIRATION IS IN FY-06 OR
FY-07.
3.  FOR SPECIFIC DETAILS, ELIGIBILITY, ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURES,
AND APPLICATION LETTER FORMAT VISIT THE AVIATION OFFICER COMMUNITY
MANAGER WEBSITE AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/AVIATION/OCM/ACCP/.
