RMKS/1. REF A ESTABLISHES THE CHIEF OF CHAPLAINS AS PROGRAM SPONSOR FOR 
THE PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF CHAPLAIN CORPS OFFICERS AND RELIGIOUS 
PROGRAM SPECIALISTS (RP).  THE CHIEF OF CHAPLAINS CONDUCTS AN ANNUAL 
SYMPOSIUM FOR SENIOR CHAPLAIN CORPS OFFICERS (O-6) AND RP'S
(E-9) TO UPDATE ESSENTIAL TECHNIQUES AND PRACTICES IN MILITARY MINISTRY.
2. THE FY07 CHAPLAIN CORPS STRATEGIC LEADERSHIP SYMPOSIUM, DRAWING ON 
THE PAST PLANNING FOR THE FUTURE WILL EQUIP CHAPLAIN CORPS SENIOR 
LEADERSHIP WITH THE KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS NECESSARY FOR THE EXECUTION OF 
THE CHAPLAIN CORPS CONCEPT OF OPERATIONS IN ITS STRATEGIC PLAN.
3. THE SYMPOSIUM WILL BE HELD 12-14 FEB 2007 (TRAVEL DATES 11 & 15
FEB) AT THE HILTON SAN ANTONIO AIRPORT, 611 NW LOOP 410, SAN ANTONIO TX.  
IN ORDER TO RECEIVE THE GOVERNMENT PER DIEM RATE OF $103 FOR A SINGLE 
ROOM, ATTENDEES MUST ADVISE THE RESERVATION CLERK OF PARTICIPATION IN 
CHIEF OF NAVY CHAPLAINS SLS.  HOTEL RESERVATIONS MUST BE MADE NO LATER 
THAN 14 JANUARY 2007 TO GUARANTEE THE GOVERNMENT RATE. CANCELLATIONS 
MUST BE 72 HOURS PRIOR TO ARRIVAL DATE.  COMPLIMENTARY 24 HOUR HOTEL 
SHUTTLE TO AND FROM THE AIRPORT
(2.5 MILES) WILL BE PROVIDED.  CAR RENTAL IS OPTIONAL DUE TO PROXIMITY.
4. UNIFORM WILL BE KHAKIS OR SERVICE EQUIVALENT.
5. THE CONFERENCE WILL BEGIN MONDAY MORNING, 12 FEBRUARY AND CONCLUDE 
WEDNESDAY, 14 FEBRUARY AT 1600. REGISTRATION WILL BEGIN ON SUNDAY, 11 
FEBRUARY FROM 1600 TO 1900.
6. COMMANDS SHOULD PROVIDE APPROPRIATED FUNDS FOR THE TRAINING PER PARA 
5E(9) OF REF B.
7. RELEASED BY RADM R. F. BURT, N097
