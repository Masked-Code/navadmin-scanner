2.  The Deputy Chief of Naval Operations for Information Dominance is pleased 
to congratulate the 2014 Copernicus Awardees.  Your efforts are critical to 
advancing warfighting capabilities and supporting our forces in harm*s way.

3.  AFCEA and USNI will send letters of congratulations to all Copernicus
