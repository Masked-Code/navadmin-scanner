AMPN/REF A IS SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY (SDAP) INSTRUCTION.// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES REVISED SDAP LEVELS FOR REGULAR 
COMPONENT AND RESERVE COMPONENT FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) SAILORS AND 
SUPERSEDES NAVADMIN 101/08.  THE SDAP PROGRAM ENHANCES THE NAVY'S 
ABILITY TO SIZE, SHAPE AND STABILIZE THE FORCE BY USING A MONETARY 
INCENTIVE TO ENCOURAGE SAILORS WITH SPECIFIC DESIGNATED SKILLS TO 
APPLY THEIR KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE TO UNIQUELY CHALLENGING 
ASSIGNMENTS. 
2.  TO RECEIVE SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY, PERSONNEL MUST BE 
FILLING A SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY AUTHORIZED BILLET WITH ONE OF 
THE SPECIAL SKILLS LISTED IN PARA 7.  UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED, 
PERSONNEL MAY NOT RECEIVE THIS PAY WHILE ASSIGNED IN EXCESS OF 
AUTHORIZED BILLETS.  COMMANDS MUST REVIEW THEIR ACTIVITY MANPOWER 
DOCUMENT AND ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT TO ENSURE THE 
NUMBER OF PERSONNEL RECEIVING THIS SPECIAL PAY DOES NOT EXCEED THE
 NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED BILLETS FOR THAT SKILL.  SERVICE MEMBERS ARE 
ONLY AUTHORIZED TO RECEIVE ONE SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY 
ENTITLEMENT. 
3.  COMMANDS HOLDING SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY BILLETS ARE REMINDED 
THAT ANNUAL RE-CERTIFICATION BEGINS 1 JUNE, OR AS OTHERWISE DIRECTED.  
THE DEADLINE FOR THE ANNUAL RE-CERTIFICATION IS 31 AUGUST.  GUIDANCE 
FOR THE PROCESS WILL BE ANNOUNCED BY A FUTURE NAVADMIN.  COMMANDS, 
VIA PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS (PSD) IF APPLICABLE, ARE 
RESPONSIBLE FOR MAKING ANY RATE ADJUSTMENTS AS DIRECTED BY THIS 
NAVADMIN.
4.  THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT REGULATION, VOLUME 
7A, GOVERNS THE POLICIES AND PROCEDURES FOR THE ADMINISTRATION AND 
PAYMENT OF SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY.  COMMANDS AND SERVICING PSDS 
ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR ADMINISTERING ALL APPLICABLE CHANGES TO SPECIAL 
DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY PER THIS NAVADMIN AND THE ABOVE-LISTED DIRECTIVES.
5.  COMMANDS SHOULD ENSURE COMPLIANCE WITH MILPERSMAN 1306-1200 
THROUGH MILPERSMAN 1306-1210 FOR SAILORS IN A LIMITED DUTY STATUS. 
SEE REF A, PARA 7D(8) FOR PATIENT STATUS GUIDANCE AS IT PERTAINS TO 
SDAP.  
6.  SKILL SPECIFIC QUESTIONS MAY BE DIRECTED TO THE APPROPRIATE 
ENLISTED COMMUNITY MANAGERS, AS LISTED IN PARA 7.  TO LOCATE THE PHONE 
NUMBER OF THE APPROPRIATE COMMUNITY MANAGER, REFER TO THE PHONE LIST 
IN THE LINK-PERSPECTIVE MAGAZINE.  ADDITIONALLY, CONTACT INFO MAY BE 
ACCESSED AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/ENLISTED/COMMUNITYMANAGERS.  YOU MUST LOG 
ONTO BUPERS ONLINE TO ACCESS THIS LISTING.
