RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN PUBLISHES THE 2011-2012 DIVERSITY EVENTS CALENDAR.

2.  REACHING THE TALENT THAT RESIDES IN EVERY PART OF OUR SOCIETY DIRECTLY 
IMPACTS NAVY MISSION SUCCESS.  OUTREACH EVENTS AND AWARDS PROVIDE EXCELLENT 
OPPORTUNITIES TO INFORM THE COMMUNITY ABOUT THE MANY NAVY PROGRAMS AVAILABLE; 
THEY ALSO FOSTER THE PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF OUR SAILORS.
PARTICIPATION ALLOWS US TO DEMONSTRATE OUR RICH NAVY HERITAGE.  ALL COMMANDS 
ARE ENCOURAGED TO SUPPORT THESE PRESTIGIOUS AWARDS AND EVENTS THROUGH 
NOMINATIONS AND ATTENDANCE.  AFFINITY GROUP AWARD INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND ON 
THE CONFERENCE WEBSITES LISTED.  FOR DOD AWARDS, PLEASE CONTACT LT JOE 
LEAVITT, NAVY DIVERSITY DIRECTORATE (OPNAV N134), AT (703) 614-6851/DSN 224 OR
