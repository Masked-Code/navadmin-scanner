RMKS/1.  REF A REQUESTED NOMINATIONS FOR THE 2010 ADMIRAL JEREMY M.
BOORDA AWARD FOR OUTSTANDING
INTEGRATION OF ANALYSIS AND POLICY-MAKING.  THE AWARD RECOGNIZES THOSE 
WHO HAVE MADE A SIGNFICANT CONTRIBUTION TO NAVY PERSONNEL READINESS 
EITHER THROUGH RESEARCH AND ANALYSIS, OR DIRECT APPLICATION OF 
ANALYTICAL RESULTS AND EFFORTS TO POLICIES AND LAWS.
2.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE I ANNOUNCE THE TEAM OF CAPT ROBERT 
WEITZMAN, LCDR BRETT ST GEORGE, AND MR. TONEY WILLIAMS OF NAVY MILITARY 
PERSONNEL COMMAND AS THE WINNERS OF THE 2010 ADMIRAL JEREMY M. BOORDA 
AWARD FOR THEIR WORK IN PREDICTING OUT-YEAR OPERATIONAL, ROTATIONAL AND 
TRAINING PERMANENT CHANGE OF STATION ORDER REQUIREMENTS.
3.  THE COMPETITION WAS EXTREMELY KEEN AND CHARACTERIZED THE HIGH 
CALIBER OF THE ANALYSTS SERVING THROUGHOUT THE NAVY IN SUPPORT OF THE 
NAVY TOTAL FORCE.  ALL NOMINEES FOR THIS AWARD MADE OUTSTANDING 
CONTRIBUTIONS AND ARE TO BE CONGRATULATED FOR THEIR SERVICE TO OUR 
NATION.
4.  THE AWARD WILL BE PRESENTED BY VADM FERGUSON, DEPUTY CHIEF NAVAL 
OPERATIONS (MANPOWER, PERSONNEL, TRAINING AND EDUCATION) (N1)/CHIEF OF 
NAVAL PERSONNEL IN THE NEAR FUTURE IN MILLINGTON, TN.  THE TEAM WILL 
RECEIVE A LETTER OF COMMENDATION AND PLAQUE AND HAVE THEIR NAMES 
ENGRAVED ON THE ADMIRAL JEREMY M. BOORDA AWARD FOR OUTSTANDING 
INTEGRATION OF ANALYSIS AND POLICY-MAKING PLAQUE RETAINED IN THE CHIEF 
OF NAVAL PERSONNEL HEADQUARTERS.
5.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
