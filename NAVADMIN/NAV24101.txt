RMKS/1.  Congratulations to the following personnel who have been 
screened for senior and master chief petty officer by the Fiscal 
Year 2025 Active-Duty Navy E8 and E9 Screening Board.  Strong 
competition between qualified professionals is one of the strengths 
of the Navy and your screening speaks highly of your abilities. 
 
2.  This NAVADMIN is not authorization for advancement.  Specific 
dates of advancement will be determined by the reporting date that 
is directed in Permanent Change of Station (PCS) orders determined 
by the billet that member is detailed to.  Personnel listed below 
may be frocked in line with reference (a), if qualified, 30 days 
prior to the execution of their PCS orders directing them to a 
billet in the next paygrade.  The gaining command will be 
responsible for validating eligibility and permanently advancing 
selectees upon reporting for duty (ACC 100) following any en route 
training. 
 
3.  Members are directed to verify their screened status via BUPERS 
Online.  The screened list below is in alphabetical order by rate 
within competitive category.  Read each line from left to right, 
name, and numerical order of advancement.  Note:  An (*) by the name 
indicates the selectee was merit reordered to the top of the 
screening list in accordance with reference (a).
