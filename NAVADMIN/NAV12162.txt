RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE SEPTEMBER 2012 (CYCLE 216) NAVY-WIDE 
ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION FOR ADVANCEMENT IN RATE OF USN/USNR (ACTIVE), FULL 
TIME SUPPORT (FTS) TO INCLUDE ACTIVE GUARD AND RESERVE (AGR), AND CANVASSER 
RECRUITER (CANREC) SAILORS.  THE EXAMINATION DATES ARE:
    E6 - 6 SEPTEMBER 2012
    E5 - 13 SEPTEMBER 2012
    E4 - 20 SEPTEMBER 2012 
    A.  REFER TO REF A REGARDING ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION OPTIONS FOR SAILORS 
WHO ARE PENDING DEPLOYMENT TO OR ARE DEPLOYED AND OPERATING IN IRAQ, 
AFGHANISTAN, OR THE HORN OF AFRICA.  ADDITIONALLY, IF CYCLE 216 IS THE FIRST 
ADVANCEMENT CYCLE IN WHICH A REDEPLOYED SAILOR IS PARTICIPATING UPON 
COMPLETION OF A TOUR TO A THEATER COVERED BY THIS POLICY, EDUCATIONAL SERVICES 
OFFICERS (ESO) MUST FOLLOW PROCEDURES IN REF A AND REF B TO EXECUTE PROCESSING 
OF MISSED EXAMINATION CYCLES.  
AFFECTED REDEPLOYED SAILORS MUST BE IDENTIFIED IN A SEPARATE ENCLOSURE TO THE 
LETTER OF TRANSMITTAL AS DIRECTED IN REF B, PARA 614.
    B.  DO NOT ADMINISTER ANY CYCLE 216 EXAMINATIONS PRIOR TO THE SCHEDULED 
EXAMINATION DATE UNLESS PROVIDED PER REF A OR REF B, OR SPECIFICALLY 
AUTHORIZED BY NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND PERS-812.
    C.  MUSICIAN (MU) SECOND CLASS PETTY OFFICERS WILL TAKE THE REGULAR MU1 
PAPER EXAMINATION ON 6 SEPTEMBER 2012.  DIGITAL MEDIA PLAYERS WILL NO LONGER 
BE NECESSARY FOR THE MU1 EXAM.  MANUSCRIPT PAPER MAY NOT BE BROUGHT INTO THE 
EXAMINING ROOM AS MANUSCRIPT LINES ARE PROVIDED ON THE BACK OF EACH EXAM.

3.  ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
    A.  SAILORS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION BY THE ENLISTED RETENTION BOARD 
(ERB) ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR FUTURE ACTIVE DUTY ADVANCEMENT AND SHALL NOT BE 
ADMINISTERED CYCLE 216 NWAE.
    B.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS OUTLINED IN REF B, 
CHAPTER 2, PRIOR TO EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION DATE FOR THEIR PAY GRADE.  
REFER TO PARAS 2.F, 2.G, AND 2.H OF THIS NAVADMIN FOR EXAMINATION 
ADMINISTRATION DATES.  CANDIDATES WHO DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS ARE 
INELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED A CYCLE 216 
ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION.
    C.  TERMINAL ELIGIBILITY DATE (TED):  THE TED FOR CYCLE 216 IS 1 JANUARY 
2013.  THIS DATE SHALL BE USED TO COMPUTE AND DETERMINE TIR ELIGIBILITY IN 
ACCORDANCE WITH REF B, TABLE 2-2.
    D.  PERFORM TO SERVE (PTS):  SAILORS WHO HAVE RECEIVED FINAL PTS DENIAL 
(DENIED FINAL - ACTIVE) AS OF THE JUNE APPLICATION RACK AND STACK RESULTS AND 
THOSE THAT HAVE A PTS STATUS OF INTENDS TO SEPARATE ARE INELIGIBLE AND SHALL 
NOT BE ADMINISTERED CYCLE 216 NWAE.
    E.  HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT):  PER REF C, ARTICLE 1160-120, CANDIDATES WHO 
REACH HYT ON OR AFTER 1 JANUARY 2013, OR WHO HAVE RECEIVED A HYT WAIVER TO 
REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY ON OR AFTER 1 JANUARY 2013, ARE ELIGIBLE TO PARTICIPATE 
IN THE ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION.  CANDIDATES WITH A HYT DATE PRIOR TO 1 JANUARY 
2013 ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED 
AN EXAMINATION.
    F.  NON-DESIGNATED SAILORS:  COMMANDS ARE REQUIRED TO USE THE FLEET RATING 
IDENTIFICATION ENGINE (FLEET RIDE) TOOL TO VALIDATE RATING QUALIFICATIONS AND 
REQUEST RATING ENTRY VIA ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION PARTICIPATION FOR NON-
DESIGNATED SAILORS.  COMPLETE FLEET RIDE APPLICATIONS IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF 
D.  ONLY FLEET RIDE APPLICATIONS WHICH HAVE BEEN SUBMITTED AND APPROVED DURING 
MARCH 2012 - AUGUST 2012 RACK/STACK ARE VALID FOR CYCLE 216 EXAMINATIONS.  
NON-DESIGNATED SAILORS WHO DO NOT MEET THIS REQUIREMENT ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR 
RATING ENTRY VIA 
CYCLE 216 AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED AN EXAMINATION.
    G.  ENLISTED NAVY LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT:  REFER TO REF E.
    H.  WARFARE QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:  REFER TO REF F.
    I.  PHYSICAL READINESS PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS:  REFER TO REF G.
    J.  TIR WAIVERS:  TIR WAIVERS AND EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION OF EARLY 
PROMOTE CANDIDATES MUST BE IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF B, PARA 216.F.  CANDIDATES 
WHO HAVE BEEN GRANTED TIR WAIVERS MUST BE IDENTIFIED DURING EXAMINATION 
ORDERING AND ON THE TRANSMITTAL LETTER TO NETPDTC (N321).
    K.  SECURITY CLEARANCE:  PER PARA 204 OF REF B, ALL PERSONNEL IN CERTAIN 
RATINGS MUST MAINTAIN, AS A MINIMUM, CONTINUOUS SECURITY CLEARANCE 
ELIGIBILITY.  A FAVORABLE INVESTIGATION ADJUDICATION MUST BE ISSUED FROM 
DEPARTMENT OF NAVY CENTRAL ADJUDICATION FACILITY (DONCAF) PRIOR TO THE 
REGULARLY SCHEDULED EXAMINATION DATE.  AN INTERIM SECURITY CLEARANCE IS NOT A 
FORMAL DETERMINATION BY AN ADJUDICATIVE AUTHORITY AND THEREFORE DOES NOT 
SATISFY THIS REQUIREMENT.  SAILORS IN THE FOLLOWING RATINGS WHO DO NOT MEET 
THIS REQUIREMENT ARE INELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED 
AN EXAMINATION:  AC, AE, AG, AO, AT, AWF, AWO, AWR, AWS, AWV, AZ, CTI, CTM, 
CTN, CTR, CTT, EOD, ET, FC, FT, GM, HT, IC, IS, IT, ITS, LN, MA, MC, MN, MT, 
ND, OS, QM, SB, SO, STG, STS,AND YN, AS WELL AS ALL NUCLEAR-TRAINED PERSONNEL.
    L.  "A" SCHOOL CANDIDATES:
        (1) SAILORS WHO HAVE REPORTED TO "A" SCHOOL FOR A RATING CONVERSION 
AND HAVE NOT GRADUATED PRIOR TO THE REGULAR EXAMINATION ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO 
PARTICIPATE IN THE EXAMINATION.
        (2) SAILORS WHO GRADUATE FROM MANDATORY CLASS "A" SCHOOL BEFORE THE 
REGULAR EXAMINATION DATE AND ARE QUALIFIED IN ALL RESPECTS ARE ELIGIBLE TO 
COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT IN THEIR NEW RATING.
        (3) NON-DESIGNATED SAILORS IN INITIAL ENTRY TRAINING, TO INCLUDE "A" 
SCHOOL, WHO ENLISTED UNDER AN "A" SCHOOL GUARANTEE PROGRAM ARE INELIGIBLE FOR 
PARTICIPATION IN THE EXAMINATION UNTIL COMPLETION OF CLASS "A" SCHOOL.
     M.  BRANCH CLASS (BRCL) AND SPECIAL PROGRAM INDICATOR (SPI) CODE:  THE 
NAVY ACCOUNTING STATUS FOR BRCL 32 SAILORS MUST BE VERIFIED VIA THE COMMAND'S 
ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT (EDVR).  PERSONNEL WITH A BLANK SPI 
CODE ARE IN AN INVALID STATUS WHICH MAY RESULT IN ASSIGNMENT OF AN INCORRECT 
ADVANCEMENT COMPETITIVE CATEGORY AND THEIR EXAMINATIONS WILL BE INVALIDATED.  
COMMANDS MUST CORRECT THE ERROR VIA NAVY STANDARD INTEGRATED PERSONNEL SYSTEM 
(NSIPS) OR THE SAILOR MAY SEEK RELIEF BY PETITIONING THE BOARD FOR CORRECTION 
OF NAVAL RECORDS (BCNR).

    A.  ENSURE ALL CANDIDATES, ESOS, PROCTORS, AND ADMINISTRATORS HAVE
