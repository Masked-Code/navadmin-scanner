RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN provides guidance for fiscal year 2024 (FY-24) active 
component (AC) command master chief (CMDCM), master chief petty officer (E-
9), and senior chief petty officer (E-8) advancement (Cycle 257).  Unless 
exempted per reference (a), eligible E-9 candidates will participate in the 
Senior Enlisted Marketplace screening board.  Exempted E-9 candidates, as 
well as eligible CMDCM and E-8 candidates will participate in enlisted 
advancement selection boards. 
 
2.  Examination status verification reports (ESVR) will be available on the 
Navy Enlisted Advancement System (NEAS) website via MyNavy Portal (MNP) no 
later than 19 December 2022 
at https://www.mnp.navy.mil/group/advancement-and-  
promotion/command-information.  Select Command Advancement Tools, then 
Advancement Profile Sheet-ESO. 
 
3.  CMDCS Candidates for CMDCM Timeline: 
    a.  19 December 2022.  CMDCS rating change deadline. 
    b.  16 January 2023.  Letter to the Board (LTB) deadline. 
    c.  30 January 2023.  CMDCM selection board convenes. 
    d.  3 February 2023.  CMDCM selection board scheduled to adjourn. 
 
4.  E-8 and E-9 (In rate, both selection and screen) Timeline: 
    a.  6 February 2023.  Change of rating deadline. 
    b.  13 March 2023.  LTB deadline. 
    c.  27 March 2023.  Selection and Screen boards convene. 
    d.  21 April 2023.  Selection and screen boards adjourn. 
 
5.  Eligibility Criteria.  Candidates must meet and maintain the eligibility 
requirements outlined in chapters 2 and 11 of reference (b) and below, 
through adjournment of the board: 
    a.  The name of the candidate appears on the board eligibility list in 
the correct competitive group on their profile sheet.  Candidates should 
register for e-mail notification for profile sheet updates via MNP at 
https://www.mnp.navy.mil/group/advancement-and-promotion/my- 
advancement.  Select My Profiles, then Advancement Profile Sheet Information-
Individual.  Information may change on a weekly basis and both commands and 
Sailors should continue to verify. 
    b.  Terminal eligibility date: 1 October 2023. 
    c.  Time-In-Rate dates: 
        (1) For E-8:  On or before 1 September 2020. 
        (2) For E-9:  On or before 1 July 2020. 
    d.  High year tenure (HYT) date or approved HYT waivers: 1 July 2023 or 
later. 
    e.  Sailors with an approved or disapproved Fleet Reserve or retirement 
request prior to the board convene date will need to closely monitor 
eligibility.  Commands may request revalidation based on request adjudication 
by sending an e- mail with the reason for revalidation to PERS-802. 
    f.  Security clearance requirements (if required).  Refer to chapter 2 of 
reference (b). 
    g.  Rate specific and competitive group eligibility requirements.   
Candidates must meet special requirements for certain ratings, as outlined in 
paragraph 203 of reference (b). 
    h.  Non-nuclear submarine Sailors designated SG or SP as of board 
convening date are not eligible for advancement, in line with reference (c). 
    i.  Senior Enlisted Academy (SEA) completion requirement is held in 
abeyance.  Reference (d) will be revised with guidance for SEA requirements 
in future boards. 
 
6.  Competitive Groups.  The below listed ratings are separated into 
competitive groups as applicable to the component of the candidate: 
    a.  CTI:(E-8)  Middle East and North Africa (Group 1), East and Far East 
Asia (Group 2), Latin and South America (Group 3) and Eastern Europe (Group 
4). 
    b.  EMN, ETN and MMN: Submarine (Group 1), Surface (Group 2). 
    c.  ITS: Electronic Warfare (Group 1) Communications (Group 2). 
    d.  MU: Premier Band (Group 1), Fleet Band (Group 2). 
    e.  NC: Career Recruiter (Group 1), Fleet (Group 2). 
    f.  ND (E-8): Master Diver (Group 1), First Class Diver (Group 2). 
 
7.  For record review and communication with the boards, refer to reference 
(e).   Board numbers are: Board 211 (CMDCM), 210 (E-9 selection and Senior 
Enlisted Marketplace screen board), 235 (E-8).  LTBs should be submitted via 
the electronic submission of selection board documents (ESSBD).  If unable to 
use ESSBD and for more information on LTBs, visit the following 
link: https://www.mynavyhr.navy.mil/Career-Management/Boards/Active-Duty-
Enlisted/General-Information/ 
 
8.  Board results will be posted via BUPERS Online and released by subsequent 
NAVADMIN, with follow-on guidance for the MyNavy Assignments process for 
Sailors screened to compete in the marketplace.  Board selectee profile 
sheets will be published by NETPDC within 30 days of selection board results 
being released. 
 
9.  Additional information regarding this cycle will be posted 
on the MyNavy HR website at https://www.mnp.navy.mil/group/advancement-and- 
promotion/active-duty-enlisted.
