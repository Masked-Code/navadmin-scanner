RMKS/1.  The Chief of Naval Operations is pleased to announce the Office of 
the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI) Military Fellowship Program for 
2017-2018.  This 10 month fellowship starts in August 2017 and is located at 
ODNI in McLean, VA.  The fellowship will provide Information Warfare 
Community Officers (18XX) a unique opportunity to interact with leaders 
across the intelligence community, increase their understanding of policy 
development and decision making at the highest levels of government while 
integrating national security, governance, and information technology 
concerns.

2.  This program is open to 18XX officers with a Master's degree and has the 
following qualifications; post milestone or post command O-5 or any O-6.  A 
candidate must have completed Joint Professional Military Education Phase I 
prior to being selected or on schedule to complete by the end of this 
program.  Sample application package, additional eligibility requirements, 
service obligation, and general utilization tour requirements are listed in 
reference (a) and available on the Department of the Navy issuances website 
(http://doni.daps.dla.mil).

3.  Applications are due no later than 19 September 2016.  Completed 
applications must be sent via encrypted e-mail to both the OPNAV point of 
contact and the candidate's detailer.  Hard copy applications will not be 
accepted.

4.  Released by Vice Admiral Ted N. Branch, Deputy Chief of Naval Operations 
for Information Warfare, OPNAV N2N6.//
