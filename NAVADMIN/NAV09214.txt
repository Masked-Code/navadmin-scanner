RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN IS AN ANNOUNCEMENT OF THE ACTIVE AND RESERVE 
CAREER RECRUITER FORCE SELECTION BOARDS.  CAREER RECRUITER FORCE (CRF) 
WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1978 TO DEVELOP A CADRE OF EXCEPTIONAL RECRUITING 
MANAGERS TO PROVIDE CONSISTENCY AND LEADERSHIP TO THE RECRUITING EFFORT.  
IT IS MADE UP OF PROVEN, VOLUNTEER RECRUITERS WHO FILL KEY MIDDLE 
MANAGEMENT BILLETS.  
THE SELECTION CRITERIA FOCUS ON PROVEN RECRUITING PERFORMANCE WITH AN 
EMPHASIS ON EFFECTIVE LEADERSHIP, MANAGEMENT, COUNSELING AND TRAINING 
ABILITIES.  
2.  THE CRF IS OPEN TO PERSONNEL WHO ARE CURRENTLY ASSIGNED OR HAVE 
SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED A RECRUITING TOUR OF DUTY WITH NEC CODES 9585, 
9586 OR 9587.  FURTHER ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND APPLICATION PROCEDURES 
ARE LISTED IN REF A.
3.  THE CONVENING DATES FOR THE NEXT FIVE SELECTION BOARDS ARE AS 
FOLLOWS:
    A.  20 AUGUST 2009
    B.  12 NOVEMBER 2009  
    C.  18 FEBRUARY 2010  
    D.  20 MAY 2010  
    E.  5 AUGUST 2010
4.  ALL APPLICATIONS ARE REQUIRED TO BE RECEIVED NOT LATER THAN TWO 
WEEKS PRIOR TO THE CONVENING OF A SCHEDULED SELECTION BOARD.  
FORWARD PACKAGES TO COMMANDER, NAVY RECRUITING COMMAND,
5722 INTEGRITY DRIVE, BLDG 784, MILLINGTON, TN 38054-5057
(ATTN:  CRF PROGRAM MANAGER, N1115) OR SCAN AND EMAIL DIRECTLY 
TO MS. PAT FERGUSON AT PATRICIA.A.FERGUSON(AT)NAVY.MIL.    
5.  ALL CANDIDATES WILL BE NOTIFIED VIA OFFICIAL MESSAGE OF THEIR 
SELECTION OR NON-SELECTION. 
6.  SELECTEES CURRENTLY ON RECRUITING DUTY WILL BE CONVERTED 
IMMEDIATELY TO NAVY COUNSELOR (RECRUITER) (NEC-2186).  SELECTEES NOT ON 
RECRUITING DUTY WILL BE IMMEDIATELY ASSIGNED TEMPORARY NEC 2199.  FLEET 
PERSONNEL WILL TAKE CURRENT RATING NAVY-WIDE ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION 
UNTIL SUCCESSFULLY GRADUATING FROM THE CRF ACADEMY.  ALL SELECTEES WILL 
ATTEND THE CAREER RECRUITER FORCE ACADEMY IN PENSACOLA, FL, IN 
CONJUNCTION WITH PERMANENT CHANGE OF STATION ORDERS AT THEIR CURRENT 
PROJECTED ROTATION DATE.
7.  POINT OF CONTACT IS NCCM RICHARD THOMPSON, CRF PROGRAM MANAGER, AT 
(901) 874-9136/DSN 882 OR EMAIL AT RICHARD.E.THOMPSON2(AT)NAVY.MIL.
8.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
