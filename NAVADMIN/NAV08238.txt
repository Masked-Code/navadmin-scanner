SHOW" IN THE 2007 RADM WILLIAM THOMPSON EXCELLENCE IN PUBLIC AFFAIRS 
AWARD FOR THE POSTHUMOUS PRESENTATION OF THE MEDAL OF HONOR (MOH) TO LT 
MICHAEL MURPHY. THE OTHER RECIPIENTS OF CALENDAR YEAR 2007 RADM WILLIAM 
THOMPSON AWARDS ARE:
A. COMMUNITY RELATIONS, AFLOAT, LARGE
1ST PLACE - COMMANDER, 7TH FLEET - "PROJECT KAIBIGAN (MEANING 
FRIENDSHIP)"
B. COMMUNITY RELATIONS, SHORE, LARGE
1ST PLACE - COMMANDER, NAVAL AIR FORCES - "DISTINGUISHED VISITOR EMBARK 
PROGRAM"
HONORABLE MENTION - COMMANDER, NAVAL SURFACE FORCE, U.S. PACIFIC FLEET,

2. THE NAVY'S PUBLIC AFFAIRS ACTIVE-RESERVE INTEGRATION EFFORTS HAVE 
BEEN SUCH THAT THERE ARE FEW RESERVE-ONLY PUBLIC AFFAIRS ACTIVITIES.  
FOR CY 07 THERE WAS JUST ONE ENTRY FOR THE REAR ADMIRAL ROBERT A. 
RAVITZ AWARDS AND THAT ENTRY WAS AN OUTSTANDING AND WELL-EXECUTED 
PUBLIC AFFAIRS PROGRAM. THE WINNER OF THE CY 07 REAR ADMIRAL ROBERT A. 
RAVITZ AWARD IS:
A. SPECIAL PROJECTS, SHORE LARGE
1ST PLACE - US EUROPEAN COMMAND DET 0130 - "COMBINED ENDEAVOR."
3.  WITH THE SUCCESSFUL INTEGRATION IN NAVY PUBLIC AFFAIRS, THE CHIEF 
OF INFORMATION HAS DIRECTED THE CONSOLIDATION OF THE REAR ADMIRAL 
WILLIAM THOMPSON AWARDS WITH THE REAR ADMIRAL ROBERT A RAVITZ AWARDS 
STARTING WITH THE CY 08 AWARDS. A NEW INSTRUCTION HAS BEEN WRITTEN AND 
WILL BE RELEASED SEPCOR.
