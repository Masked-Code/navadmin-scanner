AMPN/REF A IS NAVADMIN 350/08 ANNOUNCING INSTALLATION OF SHIPBOARD 
NAVY INFORMATION APPLICATION PRODUCT SUITE (NIAPS) 2.1 TO PROVIDE A 
NON-INTERNET VERSION OF CMS/ID FOR ACCESS BY COMMANDS DISCONNECTED 
FROM THE INTERNET. 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES REMOVAL OF CAREER MANAGEMENT 
SYSTEM/INTERACTIVE DETAILING (CMS/ID) FROM SHIPBOARD NAVY 
INFORMATION APPLICATION PRODUCT SUITE (NIAPS).
2.  TWO YEARS OF RIGOROUS AT-SEA TESTING AND OBSERVATION ON BOARD 
NINE TEST SHIPS AND TWO AVIATION SQUADRONS REVEALED THE 
TIME-CRITICAL NATURE OF CMS/ID DATA EXCEEDS OPERATIONAL CAPABILITIES 
CURRENTLY SUPPORTED BY NIAPS.  THE MAJORITY OF JOB APPLICATIONS FROM 
OPTEST PLATFORMS WERE SUBMITTED TO CMS/ID VIA INTERNET VICE NIAPS. 
AS A RESULT OF THESE FINDINGS, HOSTING CMS/ID ON NIAPS WILL BE 
DISCONTINUED. 
3.  CMS/ID REMAINS AN EFFECTIVE AND RELIABLE CAREER TOOL AFLOAT AND 
ASHORE.  ALL COMMANDS ARE ENCOURAGED TO USE CMS/ID ON THE INTERNET TO 
SUBMIT JOB APPLICATIONS AND COMMUNICATE ASSIGNMENT DESIRES.  LIKEWISE, 
COMMANDS SHOULD NOT HESITATE TO ENGAGE WELL-ESTABLISHED ALTERNATIVES, 
SUCH AS REQUESTING ASSISTANCE FROM TYCOM/ISIC COUNSELORS OR FROM 
DETAILERS, WHEN INTERNET ACCESS IS NOT AVAILABLE.
4.  WE REMAIN COMMITTED TO DELIVERING AN EASY TO USE, INTEGRATED, AND
RESPONSIVE FAMILY OF CAREER MANAGEMENT, TRAINING, AND EDUCATION 
SYSTEMS, AVAILABLE TO SAILORS WHEREVER THEY SERVE. 
5.  SPECIAL THANKS TO THE OPTEST COMMANDS AND CREWS FOR THEIR E
XTENSIVE SUPPORT, SPECIFICALLY USS NIMITZ (CVN 68), CARRIER AIR WING 
ELEVEN, USS ANTIETAM (CG 54), USS PONCE (LPD 15), USS JOHN PAUL JONES
(DDG 53), USS LABOON (DDG 58), USS DONALD COOK (DDG 75), 
USS PINCKNEY (DDG 91), USS JARRETT (FFG 33), USS GARY (FFG 51), VAW 117, 
AND VFA 41.
6.  POINT OF CONTACT:  JOHN MCCLOUD, OPNAV N163, AT (703) 692-5029 OR 
EMAIL AT JOHN.MCCLOUD(AT)NAVY.MIL.
