RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN PROVIDES DETAILS ON THE GEV PROGRAM AND GUIDANCE 
FOR FY-09 PROGRAM APPLICATION.  ELIGIBILITY, OBLIGATED SERVICE 
REQUIREMENTS, AND AUTHORIZED PROGRAM BENEFITS ARE AVAILABLE IN REF A.

2.  GEV FUNDING IS ONLY AUTHORIZED FOR DEGREE PROGRAMS THAT MEET THE 
REQUIREMENTS OF AT LEAST ONE APPROVED NAVY SUBSPECIALTY CODE AS 
VERIFIED BY NAVAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL (NPS).  FOR FY-09, THERE ARE 136 
QUOTAS AVAILABLE, APPORTIONED AS FOLLOWS:  SURFACE WARFARE OFFICERS - 
51, SUBMARINE OFFICERS - 31, SPECWAR/SPECOPS OFFICERS - 2, AND AVIATION 
OFFICERS - 52.  FY-09 QUOTAS BY WARFARE AREA AND AREA OF STUDY ARE:

3.  OFFICERS INTERESTED IN THE FY-09 GEV PROGRAM SHOULD SUBMIT A 
WRITTEN REQUEST TO THEIR DETAILER USING THE FORMAT PROVIDED IN ENCL (1) 
OF REF A.  FOR SHIPS AT SEA, APPLICATIONS VIA NAVAL MESSAGE CONTAINING 
THE REQUISITE INFORMATION WILL BE ACCEPTED.

4.  OFFICERS COMPLETING A DEGREE USING GEV BENEFITS MUST FORWARD A 
FINAL TRANSCRIPT WITH DEGREE CONFERRED TO THE NAVAL EDUCATION AND 
TRAINING PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER AND SHOULD 
EXPECT TO SERVE ONE TOUR IN A SUBSPECIALTY BILLET NOT LATER THAN THE 
SECOND TOUR FOLLOWING GRADUATION.
