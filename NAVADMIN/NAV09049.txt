RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE APPLICATION PROCEDURES 
FOR THE FY-09 GRADUATE EDUCATION PLUS TEACHING (GET) PROGRAM.  
THE UNITED STATES NAVAL ACADEMY (USNA) IS SEEKING MOTIVATED 
UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) OFFICERS WHO ARE QUALIFIED 
ACADEMICALLY AND PROFESSIONALLY TO APPLY FOR THE FY-09 
GET PROGRAM TO SERVE AS BOTH TEACHERS AND ROLE MODELS FOR 
OUR FUTURE NAVAL OFFICERS.
2.  THE GET PROGRAM PROVIDES APPROXIMATELY 12 MONTHS OF 
GRADUATE EDUCATION AT EITHER A SELECTED CIVILIAN INSTITUTION 
IN THE BALTIMORE, MD/WASHINGTON, DC AREA OR THE NAVAL 
POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL (NPS), DIRECTLY FOLLOWED BY A 
TWO-ACADEMIC-YEAR TEACHING ASSIGNMENT AT USNA AS AN 
OFFICER-INSTRUCTOR, RESULTING IN A MAXIMUM 36 MONTH 
COMBINED TOUR.
3.  REF A PROVIDES SPECIFIC INFORMATION REGARDING 
AVAILABLE DISCIPLINES AND GRADUATE SCHOOLS, AS WELL AS 
APPLICATION GUIDANCE. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND 
ON THE USNA WEBSITE AT: 
HTTP://WWW.USNA.EDU/ACDEAN/OFFPOS/GET.HTML.
4.  COMMUNITY SPECIFIC ELIGIBLE YEAR GROUPS AND ADDITIONAL 
ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
    A.  SUBMARINE:
        (1) ELIGIBLE YG:  YG05
        (2) SATISFACTORY PERS-42 EVALUATION OF SOAC GATE 
AT 7 YCS
    B.  SURFACE:
        (1) ELIGIBLE YG:  YG05 - YG06 AND EARLY ROLLERS ON 
A CASE BY CASE BASIS DETERMINED BY PERS-41.
        (2) SURFACE WARFARE OFFICER CONTINUATION PAY 
CONTRACT MUST BE SUBMITTED IMMEDIATELY UPON SELECTION FOR 
THE GET PROGRAM.
5.  TIMELINE:
    A.  30 MAR 09:  APPLICATIONS DUE TO COMMANDER, NAVY 
PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-440F).
    B.  6 APR 09:  APPLICANTS INFORMED OF 
SELECTION/NON-SELECTION.
    C.  JUN - AUG 09:  SELECTEES REPORT TO GRADUATE SCHOOL 
BASED UPON CURRICULUM START DATES.
6.  POINT OF CONTACT IS CDR PAM HOLLAND AT
