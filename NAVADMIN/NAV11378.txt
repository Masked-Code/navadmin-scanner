RMKS/1.  THE ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP SELECTION BOARD IS  SCHEDULED TO 
CONVENE ON 9 FEBRUARY 2012.  ACQUISITION CORPS  MEMBERSHIP IS A PREREQUISITE 
FOR ASSIGNMENT TO ONE OF APPROXIMATELY  700 DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY (DON) 
CRITICALLY CODED ACQUISITION  BILLETS THROUGHOUT THE FORCE.

2.  POLICY AND GUIDANCE ON ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP FOR DON  OFFICERS IS 
UNDER REVIEW AND WILL BE UPDATED PRIOR TO CONVENING THE  BOARD.  APPLICATION 
PROCEDURES WILL REMAIN THE SAME.  THE MOST  UP-TO-DATE VERSION OF THE DON 
DAWIA OPERATING GUIDANCE MAY BE FOUND  AT THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE:
HTTPS://ACQUISITION.NAVY.MIL/RDA/HOME/ACQUISITION_WORKFORCE/STRATEGY_
POLICY/DAWIA_OP_GUIDE.
OFFICERS MUST BE A LCDR (O4) (WEARING O4 RANK) OR ABOVE TO BE A  MEMBER OF THE 
ACQUISITION CORPS OR SELECTED AS ACQUISITION CORPS  ELIGIBLE.

3.  APPLICATIONS (NO ENDORSEMENTS REQUIRED) MUST BE RECEIVED BY 9  JANUARY
2012 TO BE CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.  THE REQUIRED APPLICATION COVER  LETTER 
AND DATA SHEET ARE AVAILABLE FROM HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-
NPC/OFFICER/DETAILING/ACQUISITION/P
AGES/DEFAULT2.ASPX.
ALL APPLICANTS ARE REQUIRED TO PROVIDE INFORMATION AS DELINEATED IN THE SAMPLE 
DATA SHEET.  COPIES OF ORIGINAL COLLEGE TRANSCRIPTS ARE ACCEPTABLE TO DOCUMENT 
COMPLIANCE WITH THE BUSINESS ACADEMIC  REQUIREMENT.

4.  NAVY RESERVE COMMUNITIES INVOLVED IN NAVY ACQUISITION PROCESSES  SHOULD 
PRE-SCREEN ANY RESERVE OFFICER APPLICANTS FOR CONSISTENCY IN  ACCOUNTING FOR 
EXPERIENCE BEFORE FORWARDING THESE APPLICATIONS.
 THIS PRE-SCREENING DOCUMENTATION SHOULD BE INCLUDED WITH THEIR  APPLICATION.  
RESERVE EXPERIENCE CAN ONLY BE ACCRUED EITHER ON  INDIVIDUAL RESERVE 
ASSIGNMENTS CONSISTING OF 120 DAYS OR LONGER OR  FROM CIVILIAN ACQUISITION 
EXPERIENCE WHEN THE DUTIES AND  RESPONSIBILITIES MATCH THE PRIMARY CAREER 
FIELD LISTED ON THE APPLICATION.
