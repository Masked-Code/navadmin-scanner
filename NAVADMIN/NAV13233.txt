GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS MESSAGE APPLIES TO ALL NAVY PERSONNEL,
UNIFORMED AND CIVILIAN.  ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL REGIONAL
COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND
TENANT COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS FOR IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT.

2.  IN RESPONSE TO THE EVENTS THAT OCCURRED ON THE WASHINGTON NAVY
YARD, THE FOLLOWING GEOGRAPHIC AREAS OF INTEREST (GAOI) HAVE BEEN
ESTABLISHED:
    A.  WASHINGTON DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA.
    B.  VIRGINIA:
        (1) THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES AND CITIES IN THE STATE OF VA:
ALEXANDRIA, ARLINGTON, FAIRFAX, FALLS CHURCH, LOUDOUN.
    C.  MARYLAND:
        (1) THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES AND CITIES IN THE STATE OF MD:
CHARLES, MONTGOMERY, PRINCE GEORGES.

3.  PERSONNEL ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) REPORTING FOR THIS ORDER TO
ACCOUNT WILL BE CONDUCTED IMMEDIATELY AND WILL CONTINUE UNTIL THE
PERSONNEL ACCOUNTABILITY OBJECTIVE MEETS THE SATISFACTION OF USFF
AND DCNO (N1).  COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO EXECUTE PA TO 100 PERCENT
COMPLIANCE UPON PA COMMENCEMENT.  

4.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO:
    A.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL AFFECTED PERSONNEL, INCLUDING
APPLICABLE FAMILY MEMBERS.  COMMANDING OFFICERS (COS) AND/OR
COMMANDING OFFICERS REPRESENTATIVES (CORS) ARE TO DETERMINE ACCURATE
STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF THEIR PERSONNEL AND MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR
THEM AT CURRENT RESIDENCE, DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI
WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE,
SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER).  IN DOING SO,
AN ACCURATE ACCOUNTING OF ALL PERSONNEL IN ALL ACCOUNTING CATEGORIES 
CAN BE QUICKLY DETERMINED.
    B.  COS AND/OR CORS UTILIZE THE NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND
ASSESSMENT SYSTEM (NFAAS) SUPPORT SITE AT
HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL TO ACCOUNT FOR EACH INDIVIDUAL REQUIRED
TO BE REPORTED PER REFS (A) AND (B).
    C.  INDIVIDUALS OR FAMILY MEMBERS NOT ABLE TO CONTACT THEIR
COMMAND CAN LOG ON TO NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL AND CHECK
IN (MUSTER) ON THE NAVY FAMILY MEMBERS SITE.  IF UNABLE TO LOG ON TO
NFAAS, CALL THE NFAAS HELP DESK AT 866-946-9183.
    D.  IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, COMMANDS ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS
OF PA TO THE AFFECTED REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) OR DIRECT CHAIN
OF COMMAND.
    E.  DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS
COMPLETE.  REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED
INCLUDING THE POSTING OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES AREA ON THE
INDIVIDUAL'S ACCOUNTING LINE.

5.  NAVY FAMILIES ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO LOG INTO NFAAS,
HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL, TO UPDATE CONTACT INFORMATION AT THEIR
EARLIEST CONVENIENCE.  FOR FURTHER ASSISTANCE, CALL NAVY PERSONNEL
COMMAND EMERGENCY COORDINATION CENTER (NPC) EMERGENCY COORDINATION
CENTER (ECC)) AT 877-414-5358 OR E-MAIL NPC.CAT.CAPTAIN(AT)NAVY.MIL.
