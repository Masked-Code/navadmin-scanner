RMKS/1.  THE DIRECTOR OF NAVAL INTELLIGENCE TAKES PRIDE IN ANNOUNCING THE 2012 
REAR ADMIRAL THOMAS A. BROOKS INTELLIGENCE JUNIOR OFFICERS OF THE YEAR.  THESE 
AWARDS BEAR THE NAME OF REAR ADMIRAL THOMAS A.
BROOKS IN HONOR OF HIS CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE NAVAL INTELLIGENCE COMMUNITY.  
UNITED STATES FLEET FORCES COMMAND (N2/39), OFFICE OF NAVAL INTELLIGENCE AND 
NAVY EXPEDITIONARY COMBAT COMMAND HAD THE DIFFICULT TASK OF SELECTING THE 
FINAL NOMINEES IN THE AFLOAT, ASHORE AND EXPEDITIONARY CATEGORIES.  THE NAVAL 
INTELLIGENCE FOUNDATION WILL HONOR EACH SELECTEE WITH A COMMEMORATIVE WALL 
PLAQUE AND A FIVE-YEAR MEMBERSHIP IN THE NAVAL INTELLIGENCE PROFESSIONALS.

OF THE YEAR (AFLOAT) IS AWARDED TO:  LT KREG A. LESAGE, ELECTRONIC ATTACK 
SQUADRON ONE THREE EIGHT.

SEVEN, AND LT CHRISTOPHER R. LOPER, U.S. PACIFIC COMMAND JOINT INTELLIGENCE
