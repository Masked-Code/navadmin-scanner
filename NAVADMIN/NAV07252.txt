SUBJ/NOTICE OF CONVENING OF FISCAL YEAR 2008 (FY-08) SELECTIVE EARLY 
RETIREMENT (SER) BOARDS// 
RMKS/1.  CORRECTED COPY RELEASED DUE TO CHANGE IN BOARD CONVENING DATES 
TO 10 DEC 07 VICE 13 DEC 07. 
2.  THE SECRETARY OF THE NAVY APPROVED THE CONVENING OF FY-08 SER 
BOARDS, AS REQUIRED, FOR REGULAR CAPTAINS AND COMMANDERS ON THE ACTIVE 
DUTY LIST BY GRADE AND COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.  BOARDS ARE SCHEDULED FOR 
ONLY THOSE COMPETITIVE CATEGORIES AND GRADES WHICH CURRENTLY FORECAST 
INVENTORY OVERAGES.  THE BOARDS WILL CONVENE ON THE FOLLOWING DATE:
10 DECEMBER 2007 - CHAPLAIN CORPS CAPTAIN 10 DECEMBER 2007 - CHAPLAIN 
CORPS COMMANDER 
3.  THE FOLLOWING OFFICERS MAY BE CONSIDERED FOR SER:
    A.  CAPTAINS (O-6).  OFFICERS WHO HAVE DATES OF RANK ON OR BEFORE 1 
DEC 03 AND WHO ARE WITHIN THE ZONE DEFINED IN PARA 4 FOR THEIR 
COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.
    B.  COMMANDERS (O-5). OFFICERS WHO HAVE FAILED OF SELECTION FOR 
PROMOTION TO THE GRADE OF CAPTAIN TWO OR MORE TIMES AND ARE WITHIN THE 
ZONE DEFINED IN PARA 4 FOR THEIR COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.
4.  THE FOLLOWING OFFICERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR CONSIDERATION BY THE 
SER BOARDS:
    A.  OFFICERS WITH APPROVED VOLUNTARY RETIREMENT REQUESTS,
    B.  OFFICERS WITH STATUTORY RETIREMENTS IN FY-08 OR FY-09, AND
    C.  OFFICERS WHOSE NAMES ARE ON A LIST OF OFFICERS RECOMMENDED FOR 
PROMOTION.

NUMBER, AND DATE OF RANK OF THE SENIOR OFFICER AND JUNIOR OFFICER WHO 
ARE ELIGIBLE FOR EARLY RETIREMENT IN THE RESPECTIVE COMPETITIVE 
CATEGORY.

6.  OFFICERS IN THE ZONES DEFINED ABOVE WHO ARE ON ACTIVE DUTY WHEN THE 
BOARDS CONVENE BUT DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS SPECIFIED IN 
PARA 2, OR ARE NOT ELIGIBLE PER PARA 3, WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED FOR 
EARLY RETIREMENT BY THE SELECTIVE EARLY RETIREMENT BOARDS.
7.  SELECTIVE EARLY RETIREMENT ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY REQUEST VOLUNTARY 
RETIREMENT AND EXCLUDE THEMSELVES FROM SER BOARD CONSIDERATION BY 
REQUESTING A RETIREMENT DATE OF 1 OCTOBER 2008 OR EARLIER.  REQUESTS 
FOR RETIREMENT DATES AFTER 1 OCTOBER 2008 WILL BE ACCEPTED; THESE 
REQUESTS WILL NOT BE APPROVED UNTIL AFTER SER BOARD RESULTS ARE 
AVAILABLE IN JANUARY 2008, AND THESE OFFICERS WILL BE ELIGIBLE FOR 
CONSIDERATION BY THE SER BOARDS.  REQUESTS TO WITHDRAW OR DELAY SER 
PROTECTED RETIREMENTS WILL LIKELY BE DISAPPROVED AS THESE RETIREMENTS 
ARE KEY FACTORS IN DETERMINING FINAL SER REQUIREMENTS.  ADDITIONAL 
INFORMATION ON FY-08 RETIREMENT POLICY AND APPLICATION PROCEDURES FOR 
SER ELIGIBLE OFFICERS WILL BE PROMULGATED BY SEPARATE NAVADMIN.
8.  BY LAW, THE NUMBER OF CAPTAINS AND COMMANDERS SELECTED FOR 
RETIREMENT WITHIN EACH COMPETITIVE CATEGORY AND GRADE MAY NOT EXCEED 30 
PERCENT OF THOSE ELIGIBLE OFFICERS CONSIDERED BY THE BOARDS.
9.  ONLY ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY COMMUNICATE WITH A SELECTION BOARD.
CORRESPONDENCE MUST ARRIVE NOT LATER THAN THE DAY BEFORE THE CONVENING 
DATE OF THE BOARD AND SHALL BE ADDRESSED TO:  NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND
(NPC) CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER, PRESIDENT, FY-08 ACTIVE DUTY (GRADE) 
(COMPETITIVE CATEGORY) SELECTIVE EARLY RETIREMENT BOARD, BOARD #XXX 
(BOARD NUMBER IS A REQUIRED FIELD AND MAY BE FOUND ON THE SELECTION 
BOARD SCHEDULE LOCATED AT 
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/SELECTIONBOARDSUPPORT), 5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE, 
MILLINGTON, TN 38055.  TO CHECK ON RECEIPT OF YOUR PACKAGE, GO TO 
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/ACTIVEDUTYOFFICER, EMAIL THE CUSTOMER SERVICE 
CENTER AT CSCMAILBOX(AT)NAVY.MIL, OR CONTACT THE CUSTOMER SERVICE 
CENTER AT (866) 827-5672.  WRITTEN COMMUNICATION MAY BRING TO THE 
ATTENTION OF THE BOARD ANY MATTER CONCERNING HIMSELF/HERSELF WHICH THE 
OFFICER CONSIDERS IMPORTANT. THE WRITTEN COMMUNICATION MUST BE PER 
MILPERSMAN 1420-010 AND MAY INCLUDE, AS ENCLOSURES, CORRESPONDENCE FROM 
ANY INDIVIDUAL CONCERNING THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER.  CORRESPONDENCE NOT 
ORIGINATED BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER, INCLUDING ENDORSEMENTS TO THE 
OFFICER'S LETTER, AND LETTERS WRITTEN ON BEHALF OF THAT OFFICER, MUST 
CONTAIN A WRITTEN ACKNOWLEDGEMENT BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER STATING THAT 
HE OR SHE DESIRES SUCH CORRESPONDENCE BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD. 
CORRESPONDENCE WITHOUT SUCH ACKNOWLEDGEMENT IS CONSIDERED THIRD PARTY 
CORRESPONDENCE AND WILL BE RETURNED.  CLASSIFIED CORRESPONDENCE WILL 
NOT BE ACCEPTED.
10.  OFFICERS SELECTED FOR EARLY RETIREMENT WILL BE FORMALLY NOTIFIED 
AS SOON AS POSSIBLE AFTER THE BOARDS? PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED. 
OFFICERS SELECTED FOR EARLY RETIREMENT ARE REQUIRED BY LAW TO BE 
RETIRED, IF RETIREMENT ELIGIBLE, ON THE FIRST DAY OF THE SEVENTH MONTH 
AFTER THE MONTH IN WHICH THE BOARD REPORT IS APPROVED.
FOR THE FY-08 SER, THIS MANDATORY RETIREMENT DATE IS ANTICIPATED TO BE
1 AUGUST 2008.
11.  THIS NAVADMIN IS CANCELLED FOR RECORD PURPOSES ON 31 MARCH 2008.
