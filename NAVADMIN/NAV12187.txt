SUBJ/2012 NATIONAL NAVAL OFFICERS ASSOCIATION PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND 
TRAINING CONFERENCE//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE NATIONAL NAVAL OFFICERS  ASSOCIATION 
(NNOA) PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING CONFERENCE  BEING HELD 23-27 JULY 
2012 AT THE ROOSEVELT HOTEL NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA.  

2.  NNOA FOSTERS THE ADVANCEMENT AND RECOGNITION OF MINORITY OFFICERS IN THE 
UNIFORMED NAVAL SERVICES.  THE 2012 CONFERENCE THEME IS "NNOA:  CELEBRATING 40 
YEARS OF SEA SERVICE, ENHANCING DIVERSITY IN THE SEA SERVICES." 

3.  THE UNIFORM WILL BE SUMMER WHITE FOR NAVY PERSONNEL AND BUSINESS ATTIRE 
FOR CIVILIANS.  NAVY UNIFORM FOR THE FORMAL AWARDS BANQUET WILL BE DINNER 
DRESS WHITE JACKET (OR OPTIONAL SERVICE DRESS WHITE FOR O3 AND BELOW) AND 
BLACK TIE (OR EQUIVALENT) FOR CIVILIANS ANDGUESTS.

4.  AUTHORIZATION TO EXPEND APPROPRIATED FUNDS FOR ATTENDANCE OR SUPPORT AT 
THIS CONFERENCE IS PENDING OSD APPROVAL AND WILL BE PROMULGATED AT A LATER 
DATE.   

5.  REGISTRATION, TRANSPORTATION, AND LODGING INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND ON THE 
NNOA WEBSITE AT WWW.NNOA.ORG.  CONFERENCE FEES ARE $350 FOR LIFE MEMBERS, $375 
FOR MEMBERS, AND $475 FOR NON-MEMBERS.  AN ADDITIONAL FEE WILL BE APPLIED FOR 
ATTENDEES WHO REGISTER AFTER

7 JULY 2012.  NNOA IS WAIVING REGISTRATION FEES FOR THE FIRST 100 JUNIOR 
OFFICERS (LIEUTENANT COMMANDER AND BELOW).  OFFICERS WHO ARE PLANNING TO 
ATTEND SHOULD REGISTER EARLY FOR THE CONFERENCE AND THE HOTEL TO ENSURE 
RESERVATIONS ARE AVAILABLE.  

6.  POINTS OF CONTACT ARE:  
    A.  CAPT JOEL PARKER AT (301) 400-0563 OR VIA E-MAIL AT
NAVY-REP(AT)NNOA.ORG OR JOEL.PARKER(AT)MED.NAVY.MIL.
    B.  LCDR BEULAH HENDERSON (RET) AT (703) 231-8554 OR VIA 
E-MAIL AT EA(AT)NNOA.ORG 

7.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
