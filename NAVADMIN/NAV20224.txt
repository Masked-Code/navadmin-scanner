SUBJ/ESTABLISHMENT OF NAVY FOREIGN MATERIEL PROGRAM DISTRIBUTION LIST FOR 
FORMAL CORRESPONDENCE//

RMKS/1.  The Navy Foreign Materiel Program (NFMP) mission is to enhance 
survivability, lethality, and force protection of the U.S. Navy through 
analysis, testing, evaluation, and documentation of foreign materiel through 
Scientific and Technical Intelligence.  NFMP assesses capabilities and 
vulnerabilities, and the evaluation of operational performance against U.S.
systems and countermeasures (Research, Development, Test and Evaluation).

2.  To better serve our Navy community, NFMP is creating a Navy-wide 
distribution list for NFMP communication with Navy stakeholders for formal 
correspondence.

3.  To be included on the NFMP distribution list, please provide your name, 
Secret Internet Protocol Router (SIPR) network and Joint Worldwide 
Intelligence Communications Systems (JWICS) e-mail, and a brief justification 
paragraph to OPNAV N2N6 NFMP Requirements Manager, SIPR:  NIA-
X_NFMP(at)navy.smil.mil;
JWICS:  fmacollect(at)nmic.ic.gov.

4.  Released by VADM Jeffrey E. Trussler, Deputy Chief of Naval Operations 
for Information Warfare, OPNAV N2N6.//
