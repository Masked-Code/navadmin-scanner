RMKS/1. THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE FISCAL YEAR 2007 CPO
CONSTITUTION LEADERSHIP TRAINING WEEKS AND TURNAROUND CRUISE. THE
USS CONSTITUTION, LOCATED IN BOSTON MASSACHUSETTS WILL HOST CPO
SELECTEES FROM 21-25 AUGUST AND 28 AUGUST - 1 SEPTEMBER 2006.

2. CPO SELECTEES WILL LIVE ABOARD "OLD IRONSIDES" FOR FIVE DAYS AND
FOUR NIGHTS. THEY WILL LEARN HOW TO SET THE SAILS, FIRE THE 19TH
CENTURY GUNS, AND CULMINATE THEIR HISTORICAL TRAINING WITH A TWO
HOUR UNDERWAY PERIOD.

3. THERE ARE 300 TOTAL QUOTAS AVAILABLE, DUE TO LIMITED CAPACITY,
ONLY 150 SELECTEES AND 15 MENTORING CHIEFS ARE AUTHORIZED PER WEEK.
PER REQUIREMENTS PREVIOUSLY SET, REQUEST FLEET, FORCE, AND
CNO-DIRECTED MASTER CHIEFS PROVIDE QUOTA REQUESTS TO THE OPNAV
COMMAND MASTER CHIEF'S OFFICE VIA EMAIL TO STEVEN.H.HALL@NAVY.MIL.
PROVIDE REQUESTS NOT LATER THAN 7 AUGUST 2006.

4. TRANSPORTATION IS NOT PROVIDED FOR THIS EVENT AND SHOULD BE
COORDINATED THROUGH LOCAL COMMANDS AND FLEETS.

5. COST/LOGISTICS INFORMATION HAS BEEN DELINATED BY SEPARATE
CORRESPONDENCE TO THE FLEET, FORCE, AND CNO-DIRECTED MASTER CHIEFS.

6. RELEASED BY VADM A. E. RONDEAU, DIRECTOR, NAVY STAFF.//
