SUBJ/CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS FLEET DIVERSITY COUNCIL// 
RMKS/1.  THE CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS SEVENTH SEMI-ANNUAL FLEET 
DIVERSITY COUNCIL WILL BE HELD ON TUESDAY, 15 APRIL 2008 FROM 0800- 
PAGE 02 RUCOMFB0715 UNCLAS 1600, AT THE ADMIRAL KIDD CATERING AND 
CONFERENCE CENTER ON NAVAL BASE POINT LOMA.
2.  THE PURPOSE OF THE FLEET DIVERSITY COUNCIL IS TO EDUCATE SAILORS, 
OFFICERS, AND CIVILIANS ON THE VALUE OF DIVERSITY AND THE NAVYS MANY 
DIVERSITY INITIATIVES.  THIS COUNCIL MEETING SPECIFICALLY TARGETS 
PARTICIPATION FROM NAVAL BASE POINT LOMA, NAVAL BASE SAN DIEGO, AND 
NAVAL BASE CORONADO.  COMMAND FUNDED TRAVEL FROM OTHER AREAS IS 
AUTHORIZED.
3.  COUNCIL ATTENDANCE IS LIMITED TO 300 PERSONNEL.  YOUR ASSISTANCE IS 
NEEDED IN IDENTIFYING VOLUNTEERS TO PARTICIPATE IN THIS COUNCIL MEETING.  
VOLUNTEERS ARE SOUGHT FROM ALL RANKS, RATINGS, GS LEVELS, GENDER, AND 
ETHNICITIES.
4.  THIS COUNCIL MEETING WILL BE CONDUCTED IN TWO SEPARATE SESSIONS.
COMMANDING OFFICERS, OFFICERS-IN-CHARGE, EXECUTIVE OFFICERS, COMMAND 
MASTER CHIEFS AND CHIEFS OF BOATS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO ATTEND THE 
FIRST SESSION FROM 0800-1000.  ALL OTHER PERSONNEL SHOULD ATTEND THE 
SECOND SESSION FROM 1300-1600.  THE FIRST SESSION WILL FOCUS PRIMARILY 
ON LEADERSHIP INVOLVEMENT IN DIVERSITY AND SOLICIT FEEDBACK PAGE 03 
RUCOMFB0715 UNCLAS FROM SENIOR LEADERS.  THE SECOND SESSION WILL 
CONSIST OF GENERAL DIVERSITY TRAINING FOR ALL RANKS THROUGH BRIEFS AND 
DISCUSSIONS.
5.  TO ASSIST IN PLANNING, PLEASE SEND ATTENDEE NAMES TO BELOW POINT OF 
CONTACT AS SOON AS PRACTICAL.  COMMAND MANAGED EQUAL OPPORTUNITY 
OFFICERS AND EQUAL OPPORTUNITY ADVISORS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO 
ATTEND.  DRESS WILL BE UNIFORM OF THE DAY OR CIVILIAN EQUIVALENT.
FOR MORE INFORMATION ON CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS FLEET DIVERSITY 
COUNCIL AND OTHER NAVY DIVERSITY INITIATIVES, VISIT 
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/COMMANDSUPPORT/DIVERSITY/.
6.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LT MATT HOOKER, DIVERSITY DIRECTORATE (OPNAV 
N134), AT (703) 695-6203/DSN 225 OR EMAIL AT MATT.HOOKER (AT)NAVY.MIL.
7.  RELEASED BY MR. PATRICK MCLAUGHLIN, N1B.//
