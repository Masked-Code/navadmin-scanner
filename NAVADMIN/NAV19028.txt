RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the winners of the 2018 Community 
Service Health, Safety and Fitness Flagship Awards.  Each year the 
Navy recognizes various shore, sea, and overseas commands for 
outstanding community service projects by presenting awards in five 
flagship sponsor categories:  Personal Excellence Partnership, Health, 
Safety and Fitness, Campaign Drug Free, Project Good Neighbor and 
Environmental Stewardship.

2.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2018 Community 
Service Health, Safety and Fitness Flagship Award Winning Commands:
    a.  SMALL OVERSEAS WINNER:  Naval Airborne Weapons Maintenance 
Unit ONE, Guam
    b.  MEDIUM OVERSEAS HONORABLE MENTION:  USS GERMANTOWN (LSD 42), 
Sasebo, Japan
    c.  LARGE OVERSEAS WINNER:  U.S. Naval Hospital, Guam
    d.  MEDIUM SEA WINNER:  Helicopter Sea Combat Squadron TWO THREE, 
San Diego, CA
    e.  LARGE SEA WINNER:  USS FRANK CABLE (AS 40), Guam
    f.  LARGE SEA HONORABLE MENTION:  U.S. Naval Mobile Construction 
Battalion 3, Port Hueneme, CA
    g.  SMALL SHORE WINNER:  Center for Naval Aviation Technical 
Training Unit Lemoore, CA
    h.  MEDIUM SHORE WINNER:  Cryptologic Warfare Activity Sixty-Six, 
Fort George G. Meade, MD
    i.  MEDIUM SHORE HONORABLE MENTION:  Naval Air Station Whiting 
Field, Milton, FL
    j.  LARGE SHORE WINNER:  Navy Information Operations Command, 
Georgia, Fort Gordon, GA
    k.  LARGE SHORE HONORABLE MENTION:  Joint Base Pearl Harbor- 
Hickam, Pearl Harbor, HI

3.  Congratulations to all who participated in noteworthy efforts 
leading to this recognition.  I offer my sincere appreciation for your 
personal commitment and continued support of our Navy Community 
Service Program.

4.  All award winners will receive commemorative plaques and the 
honorable mentions will receive certificates from the Flag Sponsor, 
Commander, Naval Education and Training Command, in recognition of 
their exceptional accomplishments.

5.  Point of Contact is Ms. Ronda Gray, NETPDC N1C, at (850)473- 
6449/DSN 753, or via e-mail at ronda.gray(at)navy.mil.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral R. P. Burke, N1.//
