RMKS/1.  THIS MESSAGE APPLIES TO ALL ACTIVE DUTY AND RESERVE NAVY PERSONNEL 
AND APPLICABLE FAMILY MEMBERS.  ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL REGIONAL 
COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND TENANT 
COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS FOR IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT.

2.  THE FOLLOWING GEOGRAPHIC AREA OF INTEREST (GAOI) HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED FOR 
THE SIMULATED EARTHQUAKES IN CONJUNCTION WITH CR-17:
A.  THE STATE OF WASHINGTON
B.  THE STATE OF OREGON
C.  THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES IN THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA: IMPERIAL, RIVERSIDE, 
ORANGE, AND SAN DIEGO.

3.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO:
A.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL AFFECTED TO ALL ACTIVE DUTY/FTS, 
SELRES/DRILLING RESERVES, RESERVE PERSONNEL (AT/ADT/ADSW/MOB), NAVY CIVILIAN 
EMPLOYEES, NON APPROPRIATED FUNDS (NAF) EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES, CONUS 
CONTRACTOR AND ALL DEPENDENTS IN THE GAOI. ALL MEMBERS IN THE AFFECTED GAOI 
WILL RECEIVE NOTIFICATION FROM NFAAS VIA TEXT/EMAIL TO MUSTER THEMSELVES AND 
DEPENDENTS IN NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT SYSTEM (NFAAS). 
COMMANDING OFFICERS (COS) AND/OR COMMANDING OFFICERS REPRESENTATIVES (CORS) 
WILL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION OF REQUIREMENT FOR INDIVIDUAL SPONSOR MUSTER 
IN NFAAS.  IN CONJUCTION WITH INDIVIDUAL SPONSOR MUSTER, COS/CORS WILL 
DETERMINE ACCURATE STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF THEIR PERSONNEL AND MUSTER 
(PERSONNEL NOT MUSTERED IN NFAAS 24 HOURS AFTER EARTHQUAKE) AND ACCOUNT FOR 
THEM AT HOME, WORK, TELEWORK, EVACUATED, OR DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE 
GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, 
SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER).
B.  MEMBERS, COS AND/OR CORS UTILIZE THE NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND 
ASSESSMENT SYSTEM (NFAAS) SUPPORT SITE AT HTTPS:/ /NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL TO 
ACCOUNT FOR EACH INDIVIDUAL REQUIRED TO BE REPORTED PER REFS A AND B.
COMMANDS SHALL DIRECT MEMBERS TO INDIVIDUALLY MUSTER IN NFAAS.
C.  IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, COMMANDS ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS OF PERSONNEL 
ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) TO THE AFFECTED REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) OR DIRECT 
CHAIN OF COMMAND.
D.  DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS COMPLETE.
REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED INCLUDING THE POSTING 
OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES AREA ON THE INDIVIDUALS ACCOUNTING LINE.

4.  ALL INDIVIDUALS (ACTIVE DUTY/FTS, SELRES/DRILLING RESERVES, RESERVE 
PERSONNEL (AT/ADT/ADSW/MOB), NAVY CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES, NON APPROPRIATED FUNDS 
(NAF) EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES, CONUS CONTRACTOR AND ALL DEPENDENTS) ARE 
EXPECTED TO:
A.  MUSTER IN NFAAS, HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL B. IF UNABLE TO ACCESS NFAAS 
ACTIVE DUTY AND RESERVE NAVY PERSONNEL SHOULD CONTACT THEIR COMMAND MUSTERING 
OFFICER OR SUPERVISOR TO CHECK IN (MUSTER).
B.  INDIVIDUALS WILL PROVIDE COMMAND REPRESENTATIVES WITH CURRENT RESIDENCE, 
DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, 
TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/ DESERTER, OTHER).
C.  FAMILY MEMBERS CAN LOG ON TO NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL AND 
CHECK IN (MUSTER) ON THE NAVY FAMILY MEMBERS SITE.  IF UNABLE TO LOG ON TO 
NFAAS, CALL THE NFAAS HELP DESK AT 866-946-9183.

5.  THERE WILL NOT BE A NEEDS ASSESSMENT CONDUCTED FOR THIS EXERCISE.
