SUBJ/MODIFICATION OF RATES FOR NUCLEAR OFFICER BONUS AND INCENTIVE PAY//

REF/A/DOC/OPNAV/18MAY17//
AMPN/REF A IS OPNAVINST 7220.11F, NUCLEAR OFFICER BONUS AND INCENTIVE PAY.//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN promulgates a modification to enclosure (1) of 
reference (a).

2.  Effective 1 October 2018:
    a.  Submarine (1120) officers serving or who have served in Major Command 
with greater than 26 years commissioned service may request 1-, 2-, 3-, or 4-
year Nuclear Officer Continuation Bonus contracts.
    b.  1-year contracts will be paid at $35,000 per year.
    c.  For serving/served Major Commanders, 2-, 3-, and 4-year contracts 
will be paid at $45,000 per year.
    d.  For serving/served Major Commanders, 2-, 3-, and 4-year contracts 
will be paid at $45,000 per year if the term of the contract extends to 
statutory retirement or at least 24 months past the projected rotation date 
of the officer.  Shorter terms of obligated service will be paid at $35,000 
per year.

3.  The point of contact for questions regarding nuclear officer bonus 
incentive pay is LT Chase Cummins, PERS-421D, who can be reached at (901) 
874-3934/DSN 884 or chase.m.cummins(at)navy.mil.

4.  This NAVADMIN will remain in effect until a revision to reference (a) is 
promulgated.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral R. P. Burke, N1.//
