SUBJ/NOTICE OF CONVENING FY-07 OFFICER CONTINUATION BOARD
/ANNOUNCEMENT OF CONTINUATION POLICY//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  ACTIVE DUTY OFFICER CONTINUATION BOARDS WILL
CONVENE WHEN REQUIRED, IMMEDIATELY FOLLOWING CORRESPONDING FY-07
PROMOTION BOARDS.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE DUTY LIST WHO ARE
FULLY QUALIFIED TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY AND MEET THE REQUIREMENTS
IN PARAGRAPHS 2 THROUGH 4 WILL BE CONSIDERED FOR CONTINUATION.
WHERE APPLICABLE, CONTINUATION PERIOD IS BASED ON YEARS OF ACTIVE
SERVICE (YOAS) CALCULATED FROM THE ACTIVE DUTY BASE DATE TO
APPROXIMATELY SEVEN MONTHS AFTER APPROVAL OF THE PROMOTION BOARD
RESULTS.
2.  LIEUTENANT COMMANDER UNRESTRICTED LINE, RESTRICTED LINE, AND
STAFF CORPS (LIMITED DUTY OFFICERS DISCUSSED IN PARAGRAPH 3).
    A.  2XFOS LCDRS WITH 14-18 YOAS.  OFFICERS SELECTED FOR
CONTINUATION CAN CONTINUE UNTIL ELIGIBLE FOR RETIREMENT AT THE
COMPLETION OF 20 YOAS.
    B.  2XFOS LCDRS WITH LESS THAN 14 YOAS.  OFFICERS SELECTED FOR
CONTINUATION CAN CONTINUE IN THREE YEAR INCREMENTS OR UNTIL
RETIREMENT ELIGIBLE AT THE COMPLETION OF 20 YOAS.
    C.  2XFOS LCDRS WITH 18 OR MORE YOAS, AS PROVIDED BY LAW, WILL
NOT BE CONSIDERED FOR CONTINUATION, BUT WILL BE RETAINED ON ACTIVE
DUTY UNTIL RETIREMENT ELIGIBLE AT 20 YOAS.
3.  COMMANDER, LIEUTENANT COMMANDER, AND LIEUTENANT LIMITED DUTY
OFFICERS (LDO).
    A.  ALL PERMANENT LDOS SELECTED FOR PROMOTION IN FY-07 WHO ARE
SUBJECT TO STATUTORY RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THEIR SCHEDULED
PROMOTION DATES AND/OR PRIOR TO COMPLETING THE TIME-IN-GRADE
NECESSARY TO RETIRE IN THE NEXT HIGHER GRADE AFTER PROMOTION WILL BE
CONSIDERED FOR CONTINUATION.  THESE OFFICERS WILL BE CONTINUED FOR A
PERIOD SUFFICIENT TO ALLOW THEM TO ACCEPT PROMOTION AND, AS
REQUIRED, ATTAIN SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT THE HIGHER
GRADE.
    B.  ALL PERMANENT LDOS WHO ARE IN-ZONE FOR PROMOTION AND WHO ARE
SUBJECT TO STATUTORY RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THE END OF FY-06.
THESE OFFICERS WILL BE OFFERED CONTINUATION FOR A PERIOD SUFFICIENT
TO ALLOW THEM TO ACCEPT PROMOTION AND, AS REQUIRED, ATTAIN
SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT THE HIGHER GRADE (IF SELECTED
FOR PROMOTION) OR UNTIL 30 SEP 06 (IF NOT SELECTED FOR PROMOTION).
    C.  2XFOS LDO LCDRS SELECTED FOR CONTINUATION WILL BE CONTINUED
TO RETIREMENT ELIGIBILITY AT THE COMPLETION OF 20 YOAS.
4.  CHIEF WARRANT OFFICERS (CWO).
    A.  ALL CWOS SELECTED FOR PROMOTION IN FY-07 WHO ARE SUBJECT TO
STATUTORY RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THEIR SCHEDULED PROMOTION
DATES AND/OR PRIOR TO COMPLETING THE TIME-IN-GRADE NECESSARY TO
RETIRE IN THE NEXT HIGHER GRADE AFTER PROMOTION WILL BE CONSIDERED
FOR CONTINUATION.  THESE OFFICERS WILL BE CONTINUED FOR A PERIOD
SUFFICIENT TO ALLOW THEM TO ACCEPT PROMOTION AND, AS REQUIRED,
ATTAIN SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT THE HIGHER GRADE.
    B.  ALL CWOS WHO ARE IN-ZONE FOR PROMOTION AND WHO ARE SUBJECT
TO STATUTORY RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THE END OF FY-06.  THESE
CWOS WILL BE CONTINUED FOR A PERIOD SUFFICIENT TO ALLOW THEM TO
ACCEPT PROMOTION AND ATTAIN SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT
THE HIGHER GRADE (IF SELECTED FOR PROMOTION) OR UNTIL 30 SEP 06
(IF NOT SELECTED FOR PROMOTION).
    C.  CWO4 SELECTED FOR PROMOTION TO CWO5 WILL BE OFFERED
CONTINUATION FOR A PERIOD OF TWO YEARS PAST THEIR PROMOTION DATE IN
ORDER TO SERVE A TOUR AS A CWO5.
5.  OFFICERS SELECTED FOR CONTINUATION MAY REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY FOR
THE PERIOD SPECIFIED IN THE PRECEDING PARAGRAPHS UNLESS REACHING 62
YEARS OF AGE, AS STIPULATED BY LAW.
6.  OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE DUTY LIST WHO MEET ANY OF THE CRITERIA IN
PARAGRAPHS 2 THROUGH 4 SHALL CHECK BUPERS ONLINE, WWW.BOL.NAVY.MIL,
AFTER RELEASE OF THE RESPECTIVE SELECTION ALNAV MESSAGE, TO
DETERMINE IF THEY WERE SELECTED FOR CONTINUATION.  ONCE LOGGED INTO
BUPERS ONLINE, CLICK ON ADVANCEMENT, AND THEN ON THE APPROPRIATE
OFFICER (SELECTION/CONTINUATION) BOARD TO DETERMINE THE RESULTS.
7.  FY-07 PROMOTION BOARD MEMBERS ARE ALSO THE CONTINUATION BOARD
MEMBERS.  PROMOTION BOARD CONVENING DATES ARE AS FOLLOWS:
    A.  ACTIVE O6 LINE PROMOTION BOARD CONVENES 18 JAN 06.  THE
ACTIVE O5 LDO LINE CONTINUATION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    B.  ACTIVE O6 STAFF CORPS PROMOTION BOARD CONVENES 7 FEB 06.
THE ACTIVE O5 LDO STAFF CONTINUATION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    C.  ACTIVE O5 LINE PROMOTION BOARD CONVENES 14 FEB 06.  THE
ACTIVE O4 LINE CONTINUATION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    D.  ACTIVE O5 STAFF CORPS PROMOTION BOARD CONVENES 28 MAR 06.
THE ACTIVE O4 STAFF CONTINUATION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    E.  ACTIVE O4 LINE PROMOTION BOARD CONVENES 18 APR 06.  THE
ACTIVE O3 LDO CONTINUATION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    F.  ACTIVE O4 STAFF CORPS PROMOTION BOARD CONVENES 9 MAY 06.
THE ACTIVE O3 LDO STAFF CONTINUATION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    G.  ACTIVE CWO3/4/5 PROMOTION BOARD CONVENES 13 JUN 06.  THE
ACTIVE CWO2/3/4 CONTINUATION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
8.  CORRESPONDENCE ADDRESSED TO THE PROMOTION BOARD WILL NOT BE
FORWARDED TO A SUBSEQUENT CONTINUATION BOARD FOR CONSIDERATION.
OFFICERS MUST COMMUNICATE WITH EACH BOARD SEPARATELY.
9.  ONLY ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY COMMUNICATE WITH A SELECTION BOARD.
CORRESPONDENCE MUST ARRIVE NOT LATER THAN THE CONVENING DATE OF
THE BOARD AND SHALL BE ADDRESSED TO:  PRESIDENT, FY-07 ACTIVE DUTY
(GRADE) (COMPETITIVE CATEGORY) CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARD,
BOARD #XXX NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (NPC) CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER
PERS-00R, 5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE, MILLINGTON, TN 38055-0000.  (BOARD
NUMBER IS A REQUIRED FIELD AND MAY BE FOUND ON THE SELECTION BOARD
SCHEDULE LOCATED AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/SELECTIONBOARDSUPPORT)
TO CHECK ON RECEIPT OF YOUR PACKAGE, GO TO WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/
ACTIVEDUTYOFFICER AND ENTER THE CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER LINK (CHECK
ON YOUR LETTER TO THE BOARD) OR CONTACT THE CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER
AT (866) 827-5672.  THE WRITTEN COMMUNICATION MAY CALL ATTENTION TO
THE BOARD, ANY MATTER CONCERNING THE OFFICER'S RECORD THAT THE
OFFICER CONSIDERS IMPORTANT.  WRITTEN COMMUNICATION MAY INCLUDE, AS
ENCLOSURES, CORRESPONDENCE FROM ANY INDIVIDUAL CONCERNING THE
ELIGIBLE OFFICER. CORRESPONDENCE NOT ORIGINATED BY THE ELIGIBLE
OFFICER, INCLUDING ENDORSEMENTS TO THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER'S LETTER,
AND LETTERS WRITTEN ON BEHALF OF THAT OFFICER, MUST CONTAIN A
WRITTEN ACKNOWLEDGEMENT BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER THAT HE OR SHE
DESIRES SUCH CORRESPONDENCE BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD.
CORRESPONDENCE WITHOUT SUCH AN ACKNOWLEDGEMENT IS CONSIDERED THIRD
PARTY CORRESPONDENCE AND WILL BE RETURNED WITHOUT BEING PRESENTED TO
THE BOARD.  CLASSIFIED CORRESPONDENCE WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED.
10.  QUESTIONS REGARDING CONTINUATION SHOULD BE DIRECTED TO
COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4801C) AT (901) 874-4636/DSN 882.  ADDITIONAL
INFORMATION IS ALSO AVAILABLE AT WWW.BUPERS.NAVY.MIL/PERS8/
PERS-80/PERS80.HTM.
