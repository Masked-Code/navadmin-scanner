1421/7 (REV. 9-02).  USE THIS NAVADMIN AS REF A.

CONCERNING TRANSFER TO THE REGULAR NAVY.  DIRECT INQUIRIES TO 
COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4801G).

4.  OFFICERS CONSIDERING VOLUNTARY RETIREMENT SHOULD REVIEW SECNAVINST 
1811.3M FOR SERVICE OBLIGATION UPON ACCEPTING PROMOTION.
OFFICERS ANTICIPATING RESIGNATION OR RELEASE FROM ACTIVE DUTY UNDER
