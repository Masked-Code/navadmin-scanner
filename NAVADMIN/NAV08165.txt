RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES A NEW REQUIREMENT FOR OPREP-3 REPORTS 
OF DOMESTIC VIOLENCE INCIDENTS.  THIS CHANGE ENABLES THE NAVY TO 
MONITOR MORE CLOSELY MENTAL HEALTH ISSUES POTENTIALLY RELATED TO 
OPERATIONAL, EXPEDITIONARY, AND INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTATION DEPLOYMENTS.

2.  ACTION.  EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY, ALL OPREP 3 REPORTS OF DOMESTIC 
VIOLENCE INCIDENTS SHALL NOW INCLUDE THE DATE AND LOCATION OF THE 
SERVICE MEMBER'S MOST RECENT OPERATIONAL, EXPEDITIONARY, OR INDIVIDUAL 
AUGMENTATION (IA) DEPLOYMENT.
ADDITIONALLY, IF SERVICE MEMBER'S LAST DEPLOYMENT WAS AN EXPEDITIONARY 
OR IA DEPLOYMENT AFTER 19 MAR 04, ALSO INCLUDE WHETHER HE OR SHE HAS 
COMPLETED A POST-DEPLOYMENT HEALTH REASSESSMENT (PDHRA) BETWEEN 90 AND 
180 DAYS AFTER HIS OR HER RETURN AS REQUIRED BY REF B.

3. PER REF C, RETAIN THIS MESSAGE WITH ORIGINAL INSTRUCTION UNTIL 
CHANGES HAVE BEEN INCORPORATED INTO REF A.  A FORMAL CHANGE TRANSMITTAL 
WILL BE RELEASED WITHIN 90 DAYS OF THE RELEASE OF THIS MESSAGE.
