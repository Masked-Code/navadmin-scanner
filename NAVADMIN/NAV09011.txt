PROFESSIONAL USE.  FOR THE FIRST TIME, NAVY SAILORS NOW HAVE WEB ACCESS 
TO BOTH THEIR PERSONAL DATA RECORD THROUGH ESR AND THEIR OMPF THROUGH 
WERR.
