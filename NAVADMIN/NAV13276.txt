SUBJ/CHANGE TO NOTICE OF CONVENING NAVY RESERVE FY-15 LINE 
AND STAFF REAR ADMIRAL AND REAR ADMIRAL (LOWER HALF) SELECTION 
BOARDS//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces a change in convening dates for the
FY-15 selection boards to recommend Navy officers of the selected reserve
serving on the Reserve Active Status List (RASL) in the permanent grades of 
rear admiral (lower half) and captain, line and staff, for promotion
to the permanent grades of rear admiral and rear admiral (lower half).  
The boards will convened as follows:
     9 Dec 13 - Rear Admiral (lower half) Line and Full Time Support 
                (FTS)          
    12 Dec 13 - Rear Admiral Staff (Supply Corps and Judge Advocate 
                General's Corps)
    13 Dec 13 - Rear Admiral Line and Full Time Support (FTS)
    13 Dec 13 - Rear Admiral (lower half) Staff (Supply Corps)    
     3 Apr 14 - Rear Admiral (lower half) Line (Information Dominance  
                Corps)
2.  All other information and the eligibility zones announced in NAVADMIN 
222/13
remain current.  
3.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
