SUBJ/CALL FOR VOLUNTEERS TO SUPPORT INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTEE MINE COUNTERMEASURES 
CREWS//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE SOLICITATION FOR VOLUNTEERS TO MAN 
INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTATION (IA) MINE COUNTERMEASURES (MCM) CREWS TO PROVIDE 
SUSTAINED MCM SUPPORT FOR 5TH FLEET AREA OF RESPONSIBILITIES (AOR).  USFF IN 
COORDINATION WITH PACFLT WILL STAND UP TWO ADDITIONAL MCM CREWS THAT WILL 
REPORT TO SAN DIEGO FOR TRAINING, CERTIFICATION AND DEPLOYMENT.  THESE CREWS 
WILL BE MADE UP ENTIRELY OF INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTEES AND WILL BE KNOWN AS 
INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTATION MINE COUNTERMEASURES (IAMCM) CREWS.  IAMCM CREWS WILL 
RECEIVE FULL IA CREDIT AND DRAW TAX FREE PAY AS AUTHORIZED.
    A.  COMMANDS SHOULD PROVIDE NOMINEE INFORMATION TO USFF POC NLT
29 NOVEMBER 2012 TO INCLUDE:  NAME, RANK, SSN, UIC, PRD, EAOS, AND SECURITY 
CLEARANCE.  IF VOLUNTEERS ARE NOT FORTHCOMING, MANDATORY TASKINGS WILL FOLLOW.
    B.  COMMANDS WILL ENSURE IDENTIFIED INDIVIDUALS ARE SCREENED IAW CURRENT 
SEA DUTY SCREENING PROCEDURES AND HAVE ALL PRD, EAOS, AND FINAL SECURITY 
CLEARANCE ADJUDICATED AND RESOLVED PRIOR TO THEIR NOMINATION TO USFF (OVERSEAS 
SCREENING AND/OR EXPEDITIONARY SCREENING IS NOT REQUIRED).  THE MCMS FOR THIS 
MISSION ARE NOT CONFIGURED TO ACCOMMODATE FEMALE SAILORS, SO THIS OPPORTUNITY 
IS ONLY AVAILABLE TO MALE SAILORS.  DUE TO OPERATIONAL CONSIDERATIONS, FORWARD 
DEPLOYED NAVAL FORCES JAPAN IS EXCLUDED FROM THIS OPPORTUNITY.
    C.  PERSONNEL WILL REPORT TO NMPS SAN DIEGO ON 31 DECEMBER 2012 WITH 
FOLLOW ON ORDERS TO MCMRON THREE.  THIS IA ASSIGNMENT IS FOR A PERIOD OF UP TO 
365 DAYS.
    D.  REPORTING INSTRUCTIONS WILL FOLLOW SEPCOR.
