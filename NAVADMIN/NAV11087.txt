RMKS/1.  THE NAVY RESERVE FISCAL YEAR 2012 NATIONAL COMMAND AND SENIOR 
OFFICER NON-COMMAND BILLET SCREENING AND ASSIGNMENT "APPLY"
BOARD WILL CONVENE 5 AUGUST 2011.  ALL SELECTED RESERVE OFFICER 
COMMUNITIES WILL UTILIZE THE "APPLY" BOARD PROCESS FOR SENIOR OFFICER 
AND COMMAND ASSIGNMENTS.

2.  OFFICERS MUST REGISTER ON THE "APPLY" WEBSITE AND VALIDATE CURRENT 
ASSIGNMENT AND PERSONAL INFORMATION PRIOR TO 16 MAY 2011.

3.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS DESIRING A BILLET ASSIGNMENT ARE REQUIRED TO 
COMPLETE ALL APPLICABLE SECTIONS OF THE "APPLY" APPLICATION.
APPLICATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED THROUGH THE "APPLY" WEBSITE AT 
HTTPS://PRIVATE.NAVYRESERVE.NAVY.MIL/APPLY/INDEX.ASPX.  OFFICERS SHOULD 
INCLUDE BILLET PREFERENCES WITHIN THEIR DESIGNATOR TO MEET COMMUNITY 
FILL REQUIREMENTS.  ALTERNATE BILLET SELECTIONS MAY ALSO BE INCLUDED TO 
ENHANCE THE OPPORTUNITY FOR ASSIGNMENT.  MEMBERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED 
TO STATE THEIR WILLINGNESS TO TRAVEL FOR BILLETS NOT ON THEIR DREAM 
SHEET TO ENABLE THE BOARD TO SELECT THEM FOR UNADVERTISED VACANCIES.

4.  CORRESPONDENCE TO THE BOARD MUST BE RECEIVED NO LATER THAN 1900
(CST) 25 JULY 2011.  FITREPS WILL BE ACCEPTED UNTIL THE CONVENING DATE 
FOR EACH PANEL.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE FINALIZED AND BILLET PREFERENCES 
MADE NO LATER THAN 2359 (EST) 29 JULY 2011.

5.  OFFICERS WHO ARE NOT AFFILIATED WITH THE RESERVE COMPONENT IN A 
DRILL STATUS MUST CONTACT COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12) AT 
CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL TO REQUEST AN ACCOUNT.

6.  OFFICERS WHO ARE MOBILIZED AND UNABLE TO REGISTER IN "APPLY"
DUE TO LIMITED INTERNET CONNECTIVITY SHOULD SEEK ASSISTANCE THROUGH 
THEIR SUPPORTED COMMAND OR CONTACT COMNAVRESFOR (N12) FOR ASSISTANCE AT 
CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL.

7.  OFFICERS MUST BE QUALIFIED PER REF A TO BE CONSIDERED FOR AN 
ASSIGNMENT.

8.  OFFICERS NOT BEING CONSIDERED FOR BILLET ASSIGNMENT ARE ENCOURAGED 
TO SERVE IN A BOARD MEMBERSHIP OR SUPPORT POSITION.
SUBMIT BOARD MEMBERSHIP OR SUPPORT APPLICATIONS VIA THE "APPLY"
WEBSITE BY 31 MARCH 2011.

9.  "APPLY" AWARDED ASSIGNMENTS ARE ASSUMED TO HAVE BEEN ACCEPTED BY 
SELECTED OFFICERS.  OFFICERS WHO DESIRE TO DECLINE AWARDED BILLETS MUST 
DO SO WITHIN 14 DAYS FOLLOWING POSTING OF OFFICIAL RESULTS ON THE 
"APPLY" WEBSITE.

10.  THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF ALL ORDERS FROM THIS BOARD IS 1 DECEMBER 
2011.  ORDERS MAY NOT BE EXECUTED EARLIER THAN 1 DECEMBER 2011 UNLESS 
APPROVED BY COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12).

11.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR CHRISTIAN GASKILL, (APPLY/OFFICER 
ASSIGNMENTS, COMNAVRESFOR (N122)) AT (757) 322-2259/DSN 262, OR VIA E-
MAIL CHRISTIAN.GASKILL(AT)NAVY.MIL.

12.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
