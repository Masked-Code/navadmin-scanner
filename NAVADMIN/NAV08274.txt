CONDUCTING MORE THAN 211 MISHAP-FREE FLIGHT HOURS DURING DEPLOYMENT IN 
THIRD, FIFTH, AND SEVENTH FLEETS AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY DURING 
CALENDAR YEAR 2007, USS DUBUQUE CONDUCTED 1,009 DAY LANDINGS,
578 NIGHT LANDINGS, TRANSFERRED 486 PASSENGERS, AND TRANSFERRED 128 
TONS OF CARGO.  OF PARTICULAR NOTE, THIS IS DUBUQUE'S SECOND 
CONSECUTIVE AWARD.
