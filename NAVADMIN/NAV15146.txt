RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the FY-16 Navy Medicine Commanding Officer 
(CO) and Executive Officer (XO) Screening Board scheduled to be convened by 
Commander, Navy Personnel Command (NAVPERSCOM) on 23 September 2015.

2.  Background.  CO and XO positions are leadership positions defined by 
NAVPERSCOM, Medical Department Assignments (PERS-4415) and the Bureau of 
Medicine and Surgery (BUMED) Council of Corps Chiefs and approved by the 
Surgeon General.  These positions require special expertise, experience, and 
a documented career progression that prepares an officer for these 
responsibilities.

3.  References (a) through (d) outline application procedures, executive 
medicine required criteria, letter templates, and forms which are posted on 
the Navy Medicine website at 
www.med.navy.mil/directives/Pages/BUMEDNotes.aspx/.

4.  Deadline for application to reach PERS-4415 is 31 July 2015. Late or 
incomplete applications will not be considered.

5.  Point of contact is Ms. Gabriele Farmer at (901) 874-4097/DSN 882 or via 
e-mail at gabriele.farmer(at)navy.mil.

6.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or 30 May 2016, 
whichever occurs first.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
