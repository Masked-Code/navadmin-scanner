RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE CONVENING OF THE FY14 E8/E9 SELECTION 
BOARDS FOR NAVY RESERVE PERSONNEL ON 25 MARCH 2013.  THIS BOARD WILL CONSIDER 
PERSONNEL SERVING ON ACTIVE DUTY AS FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS), AND SELECTED 
RESERVE (SELRES), TO INCLUDE THOSE SERVING ON VOLUNTARY AND INVOLUNTARY RECALL 
AND MOBILIZATION ORDERS.
INDIVIDUAL READY RESERVE (IRR) PERSONNEL WHO ARE MOBILIZED ARE CONSIDERED 
UNDER THE SELRES REQUIREMENTS FOR ADVANCEMENT.  THE FY14
E9 AND E8 SELECTION BOARDS FOR ACTIVE DUTY PERSONNEL WILL CONVENE
15 APRIL 2013 AND 13 MAY 2013, RESPECTIVELY.  PARAGRAPH 11 OF THIS NAVADMIN 
CONTAINS A LIST OF KEY DATES AND EVENTS FOR QUICK REFERENCE.

2.  TIMELINE OF EVENTS
    A.  25 OCTOBER 2012:  COMMANDER, NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND
(COMNAVPERSCOM) (PERS-802) COMPILES AND SUBMITS INITIAL TIME-IN-RATE
(TIR) ELIGIBILITY LIST OF CANDIDATES TO NAVAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING 
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER (NETPDTC) (N321).
    B.  21 NOVEMBER 2012:  E8/E9 EXAMINATION STATUS VERIFICATION REPORTS 
(ESVR) WILL BE ACCESSIBLE BY EDUCATION SERVICE OFFICERS
(ESOS) AND COMMAND DESIGNATED PERSONNEL VIA NETPDTC'S NAVY ENLISTED 
ADVANCEMENT SYSTEM (NEAS) WEBSITE, HTTPS://NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL/.
THE ESVR IS THE SOLE SOURCE FOR E7/E8 TIR ELIGIBILITY VERIFICATION AND WILL BE 
AVAILABLE AS FOLLOWS:
        (1) FTS/SELRES E8/E9:  21 NOVEMBER 2012 - 24 MARCH 2013
        (2) ACTIVE E9:  21 NOVEMBER 2012 - 14 APRIL 2013
        (3) ACTIVE E8:  21 NOVEMBER 2012 - 12 MAY 2013
    C.  ESOS AND COMMAND DESIGNATED PERSONNEL MUST VERIFY THE ESVR FOR 
ACCURACY AND RE-VERIFY WEEKLY THEREAFTER UNTIL THE SCHEDULED BOARD CONVENING 
DATE, AND IF NECESSARY, TAKE THE FOLLOWING ACTIONS AS PROVIDED FOR IN REF A, 
PARA 615.
        (1) ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES NOT LISTED:  MAIL A COMPLETED ANSWER SHEET, 
INCLUDING BLOCKS 6 AND 13, (NETPDTC 1430/2) OR A COMPLETED WORKSHEET (NETPDTC 
1430/3) WITH A LETTER OF TRANSMITTAL TO NETPDTC (N321).
        (2) INELIGIBLE OR NOT RECOMMENDED PERSONNEL ARE LISTED:
SUBMIT NAVAL MESSAGE NOTIFICATION TO NETPDTC (N321) AND INFO COMNAVPERSCOM 
(PERS-802/812) REQUESTING INVALIDATION OF ELIGIBILITY STATUS AND PROVIDE 
EXPLANATION FOR INVALIDATION.  AS PROVIDED FOR IN REF A, PARA 721, WITHDRAWAL 
OF ADVANCEMENT RECOMMENDATION MUST BE COMPLETED AND DOCUMENTED PRIOR TO NAVAL 
MESSAGE TRANSMISSION.
        (3) CANDIDATES VALIDATED IN WRONG COMPETITIVE CATEGORY:
SUBMIT NAVAL MESSAGE NOTIFICATION TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-802/812) DETAILING 
THE DISCREPANCY.  PERS WILL RESEARCH AND COMMUNICATE RESOLUTION.
        (4) INDIVIDUAL CANDIDATES MAY VIEW THEIR SELECTION BOARD PROFILE SHEET 
AT NAVY KNOWLEDGE ONLINE, HTTPS://WWWA.NKO.NAVY.MIL.
UNDER THE CAREER MANAGEMENT TAB SELECT "NAVY ADVANCEMENT CENTER."
FROM THE NAVY ADVANCEMENT CENTER PAGE, SELECT "ADVANCEMENT PROFILE SHEET."  
PER REF A, PARA 102, CANDIDATES SHARE EQUAL RESPONSIBILITY WITH THEIR PARENT 
COMMANDS IN ENSURING THEIR NAME APPEARS ON THE SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBILITY 
LIST.  AFTER EXHAUSTING LOCAL RESOURCES, IF THE CANDIDATE'S NAME STILL DOES 
NOT APPEAR ON THE ELIGIBILITY LIST, CONTACT PERS-802/812 PRIOR TO THE BOARD 
CONVENING.  FAILURE TO DO SO MAY AFFECT APPROVAL OF SPECIAL SELECTION BOARD 
REQUESTS.
    D.  MARCH 2013 - MAY 2013:  SELECTION BOARDS CONVENE.

3.  ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
    A.  SAILORS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION BY THE ENLISTED RETENTION BOARD 
(ERB) ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR FUTURE ACTIVE DUTY ADVANCEMENT AND WILL NOT BE 
SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBLE IN CYCLE 217.
    B.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS OUTLINED IN REF A, 
CHAPTER 2, NOT LATER THAN 1 NOVEMBER 2012.
    C.  CANDIDATES WHO DO NOT HAVE A RECOMMENDATION FOR ADVANCEMENT ON THEIR 
MOST RECENT EVALUATION SHALL NOT BE VALIDATED.
    D.  TERMINAL ELIGIBILITY DATE (TED):  THE TED FOR CYCLE 217 IS
1 OCTOBER 2013.  THIS DATE SHALL BE USED TO COMPUTE AND DETERMINE TIR 
ELIGIBILITY IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF A, TABLE 2-2.
    E.  COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS:  CANDIDATES SELECTED FOR SEAMAN TO ADMIRAL, 
MEDICAL ENLISTED COMMISSIONING, AND MEDICAL SERVICE CORPS IN-SERVICE 
PROCUREMENT PROGRAMS REMAIN ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT CONSIDERATION TO SENIOR 
CHIEF AND MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICER.
SELECTION TO ANY OTHER OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS RENDERS CANDIDATES 
INELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT AND EXAMS THAT WERE ADMINISTERED MUST BE 
INVALIDATED BY THEIR COMMAND.
    F.  HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT):  PER REF B, ARTICLE 1160-120, CANDIDATES WHO 
REACH HYT ON OR AFTER 1 JULY 2013, OR WHO HAVE RECEIVED A HYT WAIVER TO REMAIN 
ON ACTIVE DUTY (OR REMAIN IN SELRES
STATUS) BEYOND 1 JULY 2013 ARE ELIGIBLE FOR CONSIDERATION BY THE SELECTION 
BOARD.  CANDIDATES WITH A HYT DATE PRIOR TO 1 JULY 2013 ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO 
COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT UNLESS THEY POSSESS AN APPROVED HYT WAIVER AND HAVE 
BEEN VALIDATED BY THEIR COMMANDS PRIOR TO THE CONVENING OF THE BOARD.
    G.  REQUESTS FOR TRANSFER TO THE FLEET RESERVE (FLTRES)/RETIRED
LIST:   VOLUNTARY FLTRES REQUESTS ARE THOSE NOT HYT MANDATED;
INVOLUNTARY FLTRES REQUESTS ARE THOSE SUBMITTED DUE TO APPROACHING QQQQ HYT 
FOR CURRENT PAYGRADE.  MEMBERS WHO HAVE SUBMITTED FORMAL WRITTEN VOLUNTARY 
FLTRES REQUESTS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT OR CONSIDERATION BY A 
SELECTION BOARD, ONCE THE REQUEST IS GENERATED AT THE COMMAND LEVEL.  MEMBERS 
WHO HAVE SUBMITTED INVOLUNTARY FLTRES REQUESTS DUE TO HYT ARE ELIGIBLE FOR 
ADVANCEMENT AND SELECTION BOARD CONSIDERATION PROVIDED THEIR HYT DATE FOR THE 
PRESENT PAYGRADE IS
1 JULY 2013 OR LATER.  REFER TO REF A, PARA 209, FOR GUIDANCE AND MANDATORY 
ADMINISTRATIVE REQUIREMENTS.  NOTE:  AN APPROVED OR PENDING FLTRES/RETIRED 
LIST APPLICATION MUST BE SUCCESSFULLY DISAPPROVED OR CANCELED PRIOR TO THE 
BOARD CONVENING TO RENDER A CANDIDATE SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBLE.  IF THE 
FLTRES/RETIRED LIST APPLICATION IS SUCCESSFULLY DISAPPROVED OR CANCELED, THE 
CANDIDATE'S COMMAND MUST REVALIDATE THE CANDIDATE PER PARA 2.C.(1) OF THIS 
NAVADMIN PRIOR TO BOARD CONVENING.
    H.  ENLISTED NAVY LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT:  REFER TO REF C.
    I.  SECURITY CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS:  ALL PERSONNEL IN CERTAIN RATINGS 
MUST, AT A MINIMUM, MAINTAIN CONTINUOUS SECURITY CLEARANCE ELIGIBILITY.  A 
FAVORABLE INVESTIGATION ADJUDICATION MUST BE ISSUED FROM DEPARTMENT OF THE 
NAVY CENTRAL ADJUDICATION FACILITY (DONCAF) OR A CO MUST GRANT TEMPORARY 
ACCESS, AN INTERIM CLEARANCE, PRIOR TO THE FIRST DAY OF THE MONTH OF THE 
RESPECTIVE BOARD CONVENING DATE, TO BE CONSIDERED BOARD ELIGIBLE.  CO'S MUST 
FOLLOW PROCEDURES FROM THE NAVY PERSONNEL SECURITY PROGRAM TO GRANT INTERIM 
ACCESS.  PERSONNEL IN THE FOLLOWING RATINGS WHO DO NOT MEET THIS REQUIREMENT 
ARE INELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT AND WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED BOARD ELIGIBLE:  AC, 
AE, AVCM, AG, AO, ATI, ATO, AWF, AWO, AWR, AWS, AWV, AZ, CTI, CTM, CTN, CTR,  
CTT, EMCM, EOD, ET, FC, FT, GM, HT, IC, IS, IT, ITS, LN, MA, MC, MN, MT,  ND, 
OS, QM, SB, SO, STG, STS AND YN, AS WELL AS ALL NUCLEAR TRAINED PERSONNEL.  
THIS GUIDANCE SUPERSEDES REF A.
    J.  BRANCH CLASS (BRCL) AND SPECIAL PROGRAM INDICATOR (SPI) CODE:
THE NAVY ACCOUNTING STATUS FOR BRCL 32 SAILORS MUST BE VERIFIED VIA  THE 
COMMAND'S ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT (EDVR).
 PERSONNEL
WITH A BLANK SPI CODE ARE IN AN INVALID STATUS WHICH MAY RESULT IN ASSIGNMENT 
OF AN INCORRECT ADVANCEMENT COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.
 COMMANDS
MUST CORRECT THE ERROR VIA NAVY
STANDARD INTEGRATED PERSONNEL SYSTEM (NSIPS) OR THE SAILOR MAY SEEK RELIEF VIA 
PETITIONING THE BOARD FOR CORRECTION OF NAVAL RECORDS (BCNR).  SAILORS IN THIS 
INVALID ACCOUNTING STATUS WILL HAVE THEIR EXAM INVALIDATED.
    K.  SAILORS NOT SELECTED TO CONTINUE FROM A SENIOR ENLISTED CONTINUATION 
BOARD ARE INELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT.

4.  SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR NUCLEAR TRAINED PERSONNEL:
    A.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E8, SUBMARINE SAILORS IN NUCLEAR 
RATINGS MUST HOLD A SUPERVISOR NEC (3363, 3364, 3365, 3366).
PERSONNEL MUST ALSO HOLD SECONDARY NEC 3302 (QUALIFIED NUCLEAR ENGINEERING 
DEPARTMENT MASTER CHIEF (EDMC)).  A NAVPERS 1221  REQUESTING A SECONDARY NEC 
OF 3302 MUST BE SUBMITTED TO OPNAV N133 BY 12 APRIL  2013.
E-MAIL THE NAVPERS 1221 AND A COPY OF QUALIFICATION CARD TO 
NXAG_N133D2(AT)NAVY.MIL.  ADDITIONAL CONTACT INFORMATION AVAILABLE  VIA 
HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-NPC/ENLISTED/COMMUNITY/NUCLEAR/PAGE
S/
NUCLEARENLISTEDPROGRAMS.ASPX.
    B.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E8, SURFACE SAILORS IN  NUCLEAR 
RATINGS MUST HOLD A SUPERVISOR NEC (3393, 3394, 3395, 3396).
    C.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E9, SUBMARINE SAILORS IN NUCLEAR 
RATINGS MUST HOLD A SUPERVISOR NEC (3363, 3364, 3365, 3366).
PERSONNEL MUST ALSO HOLD SECONDARY NEC 3302 AND HAVE SERVED OR BE CURRENTLY 
SERVING AS AN ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT MASTER CHIEF (EDMC) OF  A SUBMARINE.  
ACCEPTABLE DOCUMENTATION OF EDMC SERVICE FOR REVIEW BY  THE ADVANCEMENT BOARD 
INCLUDES  AN EVALUATION WITH "EDMC" IN BLOCK 29 OR  A LETTER FROM THE 
COMMANDING OFFICER VIA THE MEMBER TO THE BOARD  PROVIDING DATES OF EDMC 
SERVICE.  SAILORS WITHOUT EDMC SERVICE DOCUMENTED IN  THEIR SERVICE RECORD 
MUST SUBMIT DOCUMENTATION WITH THEIR LETTER TO THE  BOARD
(LTB) PROVIDING DATES OF EDMC SERVICE.
    D.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E9, SURFACE SAILORS IN  NUCLEAR 
RATINGS MUST HOLD A SUPERVISOR NEC (3393, 3394, 3395, 3396).
 PERSONNEL
MUST ALSO HAVE SERVED, OR BE CURRENTLY SERVING AS, A DIVISION LEADING CHIEF 
PETTY OFFICER OF A NUCLEAR DIVISION AT SEA (REACTOR MECHANICAL, REACTOR 
ELECTRICAL, REACTOR CONTROLS, REACTOR PROPULSION, MECHANICAL,  OR REACTOR 
LABORATORIES), REACTOR TRAINING DIVISION OFFICER AT SEA, OR LEADING CREW CHIEF 
(LCC) OF A PROTOTYPE.  E8S WHO ARE BILLETED AS REACTOR DEPARTMENT MASTER CHIEF 
(RDMC) IN LIEU OF DIVISION LEADING  CHIEF PETTY OFFICER ARE ALSO ELIGIBLE FOR 
ADVANCEMENT TO E9.  ACCEPTABLE DOCUMENTATION OF LCPO, REACTOR TRAINING 
OFFICER, LCC, OR RDMC SERVICE FOR REVIEW BY THE ADVANCEMENT BOARD INCLUDES AN 
EVALUATION WITH  "DLCPO,"
"RT DIVO," "LCC," OR "RDMC" IN BLOCK 29 OR A LETTER FROM THE  COMMANDING 
OFFICER VIA THE MEMBER TO THE BOARD PROVIDING DATES OF DLCPO, RT QQQQ  DIVO, 
LCC, OR RDMC SERVICE.  SAILORS WITHOUT DLCPO, RT DIVO, LCC, OR RDMC SERVICE 
DOCUMENTED IN THEIR SERVICE RECORD MUST SUBMIT DOCUMENTATION  WITH THEIR LTB 
PROVIDING DATES OF DLCPO, RT DIVO, LCC, OR RDMC SERVICE.

5.  SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR NAVY DIVER (ND) PERSONNEL:  TO BE  ELIGIBLE FOR

6.  SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR EXPLOSIVE ORDNANCE DISPOSAL (EOD)
