SUBJ/FY-08 ACTIVE DUTY E7 SELECTION BOARD MEMBERSHIP NOMINATIONS//

GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN SOLICITS ACTIVE DUTY MASTER CHIEFS TO 
SERVE ON THE FY-08 ACTIVE DUTY CHIEF PETTY OFFICER SELECTION BOARD
THAT IS SCHEDULED TO CONVENE 25 JUN THROUGH 27 JUL 07.  EVERY MASTER 
CHIEF SHOULD VIEW THIS AS A DUTY AND OBLIGATION THAT THEY ARE EXPECTED 
TO FULFILL IN THE DEVELOPMENT OF 21ST CENTURY LEADERS.

2.  USE THE APPLICATION FORM WHICH CAN BE ACCESSED AT 
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/ACTIVEDUTYENLISTED/CPO+SELECTION+BOARDS.HTM  
NOMINATIONS SHOULD BE ROUTED THROUGH COMMANDING OFFICERS AND COMMAND MASTER CHIEFS FOR SUBMISSION TO COGNIZANT FLEET AND FORCE MASTER CHIEFS.

3.  CAREFUL CONSIDERATION SHOULD BE GIVEN TO A NOMINEE'S AVAILABILITY 
TO SERVE ON THE BOARD (INCLUDING DEPLOYMENT SCHEDULES AND ANY UPCOMING 
TEMDU/PCS).  COMMANDING OFFICER CONCURRENCE MUST BE OBTAINED PRIOR TO 
SUBMISSION.  COMMANDING OFFICERS AND MASTER CHIEFS MUST UNDERSTAND THAT, 
ONCE SELECTED, BOARD DUTY IS NOT OPTIONAL.  MEMBERS WILL NOT BE EXCUSED 
EXCEPT IN THE MOST EXTREME CASES.

4.  SELECTION BOARD MEMBERSHIP NOMINEES SHOULD:
    A.  NOT HAVE ANY RELATIVE, WHETHER BY BLOOD OR MARRIAGE, ELIGIBLE 
FOR CONSIDERATION BY THE BOARD.
    B.  NOT HAVE BEEN A MEMBER OF LAST YEAR S BOARD.
    C.  NOT BE FROCKED.
    D.  BE AT LEAST A MUST PROMOTE ON THEIR LATEST FITREP.
    E.  BE WITHIN NAVY BODY COMPOSITION STANDARDS AND HAVE PASSED THE 
MOST RECENT PRT/PFA.
    F.  NOT HAVE A FLEET RESERVE, RETIREMENT, OR HIGH YEAR TENURE DATE 
WITHIN ONE YEAR AFTER THE BOARD ADJOURNS.
5.  POINT OF CONTACT:  PSCM(SW) V. SMALLWOOD AT (901) 874-2245/DSN
882 OR EMAIL AT VICTOR.SMALLWOOD@NAVY.MIL.
6.  RELEASED BY VADM J. C. HARVEY, JR., N1.//
