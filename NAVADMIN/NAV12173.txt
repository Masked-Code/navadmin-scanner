COMMANDS, BUT IS ONLY PRESENTED TO ONE COMMAND REGARDLESS OF COMMAND SIZE. 
NOMINATION PACKAGES MUST HAVE AT LEAST THREE OF THE FIVE FLAGSHIP PROJECTS 
INCLUDED IN THE PACKAGE.

3. PER REF A, (CNIC) IS THE NAVY'S LEAD RESPONSIBLE FOR ADMINISTERING THE NAVY 
COMMUNITY SERVICE AWARD PROGRAM AND COORDINATING THE EFFORTS OF FLAGSHIP 
SPONSORS AND REGIONAL COORDINATORS.

4. EACH FLAGSHIP SPONSOR WILL CONDUCT AN AWARDS BOARD WITH JUDGES REPRESENTING 
ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, NAVY AND COMMUNITY-BASED ORGANIZATIONS. THE AWARDS BOARD 
WILL SELECT NAVY-WIDE WINNERS IN EACH CATEGORY AND FLAGSHIP. CNIC'S FLAGSHIP 
AWARDS BOARD WILL ALSO CONSIDER NOMINATIONS FOR THE USS BAINBRIDGE (CGN 25) 
AWARD.

5. GUIDANCE ON SUBMITTING NOMINATION PACKAGES:
A. ALL NAVY COMMANDS ARE ELIGIBLE TO COMPETE FOR THE 2012 NAVY COMMUNITY 
SERVICE AWARDS.
B. DEADLINE FOR COMMAND SUBMISSIONS IS 31 JULY 2012.
C. THE AWARD PERIOD FOR ALL CATEGORIES IS FROM 1 JULY 2011 THROUGH 30 JUNE 
2012.
D. COMMANDS MUST SUBMIT NOMINATIONS TO THE NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM 
GEOGRAPHIC REGIONAL COORDINATOR (GRC) USING THE FORMAT PROVIDED IN REF B. A 
FILLABLE PDF SUBMISSION DATA SHEET AND GUIDELINES ARE AVAILABLE THROUGH THE 
REGIONAL COORDINATOR.
E. NOMINATIONS CANNOT EXCEED TEN PAGES IN LENGTH, TO CONSIST OF FIVE PAGES OF 
NOMINATION INFORMATION AND FIVE PAGES OF SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS SUCH AS NEWS 
CLIPPINGS, LETTERS OF APPRECIATION AND AWARDS FROM LOCAL COMMUNITY PRESENTED 
TO COMMAND FOR COMMUNITY SERVICE EFFORTS.
F. ONLY THE GRC IS AUTHORIZED TO SUBMIT NOMINATIONS DIRECTLY TO CNIC FOR NAVY-
WIDE AWARDS CONSIDERATION. EXCEPTIONS TO THIS POLICY ARE OVERSEAS COMMANDS 
(EXCEPT HAWAII, GUAM AND CUBA) WHO ARE AUTHORIZED TO SUBMIT THEIR NOMINATIONS 
DIRECTLY TO CNIC N00P.

6. TIMELINE FOR PROCESSING OF NOMINATIONS:
A. 31 JULY: COMMAND NOMINATION PACKAGES DUE TO REGIONS. OVERSEAS NOMINATION 
PACKAGES DUE TO CNIC N00P.
B. 30 SEPTEMBER: GRC NOMINATIONS DUE TO CNIC N00 AND FLAGSHIP SPONSORS.
C. 1 - 27 OCTOBER: NOMINATIONS PROCESSED THROUGH FLAGSHIP SPONSOR AWARDS 
BOARDS.
D. NLT 31 OCTOBER: AWARDS BOARD RECOMMENDATIONS SUBMITTED TO CNIC.
E. NLT 30 NOVEMBER: NAVADMIN RELEASED ANNOUNCING AWARDEES F. NLT 31 DECEMBER: 
AWARDS PLAQUES AND CERTIFICATES DISTRIBUTED TO AWARDEES.
