RMKS/1.  Each year the Navy recognizes shore, sea, and overseas commands for 
Project Good Neighbor.

2.  The Project Good Neighbor Award recognizes the best year-round volunteer 
supported program or special project that promotes outreach activities 
throughout the year to establish and restore hope to the community.

3.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2014 Navy Project Good 
Neighbor Community Service Flag Award winners:
    a.  Shore command category:
        (1) Small - Center for Seabees and Facilities Engineering Detachment, 
Sheppard Air Force Base.
        (2) Medium * Center for Naval Aviation Technical Training
        Unit,
Keesler Air Force Base.
        (3) Large * Navy Information Operations Command Maryland
    b.  Sea command category:
        (1) Small * USS BUFFALO (SSN 715)
        (2) Medium * USS GETTYSBURG (CG 64)
        (3) Large * Naval Mobile Construction Battalion ELEVEN
    c.  Overseas command category:
        (1) Large * Camp Lemonnier (CLDJ)

4.  My sincere thanks to all for your continued support of the Navy's 
Community Service Program.  Please accept my personal Bravo Zulu and thanks 
to the caring and dedicated Sailors, civilians, and families who selflessly 
volunteered and contributed to improving the quality of life within your 
communities.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral S. H. Swift, Director, Navy Staff.//
