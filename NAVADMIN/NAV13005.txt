RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP SELECTION 
BOARD WHICH IS SCHEDULED TO CONVENE ON 13 FEBRUARY 2013.
ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP IS A PREREQUISITE FOR ASSIGNMENT TO ONE OF 
APPROXIMATELY 700 DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY (DON) CRITICALLY CODED ACQUISITION 
BILLETS THROUGHOUT THE FORCE.

2.  PER REF A, POLICY AND GUIDANCE ON ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION 
PROCEDURES FOR DON OFFICERS MAY BE FOUND AT THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE:  
HTTPS://ACQUISITION.NAVY.MIL/RDA/HOME/
ACQUISITION_WORKFORCE/STRATEGY_POLICY/DAWIA_OP_GUIDE (LOWER CASE).

3.  IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED FOR MEMBERSHIP, APPLICANTS MUST MEET THE 
PREREQUISITES FOR DEFENSE ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP, PER CHAPTER 9 OF REF 
A.

4.  APPLICATIONS (NO ENDORSEMENTS REQUIRED) MUST BE RECEIVED NO LATER THAN 18 
JANUARY 2013 TO BE CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.  ALL APPLICANTS ARE REQUIRED TO 
PROVIDE INFORMATION AS DELINEATED IN THE SAMPLE DATA SHEET.  APPLICATION 
SHOULD INCLUDE A COPY OF THE DEFENSE ACQUISITION UNIVERSITY (DAU) TRANSCRIPT, 
ELECTRONIC DEFENSE ACQUISITION CAREER MANAGER (EDACM) WEBSITE DASHBOARD (FROM 
INDIVIDUAL'S EDACM ACCOUNT) AND COLLEGE TRANSCRIPT (COPIES OF THE COLLEGE 
TRANSCRIPTS ARE ACCEPTABLE) TO DOCUMENT COMPLIANCE WITH THE BUSINESS ACADEMIC 
REQUIREMENT.  TO ENSURE SUBMITTED PACKAGE IS ACCEPTED, ALL NECESSARY 
DOCUMENTATION MUST BE INCLUDED AND APPLICATION MUST ADHERE TO THE REQUIRED 
FORMAT.

5.  THE PREFERRED METHOD OF SUBMITTING APPLICATIONS IS BY MAIL.
SUBMISSION OF APPLICATION FROM MULTIPLE METHODS IS STRONGLY DISCOURAGED.
