RMKS/1.  It is an honor to announce the winners of Calendar Year 2017
(CY17) Fleet Awards.

2.  The Arleigh Burke Fleet Trophy awards recipients for their superior 
performance and improvement in battle efficiency.  The CY17 winners are:

    a.  U.S. Fleet Forces:  USS WEST VIRGINIA (SSBN 736) (GOLD)
    b.  Pacific Fleet:  VAQRON ONE THREE FOUR (VAQ-134)

3.  The Marjorie Sterrett Battleship Fund Award recognizes recipients for 
their superior performance in battle efficiency competition.  The CY17 
winners are:

    a.  U.S. Fleet Forces:  USS OSCAR AUSTIN (DDG 79)
    b.  Pacific Fleet:  USS STERETT (DDG 104)

4.  I commend the winning crews for their exemplary dedication to battle 
efficiency.  BRAVO ZULU!
