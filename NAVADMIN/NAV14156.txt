RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE REVISED SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY 
(SDAP) LEVELS FOR THE REGULAR COMPONENT, RESERVE COMPONENT, FULL-TIME SUPPORT 
(FTS) AND QUALIFIED SELECTED RESERVE (SELRES) ENLISTED PERSONNEL ON ACTIVE-
DUTY AND SUPERSEDES REF (A).  THE SDAP PROGRAM ENHANCES OUR NAVY'S ABILITY TO 
SIZE, SHAPE, AND STABILIZE OUR FORCE BY PROVIDING A MONETARY INCENTIVE TO 
ENCOURAGE ENLISTED SAILORS WITH SPECIFIC DESIGNATED SKILLS TO APPLY THEIR 
KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE TO UNIQUELY CHALLENGING ASSIGNMENTS.

2.  INCREASES TO EXISTING LEVELS ARE EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY.  ALL CHANGES TO 
LEVELS ARE SUMMARIZED IN PARAGRAPH 6 FOR EASE OF REFERENCE.  SDAP LEVELS, NEW 
SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENTS, SDAP REDUCTIONS, AND UNIT IDENTIFICATION CODE (UIC) 
REVISIONS ARE PROVIDED AT HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-NPC/CAREER/
PAYANDBENEFITS/SDAP/PAGES/CURRENTSDAPRATES.ASPX.  COMMANDS ARE RESPONSIBLE 
FOR ENSURING INCREASES AND DECREASES ARE SUBMITTED VIA THEIR LOCAL SERVICING 
PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS (PSD) OR INTO THE NAVY STANDARD INTEGRATED 
PERSONNEL SYSTEM.

3.  COMMANDING OFFICERS ARE REQUIRED TO CERTIFY THAT SERVICE MEMBERS MEET ALL 
QUALIFYING CRITERIA ESTABLISHED BY REFS (B), (C) AND THIS NAVADMIN PRIOR TO 
RECEIVING SDAP.  QUALIFICATION CRITERIA INCLUDES:
ALL QUALIFICATIONS COMPLETED, INCLUDING PERSONAL QUALIFICATION STANDARDS AND 
UNDER INSTRUCTION (UI) WATCHES; AND ASSIGNED TO AND WORKING IN A BILLET ON 
THE COMMAND'S ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT (EDVR) IDENTIFIED AS 
AN SDA.

4.  EACH SAILOR IS ELIGIBLE FOR ONLY ONE SDAP ENTITLEMENT AT A TIME AND, 
UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED, COMMANDS MUST ENSURE THE NUMBER OF PERSONNEL 
RECEIVING SDAP DOES NOT EXCEED THE NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED SDAP BILLETS.

5.  REF (B) REQUIRES ANNUAL RECERTIFICATION OF SDAP BILLETS.
GUIDANCE FOR THE RE-CERTIFICATION PROCESS WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA SEPARATE 
CORRESPONDENCE.  COMMANDS, VIA PSD IF APPLICABLE, ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR MAKING 
ANY RATE ADJUSTMENTS AS DIRECTED BY THIS NAVADMIN.
