RMKS/1.  AS PER REF  A, NOMINATIONS ARE REQUESTED FOR SUBJECT ANNUAL 
AWARD.  CATEGORIES INCLUDE:
A.  MILITARY LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR.
B.  CIVILIAN LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR.
C.  LOGISTICS TEAM (S) OF THE YEAR.
2.  THE INTENT OF THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR AWARD IS TO RECOGNIZE NAVY 
MILITARY AND CIVILIAN INDIVIDUALS AND TEAMS WHOSE CONTRIBUTIONS AND 
SUGGESTIONS DURING CALENDAR YEAR 2006, HAVE:
A.  MEASURABLY SUPPORTED THE LOGISTICS MISSION OF THE NAVY.
B.  PROMOTED INNOVATIVE IDEAS AND PRACTICABLE BENEFITS TO THE NAVY.
C.  ENHANCED THE LOGISTICS PROFESSION.
3.  NOMINEES MAY BE INVOLVED IN ANY AND ALL PHASES OF NAVY ACQUISITION, 
OPERATIONAL, AND JOINT SERVICES LOGISTICS, FROM EARLY LIFE-CYCLE 
PLANNING TO IN-SERVICE LOGISTICS SUPPORT.
4.  INDIVIDUALS AND TEAM (S) SELECTED TO RECEIVE THE ADMIRAL STAN 
ARTHUR AWARDS FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2006 WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA NAVAL 
MESSAGE ON OR ABOUT THE LAST WEEK OF MAY.  AWARDS WILL BE PRESENTED IN 
A CEREMONY AT THE PENTAGON, ARLINGTON, VA ON 26 JUNE 2007.
5.  AWARD SELECTEES RECEIVE THE FOLLOWING:
A.  PLAQUE FOR INDIVIDUAL CATEGORY AND EACH TEAM WINNER.
B.  $5,000 EACH FOR THE MILITARY AND CIVILIAN WINNERS.
C.  $10,000 FOR EACH LOGISTICS TEAM TO SHARE.
THE CASH COMPONENT OF EACH AWARD WILL BE PAID THROUGH DIRECT DEPOSIT.
6.  NOMINATIONS MAY ONLY BE SUBMITTED BY/VIA ECHELON TWO COMMANDS TO 
DCNO (MR&L) CODE N40.  ECHELON TWO COMMANDS MAY SUBMIT A TOTAL OF THREE 
NOMINATIONS, NO MORE THAN ONE PER AWARD CATEGORY.  EMPHASIS ON SUPPORT 
OF THE NAVY THROUGH JOINT OPERATIONAL LOGISTICS PROGRAMS IS ENCOURAGED 
IN THE ECHELON TWO REVIEW/NOMINATION PROCESS.  AWARD NOMINATIONS MUST 
BE RECEIVED BY COB 16 APR 2007.  TO RECEIVE CONFIRMATION OF NOMINATION, 
NOMINATION PACKAGE MUST INCLUDE E-MAIL ADDRESS AND DSN OF ECHELON TWO 
POC.
7.  HTTPS:/USN.HQ.NAVY.MIL/STANARTHUR PROVIDES AN OVERVIEW OF THE 
PROGRAM, THE MEANS FOR SUBMISSION, AND A LIST OF PRIOR RECIPIENTS.
