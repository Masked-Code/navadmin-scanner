RMKS/1.  It is an honor to announce the USS WINSTON CHURCHILL (DDG 81) 
as the 2019-2020 USS ARIZONA Memorial Trophy winner!  BRAVO ZULU to this 
year's runner up, USS PAUL HAMILTON (DDG 60). 
 
2.  The USS ARIZONA Memorial Trophy is awarded biennially to the ship 
achieving the greatest combat readiness in strike warfare, surface fire 
support, and anti-surface warfare. 
 
3.  USS WINSTON CHURCHILL's (DDG 81) accomplishments are a testament 
to the professionalism and dedication of her crew and set a combat 
readiness benchmark for all to emulate.  Your hard work and commitment to 
excellence, especially during the year's COVID challenges, embody the 
relentless fighting spirit of the USS ARIZONA and are deserving of this 
prestigious award. 
 
4.  Released by Mr. Andrew S. Haeuptle, Director, Navy Staff.//
