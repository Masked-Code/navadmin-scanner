RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the winners of the 2019 Community Service 
Health, Safety and Fitness Flagship Awards.  Each year the Navy recognizes 
various Shore, Sea and Overseas commands for outstanding community service 
projects by presenting awards in five flagship sponsor categories:  Personal 
Excellence Partnership, Health, Safety and Fitness, Campaign Drug Free, 
Project Good Neighbor and Environmental Stewardship.

2.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2019 Community Service 
Health, Safety and Fitness Flagship Award Winning Commands:
    a.  SMALL OVERSEAS WINNER:  Naval Airborne Weapons Maintenance Unit ONE, 
Guam
    b.  LARGE OVERSEAS WINNER:  U.S. Naval Hospital, Guam
    c.  MEDIUM SEA HONORABLE MENTION:  Helicopter Sea Combat Squadron TWO 
THREE, San Diego, CA
    d.  LARGE SEA WINNER:  USS GERALD R. FORD (CVN 78), Norfolk, VA
    e.  SMALL SHORE WINNER:  USS CONSTITUTION, Boston, MA
    f.  SMALL SHORE HONORABLE MENTION:  Naval Technical Training Center, 
Meridian, MS and Afloat Training Group Middle Pacific, Pearl Harbor, HI
    g.  MEDIUM SHORE WINNER:  Cryptologic Warfare Activity SIXTY SIX, Fort 
George G. Meade, MD
    h.  MEDIUM SHORE HONORABLE MENTION:  Naval Air Station, Whiting Field, 
Milton, FL
    i.  LARGE SHORE WINNER:  Joint Base Pearl Harbor-Hickam, Pearl Harbor, HI
    j.  LARGE SHORE HONORABLE MENTIONS:  Naval Air Technical Training Center, 
Pensacola, FL

3.  Congratulations to all who participated in noteworthy efforts leading to 
this recognition.  I offer my sincere appreciation for your personal 
commitment and continued support of our Community Service Programs.

4.  All award winners will receive commemorative plaques and the honorable 
mentions will receive certificates from the Flag Sponsor, Commander, Naval 
Education and Training Command, in recognition of their exceptional 
accomplishments.

5.  Point of contact is Ms. Ronda Gray, Naval Education Training Professional 
Development Center Civilian Personnel Programs, (850)473-6449/DSN 753 or via 
e-mail at ronda.gray(at)navy.mil.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral John B. Nowell, Jr, N1.//
