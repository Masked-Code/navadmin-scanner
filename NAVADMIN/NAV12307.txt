SUBJ/FAMILYGRAM 05-12 - EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN PROVIDES INFORMATION ON EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS AND 
REFERENCES MANY USEFUL AND VALUABLE TOOLS INCLUDING WEBSITES, LINKS, MOBILE 
APPS, SOCIAL MEDIA, AND THE NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT SYSTEM 
(NFAAS).  THE SUPERB CONTENT IN THESE RESOURCES RESULTS FROM LESSONS LEARNED 
FROM RECENT NATURAL DISASTERS THAT HAVE IMPACTED OUR NAVY FAMILIES.  ALL HANDS 
SHOULD USE THE TOOLS LISTED TO PLAN AND PREPARE FOR SUCH EVENTS.

2.  TWO EXCELLENT ONLINE RESOURCES ARE READY NAVY AND READY.GOV.
    A.  READY NAVY.  EFFECTIVE 1 SEPTEMBER 2012, OPERATION PREPARE CHANGED ITS 
NAME TO "READY NAVY".  THE PROGRAM PROVIDES NAVY FAMILIES WITH USEFUL TOOLS 
AND RESOURCES FOR EMERGENCY PLANNING AND  PREPARATION.
KEY ELEMENTS FOR EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS ARE:
        (1) BE INFORMED;
        (2) HAVE AND PERIODICALLY PRACTICE AN EVACUATION PLAN;
        (3) HAVE AND PERIODICALLY PRACTICE A FAMILY COMMUNICATIONS  PLAN; AND
        (4) HAVE EMERGENCY KITS READILY AVAILABLE.
MORE DETAILED INFORMATION IS AVAILABLE ON THE READY NAVY WEBSITE AT 
HTTP://WWW.READY.NAVY.MIL.
    B.  READY.GOV.  THIS WEB SITE PROVIDES ADDITIONAL INFORMATION AND TIPS FOR 
PLANNING AND PREPARATION, BUILDING ON THE SAME CONCEPTS AS  READY NAVY.  MORE 
DETAILED INFORMATION IS AVAILABLE ON THE READY.GOV WEB  SITE AT 
HTTP://WWW.READY.GOV.

3.  NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT SYSTEM (NFAAS) ACCESS  AND 
REQUIREMENTS.
    A.  MUSTER.  AFTER A DISASTER, ALL NAVY PERSONNEL (BOTH MILITARY  AND
CIVILIAN) ARE REQUIRED TO MUSTER AT THE FIRST AVAILABLE OPPORTUNITY.
THERE ARE THREE AVAILABLE OPTIONS:
        (1) MUSTER DIRECTLY WITH YOUR COMMAND AND PROVIDE YOU AND  YOUR 
FAMILY'S STATUS AND LOCATION.  THE COMMAND WILL UPDATE INFORMATION IN  NFAAS.
        (2) LOG ON TO NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL VIA  COMPUTER OR 
SMART PHONE.
        (3) PHONE THE NAVY EMERGENCY COORDINATION CENTER AT
 1-877-414-5353
OR 1-866-287-1971 (TEXT TELEPHONE DEVICE - TTD).
    B.  PER NAVADMIN 275/11, UPDATING AND VERIFYING NFAAS INFORMATION  IS A 
SEMI-ANNUAL REQUIREMENT.
    C.  NFAAS TRAINING MATERIALS USER GUIDES FOR FAMILY MEMBERS ARE  AVAILABLE 
AT HTTP://WWW.YOUTUBE.COM/USER/CNICN91 (ALL LOWERCASE) AND 
HTTP://WWW.NOVONICSTTL.COM/CNIC/FFR/VIRTUALOFFICE/N91/DOCS/NFAAS/PLAY
ER.HTML.
