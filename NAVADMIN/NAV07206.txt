SUBJ/NOVEMBER 2007 OFFICER TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION BOARD// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE NOVEMBER 2007 OFFICER TRANSFER-
REDESIGNATION BOARD.  ONE ELEMENT OF NAVY?S TOTAL FORCE STRATEGY IS THE 
ABILITY TO PROPERLY BALANCE THE SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE RESIDENT IN THE 
OFFICER CORPS.  AS PART OF THE PROCESS, APPLICATIONS FROM QUALIFIED 
UNRESTRICTED LINE, RESTRICTED LINE, STAFF CORPS, AND LIMITED DUTY 
OFFICERS ARE NOW BEING ACCEPTED FOR AN OFFICER TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION 
BOARD WHICH CONVENES 5 NOVEMBER 2007.
2.  IN ADDITION TO THE YEAR GROUP (YG) LIMITATIONS IMPOSED IN PARA 6, 
APPLICANTS MUST ALSO MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS DETAILED IN 
OPNAVINST 1210.5 AND MILPERSMAN 1212-010 OR 1212-030.
3.  SUBMIT APPLICATIONS PER MILPERSMAN 1212-010 FORMAT AND A COPY FOR 
EACH DESIGNATOR REQUESTED.  SUBMIT APPLICATION AND WAIVER REQUEST, 
SEPARATELY, TO:
    NOVEMBER 2007 TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION BOARD (BOARD ID #40)
    COMMANDER NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-63)
    5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE
    MILLINGTON TN 38055-0000
4.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE RECEIVED NOT LATER THAN 14 SEP 07.  FAX COPIES 
WILL BE ACCEPTED FOR THOSE APPLICANTS WHO HAVE EXHAUSTED ALL OTHER 
MEANS OF DELIVERY.  FAX APPLICATIONS TO (901) 874- 2044/DSN 882.
5.  TO CONFIRM APPLICATION RECEIPT, CHECK WITH THE COMNAVPERSCOM 
CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER AT (866) 827-5672/DSN 882, OR ONLINE AT 
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL AND CLICK ON CUSTOMER SERVICE.
