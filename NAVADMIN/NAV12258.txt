RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES ADDITIONAL COMPLETION OPTIONS FOR SAPR-L 
TRAINING, AS WELL AS CLARIFICATION OF REPORTING REQUIREMENTS.

2.  PER REF A, SAPR-L/F TRAINING IS A CRITICAL ELEMENT OF NAVY'S CAMPAIGN TO 
STOP SEXUAL ASSAULT AND PROMOTE A CULTURE OF RESPECT AND PROFESSIONALISM IN 
OUR FORCE.  PER REF B, COMPLETION OF SAPR-L TRAINING MUST BE REPORTED USING 
FLEET TRAINING MANAGEMENT AND PLANNING SYSTEM (FLTMPS) NO LATER THAN 30 
SEPTEMBER 2012.

3.  SAPR-L MASTER MOBILE TRAINING TEAM (MMTT) FACE-TO-FACE (F2F) TRAINING FOR 
COMMAND LEADERSHIP TRIADS (COMMANDING OFFICER/OFFICER IN CHARGE, EXECUTIVE 
OFFICER/ASSISTANT OFFICER IN CHARGE, AND COMMAND MASTER CHIEF/CHIEF OF THE 
BOAT/SENIOR ENLISTED ADVISOR) WAS COMPLETED 10 AUGUST 2012.  COMMAND 
LEADERSHIP TRIADS ARE NOW RESPONSIBLE FOR DELIVERING SAPR-L TRAINING TO THEIR 
COMMAND LEADERSHIP (E7 AND ABOVE) USING THE SAPR-L INTERACTIVE VIDEO AND 
FACILITATING F2F DISCUSSION.  COMMAND LEADERSHIP TRIADS SHOULD ENSURE 
DOCUMENTATION OF SAPR-L COMPLETION BY 30 SEPTEMBER 2012.

4.  COMMAND LEADERSHIP TRIADS THAT WERE NOT ABLE TO ATTEND MMTT LED SAPR-L 
TRAINING CAN STILL COMPLETE THE TRAINING BY ONE OF THE FOLLOWING METHODS:
    A.  REGISTERING FOR AND ATTENDING ONE OF THE MMTT DELIVERED DEFENSE 
CONNECT ONLINE SAPR-L TRAINING SESSIONS (EXTENDED UNTIL
14 SEPTEMBER 2012).  REGISTER ONLINE AT:
HTTPS://ESAMS.CNIC.NAVY.MIL/ESAMS_GEN_2/LOGINESAMS.ASPX.
    B.  ATTENDING A SAPR-L TRAINING SESSION CONDUCTED BY THEIR PARENT COMMAND.
    C.  VIEWING A RECORDED MMTT LED TRAINING SESSION.  COPIES OF THIS VIDEO 
ARE AVAILABLE VIA MAIL FROM THE SAPR TASK FORCE (TF).

5.  ADDITIONAL RESOURCES, INCLUDING ANSWERS TO FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS 
DURING SAPR-L TRAINING, ARE AVAILABLE AT THE SAPR L/F TRAINING WEBPAGE, 
HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-NPC/SUPPORT/SAPR/PAGES/TRAINING.ASP
X.
