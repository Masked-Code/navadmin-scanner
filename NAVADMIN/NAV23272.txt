RMKS/1.  The Navy Public Affairs Awards (NPAA) program recognizes outstanding 
achievement in communication efforts that advance Department of the Navy 
objectives. 
 
2.  SUBMISSION PROCEDURES: Reference (a) is the guiding program instruction 
and reference (b) is the standard operating procedure for submissions. They 
can be found at the links above or on the CHINFO Portal under the resources 
section (click on PA & VI awards). Entries will be accepted from 0001 on 1 
December 2023 to 2359 on 1 February 2024.  The SOP specifies whether 
categories are submitted via DVIDS or the CHINFO Portal in the ACTION Field. 
 
3.  CHINFO PORTAL: Access to the CHINFO Portal is restricted to Navy PA/VI 
professionals only. Non PA/VI professionals should contact the program 
administrator or coordinators for submission assistance. 
 
4.  CATEGORIES:  Content Award (Individual and Team), 'Of the Year', and 
Campaign: 
    a.  Content Awards (Individual): 
        (1) Graphic Design 
        (2) Photography 
        (3) Videography 
        (4) Writing 
    b.  Content Awards (Team): 
        (1) Print and Digital publications 
        (2) Audio Production 
        (3) Video Production 
    c.  'Of the Year' Awards: 
        (1) Videographer of the year (civilian and military) 
        (2) Writer of the year (civilian and military) 
        (3) Photographer of the year (civilian and military) 
        (4) Graphic Designer of the year (civilian and military) 
        (5) Junior MC of the Year 
        (6) MC of the Year 
        (7) Reserve Junior MC of the Year 
        (8) Reserve MC of the Year 
        (9) Junior PAO of the Year 
        (10) Civilian Public Affairs Specialist of the Year 
        (11) Unit Public Affairs Representative (UPAR) of the Year 
        (12) Junior Reserve PAO of the Year 
    d.  Campaign Awards (Subdivided into four groups:  afloat large, afloat 
small, shore large, and shore small): 
        (1) Best in Show 
        (2) Community Outreach 
        (3) Crisis Communication 
        (4) Special Event 
        (5) Internal Communication 
        (6) Public Information 
        (7) Deployment Excellence 
 
5.  ADDITIONAL ELIGIBILITY.  Commands without designated public affairs 
personnel are welcome to submit entries for appropriate award categories. 
Commands should contact the program administrator or coordinators for more 
info.
