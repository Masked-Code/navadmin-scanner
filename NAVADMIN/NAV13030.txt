RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES ADDITIONAL COMPLETION OPTIONS FOR SEXUAL 
ASSAULT PREVENTION AND RESPONSE-FLEET (SAPR-F) TRAINING.//

2.  BACKGROUND.  AS ANNOUNCED IN REF A, SAPR-F TRAINING IS THE NEXT STEP IN 
THE NAVY'S CONTINUUM OF SEXUAL ASSAULT PREVENTION TRAINING. THIS TRAINING IS A 
CRITICAL COMPONENT OF THE NAVY'S CAMPAIGN TO STOP SEXUAL ASSAULT AND PROMOTE A 
CULTURE OF RESPECT AND PROFESSIONALISM IN OUR FORCE.  ALL COMMANDS ARE 
DIRECTED TO COMPLETE SAPR-F TRAINING FOR E-6 AND BELOW PERSONNEL NLT 31 MARCH 
2013.

3.  SAPR-F MASTER MOBILE TRAINING TEAM (MMTT) TRAINING SESSIONS FOR COMMAND 
TRAINING TEAMS COMMENCED IN DECEMBER 2012 AND COMPLETED FACE-TO-FACE (F2F) 
DELIVERY ON 28 JANUARY 2013.  COMMAND TRAINING TEAMS ARE NOW RESPONSIBLE FOR 
DELIVERING SAPR-F TRAINING TO E-6 AND BELOW PERSONNEL USING THE SAPR-F:  TAKE 
THE HELM INTERACTIVE VIDEO ALONG WITH F2F FACILITATED DISCUSSION.

4.  COMMAND TRAINING TEAMS THAT WERE NOT ABLE TO ATTEND F2F SAPR-F PREPARATION 
TRAINING CAN STILL COMPLETE THE TRAINING BY REGISTERING FOR AND ATTENDING A 
DEFENSE CONNECT ONLINE (DCO)/TELECONFERENCE SAPR-F PREPARATION TRAINING 
SESSION (AVAILABLE UNTIL 15 MARCH 2013).  REGISTER ONLINE AT: 
HTTPS://ESAMS.CNIC.NAVY.MIL/ESAMS_GEN_2/LOGINESAMS.ASPX.

5.  COMMANDS UNABLE TO COMPLETE SAPR-F PREPARATION TRAINING FOR THEIR MID-
LEVEL LEADERSHIP TRAINING TEAM(S) DUE TO OPERATIONAL COMMITMENTS, AS REQUIRED 
PER REF (A) MAY QUALIFY THEIR OWN TRAINING TEAMS WITH THE APPROVAL OF THEIR 
ISIC.  THIS CAN BE ACCOMPLISHED BY VIEWING THE RECORDED MMTT LED TRAINING 
SESSION ON THE SAPR-F SUPPORTING MATERIALS DVD (SHIPPED IN NOVEMBER 2012 WITH 
SAPR-F:  
TAKE THE HELM DVD).  DVD COPIES CAN ALSO BE OBTAINED FROM THE SAPR TASK FORCE 
POC'S LISTED IN PARA 8.

6.  FOLLOWING COMPLETION OF SAPR-F PREPARATION TRAINING, COMMAND MID-LEVEL 
LEADERSHIP TRAINING TEAMS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR DELIVERING AND DOCUMENTING 
COMPLETION IN FLTMPS WITHIN 30 DAYS.  ALL TRAINING AND DOCUMENTATION MUST BE 
COMPLETE BY 31 MARCH 2013.  REF B CONTAINS GUIDANCE ON OBTAINING FLTMPS ACCESS 
AND DOCUMENTATION INSTRUCTIONS. 

7.  ADDITIONAL RESOURCES, INCLUDING ANSWERS TO FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS 
DURING SAPR-F TRAINING, ARE AVAILABLE AT THE SAPR L/F TRAINING WEBPAGE 
HTTP://WWW/PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-NPC/SUPPORT/SAPR/PAGES/TRAINING.ASPX)
