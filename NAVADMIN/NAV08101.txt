REF/A/DOC/OPNAVINST 1160.6B/29DEC2006// AMPN/REF A IS SPECIAL DUTY 
ASSIGNMENT PAY (SDAP) INSTRUCTION.// RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES 
REVISED SDAP LEVELS FOR THE REGULAR COMPONENT, RESERVE COMPONENT AND 
FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) SAILORS AND SUPERSEDES NAVADMIN 140/07.  THE 
SDAP PROGRAM ENHANCES THE NAVY'S ABILITY TO SIZE, SHAPE AND STABILIZE 
THE FORCE BY USING A MONETARY INCENTIVE TO ENCOURAGE SAILORS WITH 
SPECIFIC DESIGNATED SKILLS TO APPLY THEIR KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE TO 
UNIQUELY CHALLENGING ASSIGNMENTS AND TO STAY NAVY. 
2.  NEW STARTS AND INCREASES TO PAY LEVELS ARE EFFECTIVE UPON RELEASE 
OF THIS NAVADMIN, AND ARE SUMMARIZED IN PARA 9 FOR EASE OF REFERENCE.
THIS NAVADMIN INCLUDES NO REDUCTIONS OR TERMINATIONS OF PREVIOUS LEVELS.  
ADMINISTRATIVE BILLET DESCRIPTION CHANGES WHICH DO NOT AFFECT PREVIOUS 
SDAP LEVELS ARE INDICATED IN PARA 10 FOR EASE OF REFERENCE.  
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ON SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY IS LOCATED AT 
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/CAREERINFO/PAYANDBENEFITS/N130/N130D.HTM.
3.  TO RECEIVE SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY, PERSONNEL MUST BE FILLING A 
SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY AUTHORIZED BILLET WITH THE SPECIAL SKILL 
LISTED IN PARA 8.  UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED, PERSONNEL MAY NOT RECEIVE 
THIS PAY WHILE ASSIGNED IN EXCESS OF AUTHORIZED BILLETS.  COMMANDS MUST 
REVIEW THEIR ACTIVITY MANPOWER DOCUMENT AND ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION 
VERIFICATION REPORT TO ENSURE THE NUMBER OF PERSONNEL RECEIVING THIS 
SPECIAL PAY DOES NOT EXCEED THE NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED BILLETS FOR THAT 
SKILL.  SERVICE MEMBERS ARE ONLY AUTHORIZED TO RECEIVE ONE SPECIAL DUTY 
ASSIGNMENT PAY ENTITLEMENT. 
4.  COMMANDS HOLDING SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY BILLETS ARE REMINDED 
THAT ANNUAL RE-CERTIFICATION BEGINS 1 JUNE, OR AS DIRECTED.
THE DEADLINE FOR THE ANNUAL RE-CERTIFICATION IS 31 AUGUST.  GUIDANCE 
FOR THE PROCESS WILL BE ANNOUNCED BY A FUTURE NAVADMIN.  COMMANDS, VIA 
PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS (PSD) IF APPLICABLE, ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR 
MAKING ANY RATE ADJUSTMENTS AS DIRECTED BY THIS NAVADMIN.
5.  THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT REGULATION, VOLUME 
7A, GOVERNS THE POLICIES AND PROCEDURES FOR THE ADMINISTRATION AND 
PAYMENT OF SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY.  COMMANDS AND SERVICING PSDS 
ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR ADMINISTRATING ALL APPLICABLE CHANGES TO SPECIAL 
DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY PER THIS NAVADMIN AND THE ABOVE-LISTED DIRECTIVES.
6.  COMMANDS SHOULD ENSURE COMPLIANCE WITH MILPERSMAN 1306-1200 THROUGH 
MILPERSMAN 1306-1210 FOR SAILORS IN A LIMITED DUTY STATUS. 
SEE REF A, PARA 7D(8) FOR PATIENT STATUS GUIDANCE AS IT PERTAINS TO 
SDAP.  
7.  SKILL SPECIFIC QUESTIONS MAY BE DIRECTED TO THE APPROPRIATE 
ENLISTED COMMUNITY MANAGERS, AS LISTED IN PARA 8.  TO LOCATE THE PHONE 
NUMBER OF THE APPROPRIATE COMMUNITY MANAGER, REFER TO THE PHONE LIST IN 
THE LINK-PERSPECTIVE MAGAZINE.  ADDITIONALLY, CONTACT INFO MAY BE 
ACCESSED AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/ENLISTED/COMMUNITYMANAGERS.  YOU MUST LOG 
ONTO BUPERS ONLINE TO ACCESS THIS LISTING.
