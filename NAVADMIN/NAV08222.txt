RMKS/1.  THE FLEET RESERVE ASSOCIATION (FRA) 81ST NATIONAL CONVENTION

2.  THE CONVENTION AGENDA INCLUDES SEVERAL BUSINESS MEETINGS AND 
WORKSHOPS.

SERVICE RESERVE SENIOR ENLISTED LEADERS AND NAVY RECRUITING COMMAND AND 
WORKSHOPS ON DOD HEALTH CARE AND DENTAL BENEFITS, VETERANS BENEFITS, 
THE LEGISLATIVE ADVOCACY PROCESS AND OTHER SESSIONS FOCUSED ON BENEFITS 
AND QUALITY OF LIFE PROGRAMS.
4.  ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE AND RETIRED NAVY, MARINE CORPS, AND COAST 
GUARD PERSONNEL ARE INVITED TO ATTEND THE OPENING CEREMONIES, 
LEADERSHIP FORUM AND SPECIAL SESSIONS WORKSHOPS.  PERMISSIVE TDY ORDERS 
MAY BE ISSUED TO ATTEND THIS CONFERENCE.

WWW.FRA.ORG OR BY CALLING 1-800-FRA-1924.
