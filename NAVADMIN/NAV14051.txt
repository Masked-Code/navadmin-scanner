RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE JUNE 2014 COMMANDER, NAVAL AIR SYSTEMS 
COMMAND ACQUISITION CORPS ASSIGNMENT SLATING PANEL.  A  SLATING PANEL WILL

CAREERS MENU TAB).
