SUBJ/MARCH 2009 (CYCLE 203) NAVY-WIDE PETTY OFFICER (E4-E6) ADVANCEMENT 
EXAMINATIONS FOR USN, USNR (ACTIVE), FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS), AND 
CANVASSER RECRUITER (CANREC) SAILORS//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE MARCH 2009 (CYCLE 203) NAVY-WIDE 
ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION FOR ADVANCEMENT IN RATE OF USN/USNR (ACTIVE), 
FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) TO INCLUDE ACTIVE GUARD AND RESERVE (AGR), AND 
CANVASSER RECRUITER (CANREC) SAILORS.  THE EXAMINATION DATES ARE:
    E6 -  5 MAR 09
    E5 -  12 MAR 09
    E4 -  19 MAR 09
    A.  REFER TO NAVADMIN 336/07 REGARDING ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION 
OPTIONS FOR SAILORS WHO ARE PENDING DEPLOYMENT TO, OR ARE DEPLOYED AND 
OPERATING IN IRAQ, AFGHANISTAN, OR THE HORN OF AFRICA.
    B.  DO NOT ADMINISTER ANY CYCLE 203 EXAMINATIONS PRIOR TO THE 
SCHEDULED EXAMINATION DATE UNLESS PROVIDED BY BUPERSINST 1430.16F, 
NAVADMIN 336/07, OR SPECIFICALLY AUTHORIZED BY COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-811 
FOR ACTIVE/PERS-812 FOR FTS AGR AND CANREC).
    C.  MUSICIAN (MU) SECOND CLASS PETTY OFFICERS WILL TAKE THE REGULAR 
PAPER EXAMINATION ON 5 MAR 09 AND WILL BE AUTHORIZED TO USE A DIGITAL 
MEDIA PLAYER PROVIDED BY NETPDTC.  THESE ITEMS WILL BE SHIPPED 
SEPARATELY FROM EXAMINATIONS TO COMMANDS WITH MU1 CANDIDATES.

2.  TIMELINE OF EVENTS:
    A.  EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY:  EDUCATIONAL SERVICES OFFICERS (ESOS) 
MUST ORDER CRYPTOLOGIC TECHNICIAN (COLLECTION/NETWORK/INTERPRETIVE) 
EXAMINATIONS AT THE NAVY ENLISTED ADVANCEMENT SYSTEM (NEAS) WEBSITE 
HTTPS://NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL/NEAS/.  SUPPLEMENTAL ORDERS FOR SENSITIVE 
COMPARTMENTED INFORMATION (SCI) EXAMINATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED VIA 
MESSAGE TO NETPDTC PENSACOLA FL (N321) WITH INFO ADDRESSEE COMMANDS TO 
WHICH EXAMINATIONS ARE FORWARDED.  COMMANDS MUST INCLUDE THEIR 
DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE ACTIVITY ADDRESS CODE (DODAAC) ACCOUNT NUMBER, A 
POINT OF CONTACT
(POC) AND THE NUMBER OF EXAMINATIONS REQUIRED.  NETPDTC SCI EXAMINATION 
POC CAN BE REACHED AT (850)452-1620/1045/DSN 922.
    B.  15 DEC 08 - 8 FEB 09:
        (1) TIME-IN-RATE (TIR) ELIGIBILITY LISTS FOR EXAMINATION 
ORDERING OF ALL OTHER RATINGS WILL BE AVAILABLE AT THE NEAS WEBSITE AS 
FOLLOWS:
            E6 - 15 DEC 08 TO 4 JAN 09
            E5 - 15 DEC 08 TO 11 JAN 09
            E4 - 15 DEC 08 TO 18 JAN 09
        (2) AFTER THE CLOSING DATE FOR THE INITIAL EXAMINATION ORDER, 
THIS SITE WILL RE-OPEN FOR SUPPLEMENTAL EXAMINATION ORDERS AS FOLLOWS:
            E6 - 5 JAN 09 TO 25 JAN 09
            E5 - 12 JAN 09 TO 1 FEB 09
            E4 - 19 JAN 09 TO 8 FEB 09
        (3) COMMANDS MUST VERIFY THE EXAMINATION ORDER STATUS ON THE 
NEAS WEBSITE WITHIN 14 DAYS OF PLACING EXAMINATION ORDERS.  IF 
DISCREPANCIES ARE NOTED, CONTACT NETPDTC AT (850)452-1252/DSN 922.
    C.  6 APR 09:  DEADLINE FOR SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATION REQUESTS.  
SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATIONS WILL BE ADMINISTERED WITHIN SEVEN DAYS OF 
RECEIPT BUT NOT LATER THAN 27 APR 09.
        (1) SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATION ORDERS SHOULD BE HELD TO A MINIMUM 
IN ORDER TO ACCURATELY DETERMINE ADVANCEMENT QUOTAS.
        (2) SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATION REQUESTS MUST BE IN BUPERSINST 
1430.16F FIGURE 4-2 FORMAT, ADDRESSED TO NETPDTC PENSACOLA FL (N321).  
REQUESTS MUST INCLUDE VALID JUSTIFICATION FOR MISSING REGULAR 
EXAMINATION.  DETAILS FOR "ADMIN ERROR" OR "OPERATIONAL COMMITMENT" ARE 
REQUIRED.

3.  ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
    A.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS OUTLINED IN 
BUPERSINST 1430.16F, CHAPTER 2, NOT LATER THAN 1 FEB 09.
    B.  CANDIDATES WHO REACH HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT) ON OR AFTER 1 JUL 
09 (THE FIRST DAY OF THE ADVANCEMENT CYCLE) OR HAVE RECEIVED A HYT 
WAIVER TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY ON OR AFTER 1 JUL 09, ARE ELIGIBLE TO 
PARTICIPATE IN THE ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION PER MILPERSMAN 1160-120.
    C.  1 JUL 09 IS THE TERMINAL ELIGIBILITY DATE USED TO COMPUTE TIR.  
REFER TO BUPERSINST 1430.16F, TABLE 2-2 FOR SPECIFIC DETAILS.
    D.  EARLY PROMOTE TIR WAIVERS MUST BE IN ACCORDANCE WITH BUPERSINST 
1430.16F, PARA 216.F.  CANDIDATES WHO HAVE BEEN GRANTED TIR WAIVERS 
MUST BE IDENTIFIED IN THE TRANSMITTAL LETTER TO NETPDTC (N321).  THIS 
WILL PREVENT AUTOMATIC SYSTEM INVALIDATION AS A RESULT OF INSUFFICIENT 
TIR.
    E.  NEW LEADERSHIP CONTINUUM POLICY IS CONTAINED IN NAVADMIN 272/08.
    F.  WARFARE QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR E6 CANDIDATES ARE 
CONTAINED IN APPLICABLE 1414 SERIES OPNAV INSTRUCTION.
    G.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS (CITIZENSHIP, 
SECURITY CLEARANCE, MEDICAL, ASVAB) FOR CERTAIN RATINGS AS OUTLINED IN 
BUPERSINST 1430.16F, PARA 204.
    H.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET PHYSICAL READINESS PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS OF 
OPNAVINST 6110.1H.
    I.  PERFORMANCE TESTS REQUIREMENTS FOR CERTAIN RATINGS ARE 
CONTAINED IN NAVPERS 18068F VOL I.
    J.  "A" SCHOOL CANDIDATES:
        (1) SAILORS IN "A" SCHOOL FOR A RATING CONVERSION DURING THE 
REGULAR EXAMINATION DATE ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO PARTICIPATE IN THE 
EXAMINATION.
        (2) SAILORS WHO COMPLETE MANDATORY CLASS "A" SCHOOL AFTER 1 FEB 
09 AND BEFORE THE REGULAR EXAMINATION DATE AND ARE QUALIFIED IN ALL 
RESPECTS, ARE ELIGIBLE TO COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT IN THEIR NEW RATING.  
SAILORS COMPLETING MANDATORY "A"
SCHOOL FOR THE RATING ENTRY AFTER THE REGULARLY SCHEDULED EXAMINATION 
ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR REGULAR OR SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATIONS IN THEIR NEW 
RATING.

4.  PERFORMANCE MARK AVERAGE (PMA):  COMPUTE PMA AS OUTLINED IN 
BUPERSINST 1430.16F.
EVALS USED TO COMPUTE PMA MUST BE INCLUSIVE OF DATES LISTED BELOW:
    PROSPECTIVE        EVALUATION     COMPUTATION
    PAYGRADE           PAYGRADE       PERIOD
       E-4                E-3         1 JUN 08 TO 28 FEB 09
       E-5                E-4         1 DEC 07 TO 28 FEB 09
       E-6                E-5         1 MAR 06 TO 28 FEB 09
NOTE:  IF SERVICE MEMBER DOES NOT HAVE AN EVALUATION WITH ABOVE 
CRITERIA, PREPARE A SPECIAL EVALUATION WITH 28 FEB 09 ENDING DATE IN 
BLOCK 15.

5.  GENERAL EXAMINATION INFORMATION:
    A.  POINTS FOR OFF-DUTY EDUCATION:  SAILORS COMPETING FOR 
ADVANCEMENT TO PAYGRADES
E4-E6 WILL BE AWARDED TWO POINTS FOR AN ACCREDITED ASSOCIATES DEGREE 
AND FOUR POINTS FOR AN ACCREDITED BACCALAUREATE DEGREE OR ABOVE.  
EDUCATION POINTS WILL BE AWARDED FOR THE HIGHEST DEGREE HELD.  TO ALLOW 
SUFFICIENT TIME FOR THE NAVY COLLEGE CENTER TO PROCESS ALL DOCUMENTS, 
TRANSCRIPTS FOR SAILORS COMPETING FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E4-E6 MUST BE 
RECEIVED BY THE NAVY COLLEGE CENTER NO LATER THAN 1 MAR 09.  THE NAVY 
COLLEGE CENTER WILL VALIDATE THE TRANSCRIPT AND ENTER EDUCATION DATA 
INTO THE NAVY COLLEGE MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM (NCMIS).  NCMIS 
WILL UPDATE SMART AND BE USED TO CALCULATE THE E4-E6 FMS.
    B.  FOR RATINGS WITH MULTIPLE EXAMINATIONS, IT IS IMPERATIVE THAT 
ESOS ENSURE PROPER EXAMINATION IDENTIFICATION IS USED DURING 
EXAMINATION ORDERING AND ANSWER SHEET VERIFICATION (IE. NCR/NCC, 
ATI/ATO, ETR/ETV/ETN).  INCORRECT EXAMINATION IDENTIFICATION WILL CAUSE 
EXAMINATIONS TO BE INCORRECTLY GRADED RESULTING IN ONE OF THE MOST 
COMMON EXAMINATION DISCREPANCIES.
    C.  NAVY COUNSELOR (NC) EXAMINATIONS:  ADMINISTER NCR1 EXAMINATIONS 
TO NC'S HOLDING NAVY ENLISTED CLASSIFICATION (NEC) 2186 AND ASSIGNED TO 
RECRUITING DUTY AS A MEMBER OF THE CAREER RECRUITING FORCE.  ADMINISTER 
NCC1 EXAMINATIONS TO NAVY COUNSELORS (LESS CAREER RECRUITING FORCE).
    D.  AVIATION ELECTRONICS TECHNICIAN (AT) EXAMINATIONS:  ADMINISTER 
ATI EXAMINATIONS FOR INTERMEDIATE MAINTENANCE AND ATO EXAMINATIONS FOR 
ORGANIZATIONAL MAINTENANCE.
    E.  NUCLEAR ELECTRONICS TECHNICIAN (ETN), ELECTRICIAN'S MATE (EMN), 
MACHINIST'S MATE
(MMN) EXAMINATIONS:  NUCLEAR TRAINED ET, EM AND MM RATINGS WILL TAKE 
NUCLEAR SPECIFIC RATING EXAMINATIONS (EMN/ETN/MMN).  NUCLEAR SURFACE 
AND SUBMARINE CANDIDATES WILL TAKE THE SAME EXAMINATION.  CANDIDATES IN 
THE NUCLEAR TRAINING PIPELINE ON THE REGULAR SCHEDULED EXAMINATION DATE 
WILL TAKE RESPECTIVE NUCLEAR EXAMINATION AND COMPETE IN "TRAINEE" 
COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.
    F.  ADMINISTER THE APPROPRIATE EXAMINATION IN ACCORDANCE WITH NAVY 
ENLISTED CLASSIFICATION (NEC):
        (1) NON-NUCLEAR ELECTRONIC TECHNICIANS (ET) EXAMINATIONS:
            ETR - NEC 14BH, 14HH, 14RO, 14TO, 14CM, 14TM, 14EM, 14RM
            ETV - NEC 14NM, 14NO, 14NV, 14XO, 14SM, 14IC, 14QM
NOTE:  ET SURFACE - ADMINISTER ET EXAMINATIONS.
        (2) NON-NUCLEAR MACHINIST'S MATE (MM) EXAMINATIONS:
            MME - NEC 4230, 4231, 4234
            MMW - NEC 4232, 4233, 4235
NOTE:  MM SURFACE - ADMINISTER MM EXAMINATIONS.
    G.  NAVADMIN 153/08 ANNOUNCED THE CREATION OF FIVE NEW NAVAL 
AIRCREWMAN SERVICE
RATINGS:  AWR, AWS, AWO, AWV, AWF.  AW IS NO LONGER A VALID EXAMINATION 
RATING.  ENSURE CORRECT THREE LETTER NAVAL AIRCREWMAN EXAMINATION IS 
ORDERED.
