RMKS/1.  PER REF A, EACH YEAR THE NAVY RECOGNIZES SHORE, SEA, AND OVERSEAS 
COMMANDS FOR OUTSTANDING COMMUNITY SERVICE, INCLUDING SERVICE THAT PROMOTES 
GOOD STEWARDSHIP OF ENVIRONMENTAL RESOURCES.  FOR THE 2011 NAVY ENVIRONMENTAL 
STEWARDSHIP FLAGSHIP AWARDS COMPETITION, NOMINATIONS WERE RECEIVED IN THE 
SMALL, MEDIUM, AND LARGE SHORE AND SEA CATEGORIES, AND SMALL AND LARGE 
OVERSEAS CATEGORIES. 

2.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE WINNERS AND HONORABLE 
MENTIONS OF THE 2011 NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP FLAGSHIP 
AWARDS COMPETITION: 
A.  SMALL SHORE 
    WINNER: CENTER FOR NAVAL AVIATION TECHNICAL TRAINING UNIT, WHIDBEY, OAK 
    HARBOR, WA 
    HONORABLE MENTION: AFLOAT TRAINING GROUP MIDDLE PACIFIC, PEARL HARBOR, HI 
B.  MEDIUM SHORE 
    WINNER: NAVY INFORMATION OPERATIONS COMMAND SUGAR GROVE, WV
    HONORABLE MENTION: NAVAL BASE CORONADO, CA
C.  LARGE SHORE 
    WINNER: NAVAL AIR STATION PATUXENT RIVER, MD 
    HONORABLE MENTION: NAVAL SUBMARINE BASE KINGS BAY, GA
D.  SMALL SEA 
    WINNER: FBM OPERATIONAL TEST SUPPORT UNIT TWO, CAPE CANAVERAL, FL 
E.  MEDIUM SEA 
    WINNER: FLEET LOGISTICS SUPPORT SQUADRON 57, SAN DIEGO, CA
F.  LARGE SEA 
    WINNER: USS THEODORE ROOSEVELT (CVN 71) 
    HONORABLE MENTION: USS CARL VINSON (CVN 70)
G.  SMALL OVERSEAS
    WINNER: NAVY MUNITIONS COMMAND, EAST ASIA DIVISION, UNIT GUAM
H.  LARGE OVERSEAS
    WINNER: USS FRANK CABLE (AS40)

3.  WINNERS WILL RECEIVE PLAQUES.  HONORABLE MENTIONS WILL RECEIVE 
CERTIFICATES.  OPNAV N45 STAFF WILL CONTACT COMMANDS TO ARRANGE DELIVERY OF 
THE AWARDS. 

4.  CONGRATULATIONS TO ALL AWARD WINNERS AND HONORABLE MENTIONS, AND TO THE 
MANY OTHER NOMINEES.  YOU SHOULD BE PROUD OF YOUR ACCOMPLISHMENTS LEADING BY 
EXAMPLE TO PROTECT THE ENVIRONMENT AND STRENGTHEN COMMUNITY TIES.  YOUR 
ACTIONS EXEMPLIFY THE NAVY'S COMMITMENT TO PROTECTING THE ENVIRONMENT AND 
ENHANCING LOCAL COMMUNITIES AS A GLOBAL FORCE FOR GOOD.
