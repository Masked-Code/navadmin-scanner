SUBJ/FY-07 MEDICAL AND DENTAL OFFICER SPECIAL PAY PLAN//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE UPDATED FY-07 MEDICAL 
AND DENTAL OFFICER SPECIAL PAY PLANS.  THESE SUPPLEMENTAL PAYS ARE 
AVAILABLE TO ATTRACT AND RETAIN QUALIFIED MEDICAL AND DENTAL CORPS 
OFFICERS WHILE SIMULTANEOUSLY PROVIDING A DEGREE OF PAY PARITY 
COMMENSURATE WITH THEIR CIVILIAN COUNTERPARTS. 
2.  BUMED M1C1 WILL DETERMINE ACTIVE DUTY OBLIGATION (ADO) INCURRED AND 
NOTIFY THE OFFICER OF HIS/HER ADO FOR MULTI-YEAR SERVICE AGREEMENTS VIA 
AN APPROVAL NOTIFICATION LETTER.  OFFICERS ARE REQUIRED TO RESPOND WITH 
THE ACCEPTANCE/DECLINATION ENCLOSURE TO THE BUMED M1C1 LETTER TO 
INITIATE PAYMENT. ACCEPTANCE OF FIRST PAYMENT WILL INDICATE AGREEMENT TO 
THE TERMS OF ALL SERVICE AGREEMENTS AND ADO. 
3.  GENERAL ELIGIBILITY AND ADMINISTRATIVE REQUIREMENTS FOR ADDITIONAL 
SPECIAL PAY, INCENTIVE SPECIAL PAY, BOARD CERTIFIED PAY, MULTI-YEAR 
SPECIAL PAY, AND DENTAL OFFICER MULTI-YEAR RETENTION BONUS ARE AS 
FOLLOWS: 
A.  ADDITIONAL SPECIAL PAY.  MEDICAL OR DENTAL CORPS OFFICERS ON ACTIVE 
DUTY FOR A PERIOD OF NOT LESS THAN ONE YEAR, NOT UNDERGOING INTERNSHIP 
OR INITIAL RESIDENCY TRAINING, AND WHO EXECUTE A SERVICE AGREEMENT TO 
REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY FOR A PERIOD OF NOT LESS THAN ONE YEAR. 
B.  INCENTIVE SPECIAL PAY.  MEDICAL CORPS OFFICERS AND DENTAL CORPS ORAL 
AND MAXILLOFACIAL OFFICERS BELOW THE GRADE OF 0-7, WHO HAVE A CURRENT 
AND VALID UNRESTRICTED LICENSE OR APPROVED WAIVER HAVE COMPLETED 
SPECIALTY QUALIFICATION BEFORE 1 OCT 06, AND EXECUTE A SERVICE AGREEMENT 
TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY FOR A PERIOD OF NOT LESS THAN ONE YEAR. 
C.  BOARD CERTIFIED PAY. 
MEDICAL OR DENTAL CORPS OFFICERS ON ACTIVE DUTY FOR A PERIOD OF NOT LESS 
THAN ONE YEAR WHO HAVE A CURRENT AND VALID UNRESTRICTED LICENSE OR 
APPROVED WAIVER AND ARE BOARD CERTIFIED IN ACCORDANCE WITH DODI 6000.13. 
SUBMIT REQUEST AND A COPY OF BOARD CERTIFICATION TO BUMED M1C1. 
D.  MULTI-YEAR SPECIAL PAY FOR MEDICAL CORPS OFFICERS AND DENTAL CORPS 
OFFICER'S MULTI-YEAR RETENTION BONUS.  MEDICAL AND DENTAL CORPS OFFICERS 
BELOW THE GRADE OF 0-7 WHO HAVE A CURRENT AND VALID UNRESTRICTED LICENSE 
OR APPROVED WAIVER, HAVE AT LEAST EIGHT YEARS OF CREDITABLE SERVICE OR 
HAVE COMPLETED ANY ACTIVE DUTY SERVICE COMMITMENT INCURRED FOR MEDICAL 
EDUCATION AND TRAINING, HAVE COMPLETED INITIAL RESIDENCY TRAINING OR 
SCHEDULED TO COMPLETE INITIAL RESIDENCY TRAINING BEFORE 1 OCT 06, AND 
WHO EXECUTE A SERVICE AGREEMENT TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY FOR TWO, THREE, 
OR FOUR YEARS. 
4.  SPECIFIC PAY RATES FOR THESE VARIOUS SPECIAL PAYS, SERVICE 
AGREEMENTS, AND REQUEST FORMS CAN BE FOUND AT 
HTTP:/NAVYMEDICINE.MED.NAVY.MIL (CLICK ON BUMED).  THE SPECIAL PAYS LINK 
CAN BE FOUND UNDER DEPARTMENTS.  ALL SERVICE AGREEMENTS AND PAY REQUESTS 
SHOULD BE FAXED TO BUMED M1C1 FOR APPROVAL AND PROCESSING AT THE NUMBER 
LISTED BELOW.  ENSURE ALL SPECIAL PAY REQUESTS FOR 
FY-07 REFERENCE THIS NAVADMIN OR THEY WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED BY BUMED 
M1C1.
