SUBJ/MAR 08 ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP SELECTION BOARD// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THAT THE ACQUISITION CORPS (AC) 
MEMBERSHIP SELECTION BOARD IS SCHEDULED TO CONVENE ON 10 MAR 08.
ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP IS A PREREQUISITE FOR ASSIGNMENT TO ONE OF 
APPROXIMATELY 700 DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY (DON) CRITICAL ACQUISITION 
POSITIONS (CAPS) AND KEY LEADERSHIP POSITIONS (KLPS).
2.  POLICY AND GUIDANCE ON ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP PROCEDURES CAN 
BE FOUND AT:
ACQUISITION.NAVY.MIL/CAREER_MANAGEMENT/DEFENSE_ACQUISITION_CORPS.
3.  APPLICATIONS (NO ENDORSEMENTS REQUIRED) MUST BE SUBMITTED FOR 
CONSIDERATION BY THE BOARD NOT LATER THAN 25 FEB 08.  A SAMPLE 
APPLICATION PACKAGE IS AVAILABLE AT 
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/ACQUISITION.  APPLICANTS ARE REQUIRED TO 
PROVIDE ALL INFORMATION AS DELINEATED IN THE SAMPLE COVER LETTER AND 
DATA SHEET.
4.  THE PREFERRED METHOD OF SUBMITTING APPLICATIONS IS BY MAIL.
APPLICATIONS WILL ALSO BE ACCEPTED VIA FAX OR EMAIL (SIGNED COPIES
ONLY) FOR THOSE OFFICERS DEPLOYED ABOARD SHIPS AND AT REMOTE SITES.
