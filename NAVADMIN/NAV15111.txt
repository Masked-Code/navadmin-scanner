NAVADMIN 111/15

SUBJ/FY-15 FULL TIME SUPPORT AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY//

MSGID/GENADMIN/CNO WASHINGTON DC/N1/MAY//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the FY-15 Full Time Support (FTS) Aviation 
Career Continuation Pay (ACCP) program.  A vital part of developing a total 
force strategy and maintaining combat readiness is to provide appropriate 
incentives to retain skilled personnel for critical Naval Aviation Enterprise 
billets.  The FTS program provides selected bonuses as an incentive to 
eligible pilots and Naval Flight Officers (NFO) through Department Head 
billets.

2.  The FY-15 FTS ACCP program continues the bonus system of previous years 
that is tied directly to force structure and targets initial eligible 
aviators.  Annual rates are listed below and vary by community from $5,000 to 
$25,000 for squadron Department Head. Department Head retention bonuses will 
be paid in equal annual installments.  The following rates are for squadron 
Department Head/Wing Staff contracts:

HM - Pilot/NFO          $10,000/YR
HSC - Pilot/NFO         $10,000/YR    (DECREASED $10,000/YR)
HSL - Pilot/NFO         $25,000/YR
HT - Pilot/NFO           $5,000/YR
VAQ - Pilot/NFO          $5,000/YR
VFA/C - Pilot/NFO       $25,000/YR
VP - Pilot/NFO          $10,000/YR
VR - Pilot/NFO          $20,000/YR    (INCREASED $10,000/YR)
VT(JET) - Pilot/NFO     $15,000/YR
VT(PROP) - Pilot/NFO    $10,000/YR

3.  FY-15 FTS ACCP applications must be received prior to 31 August 2015.

4.  For specific details, eligibility, and application procedures visit the 
FTS aviation officer website at http://www.public.navy.mil
/bupers-npc/officer/detailing/fulltimesupport/pages/aviation.aspx.

5.  Point of contact is LCDR Charles Minski at (901) 874-4146/DSN 882 or via 
e-mail at charles.minski(at)navy.mil.

6.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or canceled, 
whichever occurs first.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
