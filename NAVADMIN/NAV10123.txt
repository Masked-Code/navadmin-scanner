SUBJ/2009 NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM (NCSP)/PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR 
COMMUNITY SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS//

RMKS/1.  EACH YEAR THE NAVY RECOGNIZES SHORE, SEA AND OVERSEAS COMMANDS FOR 
OUTSTANDING COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECTS BY PRESENTING AWARDS IN FIVE FLAGSHIP 
CATEGORIES:
PERSONAL EXCELLENCE PARTNERSHIP; HEALTH, SAFETY, AND FITNESS; CAMPAIGN DRUG 
FREE; PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR; AND ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP.

2.  THE PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR AWARD RECOGNIZES THE BEST YEAR-ROUND VOLUNTEER-
SUPPORTED PROGRAM OR SPECIAL PROJECT THAT PROMOTES OUTREACH ACTIVITIES 
THROUGHOUT THE YEAR TO ESTABLISH AND RESTORE HOPE TO THE COMMUNITY.

3.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE TO ANNOUNCE THE 2009 NAVY PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR
