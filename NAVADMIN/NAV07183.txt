SUBJ/CHANGE TO THE FINAL MULTIPLE SCORE ALGORITHM FOR NAVY 
ADVANCEMENTS// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES CHANGES TO THE NAVY ENLISTED 
ADVANCEMENT SYSTEM (NEAS) THAT WILL BE IMPLEMENTED FOR PAYGRADES 
E-4 THROUGH E-6 BEGINNING IN ADVANCEMENT CYCLE 081 (RESERVE) 
AUGUST 2007 AND ADVANCEMENT CYCLE 196 (ACTIVE/FTS) SEPTEMBER 2007. 
2.  CHANGES TO THE FINAL MULTIPLE SCORE (FMS) ARE AS FOLLOWS:  THE 
WEIGHT OF THE PERFORMANCE MARK AVERAGE (PMA) FROM A SAILOR S 
EVALUATION PROMOTION RECOMMENDATION HAS INCREASED BY 7 PERCENT 
WITH THE GRADATION INCREASED FROM 12 TO 16 POINTS BETWEEN AN EARLY 
PROMOTE AND A PROMOTABLE RECOMMENDATION.  THE WEIGHTINGS OF 
SERVICE IN PAYGRADE (SIPG) AND PASS NOT ADVANCED (PNA) POINTS 
HAVE DECREASED BY APPROXIMATELY ONE-HALF. 
3.  THE NEW FMS COMPUTATION FOR E-4/5 IS AS FOLLOWS:

6.  THESE CHANGES RESULT FROM DETAILED ANALYSIS BY NAVAL EDUCATION 
AND TRAINING PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER 
(NETPDTC), NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (NPC) AND OPNAV N131/2 AND ARE 
INTENDED TO PROVIDE COMMANDING OFFICERS WITH MORE INFLUENCE ON 
THEIR SAILORS  ADVANCEMENTS. 
7.  THE CREDITING OF POINTS TOWARD THE FMS FOR COMPLETION OF FORMAL 
EDUCATION BEGINNING IN ADVANCEMENT CYCLE 085 (RESERVE) AUGUST 2008 
AND ADVANCEMENT CYCLE 200 (ACTIVE/FTS) SEPTEMBER 2008 WILL RESULT 
IN A NEW FMS COMPUTATION.  A SPECIFIC UPDATE DETAILING THAT 
INITIATIVE WILL BE RELEASED SEPCOR.
