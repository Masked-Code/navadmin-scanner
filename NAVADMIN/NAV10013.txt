THE ABILITY TO RETAIN THE RIGHT MIX OF SKILLED AND MOTIVATED NURSES WHO ARE

PAYMENT BY THE MEMBER INDICATES AGREEMENT TO ALL TERMS OF THE CONTRACT AND 
ADO.
