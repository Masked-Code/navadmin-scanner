SUBJ/FY08 MEDICAL DEPARTMENT COMMAND SCREENING BOARD// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE FY08 MEDICAL DEPARTMENT COMMAND 
SCREENING BOARD SCHEDULED TO CONVENE 10 SEPTEMBER 2007.  THIS BOARD 
WILL SCREEN MEDICAL DEPARTMENT CAPTAINS AND CAPTAINS (SELECT) FOR 
COMMANDING OFFICER AND EXECUTIVE OFFICER POSITIONS WITHIN NAVY MEDICINE.
2.  ALL MEDICAL DEPARTMENT CAPTAINS ARE ENCOURAGED TO SUBMIT AN 
APPLICATION FOR CONSIDERATION BY THE BOARD NOT LATER THAN 17 AUGUST 
2007. 
3.  ALL CAPTAIN (SELECT) RECORDS WILL BE REVIEWED BY THE BOARD. 
THESE OFFICERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO SUBMIT A COMPLETED 
APPLICATION FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION NOT LATER THAN 17 AUGUST 2007.
4.  APPLICATION PROCEDURES CAN BE FOUND AT HTTPS:/WWWA.NKO.NAVY.MIL. 
ONCE LOGGED INTO NKO, SCROLL DOWN THE LEFT SIDE TO "ORGANIZATIONS AND 
COMMUNITIES" AND CLICK ON "COMMUNITIES".  UNDER THE "COMMUNITIES OF 
PRACTICE" COLUMN, CLICK ON "NAVY MEDICINE".  CLICK ON THE "SENIOR 
EXECUTIVE MEDICINE" BOOKMARK ON THE LEFT SIDE OF THE SCREEN AND REVIEW 
ALL INFORMATION INCLUDED IN THIS WINDOW.  COMPLETE STEPS 1 THROUGH 3 IN 
THEIR ENTIRETY.  IF INTERNET SERVICES ARE NOT AVAILABLE, THE POINT OF 
CONTACT IS LISTED BELOW FOR A FACSIMILE OR EMAIL COPY OF INSTRUCTIONS 
AND FORMS.
5.  THE PREFERRED METHOD OF SUBMITTING APPLICATIONS IS BY MAIL. 
APPLICATIONS WILL ALSO BE ACCEPTED VIA FAX OR EMAIL (SIGNED COPIES ONLY) 
FOR THOSE OFFICERS DEPLOYED OR AT REMOTE SITES AROUND THE GLOBE.
