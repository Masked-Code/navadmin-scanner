RMKS/ 1.  The currently published website for the Department of the Navy 
Classified Issuances for Office of the Chief of Naval Operations (OPNAV) 
directives is no longer available, and a new site has been established. 
    a.  The currently advertised Uniform Resource Locator (URL) is: 
https://hqweb.cno.navy.smil.mil is no longer the official repository for 
OPNAV Classified issuances, and should not be used. 
    b.  The updated URL 
is https://www.uar.cas.navy.smil.mil/secret/navy/72/site.nsf, Anyone attempting 
to locate any classified OPNAV Directives should use this URL.
