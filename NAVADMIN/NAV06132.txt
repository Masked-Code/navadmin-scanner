SUBJ/ESTABLISHMENT OF THE SPECIAL WARFARE OPERATOR (SO) RATING//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  CNO HAS APPROVED THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE SPECIAL
WARFARE OPERATOR (SO) GENERAL RATING.  THE SO RATING WILL IMPROVE
NAVAL SPECIAL WARFARE READINESS, SUPPORT OPTIMAL MANNING, AND ALIGN
NAVAL SPECIAL WARFARE ENLISTED PERSONNEL WITH THE SKILL SETS
DEMANDED FOR THE GWOT.  BY ALLOWING SAILORS TO FOCUS ON RATING
SPECIFIC TECHNOLOGY AND TRAINING SYSTEMS, THE NAVY WILL REDUCE
TRAINING INFRASTRUCTURE COSTS, WHILE BROADENING THE PROFESSIONAL
DEVELOPMENT, CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AND QUALITY OF SERVICE FOR OUR
SAILORS.
2.  CONVERSION FROM SOURCE RATINGS TO THE SO RATING FOR NEC 5320,
5323, 5326, 8491, AND 8492 DESIGNATED PERSONNEL WILL BE AUTOMATIC.
CONVERSION FOR ACTIVE E1-E9 AND RESERVE E6-E9 PERSONNEL WILL OCCUR
ON 1 OCT 06, AND CONVERSION FOR RESERVE E1-E5 WILL OCCUR ON 1 MAR
07.  NO INDIVIDUAL ACTION BY THE MEMBER WILL BE REQUIRED.  ONLY
SELECTED RESERVES E1-E9 CURRENTLY SERVING IN VALID NAVAL SPECIAL
WARFARE RESERVE BILLETS BY BILLET IDENTIFICATION NUMBER AND RESERVE
BILLET SEQUENCE CODE WILL BE CONVERTED TO THE SO RATING.  SELECTED
RESERVES HOLDING NEC 5320, 5323, 5326, 8491, AND 8492 NOT CURRENTLY
SERVING IN A VALID NAVAL SPECIAL WARFARE RESERVE BILLET HAVE UNTIL 1
SEP 06 TO TRANSFER TO A VALID RESERVE NAVAL SPECIAL WARFARE BILLET.
ALL OTHERS WILL REMAIN IN THEIR SOURCE RATING AND NEC WILL BE
REMOVED BY EPMAC.
3.  ACTIVE DUTY PERSONNEL WITH NEC 5320, 5323, 5326, 8491, AND 8492
WHO DO NOT DESIRE TO CONVERT TO THE SO RATING MUST SUBMIT A NAVY
ENLISTED CLASSIFICATION CHANGE REQUEST (EPMAC 1221/2) TO REMOVE THE
5320, 5323, 5326, 8491, AND 8492 NEC AND AN ENLISTED PERSONNEL
ACTION REQUEST (NAVPERS 1306/7) TO REMAIN IN THEIR SOURCE RATING NOT
LATER THAN 1 JUL 06.  PERSONNEL NOT APPROVED TO REMAIN IN THEIR
SOURCE RATING MUST REQUEST LATERAL CONVERSION TO A CREO 1 OR CREO 2
RATING FOR WHICH THEY QUALIFY.  THESE CONVERSIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED
DIRECTLY TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4811).
4.  THE SO RATING BADGE WILL BE ALL BLACK CONSISTING OF AN ANCHOR
CROSSED HORIZONTALLY BY A TRIDENT AND DIAGONALLY BY A FLINTLOCK
PISTOL.  ALL ACTIVE E1-E9 AND RESERVE E6-E9 DESIGNATED NEC 5320,
5323, 5326, 8491, AND 8492 PERSONNEL CAN CONVERT TO THE NEW SO
RATING BADGE ON 1 OCT 06, AND RESERVE E1-E5 DESIGNATED NEC 5320,
5323, 5326 8491 AND 8492 PERSONNEL CAN CONVERT TO THE NEW SO RATING
BADGE ON 1 MAR 07.
5.  PER UNIFORM REGULATIONS, PARA 4235, ALL ACTIVE E1-E9 AND RESERVE
E6-E9 PERSONNEL MAY CONTINUE WEARING THEIR SOURCE RATING BADGE UNTIL
THEIR NEXT ADVANCEMENT OR UNTIL 30 SEP 08, AND RESERVE E1-E5
PERSONNEL MAY CONTINUE WEARING THEIR SOURCE RATING BADGE UNTIL THEIR
NEXT ADVANCEMENT OR UNTIL 28 FEB 09.
6.  THE ENLISTED SO WARFARE DESIGNATION WILL BE SEAL (SEA, AIR,
LAND).  THE SEAL SPECIAL WARFARE INSIGNIA CURRENTLY EXISTS AND WILL
NOT CHANGE.  THE SEAL INSIGNIA WILL BE WORN IN THE SUPERIOR POSITION
(ABOVE THE RIBBONS) WHEN SERVING IN AN SO BILLET FULFILLING SO
DUTIES, AND WARFARE DESIGNATOR (SEAL) WILL NOW BE PLACED NEXT TO THE
SO MEMBERS RATING DESIGNATOR (I.E. SOCM(SEAL) SMITH).
