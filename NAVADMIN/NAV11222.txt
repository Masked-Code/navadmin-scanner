AUTHORITY WILL IMMEDIATELY FORWARD THE RECOMMENDATION TO DELAY WITH 
SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-83), COPY TO COMNAVPERSCOM 
(PERS-831).
