SUBJ/2014 CAPT JOY BRIGHT HANCOCK AND MASTER CHIEF ANNA DER-VARTANIAN 
LEADERSHIP AWARD RECIPIENTS// 

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the recipients of the 2014 Captain Joy 
Bright Hancock and Master Chief Anna Der-Vartanian Leadership Awards.  
Congratulations to the following award recipients:  
    a.  LCDR Pamela Klepac-Tulensru, Camp Lemonnier, Djibouti, senior officer 
category;  
    b.  CWO3 Vanderla L. Akaka, Afloat Training Group Middle Pacific, Pearl 
Harbor, HI, junior officer category;  
    c.  CMDCM(AW/SW/IDW) Cynthia Patterson, Strategic Communications Wing 
One, Tinker Air Force Base, OK, senior enlisted category; 
    d.  YN1(SW) Shanika Jones, USS MOBILE BAY (CG 53), San Diego, CA, junior 
enlisted category.

3.  The Leadership Awards Board received over 150 recommendations making 
selection a challenge due to the accomplishments of those recommended.  Thank 
you to those who took the time to acknowledge the many inspirational leaders 
we have throughout the Navy.  Information on the recipients can be found at 
http://www.public.navy.mil/bupers-
npc/organization/bupers/womenspolicy/pages/hancockaward.aspx.

4.  Additional information regarding the presentation of the awards will be 
provided via a future NAVADMIN.

5.  This message is cancelled for record purposes on 31 December 2014.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
