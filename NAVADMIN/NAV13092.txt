RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES CONVENING OF THE FY-14 FTS CAPTAIN SELECTIVE RETENTION 
(SELRET) BOARD AND CANCELLATION OF THE FY-14 FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) COMMANDER SELECTIVE 
EARLY RELEASE FROM ACTIVE DUTY (SERAD) BOARD.

2.  THE FTS CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD IS DESIGNED TO BRING FTS CAPTAIN INVENTORY IN LINE WITH 
AUTHORIZED END STRENGTH, ALLOW FOR REASONABLE PROMOTION OPPORTUNITY, AND PERMIT 
SUFFICIENT JUNIOR OFFICER ACCESSIONS TO MEET FTS OFFICER OPERATIONAL REQUIREMENTS.

3.  PER REF A, THE FTS CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD WILL CONVENE ON 10 SEPTEMBER 2013 TO CONSIDER 
FTS CAPTAINS FOR CONTINUATION BEYOND THEIR RESPECTIVE GATES.  THIS BOARD WILL CONSIDER 
FIRST GATE FTS CAPTAINS WHO WERE PROMOTED DURING FY-12, AND SECOND GATE FTS CAPTAINS 
WHO WERE PROMOTED DURING FY-09.

4.  FIRST GATE FTS CAPTAINS WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT NUMBERS TO MAINTAIN FTS CAPTAIN 
END STRENGTH.  SECOND GATE CAPTAINS WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT NUMBERS TO FILL SPECIFIC 
SENIOR MANAGEMENT BILLETS.

5.  FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION WILL BE NOTIFIED AS SOON AS PRACTICABLE AFTER 
THE BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED.  PER REF A, FIRST GATE FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR 
RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED FROM ACTIVE DUTY NLT THE DATE THEY OBTAIN THREE YEARS TIME IN 
GRADE.
SECOND GATE FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED FROM ACTIVE DUTY 
NLT 1 SEPTEMBER 2014.

6.  AMPLIFYING INFORMATION WILL BE POSTED ON THE FTS COMMUNITY MANAGER WEBSITE AS IT 
BECOMES AVAILABLE.  THE EXPECTED NUMBER OF CONTINUATIONS AND THE EFFECTIVE 
CONTINUATION RATE WILL BE POSTED NLT
3 SEPTEMBER 2013.  THE FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT CAN BE ACCESSED AT 
HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-
NPC/OFFICER/COMMUNITYMANAGERS/RESERVE/FTS/PAGES/DEFAULT.ASPX.

7.  COMMUNICATION TO THE CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD SHALL FOLLOW THE SAME PROCEDURES AND 
POLICIES THAT ARE USED FOR THE STATUTORY PROMOTION BOARDS.  ADDRESS ALL CORRESPONDENCE 
TO THE PRESIDENT, FY-14 FTS CAPT SELECTIVE RETENTION BOARD, BOARD #14416.  FOR ADDITIONAL 
GUIDANCE, REFER TO THE GENERAL BOARD INFORMATION PAGE ON THE NPC WEBSITE AT
HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-
NPC/OFFICER/COMMUNITYMANAGERS/RESERVE/FTS/PAGES/DEFAULT.ASPX

8.  POINT OF CONTACT IS CDR DOUG HOWELL, FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGER, AT (901) 874-
4346/DSN 882 OR VIA E-MAIL AT DOUGLAS.HOWELL(AT)NAVY.MIL.
