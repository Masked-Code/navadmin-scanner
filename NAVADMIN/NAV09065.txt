SUBJ/JUNE 2009 OFFICER TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION BOARD//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE JUNE 2009 ACTIVE DUTY OFFICER 
TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION BOARD.  ONE ELEMENT OF NAVY'S TOTAL FORCE 
STRATEGY IS THE ABILITY TO PROPERLY BALANCE THE SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE 
RESIDENT IN THE OFFICER CORPS.  AS PART OF THE PROCESS, APPLICATIONS 
FROM QUALIFIED UNRESTRICTED LINE, RESTRICTED LINE, STAFF CORPS, LIMITED 
DUTY, ACTIVE DUTY, FULL-TIME SUPPORT (FTS), AND SELECTED RESERVE 
(SELRES) OFFICERS ARE NOW BEING ACCEPTED FOR AN ACTIVE DUTY OFFICER 
TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION BOARD WHICH CONVENES 8 JUNE 2009.

2.  IN ADDITION TO THE YEAR GROUP (YG) LIMITATIONS IMPOSED IN PARA 6, 
APPLICANTS MUST ALSO MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS DETAILED IN 
OPNAVINST 1210.5 AND MILPERSMAN 1212-010 OR 1212-030.
    A.  MEMBERS WHO DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS PER OPNAVINST 
1210.5 AND MILPERSMAN 1212-010 OR 1212-030 SUCH AS TIME- IN-GRADE (TIG), 
YEARS-OF-COMMISSIONED SERVICE (YCS), OR TIME-IN- SERVICE (TIS) MUST 
SUBMIT A WAIVER PACKAGE IN ADDITION TO THEIR LATERAL 
TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION APPLICATION.
    B.  WAIVER PACKAGE MUST INCLUDE MEMBER'S WAIVER REQUEST AND 
COMMANDING OFFICER'S ENDORSEMENT.  THIS ENDORSEMENT IS SEPARATE FROM 
ANY ENDORSEMENTS CONTAINED IN THE MEMBERS' LATERAL 
TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION APPLICATION AND MUST SPECIFICALLY ADDRESS THE 
ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENT REQUIRING WAIVER.

3.  SUBMIT APPLICATIONS PER MILPERSMAN 1212-010 FORMAT AND WAIVER 
PACKAGES TO THE ADDRESS BELOW:
    COMMANDER NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND
    CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER
    JUNE 2009 TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION (BOARD #240)
    5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE

4.  APPLICATION AND WAIVER PACKAGES MUST BE RECEIVED NOT LATER THAN
3 APRIL 2009.  PDF OR FAX COPIES WILL BE ACCEPTED FOR THOSE APPLICANTS 
WHO HAVE EXHAUSTED ALL OTHER MEANS OF DELIVERY.  FAX APPLICATIONS TO 
(901) 874-2044/DSN 882.

5.  TO CONFIRM APPLICATION RECEIPT, CHECK WITH THE COMNAVPERSCOM

7.  NOTES:
    A.  MUST HAVE SURFACE WARFARE OFFICER (SWO) QUALIFICATION COMPLETED 
TO APPLY FOR TRANSFER.  OFFICERS UNDER SURFACE WARFARE OFFICER 
CONTINUATION PAY CONTRACT ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR TRANSFER UNTIL 
COMPLETION OF DEPARTMENT HEAD TOURS.
    B.  ALL OFFICERS SCREENED "NOT CLEARED" FOR THEIR NEXT CAREER 
MILESTONE ARE ALSO ELIGIBLE.
    C.  ALL OFFICERS YG 97 AND SENIOR ARE ELIGIBLE, REGARDLESS OF 
PLATFORM.
    D.  NUCLEAR LDO TRANSFER REQUESTS WILL BE REVIEWED ON A CASE BY 
CASE BASIS CONSIDERING SPECIFIC YG INVENTORY DEMANDS.
    E.  OFFICERS YG 05 AND SENIOR APPLYING FOR SWO ARE ENCOURAGED TO 
CONTACT THE SURFACE WARFARE OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGER TO DISCUSS SWO 
CAREER MILESTONE REQUIREMENTS.

    G.  SUPPLY CORPS HAS QUOTAS AVAILABLE FOR JUNIOR LCDRS FROM YG97-03.  
OFFICERS SELECTED WILL LIKELY RECEIVE FOLLOW ON ORDERS TO POST GRADUATE 
SCHOOL TO EARN SUPPLY CORPS SUBSPECIALTY CODES.

8.  EACH APPLICANT IS REQUIRED TO LIST THEIR FIRST AND SECOND LATERAL 
TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION CHOICE BY PRIORITY IN THEIR APPLICATION.  
ADDITIONALLY, EACH APPLICANT IS REQUIRED TO INDICATE THEIR DESIRE TO BE 
CONSIDERED/NOT CONSIDERED FOR LATERAL TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION BY 
COMMUNITIES NOT LISTED AS ONE OF THEIR PRIMARY CHOICES IN THE 
APPLICATION WITH THE FOLLOWING STATEMENT:
"IF NOT SELECTED BY ONE OF MY PRIMARY CHOICES, I DESIRE/DO NOT DESIRE 
MY PACKAGE TO BE REVIEWED BY OTHER COMMUNITIES DURING THE LATERAL 
TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION BOARD."  IF SELECTED BY A COMMUNITY NOT LISTED 
AS ONE OF THEIR PRIMARY CHOICES, THESE APPLICANTS WILL BE NOTIFIED VIA 
OFFICIAL CORRESPONDENCE AFTER THE SELECTION RESULTS ARE PUBLISHED VIA 
NAVADMIN.
