RMKS/1.  EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY, THIS NAVADMIN SUPERSEDES REF A AND PROVIDES 
THE CURRENT BONUS LISTING OF ELIGIBLE SPECIALTIES FOR SELECTED RESERVE 
(SELRES) PRIOR SERVICE AFFILIATION BONUSES AND SELRES NEW ACCESSION TRAINING 
(NAT) ENLISTMENT BONUSES (EB).
EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY, THIS NAVADMIN UPDATES REF B.  WHERE REF B AND THIS 
NAVADMIN DIFFER, THIS NAVADMIN TAKES PRECEDENCE.

2.  TO MAINTAIN OPERATIONAL EFFECTIVENESS, OUR NAVY MUST ATTRACT THE HIGHEST 
QUALITY RECRUITS AND PRIOR SERVICE VETERANS.  A VIGOROUS RECRUITING PLAN 
REQUIRES FLEXIBLE TOOLS TO ENSURE WE RECRUIT THE CORRECT NUMBER OF SAILORS 
WITH THE RIGHT SKILLS TO OPTIMIZE OUR WAR- FIGHTING CAPABILITY.

3.  THE FOLLOWING PARAGRAPHS PROVIDE DETAILS FOR EACH PROGRAM.
    A.  GENERAL:  ALL BONUSES GOVERNED BY THIS MESSAGE WILL BE PAID IN 
INSTALLMENTS.  ALL INITIAL PAYMENTS WILL EQUAL ONE HALF OF THE TOTAL BONUS 
AMOUNT.  NEW ACCESSION TRAINING AND IN-RATE AFFILIATION BONUS RECIPIENTS WILL 
RECEIVE SUBSEQUENT EQUAL ANNIVERSARY PAYMENTS BASED ON THE LENGTH OF THEIR 
CONTRACT.  PRIOR SERVICE REENLISTMENT ELIGIBILITY-RESERVE (PRISE-R) 
AFFILIATION BONUS RECIPIENTS WILL RECEIVE THE SECOND INSTALLMENT EQUAL TO THE 
REMAINING ONE HALF AFTER COMPLETING REQUIREMENTS TO MAKE THEIR RATE PERMANENT.  
ELIGIBILITY FOR CONTINUED ANNIVERSARY PAYMENTS IS CONTINGENT UPON MEMBER 
MAINTAINING SATISFACTORY PARTICIPATION, PER REF B, IN THE DESIGNATED 
RATE/RATING OR SPECIALTY FOR EACH YEAR OF THEIR BONUS CONTRACT.
    B.  SERVICE OBLIGATION FOR AFFILIATION BONUSES.
        (1) EXTENSIONS ARE NOT AUTHORIZED TO GAIN ELIGIBILITY FOR PRIOR 
SERVICE AFFILIATION BONUSES.
        (2) ALL PRISE-R MEMBERS MUST ENLIST FOR SIX YEARS TO QUALIFY FOR A 
BONUS UNDER THIS MESSAGE.
        (3) PER REF D, ALL SAILORS WHO ENLIST OR REENLIST WITHIN THREE MONTHS 
(SIX MONTHS FOR CREO 1 AND 2 RATINGS) OF THEIR DISCHARGE OR RELEASE FROM 
ACTIVE DUTY, MAY REENLIST UNDER CONTINUOUS SERVICE CONDITIONS.  REF D 
AUTHORIZES SAILORS TO REENLIST WITHIN ONE YEAR OF THEIR CONTRACT END DATE.
        (4) PRIOR SERVICE MEMBERS WITH REMAINING MILITARY SERVICE OBLIGATION 
OF 12 MONTHS OR GREATER WHO DO NOT MEET CONTINUOUS SERVICE CONDITIONS 
DESCRIBED ABOVE ARE AUTHORIZED TO REENLIST PROVIDED THE NEW CONTRACT END DATE 
EXCEEDS THEIR REMAINING MSO AND CURRENT EOS.
        (5) PRIOR SERVICE MEMBERS QUALIFIED FOR SELRES AFFILIATION WHO 
INCURRED A 3 YEAR OBLIGATION IN THE INDIVIDUAL READY RESERVE
(IRR) TO RECEIVE INVOLUNTARY SEPARATION PAY ARE AUTHORIZED TO REENLIST UNDER 
THE PROVISIONS OF PARAGRAPH 3.B.(4).
    C.  PRIOR SERVICE AFFILIATION BONUS
        (1) ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
            (A) MUST HAVE SATISFACTORILY COMPLETED ANY PRIOR OBLIGATED ACTIVE 
DUTY MILITARY SERVICE.
            (B) MUST NOT HAVE COMPLETED MORE THAN 16 YEARS OF TOTAL MILITARY 
SERVICE FROM THEIR PAY ENTRY BASE DATE (PEBD) PRIOR TO THEIR  CURRENT 
AFFILIATION/ENLISTMENT DATE.
            (C) MUST NOT HAVE BEEN DRILLING IN THE NAVY RESERVE AT ANY TIME 
WITHIN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING THEIR CURRENT AFFILIATION/ ENLISTMENT DATE.
            (D) THOSE AFFILIATING IN-RATE MUST REENLIST AND AGREE TO 
PARTICIPATE AS A SELRES FOR A MINIMUM OF THREE YEARS TO QUALIFY FOR A THREE-
YEAR BONUS OR SIX YEARS TO QUALIFY FOR A SIX-YEAR BONUS.
THOSE ENLISTING IN-RATE FOR FOUR OR FIVE YEARS QUALIFY FOR A THREE- YEAR 
BONUS.
             (E) THOSE APPLYING FOR ELIGIBILITY BASED ON AN NEC MUST MAINTAIN 
THEIR BONUS RELATED NEC FOR THE ENTIRE TERM DURING WHICH THE BONUS IS AWARDED.

6.  NAT PROGRAM ENLISTMENT BONUS.
    A.  NAT ENLISTMENT BONUS ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
        (1) MEMBER MUST HAVE NO PRIOR MILITARY SERVICE AND BE ENLISTING IN THE 
ARMED FORCES FOR THE FIRST TIME.
        (2) ENLIST AND AGREE TO PARTICIPATE AS A SELRES FOR A MINIMUM OF SIX 
YEARS.
        (3) AFFILIATE WITH A NAVY RESERVE ACTIVITY (NRA) IMMEDIATELY UPON 
COMPLETION OF INITIAL ACTIVE DUTY FOR TRAINING (IADT).
    B.  BONUS EFFECTIVE DATE IS DETERMINED BY THE DATE A SAILOR CONTRACTS IN 
THE ARMED FORCES (ENTERS DELAYED ENTRY PROGRAM (DEP) DATE), NOT THE DATE A 
SAILOR SHIPS TO RECRUIT TRAINING.
    C.  PER REF B, NAT ENLISTEES WHO RECLASSIFY ON OR AFTER THE DATE OF THIS 
MESSAGE ARE SUBJECT TO THE AMOUNTS ON THIS MESSAGE.
    D.  INITIAL BONUS PAYMENT WILL BE PROCESSED UPON MEMBER'S SUCCESSFUL 
COMPLETION OF IADT AND AFTER REPORTING TO THEIR NRA.
