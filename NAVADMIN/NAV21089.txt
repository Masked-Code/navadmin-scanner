AMPN/REF A IS NAVADMIN 263/18, UPDATE TO NAVY GRADUATE EDUCATION PROGRAM.//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the Fiscal Year (FY) 2022 Navy Strategic 
Education Alignment (SEA) Plan.

2.  The Naval Education Enterprise (NEE) supports the National Defense and 
Navy Strategies by developing Navy leaders who understand the art and science 
of warfare and can lead in a dynamic strategic environments.
To this end, the FY-22 SEA Plan provides over 1,400 opportunities for degree 
and non-degree programs.  These educational opportunities provide our leaders 
the knowledge, skills, and abilities they will need to solve complex problems 
and prevail in conflict.

3.  To align with policy changes for In-Residence Graduate Education
(IRGE) included in reference (a), selection for FY-22 SEA Plan opportunities 
for unrestricted line officers will be conducted through administrative 
boards to screen the top, fully qualified, due course officers for in-
residence graduate education and in-residence Joint Professional Military 
Education opportunities.

4.  Specific Program announcements will be delivered through MyNavy 
Education, a web-based application.  Sailors are strongly encouraged to log 
into the system and update their profile and e-mail addresses in MyNavy 
Education to receive alerts regarding program updates.  For a list of 
education programs and instructions on how to receive notifications via 
MyNavy Education, please visit https://www.mnp.navy.mil/group/training-
education-qualifications/
e-graduate-education-and-fellowship-programs.

5.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or canceled.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral J. W. Hughes, Deputy Chief of Naval Operations 
for Warfighting Development (N7).//
