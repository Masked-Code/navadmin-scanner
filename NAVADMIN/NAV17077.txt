RMKS/1.  Per reference (a) the Navy annually recognizes commands engaged in 
exemplary voluntary community service activities that promote academics, life 
skills, and civic education for youth.

2.  For the 2016 Personal Excellence Partnership Flagship awards competition, 
nominations were received in the small, medium, and large shore categories; 
medium and large sea categories; and small overseas category.

3.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the winners of the 2016 
Personal Excellence Partnership Flagship Awards competition and those earning 
honorable mention.
    a. Small Shore Winner:  Fleet Readiness Center Southeast Detachment Key 
West Honorable Mention:  Performance Monitoring Team, Pearl Harbor
    b. Medium Shore Winner:  Navy Medicine Professional Development Center 
Honorable Mention:  Pacific Missile Range Facility, Hawaii(Tie) Honorable 
Mention:  Naval Air Station Whiting Field (Tie)
    c. Large Shore Winner:  Puget Sound Naval Shipyard and Intermediate 
Maintenance Facility Honorable Mention:  Information Warfare Training 
Command, Monterey
    d. Medium Sea Winner:  Helicopter Maritime Strike Squadron SEVEN ZERO
    e. Large Sea Winner:  Naval Mobile Construction Battalion THREE Honorable 
Mention:  USS FRANK CABLE (AS 40)
    f. Small Overseas Winner:  Commander, Fleet Activities, Chinhae Honorable 
Mention:  Navy Munitions Command, East Asia Division, Unit Guam

4.  Congratulations to all our deserving award winners.  I thank you for your 
partnership with the surrounding communities and ongoing support of the Navy 
Community Service Program.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral J. G. Foggo, Director, Navy Staff.//
