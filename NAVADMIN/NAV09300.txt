RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE UPDATES FOR THE 
2009/2010 DIVERSITY EVENTS CALENDAR.  IMPROVING NAVY 
DIVERSITY IS A STRATEGIC IMPERATIVE AND WE ARE 
COMMITTED TO DEVELOPING, IMPLEMENTING, AND IMPROVING 
PROGRAMS TO PROMOTE A DIVERSE WORKFORCE IN WHICH ALL 
SAILORS FULFILL THEIR MAXIMUM POTENTIAL.

2.  OUR ABILITY TO REACH THE TALENT IN EVERY PART OF 
OUR SOCIETY DIRECTLY IMPACTS NAVY MISSION SUCCESS.  OUTREACH 
EVENTS AND AWARDS PROVIDE EXCELLENT OPPORTUNITIES TO INFORM THE 
COMMUNITY ABOUT THE MANY NAVY PROGRAMS AVAILABLE TO OUR YOUTH; 
THEY ALSO FOSTER THE PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF OUR 
SAILORS.  PARTICIPATION ALLOWS US TO DEMONSTRATE OUR RICH NAVY HERITAGE 
AND COMMITMENT TO GROWING A CULTURALLY DIVERSE FORCE.  ALL COMMANDS ARE 
ENCOURAGED TO SUPPORT THESE PRESTIGIOUS AWARDS AND 
EVENTS THROUGH NOMINATIONS AND ATTENDANCE.
