RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE CALL FOR COMMAND MASTER CHIEF (CMC) 
AND COMMAND SENIOR CHIEF (CSC) SELECTION BOARD APPLICATIONS.  THE 
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE CMC AND CSC ARE CONTAINED IN REF A.
2.  HARD CHARGING AND HIGHLY MOTIVATED MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS AND 
SENIOR CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS WHO MEET THE REQUIREMENTS OF REF A ARE 
STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO APPLY FOR THE PROGRAM.  THE DEADLINE TO APPLY 
FOR THE UPCOMING BOARD IS 1 DECEMBER 2009.  PACKAGES POST MARKED AFTER 
1 DECEMBER 2009 WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED UNLESS SPECIFICALLY GIVEN A TIME 
WAIVER BY THE CMC DETAILER AND THE FORCE MASTER CHIEF DUE TO 
OPERATIONAL COMMITMENTS.  PACKAGES NOT COMPLETED IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF 
A WILL BE RETURNED WITHOUT BEING PRESENTED TO THE SELECTION BOARD.
3.  MASTER CHIEFS AND SENIOR CHIEFS SELECTED INTO THE CMC/CSC PROGRAM 
WILL BE ASSIGNED BY THE CMC DETAILER BASED ON BILLET AVAILABILITY, 
EXPERIENCE, QUALIFICATIONS, AND DESIRES.  INITIAL CMC ASSIGNMENTS WILL 
NORMALLY BE SEA-SHORE CODE 2 OR 4, REGARDLESS OF CURRENT DUTY 
ASSIGNMENT.  IF AN AUTHORIZED CSC BILLET IS NOT AVAILABLE, MEMBER MAY 
BE DETAILED BY THEIR SOURCE RATING DETAILER UNTIL ONE BECOMES AVAILABLE.  
4.  POINT OF CONTACT IS CMDCM(SW/AW) BILLY J. HILL, PERS-40FF, (901) 
874-4560/DSN 882 OR EMAIL BILLY.HILL1(AT)NAVY.MIL.
5.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
