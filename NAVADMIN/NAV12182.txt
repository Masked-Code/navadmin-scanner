RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE NAVAL RESERVE FISCAL YEAR 2013 NATIONAL 
COMMAND AND SENIOR OFFICER NON-COMMAND BILLET SCREENING AND ASSIGNMENT "APPLY" 
BOARD.  THIS BOARD WILL CONVENE 7 AUGUST 2012.
ALL SELECTED RESERVE OFFICER COMMUNITIES WILL UTILIZE THE "APPLY"
BOARD PROCESS FOR SENIOR OFFICER AND COMMAND ASSIGNMENTS.

2.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS DESIRING A BILLET ASSIGNMENT ARE REQUIRED TO COMPLETE 
ALL APPLICABLE SECTIONS OF THE "APPLY" APPLICATION.
APPLICATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED VIA THE "APPLY" WEBSITE AVAILABLE AT 
HTTPS://PRIVATE.NAVYRESERVE.NAVY.MIL/APPLY/INDEX.ASPX.  OFFICERS SHOULD 
INCLUDE BILLET PREFERENCES WITHIN THEIR DESIGNATOR TO MEET COMMUNITY FILL 
REQUIREMENTS.  ALTERNATE BILLET SELECTIONS MAY ALSO BE INCLUDED TO ENHANCE THE 
OPPORTUNITY FOR ASSIGNMENT.  MEMBERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO STATE THEIR 
WILLINGNESS TO TRAVEL FOR BILLETS NOT ON THEIR DREAM SHEET TO ENABLE THE BOARD 
TO SELECT THEM FOR UNADVERTISED VACANCIES.

3.  CORRESPONDENCE TO THE BOARD MUST BE RECEIVED NO LATER THAN 1900 CENTRAL 
STANDARD TIME (CST) 16 JULY 2012.  FITNESS REPORTS WILL BE ACCEPTED UNTIL THE 
CONVENING DATE FOR EACH PANEL.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE FINALIZED AND BILLET 
PREFERENCES MADE NO LATER THAN 2359 EASTERN STANDARD TIME (EST) 16 JULY 2012.

4.  OFFICERS WHO ARE NOT AFFILIATED WITH THE RESERVE COMPONENT IN A DRILL 
STATUS MUST CONTACT COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12) AT CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL TO 
REQUEST AN ACCOUNT.

5.  OFFICERS WHO ARE MOBILIZED AND UNABLE TO REGISTER IN "APPLY" DUE TO 
LIMITED INTERNET CONNECTIVITY SHOULD SEEK ASSISTANCE THROUGH THEIR SUPPORTED 
COMMAND OR CONTACT COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12) FOR ASSISTANCE BY E-MAIL AT 
CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL.

6.  OFFICERS MUST BE QUALIFIED PER REF A TO BE CONSIDERED FOR AN ASSIGNMENT.

7.  OFFICERS NOT BEING CONSIDERED FOR BILLET ASSIGNMENT ARE  ENCOURAGED TO 
SERVE IN A BOARD MEMBERSHIP OR SUPPORT POSITION.

8.  "APPLY" AWARDED ASSIGNMENTS ARE ASSUMED TO HAVE BEEN ACCEPTED BY SELECTED 
OFFICERS.  OFFICERS WHO DESIRE TO DECLINE AWARDED BILLETS MUST DO SO WITHIN 14 
DAYS FOLLOWING POSTING OF OFFICIAL RESULTS ON  THE "APPLY" WEBSITE.

9.  THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF ALL ORDERS FROM THIS BOARD IS
1 DECEMBER 2012.  ORDERS MAY NOT BE EXECUTED EARLIER THAN
1 DECEMBER 2012 UNLESS APPROVED BY COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12).

10.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR LYDIA DOYE, APPLY/OFFICER ASSIGNMENTS, 
COMNAVRESFORCOM (N122) (757) 322-2259/DSN 262 OR VIA E-MAIL AT 
LYDIA.DOYE(AT)NAVY.MIL.

11.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
