RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE ANNUAL ARMED FORCES COMMUNICATIONS AND 
ELECTRONICS ASSOCIATION (AFCEA) INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY LEADERSHIP AWARD.  
AFCEA DC IS SPONSORING THE LEADERSHIP AWARD FOR TWO MEMBERS (ONE OFFICER AND 
ONE ENLISTED) FROM EACH OF THE ARMED SERVICES, AND FOUR ADDITIONAL AWARDS FOR 
PERSONNEL ASSIGNED TO JOINT DUTY WHO HAVE MADE A SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTION TO 
THE WARFIGHTER THROUGH THE USE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY.

2.  NOMINATIONS ARE RESTRICTED TO ONE PAGE AND DUE NO LATER
THAN       11 MARCH 2011.  PLEASE SUBMIT NOMINATIONS ONLINE AT:
(TYPE URL INTO BROWSER ALL LOWERCASE) HTTP://WWW.AFCEADC.ORG 
/LEADERSHIPAWARDNOMINATIONS.
CONTACT THE POC ABOVE TO MAKE ALTERNATE ARRANGEMENTS TO SUBMIT
BY     E-MAIL IF INTERNET ACCESS IS LIMITED.  THE NOMINEE MUST HAVE
BEEN A MEMBER OF THE ARMED SERVICES, EITHER RESERVE OR ACTIVE DUTY,
BETWEEN   1 JANUARY 2010 AND 31 DECEMBER 2010.

3.  AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE COMPOSED OF RETIRED FLAG AND GENERAL OFFICERS FROM 
EACH SERVICE WILL MAKE THE FINAL SELECTION.  AWARD RECIPIENTS WILL RECEIVE 
RECOGNITION FROM AFCEA DC, IDENTIFYING THEIR CONTRIBUTION TO THE WARFIGHTER AT 
THE AFCEA SPRING LEADERSHIP AWARDS DINNER CELEBRATING ARMED FORCES DAY ON 
THURSDAY, 19 MAY 2011, AT THE HAY ADAMS HOTEL IN WASHINGTON, D.C., AND AT A 
LUNCHEON FRIDAY, 20 MAY 2011, AT THE RITZ CARLTON IN PENTAGON CITY.  IF 
AWARDEES ARE UNABLE TO ATTEND, A REPRESENTATIVE MAY ACCEPT THE AWARD ON THEIR 
BEHALF.  ALL AWARD RECIPIENTS WILL BE NOTIFIED 20 APRIL 2011.

4.  POINTS OF CONTACT FOR QUESTIONS ABOUT THE AFCEA DC SPRING LEADERSHIP AWARD 
NOMINATIONS CAN BE DIRECTED TO:
A.  MS. LESLIE MCINDOE, AWARDS COMMITTEE VICE CHAIR AT LESLIE.MCINDOE 
@AFCEADC.ORG OR 703-853-9219.
B. QUESTIONS REGARDING THE AWARDS GALA, CONTACT DENISE.DEMERS@AFCEADC .ORG OR 
703-638-0264.
