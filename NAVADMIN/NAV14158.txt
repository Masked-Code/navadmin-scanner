RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE 2014 ASSOCIATION OF NAVAL SERVICES 
OFFICERS (ANSO) EASTERN REGION LEADERSHIP SYMPOSIUM ON
9 SEPTEMBER 2014 AT THE FOLLOWING LOCATION:
         FORT BELVOIR OFFICERS CLUB
         5500 SCHULZ CIRCLE #20
         FORT BELVOIR, VA 22060

2.  ANSO IS AN ORGANIZATION THAT FOCUSES ON THE MENTORSHIP AND PROFESSIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT OF HISPANIC SERVICE MEMBERS IN THE SEAFARING SERVICES.  THE 2014 
LEADERSHIP SYMPOSIUM WILL BRING TOGETHER OFFICERS, ENLISTED PERSONNEL, 
CIVILIAN EXECUTIVES, AND OTHER SENIOR LEADERS IN THE NAVY, MARINE CORPS, 
COAST GUARD AND MARITIME INDUSTRY TO LEARN FROM, NETWORK WITH, AND MENTOR ONE 
ANOTHER, AS WELL AS CONDUCT PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT TRAINING.  IN ORDER TO 
MAKE THE MOST OF THE SYMPOSIUM, IT IS RECOMMENDED THAT ATTENDEES BRING ALONG 
THEIR EVALUATIONS, FITNESS REPORTS, AND OTHER PERFORMANCE RECORDS.

3.  PER REF (A), NAVY-FUNDED PARTICIPATION FOR THIS SYMPOSIUM HAS BEEN 
APPROVED WITHIN A LIMITED SCOPE.  NAVY WILL SPONSOR 100 SERVICE MEMBERS FOR 
THIS SYMPOSIUM BY FUNDING THEIR CONFERENCE REGISTRATION FEES.  UNIFORM FOR 
NAVY PERSONNEL WILL BE SERVICE KHAKI FOR E7 AND ABOVE AND SERVICE UNIFORM FOR 
E6 AND BELOW.  ALL NAVY-FUNDED REGISTRATIONS WILL BE COORDINATED THROUGH THE 
NAVY OFFICE OF DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION AND WILL REQUIRE PARTICIPATION IN 
BRIEF PRE-EVENT AND POST EVENT SURVEYS.

4.  COMMANDS SHALL NOT FUND CONFERENCE REGISTRATION FEES.  COMMANDS ARE NOT 
AUTHORIZED TO EXPEND TRAVEL FUNDS, INCLUDING LODGING, TRANSPORTATION, MEALS 
AND INCIDENTAL EXPENSES.  FILING OF TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY OR LOCAL TRAVEL 
CLAIMS FOR THIS SYMPOSIUM IS NOT AUTHORIZED.  LUNCH WILL BE PROVIDED FOR 
PERSONNEL ATTENDING THE SYMPOSIUM.  ALL OTHER MEALS MUST BE PURCHASED BY THE 
ATTENDEE.

5.  POINTS OF CONTACT TO OBTAIN CONFERENCE REGISTRATION RESERVATIONS
ARE:
    A.  LCDR RAUL ROJAS AT (703) 604-5082/DSN 664 OR VIA E-MAIL AT 
RAUL.ROJAS(AT)NAVY.MIL.
    B.  LT KALEN KROPA AT (703) 604-5075/DSN 664 OR VIA E-MAIL AT 
KALEN.KROPA(AT)NAVY.MIL.

6.  MORE INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND ON THE ANSO WEBSITE AT 
HTTP://WWW.ANSOMIL.ORG.
