RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the Association of Naval Services Officers 
(ANSO) 2013 leadership symposium which will be held 6 September 2013 at the 
Crystal City Gateway Marriott, Arlington, VA.  

2.  ANSO is an organization that focuses on the mentorship and professional 
development of Hispanic service members in the seafaring services.  The 2013 
leadership symposium will bring together officers, civilian executives, 
enlisted personnel, and other senior leaders in the Navy, Marine Corps, Coast 
Guard, and maritime industry to learn from, network with, and mentor one 
another, as well as conduct professional development training.  In order to 
make the most of the symposium, it is recommended that attendees bring 
evaluations, fitness reports, and other performance records for review.  

3.  Per ref (a), participation for this conference has been approved within a 
limited scope.  Navy will sponsor 100 service members by funding their 
registrations to the conference.  The uniform for this conference will be 
summer white.  All Navy-funded registrations will be coordinated through the 
Navy Office of Diversity and Inclusion.  To obtain a conference registration 
reservation, contact LT Deirdre Collins, at (703) 604-5075 or via e-mail at 
deirdre.collins(at)navy.mil. 

4.  Commands shall not fund registrations.  Commands are not authorized to 
expend travel funds, including lodging, transportation, and meals and 
incidental expenses (M&IE).  Lunch is provided 6 September 2013 for those 
attending.  All other meals will be purchased by the service member. 

5.  More information can be found on the ANSO website at www.ansomil.org.   

6.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
