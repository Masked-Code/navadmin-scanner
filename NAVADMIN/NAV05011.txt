4.  REQUEST COMMANDING OFFICERS:
    A.  NOTIFY SELECTEE.
    B.  NOTIFY THE POINT OF CONTACT BY MAIL OR EMAIL OF SELECTEES
DECLINING APPOINTMENT NOT LATER THAN 28 JAN 05.
    C.  IF A SELECTEE IS IN RECEIPT OF PERMANENT CHANGE OF STATION
ORDERS, HOLD THE ORDERS IN ABEYANCE AND NOTIFY COMNAVPERSCOM
(PERS-40) OF THE MEMBER'S SELECTION.
    D.  ADVISE SELECTEE TO PROVIDE THEIR NAME, RATE, WORK AND HOME
PHONE NUMBERS, AND EMAIL ADDRESS BY MAIL OR EMAIL TO THE POINT OF
CONTACT.
    E.  ADVISE SELECTEE TO PROVIDE THE POINT OF CONTACT, BY MAIL OR
EMAIL, A LETTER OF ACCEPTANCE TO A QUALIFIED SCHOOL OF NURSING BY 15
APR 05 TO REPLACE CONDITIONAL AND/OR PROVISIONAL LETTERS OF
ACCEPTANCE.
    F.  ENSURE SELECTEE INCURS A 6-YEAR ENLISTED ACTIVE SERVICE
OBLIGATION BY EXTENDING OR REENLISTING AS SOON AS POSSIBLE.  ONCE
COMPLETE, FORWARD DOCUMENTATION OF THE OBLIGATION TO THE POC BY MAIL
OR EMAIL.  ORDERS WILL NOT BE ISSUED UNTIL A 6-YEAR OBLIGATION IS
RECEIVED AT THE NAVAL MEDICAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING COMMAND.
    G.  ADVISE SELECTEE THAT PERMANENT APPOINTMENT CREATES A 10-YEAR
ACTIVE COMMISSIONED SERVICE OBLIGATION FOR RETIREMENT ELIGIBILITY,
WITH NO REVERSION TO ENLISTED STATUS ALLOWED.

5.  MEMBERS NOT SELECTED ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO REAPPLY.
