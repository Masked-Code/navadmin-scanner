RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE SCHEDULE FOR ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION 
READINESS REVIEWS (AERRS) FROM JANUARY 2014 TO JUNE 2014.  THESE REVIEWS ARE 
HELD AT NAVAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY 
CENTER (NETPDTC) ON BOARD SAUFLEY FIELD, PENSACOLA, FL.  PER REFERENCE (A), 
THIS NAVADMIN SUPERSEDES REFERENCE (B) AND SHOULD BE READ IN ITS ENTIRETY.

2.  TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY (TAD) TRAVEL IN SUPPORT OF AERR EVENTS IS 
CONSIDERED MISSION CRITICAL AND IS FLAG APPROVED.  ALL COSTS PERTAINING TO 
TAD ARE FUNDED BY NETPDTC.  THESE REVIEWS ENABLE RATING SUBJECT MATTER 
EXPERTS (SMES) FROM FLEET AND SHORE-BASED COMMANDS TO REVIEW AND DEVELOP 
ENLISTED ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATIONS.
CHIEF, SENIOR CHIEF, AND MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS ON ACTIVE DUTY, FULL 
TIME SUPPORT (FTS), AND RESERVISTS ON ACTIVE DUTY FOR SPECIAL WORK (ADSW) ARE 
REQUESTED TO VOLUNTEER AND SERVE AS RATING SMES.

3.  AERRS PROVIDE AN OPPORTUNITY FOR PARTICIPANTS TO IMPACT SAILORIZATION BY 
AFFECTING THE CAREERS OF EVERY TEST-TAKER IN THE NAVY.  SELECTED CPOS TAKE 
PART IN THE PROCESS THAT ENABLES THE NAVY TO ADVANCE THE MOST QUALIFIED AND 
RATING-KNOWLEDGEABLE SAILORS.

4.  THE FOLLOWING RATINGS ARE SCHEDULED FOR AERR EVENTS BETWEEN JANUARY 2014 
AND JUNE 2014:
ABF, ABH, AC, AD, AE, AME, AO, AS, ATI, AWF, AWO, AWR, AWS, AWV, AZ, BM, BU, 
CE, CM, CS, CTI, CTM, CTN, DC, EA, EMN, EN, EO, EOD, ET, ETV, FC, GM, GSE, 
HM, HT, IS, IT, ITS, LN, LS, MME, MMN, MMW, MR, MT, MU, NCR, OS, PR, SB, SH, 
SO, STG, SW, YN

5.  SPECIFIC AERR DATES, ELIGIBILITY AND NOMINATION PACKAGE REQUIREMENTS, AND 
OTHER PERTINENT INFORMATION REGARDING AERRS CAN BE FOUND ONLINE AT 
HTTPS://WWWA.NKO.NAVY.MIL/PORTAL/CAREERMANAGEMENT/
NAVYADVANCEMENTCENTER/.
