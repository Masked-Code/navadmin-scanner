SUBJ/SUBMARINE ACQUISITION CORPS ELIGIBLE SELECTION BOARD//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the next Submarine Acquisition 
Corps (AC) eligible selection board the week of 27 August 2018.  AC eligible 
opportunities are available for command screened officers in year groups 98, 
99 and 00.

2.  AC eligible candidates must submit their applications via e-mail to the 
AC community manager no later than 6 July 2018.

3.  Application guidance and AC candidate information sheets may be obtained 
by contacting the AC community manager.

4.  Point of contact is CAPT Doug Adams, AC Community Manager, who can be 
reached at (202) 781-4907 or via e-mail at douglas.j.adams(at)navy.mil.

5.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or 30 September 2018, 
whichever comes first.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral R. P. Burke, N1.//
