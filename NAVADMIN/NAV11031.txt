RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN PROVIDES DETAILS ON THE GRADUATE EDUCATION 
VOUCHER (GEV) PROGRAM AND GUIDANCE  FOR FY-11 PROGRAM APPLICATION.  
ELIGIBILITY, OBLIGATED SERVICE  REQUIREMENTS, SUBSPECIALTY UTILIZATION  
TOUR, AND AUTHORIZED PROGRAM BENEFITS ARE PRESCRIBED IN REF A.

2.  GEV FUNDING IS AUTHORIZED ONLY FOR OFF-DUTY STUDY IN MASTER DEGREE 
PROGRAMS WHICH MEET THE REQUIREMENTS OF AT LEAST ONE APPROVED NAVY 
SUBSPECIALTY CODE AS VERIFIED BY NAVAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL (NPS).  FOR 
FY-11, 80 GEV QUOTAS ARE PLANNED, SUBJECT TO FUNDING AVAILABILITY.  
QUOTAS ARE APPORTIONED AS FOLLOWS:  AVIATION OFFICERS - 36, SUBMARINE 
OFFICERS - 13, SURFACE WARFARE OFFICERS - 28, AND SPECWAR/SPECOPS 
OFFICERS – 3.

SHOULD SUBMIT A WRITTEN REQUEST TO THEIR DETAILER USING THE FORMAT

SUBSPECIALTY BILLET NOT LATER THAN THE SECOND TOUR FOLLOWING GRADUATION.
