SUBJ/2014 CAPTAIN JOY BRIGHT HANCOCK AND MASTER CHIEF ANNA DER-VARTANIAN 
LEADERSHIP AWARD NOMINATIONS// 

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN solicits nominations for the 2014 CAPT Joy Bright 
Hancock (officers) and Master Chief Anna Der-Vartanian(enlisted) Leadership 
Awards.  The awards honor the visionary leadership of service members whose 
ideals and dedication foster a positive working environment by reinforcing 
and furthering the integration of women into the Navy.  Established in 1987, 
these awards have been presented annually to recognize the inspirational 
leadership of Navy officers and enlisted members on active or reserve duty.  

2.  There are four leadership award categories:  senior officer (O4 and 
senior); junior officer (W2-O3); senior enlisted (E7-E9); and junior enlisted 
(E5-E6).  One award winner will be chosen for each category.  Nominees should 
be mature leaders who have shown exceptional leadership over time and have 
persevered to overcome challenges while serving.  Nominees should have 
demonstrated inspirational, innovative, and imaginative leadership, both on 
and off-duty, as well as professional accomplishments and community 
involvement.  

3.  Candidates shall be nominated by their commanding officer (CO) or 
officer-in-charge (OIC).  The nominating command's immediate superior in 
command (ISIC) shall provide an endorsement prior to nomination submission.  
If an ISIC receives multiple nominations, the ISIC shall endorse and forward 
only one nomination for each category. 
Nomination packages shall include the following:
    a. ISIC endorsement;
    b. CO or OIC endorsement;
    c. Nomination write-up (shall not exceed three pages in length);
    d. Nominee's biography (shall not exceed one page in length);
    e. Most recent official military photo in uniform; and
    f. Command point of contact's name, e-mail address, and phone number.

4.  Nominations are due to OPNAV N134W NLT 1700 (Eastern Standard Time), 17 
January 2014.  Extensions will not be granted.  Nominations missing any of 
the items listed in Para. 3 will not be accepted.  
Submit electronic nomination packages directly to OSC Jessica Myers via 
encrypted e-mail at jessica.myers(at)navy.mil.  Do not mail hard copy 
nomination packages.  Do NOT include any personally identifiable information 
(PII) in any part of the nomination package.

5.  Award selections will be communicated to COs NLT 7 February 2014.
Names of award winners will be posted at http://www.public.navy.mil/ BUPERS-
npc/organization/bupers/womenspolicy/pages/hancockaward.aspx
NLT 14 February 2014.

6.  Point of contact is OSC Jessica Myers, N134W, at (703)604-5482/DSN
664 or via e-mail at jessica.myers(at)navy.mil.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.// BT
