SUBJ/FY-08 OFFICER SELECTION BOARD COMMUNITY BRIEFS//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE SECRETARY OF THE NAVY HAS AUTHORIZED THE USE
OF COMMUNITY BRIEFS FOR ACTIVE DUTY NAVY OFFICER PROMOTION
SELECTION BOARDS.  THESE PRESENTATIONS ARE AUTHORIZED TO BE USED FOR
SELECTION BOARDS BEGINNING WITH THE FY-08 ACTIVE DUTY NAVY CAPTAIN
LINE SELECTION BOARD.
2.  THE COMMUNITY BRIEFS ARE INTENDED AS GENERAL GUIDANCE TO
INFORM SELECTION BOARD MEMBERS OF COMMUNITY SPECIFIC INFORMATION.
THE INFORMATION CONTAINED IN THESE COMMUNITY BRIEFS ARE NOT TO BE
USED AS A SUBSTITUTE FOR THE GUIDANCE PROVIDED IN THE SELECTION
BOARD PRECEPT, AND SPECIFICALLY, DOES NOT ALTER THE "BEST AND FULLY
QUALIFIED" SELECTION STANDARD.  COMMUNITY BRIEFS ARE NOT A SELECTION
CRITERIA, NOR IS IT EXPECTED THAT EACH OFFICER, ELIGIBLE FOR A
SELECTION BOARD, WILL HAVE THE EXACT CAREER PATH AND GUIDELINES
DEPICTED IN THESE BRIEFS.  RATHER, THE BRIEFS CONTAIN GENERAL
INFORMATION THAT IS USED TO ASSIST OFFICERS, COMMUNITY LEADERS,
COMMUNITY MANAGERS, AND DETAILERS WHILE DEVELOPING EACH OFFICER S
CAREER PATH.
3.  THE COMMUNITY BRIEFS SHOULD BE WIDELY DISTRIBUTED AND WELL
PUBLICIZED TO ENSURE ALL OFFICERS ARE AWARE OF THEIR USE AS WELL AS
THE INFORMATION THEY CONTAIN.  THE COMMUNITY BRIEFS WILL BE POSTED
AND MAINTAINED ON THE NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND WEBSITE AT
HTTP:/WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/ACTIVEDUTYOFFICER.  AFTER THE WEBSITE
LOADS, CLICK ON "COMMUNITY BRIEFS" LOCATED ON THE RIGHT SIDE OF THE
PAGE IN THE "HELPFUL INFORMATION" BOX.
4.  QUESTIONS CONCERNING COMMUNITY BRIEFS SHOULD BE DIRECTED TO
THE APPROPRIATE COMMUNITY MANAGER OR DETAILER.
5.  RELEASED BY VADM J. C. HARVEY, JR., N1.//
