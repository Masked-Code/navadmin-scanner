RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL SELECTION 
BOARD SCHEDULED TO CONVENE 17 FEBRUARY 2010.  THIS BOARD WILL SELECT HIGHLY 
QUALIFIED NAVAL AVIATORS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS FOR THE U.S. NAVAL TEST 
PILOT SCHOOL (USNTPS), THE NAVAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL/U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT 
SCHOOL (NPS/USNTPS) COOPERATIVE PROGRAM, AND OTHER MILITARY TEST PILOT SCHOOLS 
AS QUOTAS ARE AVAILABLE (E.G., U.S. AIR FORCE TEST PILOT SCHOOL, BRITISH 
EMPIRE TPS AND FRENCH TPS).

2.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION, A COMPLETED APPLICATION LETTER, AS 
DESCRIBED IN REF A, AND A COMMAND ENDORSEMENT MUST BE RECEIVED AT THE NAVY 
PERSONNEL COMMAND CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER (CSC) NOT LATER THAN 15 JANUARY 
2010.  THE ENDORSEMENT SHOULD CONSIDER WHETHER THE APPLICANT, IF SELECTED, IS 
AVAILABLE TO REPORT FOR INITIAL TRAINING AS EARLY AS APRIL 2010.  THE SERVICE 
MEMBER?S EMAIL ADDRESS, PHONE NUMBER(S), COLLEGE TRANSCRIPTS AND POST-TPS TEST 
SQUADRON ASSIGNMENT PREFERENCES MUST BE INCLUDED WITH THE APPLICATION AND 
RECEIVED BY 15 JANUARY 2010.

NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND CSC NOT LATER THAN 12 FEBRUARY 2010.

5.  ELIGIBILITY:
    A.  DESIGNATED NAVAL AVIATORS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS THROUGH THE GRADE 
OF LIEUTENTANT COMMANDER ARE ELIGIBLE FOR SELECTION.
    B.  THE PREFERRED UNRESTRICTED LINE (13XX) AVIATORS ARE DUE COURSE JUNIOR 
OFFICERS (O-2 OR O-3) WITH FORECASTED TIMING TO COMPLETE THE SELECTED TPS 
PROGRAM, A MINIMUM 24-MONTH TEST TOUR AND RETURN FOR SQUADRON DEPARTMENT HEAD 
TOUR NOT LATER THAN SCREEN GROUP PLUS 11 YEARS.  IN THE EVENT THAT THERE ARE 
INSUFFICIENT QUANTITIES OF QUALIFIED UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) JUNIOR OFFICERS 
TO MEET REQUIREMENTS, URL LIEUTENANT COMMANDERS WITH EXCEPTIONAL RECORDS MAY 
BE SELECTED, PROVIDED THEY WOULD STILL BE ABLE TO MEET APPLICABLE CAREER 
MILESTONES.
    C.  AEROSPACE ENGINEERING DUTY OFFICER (1510) AVIATORS THROUGH THE GRADE 
OF LIEUTENANT COMMANDER MAY BE SELECTED IF ABLE TO SERVE A MINIMUM 24-MONTH 
FLIGHT TEST TOUR FOLLOWING GRADUATION FROM TPS AND CONTINUE TO MEET CAREER 
MILESTONES.
