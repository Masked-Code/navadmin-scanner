SUBJ/20TH ANNUAL WOMEN S LEADERSHIP SYMPOSIUM// 
RMKS/1.  THE 20TH ANNUAL WOMEN S LEADERSHIP SYMPOSIUM WILL BE HELD ON 
26-27 JUNE 2007 AT THE WOMEN IN MILITARY SERVICE FOR AMERICA MEMORIAL 
AT ARLINGTON NATIONAL CEMETERY, ARLINGTON, VA.  THIS YEAR S SYMPOSIUM 
THEME IS  WOMEN LEADERS:  KEEPING YOUR COMPASS CALIBRATED.  THE 
SYMPOSIUM WILL BE JOINTLY SPONSORED BY THE COAST GUARD WOMEN S 
LEADERSHIP ASSOCIATION (CGWLA) AND SEA SERVICES LEADERSHIP ASSOCIATION 
(SSLA).
2.  THE PURPOSE OF THE ANNUAL WOMEN S LEADERSHIP SYMPOSIUM IS TO FOSTER 
EDUCATION AND CAREER DEVELOPMENT AS WELL AS PROVIDE NETWORKING AND 
MENTORING OPPORTUNITIES FOR ACTIVE, RESERVE, AND RETIRED MEMBERS OF THE 
SEA SERVICES.
EVENT HIGHLIGHTS WILL INCLUDE GUEST SPEAKERS RONNA LICHTENBERG (AUTHOR 
OF  HOW A WOMAN CAN BE HERSELF AND STILL SUCCEED ) AND MISTI BURMEISTER 
(CEO OF INSPIRION, INC.) ON COACHING AND EMPOWERING GENERATIONS X AND Y 
TO PLAN FOR PROFESSIONAL SUCCESS.
2.  ATTENDANCE IS OPEN TO ALL WOMEN AND MEN SERVING IN AN ACTIVE DUTY 
OR RESERVE STATUS AS WELL AS FEDERAL GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES.  CO S ARE 
STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO SUPPORT ATTENDANCE IN ORDER TO FURTHER 
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF THEIR PERSONNEL.  ISSUANCE OF TAD ORDERS, 
TO INCLUDE REGISTRATION, TRANSPORTATION AND PER DIEM IS AUTHORIZED.
3.  REGISTRATION INFORMATION IS AS FOLLOWS:
    A.  REGISTRATION MATERIALS ARE AVAILABLE AT THE CGWLA WEBSITE 
(WWW.CGWLA.ORG).
    B.  REGISTRATION FEE FOR APPLICATIONS POSTMARKED NLT 25 MAY 2007 IS 
155 DOLLARS AND 165 DOLLARS AFTER 25 MAY 2007.  ATTENDEES MUST PRE-PAY 
BY PERSONAL CHECK.  MAKE CHECK PAYABLE TO CGWLA AND SEND TO WSC 
ASSOCIATES, PO BOX 471212, FORESTVILLE, MD 20753.
    C.  PRICES FOR SINGLE DAY REGISTRATION ARE LISTED ON THE WEBSITE.
    D.  THERE WILL BE NO ON-SITE REGISTRATION; THE REGISTRATION CUT OFF 
DATE IS 19 JUNE 2007.
    E.  FEE INCLUDES CATERED CONTINENTAL BREAKFAST AND LUNCH FOR BOTH 
DAYS.
ATTENDEES MAY NOT COLLECT PER DIEM FOR THESE MEALS.  SEPARATE SOCIAL/ 
ENTERTAINMENT EXPENSES ARE NOT REIMBURSABLE.
4.  THE RITZ-CARLTON HOTEL HAS A LIMITED NUMBER OF ROOMS AVAILABLE AT A 
SPECIAL RATE FOR SYMPOSIUM ATTENDEES.  RESERVATIONS MUST BE MADE NO 
LATER THAN 24 MAY
2007 AT (703) 415-5000.  WHEN MAKING A RESERVATION, PLEASE ASK FOR THE 
WOMEN S LEADERSHIP SYMPOSIUM RATE.  THE RITZ-CARLTON IS LOCATED IN THE 
PENTAGON ROW NEIGHBORHOOD OF ARLINGTON, VA, ADJACENT TO THE BLUE AND 
YELLOW LINES OF THE WASHINGTON DC METRO SYSTEM (WWW.WMATA.COM) AS WELL 
AS NUMEROUS EATING AND SHOPPING ESTABLISHMENTS.
