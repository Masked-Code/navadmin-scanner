RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE CALL FOR COMMAND MASTER CHIEF
(CMC) AND COMMAND SENIOR CHIEF (CSC) SELECTION BOARD APPLICATIONS.
THE DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE CMC AND CSC ARE OUTLINED IN REF 
A.

2.  HARD-CHARGING AND HIGHLY MOTIVATED MASTER CHIEF AND SENIOR CHIEF 
PETTY OFFICERS WHO MEET THE REQUIREMENTS OF REF A ARE STRONGLY 
ENCOURAGED TO APPLY FOR THE PROGRAM.  THE DEADLINE FOR APPLICATIONS IS 
1 DECEMBER 2010.  PACKAGES POSTMARKED AFTER 1 DECEMBER 2010 WILL NOT BE 
CONSIDERED UNLESS SPECIFICALLY GRANTED A WAIVER BY THE CMC DETAILER AND 
FORCE MASTER CHIEF DUE TO OPERATIONAL COMMITMENTS.  PACKAGES ARE TO BE 
COMPLETED AND SUBMITTED IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF A.  SPECIFICALLY, 
PACKAGES SHOULD INCLUDE:
    A.  LETTER OF ENDORSEMENT SIGNED BY THE COMMANDING OFFICER (CO).
    B.  LETTER OF ENDORSEMENT SIGNED BY THE MEMBER'S CMC.
    C.  NAVPERS 1306/7 SIGNED BY THE CO.
    D.  NAVPERS 1070/613 WITH THE FOLLOWING STATEMENT INCLUDED IN THE 
DOCUMENT (CHOOSE "MASTER" OR "SENIOR" AS APPROPRIATE):
"I DESIRE TO SERVE AS A COMMAND (MASTER/SENIOR) CHIEF.  I AM WORLDWIDE 
ASSIGNABLE AND, IF SELECTED, WILL INCUR SUFFICIENT OBLIGATED SERVICE 
UPON RECEIPT OF ORDERS TO COMPLETE MY INITIAL TOUR OF 36 MONTHS (UNLESS 
OTHERWISE SPECIFIED TO SATISFY DOD AREA REQUIREMENTS) AS A COMMAND 
(MASTER/SENIOR) CHIEF.  I FULLY UNDERSTAND MY APPLICATION FOR THIS 
PROGRAM IS MY DUTY  PREFERENCE, AND I WILL ACCEPT ORDERS TO WHEREVER 
THE NAVY NEEDS ME."
    E.  COPY OF PRIMS DATA WHICH SHOWS BCA AND PRT RESULTS FOR YEARS 
2008, 2009, AND 2010.  MISSING DATA SHOULD BE FULLY EXPLAINED IN 
SEPARATE CORRESPONDENCE.
    F.  5 X 7, FRONT AND SIDE COLOR PHOTOGRAPHS IN UNIFORM OF THE DAY.
    G.  CLEAR COPIES OF THE LAST FIVE EVAL REPORTS.
    H.  COPY OF GRADUATION CERTIFICATE FROM SENIOR ENLISTED ACADEMY OR 
EQUIVALENT COURSE.

3.  MASTER CHIEFS AND SENIOR CHIEFS SELECTED INTO THE CMDCM PROGRAM 
WILL BE ASSIGNED BY THE CMC DETAILER BASED ON BILLET AVAILABILITY, 
EXPERIENCE, QUALIFICATIONS, AND DESIRES.  INITIAL  CMC ASSIGNMENTS WILL 
NORMALLY BE SEA-SHORE CODE 2 OR 4, REGARDLESS OF CURRENT DUTY 
ASSIGNMENT.  IF AN AUTHORIZED CSC BILLET IS NOT  AVAILABLE, MEMBERS MAY 
BE DETAILED BY THEIR SOURCE RATING DETAILER UNTIL ONE BECOMES AVAILABLE.

4.  POINT OF CONTACT IS CMDCM(SW/AW) BILLY HILL, PERS-40FF,
(901) 874-4560/DSN 882 OR VIA E-MAIL: BILLY.HILL1(AT)NAVY.MIL
(ACTIVE) OR BMCM LLOYD JOHNSON, PERS-4012, (901) 874-3257/DSN 882  OR 
VIA E-MAIL: LLOYD.JOHNSON(AT)NAVY.MIL (FTS/RESERVE).

5.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
