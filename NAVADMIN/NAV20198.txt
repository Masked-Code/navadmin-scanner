RMKS/1.  This award recognizes both military and civilian logisticians 
who epitomize excellence in the realm of logistics operations and have 
brought a laser focus on warfighting support that has directly impacted 
Fleet Operations across the globe.  These winners produced strategic 
contributions, ensuring that delivery of capability/sustainment to the 
Fleet is hand-in-hand with timely provision of end-to-end logistics 
support, such that Navy Infrastructure and Logistics are best postured 
to provide persistent support in unpredictable operational environments. 
It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2019 Admiral Stan Arthur 
Award Winners: 
a.  2019 Military Logistician of the Year is Commander Benjamin Cone, 
USN, Fleet Support Officer, Naval Information Warfare Center Pacific. 
b.  2019 Civilian Logistician of the Year is Mr. Chad Brandenburg, 
Production Line Director, Fleet Readiness Center Southeast. 
c.  2019 Logistics Team of the Year is the Aviation Support Department 
of Commander, Strike Fighter Wing, U.S. Pacific Fleet. 
 
2.  These awardees exemplify the importance of logistics to achieving 
the mission and innovating across the DOTmLPF-P spectrum to advance 
the Navys competitive edge over near peer adversaries. 
 
3.  OPNAV N41 will contact winning commands to make arrangements for 
the presentation of plaques, cash awards and certificates. 
Additionally, the winners will have command names engraved on 
permanent display in the OPNAV N4 spaces. 
 
4.  My heartiest congratulations to all of our award winners and nominees! 
I would like to also extend my gratitude for the superlative innovative 
solutions that resulted in quantifiable benefits to the United States Navy. 
Bravo Zulu! 
 
5.  Released by VADM R. L. Williamson, OPNAV N4.//
