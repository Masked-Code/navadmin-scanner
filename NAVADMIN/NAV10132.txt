REF/A/DOC/COMNAVRESFOR/11JAN10 
APMN/REF A IS COMNAVRESFORNOTE 5400, FISCAL YEAR 2011 NATIONAL COMMAND AND 
SENIOR OFFICER NON-COMMAND BILLET SCREENING AND ASSIGNMENT PROCEDURES// 

RMKS/1.  APPLICATIONS ARE BEING ACCEPTED FOR THE NAVAL RESERVE FISCAL YEAR 
2011 NATIONAL COMMAND AND SENIOR OFFICER NON-COMMAND BILLET SCREENING AND 
ASSIGNMENT APPLY BOARD WHICH CONVENES 9 AUGUST 2010.

2.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED THROUGH THE "APPLY" WEBSITE.  OFFICERS MAY 
REGISTER ON THE "APPLY" WEBSITE UNTIL 16 MAY 2010.

3.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS DESIRING A BILLET ASSIGNMENT ARE REQUIRED TO COMPLETE 
ALL APPLICABLE SECTIONS OF THE "APPLY" APPLICATION.  OFFICERS SHOULD INCLUDE 
BILLET PREFERENCES WITHIN THEIR DESIGNATOR TO MEET COMMUNITY FILL 
REQUIREMENTS.  ALTERNATE BILLET SELECTIONS MAY ALSO BE INCLUDED TO ENHANCE THE 
OPPORTUNITY FOR ASSIGNMENT.  MEMBERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO STATE THEIR 
WILLINGNESS TO TRAVEL FOR BILLETS NOT ON THEIR DREAM SHEET TO ENABLE THE BOARD 
TO SELECT THEM FOR UNADVERTISED VACANCIES.

4.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE FINALIZED AND BILLET PREFERENCE SELECTIONS MADE NO 
LATER THAN
25 JULY 2010, 2359, (EST).

5.  OFFICERS WHO ARE NOT AFFILIATED WITH THE RESERVE COMPONENT IN A DRILL 
STATUS MUST CONTACT COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12) AT CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL TO 
REQUEST AN ACCOUNT. 

6.  OFFICERS WHO ARE MOBILIZED AND UNABLE TO REGISTER IN "APPLY" DUE TO 
LIMITED INTERNET CONNECTIVITY SHOULD SEEK ASSISTANCE THROUGH THEIR SUPPORTED 
COMMAND OR CONTACT COMNAVRESFOR (N12) FOR ASSISTANCE AT 
CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL.

7.  OFFICERS MUST BE QUALIFIED PER REF A TO BE CONSIDERED FOR AN ASSIGNMENT.

8.  UPON COMPLETION OF THE BOARD, OFFICERS SELECTED MUST ACCEPT OR DECLINE A 
BOARD SELECTED BILLET ON THE "APPLY" WEBSITE BY 15 SEPTEMBER 2010.  THE 
RESULTS OF THE BOARD WILL BE POSTED VIA THE "APPLY" WEBSITE.

9.  THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF ALL ORDERS FROM THIS BOARD IS 1 DECEMBER 2010.  
ORDERS MAY NOT BE EXECUTED EARLIER THAN 1 DECEMBER 2010 UNLESS APPROVED BY 
COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12).

10.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR MARIA MAGNO, (APPLY/OFFICER ASSIGNMENTS, 
COMNAVRESFOR
(N122)) AT (757)322-2259/DSN 626, OR EMAIL MARIA.MAGNO(AT)NAVY.MIL.
