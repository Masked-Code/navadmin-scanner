SUBJ/APRIL 2007 S-3B SUNDOWN TRANSITION SELECTION BOARD// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THAT THE NEXT S-3B SUNDOWN TRANSITION 
BOARD WILL CONVENE 5 APRIL 2007.  ALL S-3 AVIATORS IN YEAR GROUPS 2000 
AND 2001 WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY CONSIDERED REGARDLESS OF PROJECTED 
ROTATION DATE.  YEAR GROUP 2002 AND JUNIOR AVIATORS WILL ALSO BE 
CONSIDERED IF THEIR PROJECTED ROTATION DATE IS PRIOR TO FEBRUARY 08.  A 
LIST OF ELIGIBLE OFFICERS, TRANSITION PREFERENCE FORMS, AND FREQUENTLY 
ASKED QUESTIONS CAN BE FOUND AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/AVIATION/ 
S3SUNDOWNTRANSITION.
2.  IF AN ELIGIBLE OFFICER DESIRES TO BE CONSIDERED FOR A SPECIFIC 
PLATFORM, SUBMIT A TRANSITION PREFERENCE FORM TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-
433H).  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY ALSO SUBMIT CORRESPONDENCE TO THE BOARD, 
INCLUDING LETTERS OF RECOMMENDATION, WHICH MUST BE RECEIVED BY
2 APRIL 2007.  TRANSITION PREFERENCE FORMS AND OTHER CORRESPONDENCE MAY 
BE SUBMITTED VIA FAX TO (901) 874-2756/DSN 882, OR EMAIL TO THE POINT 
OF CONTACT LISTED IN PARAGRAPH 5, OR VIA MAIL TO:
COMMANDER
NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-433H)
5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE
MILLINGTON, TN 38055-4300
3.  THOSE SELECTED MUST ACCEPT THE PLATFORM OFFERED OR BECOME 
INELIGIBLE FOR CONSIDERATION BY FUTURE TRANSITION BOARDS. ACCEPTING A 
TRANSITION INCURS A FOLLOW-ON OBLIGATION (30 MONTHS FOLLOWING 
COMPLETION OF FLEET REPLACEMENT SQUADRON TRAINING) REGARDLESS OF 
ORIGINAL MINIMUM SERVICE REQUIREMENT.  AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY 
ELIGIBILITY IS NOT AFFECTED BY A TRANSITION OBLIGATION.
4.  THOSE OFFICERS NOT OFFERED A TRANSITION OPPORTUNITY FROM THIS BOARD 
MAY BE CONSIDERED ON FUTURE BOARDS BASED ON YEAR GROUP AND PROJECTED 
ROTATION DATE.
5.  POC:  LCDR CARELLI AT (901) 874-3980/DSN 882 OR EMAIL AT 
PAUL.CARELLI@NAVY.MIL.
6.  RELEASED BY RADM E. MASSO, N1B.//
