GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES (CYCLE) 190 NAVY-WIDE
ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATIONS AND FY-07 SELECTION BOARD FOR ADVANCEMENT
TO CHIEF PETTY OFFICER.  USN/USNR (ACTIVE), FULL TIME SUPPORT
(FTS), AND USNR CANVASSER RECRUITER (CANREC) EXAMINATIONS WILL BE
ADMINISTERED 19 JAN 06.  THE USN/USNR(ACTIVE) SELECTION BOARD WILL
CONVENE 26 JUN 06 AND THE USNR(FTS)/USNR(CANREC) SELECTION BOARD
WILL CONVENE 15 MAY 06.
2.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS NOT LATER
THAN 1 DEC 05.  PERSONNEL WHO HAVE NOT ATTENDED PO1 PRIMARY
LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM COURSE ARE INELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT
AS OUTLINED IN NAVADMIN 069/04.
3.  PERSONNEL WHO EXCEED HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT) ON THE FIRST DAY
OF THE ADVANCEMENT CYCLE (1 SEP 06) ARE INELIGIBLE TO TAKE THE
EXAMINATION AND FOR SELECTION BOARD CONSIDERATION UNLESS THEY
RECEIVE A WAIVER THAT EXTENDS THEIR HYT BEYOND 1 SEP 06.
4.  INTERNET REVIEW AND MODIFICATION OF THE TIME-IN-RATE (TIR) LIST
FOR EXAMINATION ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE 17 OCT 05 THROUGH 13 NOV
05.  AFTER THE CLOSING DATE FOR THE INITIAL EXAM ORDER, EXAMINATION
ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE 15 NOV 05 THROUGH 25 DEC 05 FOR
SUPPLEMENTAL EXAMINATION ORDERS AT:  HTTPS:(SLASH -
SLASH)NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL

VIA MESSAGE TO NETPDTC PENSACOLA FL (N321), WITH COMNAVPERSCOM
MILLINGTON, TN (PERS-4811E9) OR (PERS-4812A3) FOR FTS/CANREC
PERSONNEL AS INFO ADDRESSEE.
    B. REQUESTS FOR SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATIONS SUBMITTED AFTER 1 FEB
06, WILL BE DISAPPROVED.  SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATIONS MUST BE
ADMINISTERED NOT LATER THAN 28 FEB 06.
