RMKS/1.  THE ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP SELECTION BOARD IS SCHEDULED TO 
CONVENE ON 10 FEBRUARY 2011.  ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP IS A PREREQUISITE 
FOR ASSIGNMENT TO ONE OF APPROXIMATELY 700 DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY (DON) 
CRITICALLY CODED ACQUISITION BILLETS THROUGHOUT THE FORCE.

2.  POLICY AND GUIDANCE ON ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP PROCEDURES FOR DON 
OFFICERS MAY BE FOUND AT THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE:
HTTPS://ACQUISITION.NAVY.MIL/RDA/HOME/CAREER_MANAGEMENT/ACQUISITION_
CORPS.

3.  APPLICATIONS (NO ENDORSEMENTS REQUIRED) MUST BE RECEIVED BY 10 JANUARY 
2011 TO BE CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.  THE REQUIRED APPLICATION COVER LETTER AND 
DATA SHEET ARE AVAILABLE FROM WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/ACQUISITION.  ALL 
APPLICANTS ARE REQUIRED TO PROVIDE INFORMATION AS DELINEATED IN THE SAMPLE 
DATA SHEET.
COPIES OF ORIGINAL COLLEGE TRANSCRIPTS ARE ACCEPTABLE TO DOCUMENT COMPLIANCE 
WITH THE BUSINESS ACADEMIC REQUIREMENT.

4.  NAVY RESERVE COMMUNITIES INVOLVED IN NAVY ACQUISITION PROCESSES SHOULD 
PRE-SCREEN ANY RESERVE OFFICER APPLICANTS FOR CONSISTENCY IN ACCOUNTING FOR 
EXPERIENCE BEFORE FORWARDING THESE APPLICATIONS.
THIS PRE-SCREENING DOCUMENTATION SHOULD BE INCLUDED WITH THEIR APPLICATION.  
RESERVE EXPERIENCE CAN ONLY BE ACCRUED EITHER ON INDIVIDUAL RESERVE 
ASSIGNMENTS CONSISTING OF 120 DAYS OR LONGER OR FROM CIVILIAN ACQUISITION 
EXPERIENCE WHEN THE DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES MATCH THE PRIMARY CAREER FIELD 
LISTED ON THE APPLICATION.
