GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THAT THE FY-08 ACTIVE DUTY 
MASTER AND SENIOR CHIEF PETTY OFFICER SELECTION BOARDS ARE SCHEDULED TO 
CONVENE 2 APR THROUGH 13 APR 07 FOR E9 AND 16 APR THROUGH 11 MAY 07 FOR 
E8.  ACTIVE DUTY MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS ARE REQUESTED TO VOLUNTEER 
TO SERVE AS MEMBERS OF THE FY-08 ACTIVE DUTY MASTER AND SENIOR CHIEF 
PETTY OFFICER SELECTION BOARDS USING THE APPLICATION FORM WHICH CAN BE 
ACCESSED AT
HTTPS://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/NR/RDONLYRES/F7B779ED-8A87-476B-AEB2-D476369
AD7BC/0/BOARDAPPLICATIONE983.DOC.  EVERY MASTER CHIEF SHOULD VIEW THIS 
SOLICITATION AS A DUTY AND OBLIGATION THAT THEY ARE EXPECTED TO FULFILL 
IN THE DEVELOPMENT OF 21ST CENTURY LEADERS.  NOMINATIONS SHOULD BE 
ROUTED AND SCREENED THROUGH COMMANDING OFFICERS AND COMMAND MASTER 
CHIEFS FOR SUBMISSION TO COGNIZANT FLEET AND FORCE MASTER CHIEFS.
2.  CAREFUL CONSIDERATION SHOULD BE GIVEN TO A NOMINEE_S AVAILABILITY 
TO SERVE ON THE BOARDS.  THIS INCLUDES DEPLOYMENT SCHEDULES AND ANY 
UPCOMING TEMDU/PCS.  COMMANDING OFFICER CONCURRENCE MUST BE OBTAINED 
PRIOR TO SUBMISSION.  COMMANDING OFFICERS AND MASTER CHIEFS MUST 
UNDERSTAND THAT, ONCE SELECTED, BOARD DUTY IS NOT OPTIONAL.  MEMBERS 
WILL NOT BE EXCUSED EXCEPT IN THE MOST EXTREME CASES.
3.  SELECTION BOARD MEMBERSHIP NOMINEES SHOULD:
    A.  NOT HAVE ANY RELATIVE, WHETHER BY BLOOD OR MARRIAGE, ELIGIBLE 
FOR CONSIDERATION BY THE BOARD FOR WHICH THEY ARE APPLYING.
    B.  NOT HAVE BEEN A MEMBER OF THE SAME BOARD LAST YEAR.
    C.  NOT BE FROCKED.
    D.  BE AT LEAST A _MUST PROMOTE_ ON THEIR LATEST FITREP.
    E.  BE WITHIN NAVY BODY COMPOSITION STANDARDS AND HAVE PASSED THE 
MOST RECENT PRT/PFA.
    F.  NOT HAVE A FLEET RESERVE, RETIREMENT, OR HIGH YEAR TENURE DATE 
ONE YEAR AFTER THE BOARD ADJOURNS.
4.  POINT OF CONTACT:  PSCM(SW) V. SMALLWOOD AT (901)874-2245/DSN 882 
OR EMAIL AT VICTOR.SMALLWOOD@NAVY.MIL.
