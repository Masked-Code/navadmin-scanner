RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES FINAL PREPARATIONS FOR EXECUTION OF 
THE 2010 CENSUS AND ENUMERATION OF PERSONNEL ONBOARD NAVAL 
INSTALLATIONS AND SHIPS.

2.  ON 1 APRIL 2010, THE CENSUS BUREAU WILL CONDUCT THE NATION'S 
TWENTY-THIRD DECENNIAL CENSUS TO GATHER A COMPLETE COUNT OF THE 
POPULATION OF THE UNITED STATES, PUERTO RICO, AND THE ISLAND AREAS.
DATA ON OVERSEAS PERSONNEL AND THEIR FAMILY MEMBERS WILL BE COLLECTED 
VIA ADMINISTRATIVE RECORDS WITH THE HELP OF THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE.

3.  SHORE INSTALLATION ENUMERATION PROCEDURES.  COMMANDER, NAVY 
INSTALLATIONS COMMAND (CNIC) WILL COORDINATE CENSUS ACTIVITIES FOR ALL 
NAVY SHORE INSTALLATIONS IN THE UNITED STATES, GUAM, AND PUERTO RICO.  
SHORE INSTALLATION ENUMERATION IS AN ALL HANDS EVOLUTION.
ALL COMMANDERS AND COMMANDING OFFICERS ARE TO PROVIDE ASSISTANCE AS 
REQUESTED BY CNIC, REGIONAL COMMANDERS, AND INSTALLATION COMMANDING 
OFFICERS.

4.  COMMANDER, UNITED STATES FLEET FORCES COMMAND (USFF) AND COMMANDER, 
UNITED STATES PACIFIC FLEET (COMPACFLT) WILL COORDINATE PROCEDURES FOR 
SHIPS AND SUBMARINES.  COMMANDERS/COMMANDING OFFICERS WILL SUPPORT 
NAVY'S EFFORTS TO COLLECT CENSUS BUREAU SURVEYS FOR SAILORS AND MARINES 
ABOARD VESSELS.

5.  THE NAVY IS COMMITTED TO ASSISTING THE CENSUS BUREAU WITH THE 
ENUMERATION OF MILITARY PERSONNEL AND ENSURING MILITARY PERSONNEL 
SERVING OUR COUNTRY ARE FULLY INCLUDED IN THE DECENNIAL CENSUS OF 
POPULATION AND HOUSING.

6.  FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS REGARDING THE 2010 CENSUS AND 
ENUMERATION OF MILITARY PERSONNEL CAN BE FOUND AT 
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/COMMANDSUPPORT/CENSUS2010.

7.  POINT OF CONTACT:  PSC(SW) MELANIE KINCHEN, OPNAV N135, AT (703) 
695-5107/DSN 225, OR EMAIL AT MELANIE.KINCHEN(AT)NAVY.MIL.
