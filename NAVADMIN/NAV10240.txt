RMKS/1.  RECOGNIZING SUPERIOR PERFORMANCE IN INTRA-TYPE BATTLE 
EFFICIENCY, IT IS AN HONOR TO ANNOUNCE THE CALENDAR YEAR 2009 
MARJORIE STERRETT BATTLESHIP FUND AWARD WINNERS:
A.  ATLANTIC FLEET:  USS HARRY S TRUMAN (CVN 75)
B.  PACIFIC FLEET:  USS RONALD REAGAN (CVN 76)

2.  SELECTION FOR THIS AWARD REFLECTS THE PROFESSIONALISM AND 
DEDICATION OF THESE TWO CREWS.  HARRY S TRUMAN'S CREW ACHIEVED 
EXCEPTIONALLY OUTSTANDING PERFORMANCE DURING THE FLEET RESPONSE 
TRAINING PLAN.  RONALD REAGAN'S HIGH STANDARDS AND SUPERIOR 
PERFORMANCE ALLOWED THE SHIP TO SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETE ITS 
FOURTH DEPLOYMENT IN FOUR YEARS IN SUPPORT OF OPERATION ENDURING 
FREEDOM.

3.  CONGRATULATIONS TO THE CREWS OF USS HARRY S TRUMAN (CVN 75) AND 
USS RONALD REAGAN (CVN 76).  YOUR UNMATCHED DEDICATION AND 
PROFESSIONALISM EXEMPLIFIES THE HIGHEST IDEALS AND SPIRIT REPRESENTED 
BY THIS PRESTIGIOUS AWARD.

4.  RELEASED BY ADMIRAL G. ROUGHEAD, CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS.//
