SUBJ/NOTICE OF CONVENING FY-14 EXPLOSIVE ORDNANCE DISPOSAL (EOD) DEPARTMENT 
HEAD SELECTION BOARD//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE NOTICE OF CONVENING DATE FOR THE
FY-14 EXPLOSIVE ORDNANCE DISPOSAL (EOD) DEPARTMENT HEAD SELECTION BOARD IN 
CONJUNCTION WITH THE FY-14 EOD COMMANDER COMMAND/EXECUTIVE OFFICER BOARD.  THE 
FOLLOWING LIST INDICATES THE NAME, ACTIVE-DUTY LIST NUMBER, AND DATE OF RANK 
OF THE SENIOR AND JUNIOR OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE-DUTY LIST, WHO ARE ELIGIBLE 
FOR CONSIDERATION AS OF THE DATE OF THIS NAVADMIN.
SENIOR ELIGIBLE - KING, S. D.       155493-00  25 MAR 06
JUNIOR ELIGIBLE - RIDDLE, E. C.     159900-50  26 MAY 06

2.  THIS SELECTION BOARD WILL SELECT OFFICERS TO SERVE IN DEPARTMENT HEAD 
POSITIONS WITHIN THE EOD COMMUNITY.  THE BOARD WILL CONSIDER THOSE WITH THE 
EOD OFFICER WARFARE QUALIFICATION (KG5) AND COMMAND ENDORSEMENT AS QUALIFYING 
PRE-REQUISITES.  IN ADDITION, THE MEMBERS PERFORMANCE TO DATE WILL BE REVIEWED 
AND DETERMINATION FOR DEPARTMENT HEAD SCREENING WILL BE MADE.  OFFICIAL PHOTOS 
WILL BE VIEWED BY THIS BOARD.

3.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS NOT WANTING TO BE CONSIDERED BY THIS BOARD ARE STRONGLY 
ENCOURAGED TO SUBMIT A REQUEST TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE FY-14 EXPLOSIVE 
ORDNANCE DISPOSAL (EOD) DEPARTMENT HEAD SELECTION BOARD AS DIRECTED BELOW, 
INDICATING THE DESIRE THAT THEIR RECORD NOT BE CONSIDERED.  REQUESTS MAY BE 
SUBMITTED TO PERS-416 VIA E-MAIL TO THE ADDRESS LISTED IN PARA 4.  OFFICERS 
SUBMITTING REQUESTS NOT TO BE CONSIDERED WILL NO LONGER BE ELIGIBLE AND THEIR 
RECORDS WILL NOT BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD FOR CONSIDERATION.

4.  ONLY ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY COMMUNICATE WITH A SELECTION BOARD.
ALL CORRESPONDENCE MUST ARRIVE NOT LATER THAN 1 OCTOBER 2012 AND SHALL BE 
ADDRESSED TO:

6.  WRITTEN COMMUNICATION MAY CALL TO THE ATTENTION OF THE BOARD ANY MATTER 
CONCERNING HIMSELF/HERSELF WHICH THE OFFICER CONSIDERS IMPORTANT.  THE WRITTEN 
COMMUNICATION MAY INCLUDE, AS ENCLOSURES, CORRESPONDENCE FROM ANY INDIVIDUAL 
CONCERNING THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER.
CORRESPONDENCE NOT ORIGINATED BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER, INCLUDING ENDORSEMENTS 
TO THE OFFICER'S LETTER AND LETTERS WRITTEN ON BEHALF OF THAT OFFICER MUST 
CONTAIN A WRITTEN ACKNOWLEDGEMENT BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER THAT HE OR SHE 
DESIRES SUCH CORRESPONDENCE BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD.  CORRESPONDENCE WITHOUT 
SUCH ACKNOWLEDGEMENT IS CONSIDERED THIRD PARTY CORRESPONDENCE AND WILL BE 
RETURNED.
