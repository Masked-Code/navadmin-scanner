SUBJ/FY-09 MEDICAL DEPARTMENT COMMAND SCREENING BOARD// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE FY-09 MEDICAL DEPARTMENT COMMAND 
SCREENING BOARD SCHEDULED TO CONVENE 8 SEP 08.  THIS BOARD WILL SCREEN 
MEDICAL DEPARTMENT CAPTAINS AND CAPTAIN SELECTS FOR COMMANDING OFFICER 
AND EXECUTIVE OFFICER POSITIONS WITHIN NAVY MEDICINE. 
2.  EVERY INTERESTED MEDICAL DEPARTMENT CAPTAIN AND CAPTAIN SELECT ARE 
ENCOURAGED TO SUBMIT AN APPLICATION FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION NOT LATER 
THAN 15 AUG 08.
3.  ADDITIONAL INFORMATION AND APPLICATION FORMS CAN BE FOUND ON NAVY 
KNOWLEDGE ONLINE AT HTTPS://WWWA.NKO.NAVY.MIL.  ONCE LOGGED INTO NKO, 
CLICK ON "ORGANIZATIONS AND COMMUNITIES", SCROLL DOWN BLOCK LOCATED IN 
UPPER LEFT CORNER.  CLICK ON "COMMUNITIES OF PRACTICE."  UNDER THE 
"COMMUNITIES OF PRACTICE" COLUMN, CLICK ON "NAVY MEDICINE."  CLICK ON 
THE "SENIOR EXECUTIVE MEDICINE" BOOKMARK ON THE LEFT SIDE OF THE SCREEN 
AND REVIEW ALL INFORMATION INCLUDED IN THIS WINDOW.  COMPLETE STEPS 1 
THROUGH 3 IN THEIR ENTIRETY.  IF INTERNET SERVICES ARE NOT AVAILABLE, 
THE POINT OF CONTACT IS LISTED BELOW FOR A FAX OR EMAIL COPY OF 
INSTRUCTIONS AND FORMS.  
4.  THE PREFERRED METHOD FOR APPLICATION SUBMISSION IS BY MAIL.
APPLICATIONS WILL ALSO BE ACCEPTED VIA FAX OR EMAIL (SIGNED COPIES ONLY) 
FOR THOSE OFFICERS DEPLOYED OR AT REMOTE SITES AROUND THE GLOBE.
