RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE COALITION OF SAILORS AGAINST DESTRUCTIVE 
DECISIONS SEA AND SHORE CHAPTERS OF THE YEAR FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2012.

2.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE CSADD SEA AND SHORE CHAPTERS 
OF THE YEAR.  THE COMPETITION WAS FIERCE AND EACH COMMAND DID AN OUTSTANDING 
JOB PROMOTING THE IMPORTANCE OF THE CSADD PROGRAM.

3.  THE FOLLOWING CHAPTERS WERE SELECTED AS REGION SEA CHAPTERS OF THE YEAR.  
FROM THESE FOUR CHAPTERS THE WINNER OF SEA CHAPTER OF THE YEAR WAS SELECTED:
    A.  NAVY REGION MID-ATLANTIC - USS ENTERPRISE (CVN 65)
    B.  NAVY REGION SOUTHWEST - USS PELELIU (LHA 5)
    C.  NAVY REGION JAPAN - USS GEORGE WASHINGTON (CVN 73)
    D.  NAVY REGION HAWAII - PATROL SQUADRON NINE (VP 9)

4.  THE CSADD SEA CHAPTER OF THE YEAR IS THE USS GEORGE WASHINGTON  (CVN 73).
THE GEORGE WASHINGTON (CVN 73) HAS LED FROM THE FRONT BY MAKING THEIR  CSADD 
CHAPTER THE BEST IN THE FLEET.
    A.  HONORABLE MENTION (LARGE) - USS PELELIU (LHA 5)
    B.  HONORABLE MENTION (SMALL) - VP 9

5.  THE FOLLOWING CHAPTERS WERE SELECTED AS REGION SHORE CHAPTERS OF THE YEAR.  
FROM THESE SEVEN CHAPTERS THE WINNER OF SHORE CHAPTER OF THE YEAR WAS 
SELECTED:
    A.  NAVY REGION MID-ATLANTIC - NAVAL STATION NORFOLK
    B.  NAVY REGION SOUTHWEST - FLEET ANTI-SUBMARINE WARFARE TRAINING CENTER, 
SAN DIEGO
    C.  NAVY REGION MIDWEST - NAVAL STATION GREAT LAKES
    D.  NAVAL DISTRICT WASHINGTON - NAVAL SUPPORT ACTIVITY WASHINGTON D.C.
    E.  NAVY REGION SOUTHEAST - NAVY MEDICINE TRAINING SUPPORT CENTER (NMTSC) 
FORT SAM HOUSTON
    F.  NAVY REGION JAPAN - FLEET ACTIVITIES, YOKOSUKA
    G.  NAVY REGION EUROPE - NAVAL SUPPORT ACTIVITY, BAHRAIN

6.  THE CSADD SHORE CHAPTER OF THE YEAR IS FLEET ANTI-SUBMARINE WARFARE 
TRAINING CENTER, SAN DIEGO.  THE FLEASWTRACEN SAN DEIGO CHAPTER HAS SET A PACE 
FOR ALL COMMANDS TO EMULATE.
    A.  HONORABLE MENTION (TRAINING COMMAND) - NMTSC FORT SAM HOUSTON
    B.  HONORABLE MENTION (SHORE INSTALLATION) - FLEET ACTIVITIES YOKOSUKA

7.  SOME OF THE BEST PRACTICES THAT MADE THE WINNING CHAPTERS STAND OUT WERE:
    A.  THE CHAPTERS USE OF SOCIAL MEDIA TO SUPPORT THE PROGRAM.
    B.  ENCOURAGING SAILORS TO LEAD THROUGH CSADD.
    C.  STRONG SUPPORT FROM THE COMMAND LEADERSHIP TEAM.
    D.  THE CHAPTERS ABILITY TO DOCUMENT, THROUGH METRICS, THE IMPACT THE 
CSADD SAILORS MADE TO CONTRIBUTE TOWARD SAILORS AND COMMAND MISSION SUCCESS.

8.  CONGRATULATIONS TO ALL COMMANDS THAT HAVE AN ACTIVE CSADD CHAPTER AND WE 
LOOK FORWARD TO THE YEAR AHEAD.

9.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
