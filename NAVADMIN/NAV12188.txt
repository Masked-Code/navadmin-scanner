RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE 32ND ANNUAL ASSOCIATION OF NAVAL SERVICES 
OFFICERS (ANSO) LEADERSHIP, PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING GENERAL 
CONVENTION BEING HELD 6-10 AUGUST 2012 AT THE MANCHESTER GRAND HYATT, SAN 
DIEGO, CA.

2.  ANSO FOSTERS THE ADVANCEMENT AND RECOGNITION OF HISPANIC OFFICERS IN THE 
UNIFORMED NAVAL SERVICES.  THE 2012 CONVENTION THEME IS "ANSO: TODAY'S VISION-
TOMORROW'S REALITY".

3.  UNIFORM FOR NAVY PERSONNEL WILL BE SERVICE KHAKI FOR E7 AND ABOVE AND 
SERVICE UNIFORM FOR E6 AND BELOW.  CIVILIANS SHOULD WEAR EQUIVALENT BUSINESS 
ATTIRE.  THE UNIFORM FOR THE FORMAL AWARDS BANQUET WILL BE DINNER DRESS WHITE 
JACKET (OR OPTIONAL DINNER DRESS WHITE UNIFORM FOR O3 AND BELOW) AND BLACK TIE 
(OR EQUIVALENT) FOR CIVILIANS AND GUESTS.

4.  AUTHORIZATION TO EXPEND APPROPRIATED FUNDS FOR ATTENDANCE OR SUPPORT AT 
THIS CONFERENCE IS PENDING OSD APPROVAL AND WILL BE PROMULGATED AT A LATER 
DATE.

5.  REGISTRATION, TRANSPORTATION, AND LODGING INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND ON THE 
ANSO WEBSITE AT WWW.ANSOMIL.ORG.  EARLY REGISTRATION FEES FOR THE ENTIRE 
CONVENTION, INCLUDING THE AWARDS BANQUET, ARE $255 FOR LIFETIME MEMBERS, $325 
FOR MEMBERS, $400 FOR NON-MEMBERS AND $100 DOLLARS FOR MIDSHIPMEN AND CADETS.  
REGULAR REGISTRATION FEES WILL BE AN ADDITIONAL $50 FOR ATTENDEES WHO REGISTER 
AFTER    11 JULY 2012. EARLY DAILY REGISTRATION FEES ARE $125 FOR MEMBERS 
(INCLUDING LIFETIME) AND $150 FOR NON-MEMBERS.  REGULAR DAILY REGISTRATION 
FEES (NOT INCLUDING THE BANQUET) WILL BE AN ADDITIONAL $25 FOR ATTENDEES WHO 
REGISTER AFTER 11 JULY 2012.  SPECIAL RATES FOR GUEST ATTENDANCE AT THE 
SERVICE LUNCHEONS ARE $25 (E6/BELOW) AND $50 (E7/ABOVE) EACH AND $50 
(E6/BELOW) AND $75 (E7/ABOVE) FOR THE AWARDS BANQUET.

6.  NAVY PERSONNEL INTERESTED IN JOINING, REGISTERING, OR OBTAINING ADDITIONAL 
INFORMATION MAY VISIT THE ANSO WEBSITE AT WWW.ANSOMIL.ORG.
THE CHIEF OF NAVAL PERSONNEL WILL FUND THE FIRST 100 CONVENTION REGISTRATIONS 
FOR THE RANKS OF LIEUTENANT COMMANDER OR BELOW.  EARLY CONVENTION REGISTRATION 
AND ROOM RESERVATIONS ARE HIGHLY ENCOURAGED.

7.  POINTS OF CONTACT ARE:
    A.  FOR OFFICERS AND CIVILIANS, CONTACT CDR NORA M. PEREZ, USN AT
(619) 846-6940 OR VIA E-MAIL AT NORA.PEREZ(AT)AMEDD.ARMY.MIL.
    B.  FOR ENLISTED PERSONNEL, CONTACT PS1 LUIS GALVEZ-MERAZ, USN AT
(804) 765-4060 OR VIA E-MAIL AT ANSO.NAVY.ENL(AT)GMAIL.COM.
