SUBJ/FY-12 SPECIALTY CAREER PATH SELECTION BOARD// 

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE FY-12 SPECIALTY CAREER PATH
(SCP) SELECTION BOARD TO BE HELD 25-29 JULY 2011.  THE SCP PROGRAM, ORIGINALLY 
ESTABLISHED WITHIN THE SURFACE WARFARE COMMUNITY IN 2005, WAS EXPANDED TO 
INCLUDE OTHER UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) OFFICERS IN 2010.  THE SCP PROGRAM IS 
DESIGNED TO DEVELOP AND UTILIZE SELECTED OFFICERS WITHIN THE 10 DISTINCT 
SPECIALTY CAREER PATHS.  THE SCP PROGRAM HAS PROVEN TO BE A SUCCESSFUL 
INITIATIVE A HAS PROVIDED THE NAVY WITH A CADRE OF OFFICERS WITH CRITICAL 
SKILLS IN KEY NAVY MISSION AREAS.

2.  THE SCP PROGRAM PROVIDES ALTERNATIVES TO THE TRADITIONAL COMMAND AT SEA 
CAREER PATH AND SUPPORTS THE DEMAND FOR URL LCDR/CDR/CAPT EXPERTISE IN GROWING 
AND EXPANDING MISSION AREAS.  THE FOLLOWING SCP OPTIONS ARE OPEN TO QUALIFIED 
CANDIDATES:
  - OPERATIONS ANALYSIS
  - FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT
  - NAVAL OPERATIONAL PLANNER
  - ANTITERRORISM/FORCE PROTECTION
  - SHORE INSTALLATION MANAGEMENT
  - ANTISUBMARINE WARFARE
  - MINE WARFARE
  - MISSILE DEFENSE
  - STRATEGIC SEALIFT
  - EDUCATION AND TRAINING MANAGEMENT

3.  BENEFITS AND OPPORTUNITIES OF SELECTION INCLUDE DESIGNATED SCP BILLETS 
WITHIN SPECIALTY AREA, SPECIALTY TRAINING AND EXPERIENCE, CAREER MILESTONE 
BILLETS WITHIN SPECIALTY AREA, AND POTENTIAL TO SCREEN FOR CO/XO EQUIVALENT 
TOURS.

4.  OFFICERS DESIRING TO ENTER ONE OF THE SCP'S MAY DO SO UPON APPLICATION AND 
SELECTION.  THIS ADMINISTRATIVE BOARD, CHAIRED BY A FLAG OFFICER, WILL SELECT 
OFFICERS BEST QUALIFIED TO SERVE THE NEEDS OF THE NAVY IN EACH OF THE SCP'S.  
ONCE SELECTED, OFFICERS WILL RECEIVE APPROPRIATE TRAINING AND EDUCATION.  THEY 
WILL ALSO BE GIVEN PRIORITY FOR ASSIGNMENTS TO BILLETS THAT DEVELOP EXPERIENCE 
IN THEIR SPECIALTY DURING NORMAL SEA/SHORE ROTATIONS IN THEIR ORIGINAL URL 
COMMUNITY.  SELECTED OFFICERS WILL RETAIN THEIR ORIGINAL DESIGNATOR AND WILL 
RECEIVE AN ADDITIONAL QUALIFICATION DESIGNATOR (AQD) THAT INDICATES THEIR AREA 
OF SPECIALIZATION.  CAREER PATHS ARE AVAILABLE IN EACH OF THE 10 SCP PATHS 
WITH JOBS INCREASING IN COMPLEXITY AND RESPONSIBILITY.  DURING THAT 
PROGRESSION, THE OFFICER WILL GAIN EXPERIENCE AND DEVELOP MANAGEMENT AND 
LEADERSHIP SKILLS THAT WILL BEST SERVE THE NAVY WHILE PROVIDING ENHANCED 
OPPORTUNITY FOR SUCCESSFUL CAREER TRANSITION UPON RETIREMENT.  STAKEHOLDER 
POINTS OF CONTACT FOR EACH SCP AREA, SAMPLE CAREER PATHS, AND SCP BILLETS CAN 
BE VIEWED AT THE FOLLOWING NPC WEB SITE, 
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/SPECIALTYCAREERPATH.HTM.  CONTACT YOUR 
RESPECTIVE DETAILER FOR MORE INFORMATION.

6.  APPLICANTS MUST MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS AND MAY APPLY FOR UP TO TWO 
SPECIALTY CAREER PATHS.  OFFICERS CURRENTLY BEING PROCESSED FOR ADMINISTRATIVE 
SEPARATION, WHO ARE SUBJECTS OF PENDING DETACHMENT FOR CAUSE REQUESTS, OR HAVE 
BEEN DETACHED FOR CAUSE, ARE INELIGIBLE FOR THIS PROGRAM.  AN OFFICER WHO HAS 
A REQUEST FOR RESIGNATION PENDING, OR HAS A RESIGNATION ALREADY APPROVED BY 
THE SECRETARY OF THE NAVY, MUST FIRST SUBMIT A RESIGNATION WITHDRAWAL REQUEST.  
AN APPLICATION FOR THIS PROGRAM WILL NOT BE PROCESSED UNTIL THE REQUEST FOR

7.  THE FY-12 SCP BOARD WILL ALSO SELECT THE APPROPRIATE NUMBER OF SCP 
OFFICERS FOR CO AND XO EQUIVALENT BILLETS WITHIN EACH MISSION AREA.  ALL 
PREVIOUSLY UNSELECTED LCDR SCP OFFICERS IN EACH MISSION AREA WILL BE 
CONSIDERED FOR SCP XO SELECTION.  ALL PREVIOUSLY NSELECTED CDR SCP OFFICERS IN 
EACH MISSION AREA WILL BE CONSIDERED FOR SCP CO SELECTION.  SCP CO AND XO 
SCREENED OFFICERS WILL BE ELIGIBLE TO FILL DESIGNATED SCP CAREER MILESTONE 
BILLETS.
