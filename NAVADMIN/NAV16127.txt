RMKS/1.  The purpose of this NAVADMIN is to solicit the 2016 Annual Navy 
Community Service Awards program nominations.

2.  In accordance with reference (a), the Navy annually recognizes the most 
outstanding community service program initiatives by individual commands.

3.  In accordance with reference (a), CNIC is the Navy's lead responsible for 
administering the Navy Community Service Award Program and coordinating the 
efforts of Flagship sponsors and Region coordinators.

4.  Each Flagship sponsor will conduct an awards board with judges 
representing Active duty, Reserve, Navy and community-based organizations.  
The awards board will select Navy-wide winners in each category and Flagship.  
CNIC's Flagship awards board will also consider nominations for the USS 
BAINBRIDGE (CGN 25) Award for Overall Excellence.

5.  Guidance on Submitting Nomination Packages
    a.  All Navy commands are eligible to compete for the 2016 Navy Community 
Service Awards program.
    b.  Deadline for commands to submit nominations to the Navy Community 
Service Program Geographic Region Coordinator (GRC) is 31 July 2016.  
Submissions must be in the format provided in reference (b).  A fillable PDF 
submission data sheet and guidelines are available through the Region 
coordinator.
    c.  Nominations cannot exceed ten pages in length.  A maximum of five 
pages of nomination information and five pages of supporting documents, such 
as news clippings, letters of appreciation, and local community awards are 
permitted.
    d.  Only the GRC is authorized to submit nominations directly to Flagship 
sponsors and CNIC for the USS BAINBRIDGE (CGN 25) Award for Overall 
Excellence.

6.  Timeline For Processing Of Nominations
    a.  31 July:  Command nomination packages due to GRC.
    b.  30 September:  GRC nominations due to Flagship sponsors
    c.  1-27 October:  Nominations processed through Flagship sponsor awards 
boards
    d.  31 October:  Awards boards finalize nominations.
    e.  30 November:  Flagship sponsors release NAVADMIN to announce awardees
    f.  31 December:  Awards plaques and certificates distributed to awardees
