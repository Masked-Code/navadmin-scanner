SUBJ/ASSOCIATION OF NAVAL SERVICES OFFICERS (ANSO) PROFESSIONAL 
/DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING SYMPOSIUM// 
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THAT THE TWENTY-SEVENTH 
ANNUAL ASSOCIATION OF NAVAL SERVICES OFFICERS (ANSO) PROFESSIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING SYMPOSIUM WILL BE HELD 21-24 MAR 07 AT THE 
ADMIRAL KIDD CONFERENCE CENTER, IN SAN DIEGO, CA.  THE SYMPOSIUM WILL 
INCLUDE EDUCATIONAL AND TRAINING OPPORTUNITIES, AS WELL AS RELEVANT 
PRESENTATIONS FROM SENIOR SERVICE LEADERSHIP THAT IS INTENDED TO 
ENHANCE THE PROFESSIONAL KNOWLEDGE OF ATTENDEES WHILE INCREASING 
OVERALL AWARENESS OF ISSUES AFFECTING THE SEA SERVICES.
THE THEME FOR THIS YEAR S CONFERENCE IS "HISPANIC AMERICANS:  OUR RICH 
CULTURE CONTRIBUTING TO AMERICA S FUTURE."  THE SYMPOSIUM IS OPEN TO 
ALL PERSONNEL.
2.  ANSO S MISSION IS TO ASSIST OFFICER RECRUITMENT EFFORTS IN THE 
HISPANIC COMMUNITY; SUPPORT AND NURTURE JUNIOR ENLISTED PERSONNEL 
DESIRING TO APPLY FOR OFFICER PROGRAMS; AND FOSTER THE PROFESSIONAL 
GROWTH AND DEVELOPMENT OF HISPANIC SEA SERVICES PERSONNEL THROUGH 
MENTORING, NETWORKING, TRAINING, AND EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS.
3.  COMMANDING OFFICERS ARE AUTHORIZED AND ENCOURAGED TO SUPPORT 
ATTENDANCE AND MAY UTILIZE AVAILABLE TAD FUNDS.  IF UNIT FUNDS ARE NOT 
AVAILABLE, PERMISSIVE TAD ORDERS MAY BE ISSUED.  USE OF AVAILABLE 
GOVERNMENT TRANSPORTATION AND ON-BASE LODGING IS ENCOURAGED TO ENHANCE 
CONFERENCE PARTICIPATION AND TO HELP DEFRAY COSTS.
4.  CONFERENCE UNIFORM FOR NAVY PERSONNEL WILL BE SERVICE KHAKI AND 
BANQUET UNIFORM WILL BE DINNER DRESS BLUE JACKET (OR OPTIONAL SERVICE 
DRESS BLUE FOR O3 AND BELOW).  CIVILIAN ATTENDEES AND GUESTS SHOULD 
WEAR BLACK TIE OR EQUIVALENT.
5.  FOR LODGING RESERVATIONS, PLEASE CONTACT CWO4 JORGE CORTES AT
(860) 701-6438 OR EMAIL AT JORGE.CORTES@USCG.MIL.  REGISTRATION 
PROCEDURES, LODGING INFORMATION, AND ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ON ANSO AND 
THE SYMPOSIUM ARE AVAILABLE AT WWW.ANSOMIL.ORG OR BY CONTACTING LT 
MICHAEL FILES AT (619) 553-4754 OR EMAIL AT MICHAEL.FILES@NAVY.MIL.
6.  RELEASED BY VADM J. C. HARVEY, JR., N1.// BT
