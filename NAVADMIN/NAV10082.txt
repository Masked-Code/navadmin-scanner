SUBJ/FY11 NURSE CORPS MEDICAL ENLISTED COMMISSIONING PROGRAM SELECTION 
BOARD// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE FY11 NURSE CORPS (NC) MEDICAL 
ENLISTED COMMISSIONING PROGRAM (MECP) SELECTION BOARD.  MECP PROVIDES A 
PATH FOR ACTIVE DUTY ENLISTED SAILORS AND MARINES, INCLUDING FULL-TIME 
SUPPORT (FTS) AND ACTIVE DUTY RESERVE SAILORS IN ANY RATING OR MILITARY 
OCCUPATIONAL SPECIALTY WHO ALREADY HAVE SOME COLLEGE CREDIT, TO OBTAIN 
A BACCALAUREATE DEGREE IN NURSING AND A COMMISSION IN THE NURSE CORPS.  
RESERVISTS IN THE INDIVIDUAL READY RESERVE (IRR) OR THOSE SERVING ON 
ACTIVE DUTY FOR SPECIAL WORK (ADSW), ACTIVE DUTY TRAINING (ADT), OR 1-3 
YEAR RECALLS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR THIS PROGRAM.
2.  THE FY11 MECP SELECTION BOARD WILL CONVENE IN NOVEMBER 2010.  
ALL APPLICATIONS MUST BE POSTMARKED BY 24 SEPTEMBER 2010.  
ADDITIONAL OR AMPLIFYING DOCUMENTS MUST BE POSTMARKED NO LATER THAN
15 OCTOBER 2010.  INCOMPLETE PACKAGES WILL NOT BE REVIEWED BY THE BOARD.
3.  APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS AND PROCEDURES ARE FOUND IN CHAPTERS 1, 2, 
AND 5 OF OPNAVINST 1420.1B AND SHOULD BE READ IN THEIR ENTIRETY.  
ADDITIONAL APPLICATION INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND AT: 
HTTP://WWW.MED.NAVY.MIL/SITES/NAVMEDMPTE/PAGES/MEDICALENLISTEDCOMMIS
SIONINGPROGRAM.ASPX.  APPLICANTS MUST NOT HAVE REACHED THEIR 42ND 
BIRTHDAY BY THE TIME OF INITIAL COMMISSION.  AGE WAIVERS WILL NOT BE 
GRANTED.  A MARADMIN WILL OUTLINE CONDITIONAL RELEASE PROCEDURES FOR 
ACTIVE DUTY MARINES WHO ARE SELECTED TO PARTICIPATE IN THE PROGRAM.  
4.  POINTS OF CONTACT:  MS. KEMP, NAVY MEDICAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING 
COMMAND (NAVMEC MPT&E) (CODE OC3 HMDT) AT (301) 319- 4520/DSN 285, OR 
E-MAIL AT BEVERLY.KEMP(AT)MED.NAVY.MIL OR HM2 SOUCY AT (301) 295-
0925/DSN 295 OR E-MAIL:  ALLYSON.SOUCY(AT)MED.NAVY.MIL.
5.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
