RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE PERMANENT MILITARY PROFESSOR
 (PMP)
SELECTION BOARD FOR OFFICERS MATRICULATING IN GRADUATE SCHOOL IN  AUGUST 
2015.
SELECTION TO PMP WILL BE MADE BY AN INSTITUTIONAL SCREENING BOARD,  CHAIRED 
BY THE SUPERINTENDENT, UNITED STATES NAVAL ACADEMY (USNA), WHICH WILL  SELECT 
FROM QUALIFIED OFFICERS WHO SUBMIT COMPLETED APPLICATIONS.

2.  REF (A), PROVIDES SPECIFIC APPLICATION AND ELIGIBILITY GUIDANCE.
APPLICATIONS ARE DUE TO COMMANDER, NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-440)  NLT 1 
MAY 2014.
MAIL APPLICATIONS TO:
    DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY
    NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-440)
    5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE
    MILLINGTON, TN 38055-4400
IN ADDITION TO THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS IN REF (A), THESE  ADDITIONAL 
RESTRICTIONS APPLY:
    A.  CAPTAIN AND CAPTAIN SELECTS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO APPLY.
    B.  FULL-TIME SUPPORT (FTS) OFFICERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO APPLY.
    C.  APPLICANTS MUST HAVE COMPLETED AT LEAST ONE YEAR AT THEIR  CURRENT 
COMMAND BY JULY 2015.  OFFICERS SERVING IN A DESIGNATED JOINT BILLET  MUST 
HAVE COMPLETED AT LEAST 22 MONTHS BY JULY 2015.
    D.  OFFICERS WHO ARE COMMITTED TO A SPECIFIC COMMUNITY THROUGH A 
CONTINUOUS PAY AGREEMENT (E.G., NUCLEAR OFFICER INCENTIVE PAY,  AVIATION 
CAREER CONTINUATION PAY, SURFACE WARFARE CRITICAL SKILLS RETENTION
 BONUS) OR
A SIMILAR SERVICE AGREEMENT MUST BE WITHIN ONE YEAR OF COMPLETION OF  THE 
AGREED UPON SERVICE OBLIGATION BY AUGUST 2014.
    E.  OFFICERS WHO HAVE RECEIVED FUNDED GRADUATE EDUCATION FROM  THEIR 
CURRENT COMMUNITY MUST BE ABLE TO COMPLETE THE ASSOCIATED OBLIGATED  SERVICE 
IDENTIFIED IN REF (B) PRIOR TO MATRICULATING INTO GRADUATE SCHOOL IN  AUGUST 
2015.

3.  THE FY-15 PMP SELECTION BOARD MAY SELECT UP TO A TOTAL OF 10 PMPS  FOR 
ASSIGNMENT TO USNA FROM THE FOLLOWING 15 FIELDS (DOCTOR OF PHILOSOPHY DEGREE 
STUDY LOCATION - DISCIPLINE):
    A.  AFIT - AERONAUTICAL ENGINEERING
    B.  NPS - ASTRONAUTICAL ENGINEERING
    C.  NPS/CIVINS - MECHANICAL ENGINEERING
    D.  CIVINS - NUCLEAR ENGINEERING
    E.  CIVINS - CHEMISTRY
    F.  CIVINS - NAVAL ARCHITECTURE
    G.  CIVINS - OCEAN ENGINEERING
    H.  NPS - SYSTEMS ENGINEERING
    I.  NPS - MATHEMATICS (OPERATIONS RESEARCH)
    J.  NPS - COMPUTER SCIENCE/INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
    K.  NPS - PHYSICS
    L.  NPS - ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING
    M.  CIVINS - ECONOMICS
    N.  NPS - CYBER/NETWORKING (COMPUTER SCIENCE, ELECTRICAL
 ENGINEERING)
    O.  CIVINS - HISTORY
REF (A) PROVIDES SPECIFIC GUIDANCE CONCERNING QUALIFYING CIVINS FOR 
CHEMISTRY, NAVAL ARCHITECTURE, AND OCEAN ENGINEERING.  APPLICANTS FOR 
DISCIPLINES TAUGHT AT NPS ARE REQUIRED TO APPLY TO NPS AT  WWW.NPS.EDU.

4.  THE FY-15 PMP SELECTION BOARD MAY SELECT UP TO ONE PMP FOR ENROLLMENT AT 
SALVE REGINA UNIVERSITY OR ONE OF SEVERAL OTHER  CIVILIAN INSTITUTIONS WITHIN 
THE NAVAL WAR COLLEGE (NWC) LOCAL COMMUTING AREA WITH ULTIMATE  ASSIGNMENT TO 
NWC FROM THE FOLLOWING FIELDS:
    A.  POLITICAL SCIENCE (INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS, COMPARATIVE  POLITICS, 
FOREIGN POLICY ANALYSIS, OR U.S. FOREIGN POLICY).
    B.  LEADERSHIP THEORY.

5.  TO ENSURE AN ADEQUATE RETURN ON THE EDUCATION INVESTMENT IN PMP  
OFFICERS, THE FOLLOWING YEAR GROUP (YG) ELIGIBILITY APPLIES.  YG ELIGIBILITY 
IS  NOT
WAIVERABLE:
    A.  YG-97 AND JUNIOR FOR CDR OR CDR SELECT WITHOUT PHD.
    B.  YG-96 FOR CDR APPLICANTS WITHOUT A PHD COMING DIRECTLY OUT OF 
OPERATIONAL COMMAND BY JULY 2015.
    C.  YG-93 AND JUNIOR FOR CDR OR CDR SELECTS WITH A PHD.

6.  OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGERS (OCM) WILL DETERMINE DESIGNATORS AND  YGS THAT 
ARE ELIGIBLE TO TRANSFER A LIMITED NUMBER OF OFFICERS TO THE PMP  COMMUNITY.
OCMS WILL REVIEW ALL PMP APPLICANTS TO ENSURE ELIGIBILITY GUIDELINES  HAVE 
BEEN MET PRIOR TO RELEASING THE APPLICANT TO THE BOARD.

7.  THE FOLLOWING DRAFT PLAN OF THE DAY/PLAN OF THE WEEK NOTE IS QQQQ  
PROVIDED FOR USE IN THE FLEET:
"THERE IS A CONTINUED NEED FOR WARFARE QUALIFIED NAVAL OFFICERS TO  SERVE AS 
LONG-TERM OFFICER-INSTRUCTORS AT THE USNA AND NWC.  THE PMP COMMUNITY 
PROVIDES DOCTORAL DEGREE OPPORTUNITIES FOR SELECTED COMMANDERS WHO  WILL 
SERVE AS INSTRUCTORS UNTIL THEIR STATUTORY RETIREMENT.  THE DEADLINE FOR 
APPLICATIONS FOR THE FY-15 PMP SELECTION BOARD IS 1 MAY 2014 WITH  OFFICERS 
MATRICULATING INTO GRADUATE SCHOOL OR BEING ASSIGNED AS AN INSTRUCTOR  IN 
AUGUST 2015."
