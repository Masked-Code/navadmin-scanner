RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the FY-16 Medical Service Corps (MSC) Duty 
Under Instruction (DUINS) Selection Board scheduled to be convened by 
Commander, Navy Personnel Command (NAVPERSCOM) on 16 September 2015.

2.  Background.  Full-time DUINS for MSC officers provide the necessary 
preparation to meet critical education and training needs, thereby enabling 
the MSC to fulfill its mission and benefit the Navy and the individual by:  
ensuring higher levels of professional knowledge, providing incentives for 
recruitment and retention of personnel with ability, dedication and capacity 
for growth, and recognizing educational aspiration of individuals through 
advanced degrees and certification programs.  MSC officers who participate in 
full-time DUINS receive full pay and allowances of their grade.  The Navy 
Medical Department pays tuition.

3.  References (a) through (f) outline application procedures and quotas 
which are posted on the Navy Medicine Professional Development Command 
website at http://www.med.navy.mil/sites/nmpdc/professional-
development/SitePages/Medical%20Service%20Corps%20Graduate%20Programs.aspx.

4.  Deadline for applications to reach NAVPERSCOM, Medical Department 
Assignments (PERS-4415) is 31 July 2015.  Late or incomplete applications 
will not be considered.

5.  Point of contact is Ms. Gabriele Farmer at (901) 874-4097/DSN 882 or via 
e-mail at gabriele.farmer(at)navy.mil.

6.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or 31 May 2016, 
whichever occurs first.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
