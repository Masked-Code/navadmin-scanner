SUBJ/NOVEMBER 2009 OFFICER TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION BOARD//
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE NOVEMBER 2009 ACTIVE DUTY 
OFFICER TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION BOARD.  ONE ELEMENT OF NAVY'S 
TOTAL FORCE STRATEGY IS THE ABILITY TO PROPERLY BALANCE THE 
SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE RESIDENT IN THE OFFICER CORPS.  AS PART 
OF THE PROCESS, APPLICATIONS FROM QUALIFIED UNRESTRICTED LINE, 
RESTRICTED LINE, STAFF CORPS AND LIMITED DUTY OFFICERS ARE NOW 
BEING ACCEPTED FOR AN ACTIVE DUTY OFFICER TRANSFER-REDESIGNATION 
BOARD WHICH CONVENES 16 NOVEMBER 2009.  FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) 
AND SELECTED RESERVE (SELRES) OFFICERS CAN ONLY APPLY FOR 
REDESIGNATION INTO THOSE COMMUNITIES THAT HAVE SPECIFICALLY 
INDICATED BELOW THAT THEY HAVE CAPACITY TO ACCEPT RESERVISTS 
DURING THIS BOARD.
2.  IN ADDITION TO THE YEAR GROUP (YG) LIMITATIONS IMPOSED IN 
PARA 6, APPLICANTS MUST ALSO MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS 
DETAILED IN OPNAVINST 1210.5 AND MILPERSMAN 1212-010 OR 1212-030. 
    A.  MEMBERS WHO DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS PER 
OPNAVINST 1210.5 AND MILPERSMAN 1212-010 OR 1212-030, SUCH 
AS TIME-IN-GRADE (TIG), YEARS-OF-COMMISSIONED SERVICE (YCS), OR 
TIME-IN-SERVICE (TIS), MUST SUBMIT A WAIVER PACKAGE IN ADDITION 
TO THEIR LATERAL TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION APPLICATION. 
    B.  WAIVER PACKAGE MUST INCLUDE MEMBER'S WAIVER REQUEST AND 
COMMANDING OFFICER'S ENDORSEMENT.  THIS ENDORSEMENT IS SEPARATE 
FROM ANY ENDORSEMENTS CONTAINED IN THE MEMBER'S LATERAL 
TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION APPLICATION AND MUST SPECIFICALLY ADDRESS 
THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENT REQUIRING WAIVER.  
3.  SUBMIT APPLICATIONS AND WAIVER PACKAGES PER 
MILPERSMAN 1212-010 FORMAT TO THE ADDRESS BELOW:
    COMMANDER NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND
    CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER
    NOV 2009 TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION (BOARD #40)

4.  APPLICATION AND WAIVER PACKAGES MUST BE RECEIVED NOT LATER 
THAN 21 SEPTEMBER 2009.  PDF OR FAX COPIES WILL BE ACCEPTED FOR 
THOSE APPLICANTS WHO HAVE EXHAUSTED ALL OTHER MEANS OF DELIVERY.  
FAX APPLICATIONS TO (901) 874-2044/DSN 882.

7.  NOTES:
    A.  MUST HAVE SURFACE WARFARE OFFICER (SWO) QUALIFICATION 
COMPLETED TO APPLY FOR TRANSFER.  OFFICERS UNDER SURFACE WARFARE 
OFFICER CONTINUATION PAY CONTRACT ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR TRANSFER 
UNTIL COMPLETION OF DEPARTMENT HEAD TOURS.
    B.  ALL OFFICERS SCREENED "NOT CLEARED" FOR THEIR NEXT CAREER 
MILESTONE ARE ALSO ELIGIBLE.
    C.  ALL OFFICERS YG 97 AND SENIOR ARE ELIGIBLE, REGARDLESS OF 
PLATFORM.
    D.  NUCLEAR LIMITED DUTY OFFICER TRANSFER REQUESTS WILL BE 
REVIEWED ON A CASE BY CASE BASIS CONSIDERING SPECIFIC YG INVENTORY 
DEMANDS.
    E.  OFFICERS YG 05 AND SENIOR APPLYING FOR SWO ARE ENCOURAGED 
TO CONTACT THE SWO COMMUNITY MANAGER TO DISCUSS SWO CAREER 
MILESTONE REQUIREMENTS. 
    F.  SWO COMMUNITY ACCEPTING APPLICATIONS FROM SELRES/FTS 
OFFICERS.  
    G.  NAVAL SPECIAL WARFARE WILL ONLY SELECT ONE QUALIFIED 
CANDIDATE AT THIS BOARD.
    H.  YEAR GROUPS 00-99 ARE RESTRICTED TO 648X OFFICERS.
    I.  YEAR GROUPS 00-97 ARE RESTRICTED TO LCDRS ONLY, WHO 
HAVE MET THE CAREER MILESTONES AND QUALIFICATIONS CONSISTENT 
WITH THE AEROSPACE MAINTENANCE DUTY OFFICER COMMUNITY.  
APPLICANTS ARE ENCOURAGED TO CONTACT THE AEROSPACE MAINTENANCE 
DUTY OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGER TO DISCUSS MILESTONES AND 
QUALIFICATIONS.
    J.  O4 AND O5 OFFICERS APPLYING FOR FOREIGN AREA OFFICER (FAO) 
WITH MORE THAN TWO YEARS TIME IN GRADE SHOULD CONTACT THE FAO 
COMMUNITY MANAGER TO DISCUSS MILESTONE AND PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS. 
FOREIGN AREA OFFICER COMMUNITY IS ACCEPTING APPLICATIONS FROM 
SELRES OFFICERS. 
    K.  SUPPLY CORPS HAS QUOTAS AVAILABLE FOR LIEUTENANT YG04-02, 
AND JUNIOR LIEUTENANT COMMANDERS FROM YG 98-96.
8.  EACH APPLICANT IS REQUIRED TO LIST THEIR FIRST AND SECOND 
LATERAL TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION CHOICE BY PRIORITY IN THEIR 
APPLICATION.  ADDITIONALLY, EACH APPLICANT IS REQUIRED TO 
INDICATE THEIR DESIRE TO BE CONSIDERED/NOT CONSIDERED FOR 
LATERAL TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION BY COMMUNTIES NOT LISTED AS 
ONE OF THEIR PRIMARY CHOICES IN THE APPLICATION WITH THE 
FOLLOWING STATEMENT:  
"IF NOT SELECTED BY ONE OF MY PRIMARY CHOICES, I DESIRE/DO 
NOT DESIRE MY PACKAGE TO BE REVIEWED BY OTHER COMMUNITIES 
DURING THE LATERAL TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION BOARD."  IF SELECTED 
BY A COMMUNITY NOT LISTED AS ONE OF THEIR PRIMARY CHOICES, 
THESE APPLICANTS WILL BE NOTIFIED VIA OFFICIAL CORRESPONDENCE 
AFTER THE SELECTION RESULTS ARE PUBLISHED VIA NAVADMIN.
