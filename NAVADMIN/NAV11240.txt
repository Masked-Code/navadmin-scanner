RMKS/1.  PER REF A, THE GENERAL DENTIST SPECIAL PAY PLAN HAS BEEN PROMULGATED.

QUALIFIED GENERAL DENTISTS WHILE SIMULTANEOUSLY PROVIDING A DEGREE OF PAY 
PARITY COMMENSURATE WITH THEIR CIVILIAN COUNTERPARTS.  THIS IS A COMPLETE 
REVISION TO THE SUPPLEMENTAL PAY STRUCTURE FOR GENERAL DENTISTS AND THEREFORE 
THIS NAVADMIN SHOULD BE READ IN ITS ENTIRETY.

COMMAND (CNRC) TO EXECUTE ACCESSION BONUS SERVICE AGREEMENTS AND CHIEF, BUREAU

NAVADMIN AND SHALL REMAIN IN EFFECT UNTIL REPEALED OR SUPERCEDED.  RETROACTIVE 
SERVICE AGREEMENTS ARE NOT AUTHORIZED.

INCURRED FOR EACH TYPE OF BONUS AND NOTIFY THE OFFICER OF HIS/HER ADO VIA A

AGREEMENTS AND ADO.
