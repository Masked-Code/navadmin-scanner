SUBJ/ANNUAL OFFICE OF DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL INTELLIGENCE MILITARY FELLOWSHIP 
PROGRAM OPPORTUNITY FOR 2015-2016// 

REF/A/INST/OPNAV/04DEC13// AMPN/REF A IS OPNAVINST 1500.83, OFFICE OF 
DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL INTELLIGENCE MILITARY FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM.// 

RMKS/1.  The Chief of Naval Operations is pleased to announce the Office of 
Director of National Intelligence (ODNI) Military Fellowship Program for 
2015-2016.  This 10 month fellowship starts in August 2015 and is located at 
ODNI in McLean, VA.  The fellowship will provide Information Dominance Corps 
Officers (18XX) a unique opportunity to interact with leaders at the highest 
levels of the intelligence community, increase their understanding of policy 
development and decision making at the highest levels of government while 
integrating national security, governance, and information technology 
concerns.

2.  The program is open to all post-milestone active duty O5-O6 18XX officers 
with a Master's degree.  A candidate must have completed Joint Professional 
Military Education Phase I prior to being selected or on schedule to complete 
by the end of this program.  Sample application package, additional 
eligibility requirements, service obligation, and general utilization tour 
requirements are listed in reference (a), available on the Department of the 
Navy issuances website (http://doni.daps.dla.mil).

3.  Applications are due not later than 22 September 2014.  Send a completed 
application via an encrypted e-mail to both the OPNAV point of contact and 
the candidate's detailer.  A hard copy application is not required.  

4.  Point of contact is LT Kitan Bae, OPNAV N2/N6C13 at kitan.bae(at)navy.mil 
or (703) 604-6288/DSN:  644.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral Ted N. Branch, Deputy Chief of Naval Operations, 
Information Dominance, OPNAV N2N6.//
