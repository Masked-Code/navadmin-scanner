A.  ATLANTIC FLEET:  USS BARRY (DDG 52)
B.  PACIFIC FLEET:  HELICOPTER ANTI-SUBMARINE SQUADRON 2
(HELANTISUBRON TWO)

OF HORMUZ AND SUEZ CANAL TRANSITS.  HELANTISUBRON TWO'S WIDE RANGE OF
OPERATIONAL IMPROVEMENTS COUPLED WITH THEIR ESSENTIAL ROLE IN
DEVELOPING THE MK-54 TORPEDO PROGRAM AND EVALUATION OF A COMPLEX
SSBN SENSOR NETWORK MARK THEM AS READY AND IN THE LEAD.
3.  CONGRATULATIONS TO USS BARRY AND HELANTISUBRON TWO FOR YOUR HARD
WORK AND COMMITMENT TO EXCELLENCE.  YOU HAVE RAISED THE BAR.
