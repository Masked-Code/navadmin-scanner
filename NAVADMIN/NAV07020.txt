SUBJ/2007 ANNUAL MILITARY SUICIDE PREVENTION CONFERENCE// 
1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE FIFTH ANNUAL DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE 
SUICIDE PREVENTION CONFERENCE WHICH WILL BE HELD 5-8 MARCH 2007, IN 
HOLLYWOOD, FL.  THE CONFERENCE WILL PROVIDE UPDATES ON CURRENT SUICIDE 
PREVENTION INITIATIVES, STATISTICS, RESEARCH, AND SPECIFIC SERVICE 
PROGRAMS INCLUDING INSTRUCTIONS, POLICIES, AND RESOURCES.
2.  THIS YEARS CONFERENCE WILL FEATURE A NEW FORMAT AND PROVIDE VENUES 
FOR PRACTICAL AND CLINICAL INFORMATION AS WELL AS ACADEMIC AND RESEARCH 
INFORMATION.  ALL MILITARY SERVICES WILL BE REPRESENTED.
ATTENDEES WILL INCLUDE SUICIDE PREVENTION PROGRAM MANAGERS, BEHAVIORAL 
HEALTH PROFESSIONALS AND TECHNICIANS, COUNSELORS, CHAPLAINS AND 
CHAPLAIN ASSISTANTS, UNIT SUICIDE PROGRAM MANAGERS, HEALTH PROMOTION 
PROFESSIONALS, SUBSTANCE ABUSE PROFESSIONALS, COMMAND AND UNIT LEADERS, 
AND PUBLIC AFFAIRS STAFF.
3.  TO REGISTER GO TO HTTP://WWW.HA.OSD.MIL/2007MSPC/.  ALL COSTS WILL 
BE THE RESPONSIBILITY OF PARENT COMMAND.
4.  POINT OF CONTACT:  CDR ANTHONY P. DORAN (BEHAVIORAL HEALTH PROGRAM 
OFFICE, MILLINGTON, TN) AT (901) 874-6613/DSN 882 OR FAX AT
(901) 874-6874.
