AVAILABILITY.  SAILORS BEING ASSIGNED TO KEY LEADERSHIP BILLETS,
COMMAND MASTER CHIEF, CHIEF OF THE BOAT, COLLATERAL DUTY COMMAND
MASTER CHIEF/SENIOR CHIEF, AND SENIOR ENLISTED LEADERS REQUIRING THE
SKILLS TAUGHT AT THESE ACADEMIES, AND WHEN PROJECTED ROTATION DATE

ATTENDANCE ON A TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY BASIS.

ENSURE THEIR PERSONNEL MEET MINIMUM NAVY HEALTH AND PHYSICAL
READINESS STANDARDS PRIOR TO ATTENDING AN ACADEMY.  A HEIGHT,
WEIGHT, AND BODY FAT SCREENING WILL BE CONDUCTED DURING
IN-PROCESSING AT EACH ACADEMY.  PERSONNEL NOT MEETING STANDARDS WILL
NOT BE ALLOWED TO ATTEND.
