RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN DESCRIBES THE ADMINISTRATION OF CYCLE 214 E7 NAVY-WIDE 
ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATIONS AND THE FY13 ACTIVE E7 SELECTION BOARD.  IT IS THE 
RESPONSIBILITY OF THE CANDIDATE AND COMMAND TO ENSURE THE CANDIDATE'S OFFICIAL 
MILITARY PERSONNEL FILE (OMPF) IS UP TO DATE AND ANY ISSUE IMPACTING THE 
ELIGIBILITY OF THE CANDIDATE IS IDENTIFIED TO PERS-802/812.  NOTEWORTHY ITEMS 
OF INTEREST:
    A.  CUT-OFF DATE FOR CANDIDATE'S LETTER TO THE BOARD (LTB) NO LONGER 
COINCIDES WITH DATE THE LTB IS POSTMARKED.  RATHER, IT IS THE DATE THE LTB 
MUST BE RECEIVED AT NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND'S CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER.
    B.  COMMUNICATION TO THE BOARD MUST ORIGINATE FROM THE INDIVIDUAL MEMBER; 
CORRESPONDENCE FROM THE COMMAND TO THE PRESIDENT OF THE BOARD WILL NOT BE 
ACCEPTED OR CONSIDERED.

2.  TIMELINE:
    A.  TIME IN RATE (TIR) ELIGIBILITY LISTS FOR EXAMINATION ORDERING OF ALL 
OTHER RATINGS ARE AVAILABLE AT HTTPS://NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL.
COMMANDS MUST
VERIFY TIR LISTS AND TAKE THE FOLLOWING ACTIONS AS REQUIRED:
        (1) ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES NOT LISTED - COMMANDS MUST ADD CANDIDATES AND 
APPROPRIATE EXAMINATION TO THE TIR LIST.
        (2) IF LISTED CANDIDATES ARE NOT ELIGIBLE OR NO LONGER RECOMMENDED FOR 
ADVANCEMENT - COMMANDS MUST CLICK "NO" AND SUBMIT BUTTON TO DELETE NAMES FROM 
THE TIR LIST.
        (3) CANDIDATES NO LONGER ATTACHED TO COMMAND - COMMANDS MUST DELETE OR 
FORWARD EXAMINATIONS AS APPLICABLE.
        (4) APPROPRIATE EXAMINATION FOR CANDIDATE - COMMANDS MUST SELECT THE 
APPROPRIATE EXAMINATION FOR RATING CONVERSIONS, OR DIVERSE RATINGS (I.E., 
NCCC/NCCR, ATIC/ATOC).
    B.  14 NOVEMBER 2011 - 8 DECEMBER 2011:  ORDER ADDITIONAL EXAMINATIONS.  
COMMANDS/EDUCATIONAL SERVICES OFFICERS (ESO'S) MUST VERIFY THE EXAMINATION 
ORDER STATUS AT HTTPS://NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL WITHIN 14 DAYS OF PLACING REGULAR 
AND SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATION ORDERS.
IF DISCREPANCIES ARE NOTED, CONTACT THE NAVAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING 
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER (NETPDTC) AT (850) 452-1252/DSN 
922.  AFTER CLOSING OF THE TIR LISTS, EXAM ORDERS MUST BE SUBMITTED VIA 
MESSAGE TO NETPDTC PENSACOLA, FL (N321).  ESO'S MUST INFO RECEIVING COMMANDS 
TO WHICH EXAMINATIONS ARE BEING FORWARDED.
    C.  1 DECEMBER 2011:  CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS 
OUTLINED IN REF A, CHAPTER 2.  CANDIDATES WHO DO NOT MEET THE ELIGIBLITY 
REQUIREMENTS AS OF 1 DECEMBER 2011 WILL BE INVALIDATED AND WILL NOT BE 
ELIGIBLE FOR SPECIAL SELECTION BOARD CONSIDERATION.
    D.  19 JANUARY 2012:  ADMINISTRATION OF EXAMINATIONS FOR USN, FULL TIME 
SUPPORT (FTS), TO INCLUDE ACTIVE/GUARD AND RESERVE (AGR) AND USNR CANVASSER 
RECRUITER (CANREC).
    E.  31 JANUARY 2012:  CUT-OFF DATE FOR NETPDTC TO RECEIVE A REQUEST FOR A 
SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATION.
    F.  20 FEBRUARY 2012:  CUT-OFF DATE FOR ADMINISTRATION OF SUBSTITUTE 
EXAMINATIONS.
    G.  30 APRIL 2012:  LETTERS TO THE BOARD FOR FTS/AGR/CANREC
E7 SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES MUST BE RECEIVED AT NAVY PERSONNEL 
COMMAND.
    H.  14 MAY 2012:  FTS/AGR/CANREC (RESERVE) E7 SELECTION BOARD CONVENES.
    I.  4 JUNE 2012:  LETTERS TO THE BOARD FOR ACTIVE DUTY SELECTION BOARD 
ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES MUST BE RECEIVED AT NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND.
    J.  18 JUNE 2012:  USN (ACTIVE) E7 SELECTION BOARD CONVENES.

3.  ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
    A.  SAILORS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION BY THE ENLISTED RETENTION BOARD 
(ERB) ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR FUTURE ACTIVE DUTY ADVANCEMENT.  THEY SHALL NOT BE 
ADMINISTERED CYCLE 214 ADVANCEMENT EXAMS AND MAY NOT OTHERWISE QUALIFY AS 
SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBLE FOR THE FY13 E7 SELECTION BOARD.
    B.  SAILORS APPROVED FOR CHANGE IN RATING THROUGH THE ERB ARE NOT ELIGIBLE 
FOR ADVANCEMENT IN THEIR OLD RATING AND DO NOT BECOME ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT 
IN THEIR NEW RATING UNTIL THEY HAVE SUCCESSFULLY CHANGED RATING.  THESE 
SAILORS MAY TAKE THE E7 EXAM IN QQQQ THEIR OLD RATING TO ESTABLISH SELECTION 
BOARD ELIGIBILITY.  HOWEVER, PER PARAGRAPH 203.B OF REF A, SELECTION BOARD 
ELIGIBLE PERSONNEL MUST HAVE COMPLETED THE CONVERSION PROCESS AND BE IN THEIR 
NEW RATING ONE MONTH PRIOR TO BOARD CONVENING IN ORDER TO RECEIVE A REVIEW BY 
THE SELECTION BOARD IN THE NEW RATING.
    C.  TERMINAL ELIGIBILITY DATE FOR CYCLE 214 IS 1 JANUARY 2013.
PERSONNEL WITH A TIR DATE OF 1 JANUARY 2010 OR EARLIER WILL BE TIR ELIGIBLE 
FOR THIS SELECTION BOARD CYCLE.  REFER TO REF A, PARA 216, TO COMPUTE TIR.
    D.  TIR WAIVERS AND EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION OF EARLY PROMOTE CANDIDATES 
MUST BE IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF A, PARA 216.F.
CANDIDATES WHO HAVE BEEN GRANTED TIR WAIVERS MUST BE IDENTIFIED DURING 
EXAMINATION ORDERING AND ON THE LETTER OF TRANSMITTAL.  THIS WILL PREVENT 
AUTOMATIC SYSTEM INVALIDATION AS A RESULT OF INSUFFICIENT TIR.  CANDIDATES NOT 
GRANTED A TIR WAIVER BY THEIR COMMANDING OFFICER WILL NOT BE ELIGIBLE FOR 
CONSIDERATION BY EITHER THE REGULAR OR A SPECIAL SELECTION BOARD.  THE "EARLY 
PROMOTE" EVALUATIONS ON WHICH THE TIR WAIVER IS BASED MUST BE AVAILABLE TO THE 
SELECTION BOARD, OTHERWISE THE CANDIDATE WILL BE INVALIDATED.
    E.  CANDIDATES WHO REACH HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT) ON OR AFTER THE FIRST DAY 
OF THE ADVANCEMENT CYCLE (1 SEPTEMBER 2012) OR HAVE RECEIVED A HYT WAIVER TO 
REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY BEYOND 1 SEPTEMBER 2012, ARE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT 
AND WILL BE CONSIDERED BY THE SELECTION BOARD IF THEY ARE SELECTION BOARD 
ELIGIBLE.  THOSE CANDIDATES WITH A HYT DATE PRIOR TO 1 SEPTEMBER 2012 ARE NOT 
SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBLE AND SHOULD NOT BE ADMINISTERED THE JANUARY 2012 
EXAMINATION.  CANDIDATES WILL NOT BE GRANTED A HYT WAIVER FOR PARTICIPATION IN 
THE EXAMINATION OR CONSIDERATION BY THE SELECTION BOARD.
    F.  ENLISTED NAVY LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT:  REFER TO REF B.
    G.  COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS:  CANDIDATES WHO HAVE BEEN SELECTED FOR SEAMAN 
TO ADMIRAL (STA-21), MEDICAL ENLISTED COMMISSIONING, AND MEDICAL SERVICE CORPS 
IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT PROGRAMS REMAIN ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT CONSIDERATION 
TO CHIEF PETTY OFFICER. SELECTION TO ANY OTHER OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAM 
RENDERS CANDIDATES INELIGIBLE AND THEY MUST BE INVALIDATED BY THEIR COMMANDS.
    H.  BRANCH CLASS (BRCL) AND SPECIAL PROGRAM INDICATOR CODE:
BRCL 32 WITH A BLANK SPECIAL PROGRAM INDICATOR (SPI) CODE IS AN INVALID NAVY 
ACCOUNTING STATUS WHICH MAY CAUSE SAILORS TO BE REFLECTED IN THE INCORRECT 
ADVANCEMENT COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.
COMMANDS WITH SAILORS IN THIS INVALID ACCOUNTING STATUS MUST TAKE ACTION TO 
CORRECT THIS ERROR EITHER VIA NAVY STANDARD INTEGRATED PERSONNEL SYSTEM 
(NSIPS) CORRECTIVE ACTION OR THE SAILOR MUST SUBMIT A BOARD CORRECTION OF 
NAVAL RECORDS (BCNR) PETITION.  THIS ACCOUNTING STATUS IS REFLECTED ON THE 
COMMAND'S ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT (EDVR). SAILORS IN THIS 
INVALID ACCOUNTING STATUS WILL BE INVALIDATED.
    I.  SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR NUCLEAR TRAINED PERSONNEL.
TO BE SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBLE FOR E7, PERSONNEL IN NUCLEAR RATINGS MUST HAVE 
QUALIFIED AS ENGINEERING WATCH SUPERVISOR (EWS) OR PROPULSION PLANT WATCH 
SUPERVISOR (PPWS).  IF A MEMBER'S OFFICIAL RECORD DOES NOT ALREADY INCLUDE 
DOCUMENTATION OF EWS OR PPWS QUALIFICATION, THIS DOCUMENTATION MUST BE 
RECEIVED BY PERS-802 BY 4 JUNE 2012. ACCEPTABLE DOCUMENTATION OF EWS OR PPWS 
QUALIFICATION INCLUDES (1) AN EVALUATION WITH "EWS" OR "PPWS" IN BLOCK 29, OR 
(2) A LETTER FROM THE MEMBER'S COMMAND FORWARDED TO THE BOARD VIA THE MEMBER 
WHICH CERTIFIES EWS OR PPWS QUALIFICATION.
    J.  SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR NAVAL SPECIAL WARFARE (NSW) PERSONNEL.  PER 
REF C, THE NSW ALTERNATIVE FINAL MULTIPLE SCORE (AFMS) WILL BE USED TO 
DETERMINE SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBILITY FOR THE SPECIAL WARFARE OPERATOR (SO) 
AND SPECIAL WARFARE BOAT OPERATOR (SB) RATINGS.  ALL TIR-ELIGIBLE SO1 AND SB1 
MUST REVIEW AND VERIFY THEIR AFMS WORKSHEET IN NSIPS PRIOR TO TAKING THE E7 
EXAM.
SEE REF C FOR FURTHER EXPLANATION.
    K.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS (CITIZENSHIP, SECURITY 
CLEARANCE, MEDICAL, ETC.) FOR CERTAIN RATINGS AS OUTLINED IN REF A, PARA 204, 
PRIOR TO REGULARLY SCHEDULED EXAM DATE.
    L.  SECURITY CLEARANCE:  PER PARA 204 OF REF A, ALL PERSONNEL IN CERTAIN 
RATINGS MUST, AT A MINIMUM, MAINTAIN CONTINUOUS SECURITY CLEARANCE 
ELIGIBILITY.  A FAVORABLE INVESTIGATION ADJUDICATION MUST BE ISSUED FROM 
DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY CENTRAL ADJUDICATION FACILITY (DONCAF) PRIOR TO THE 
REGULARLY SCHEDULED EXAMINATION DATE.  AN INTERIM SECURITY CLEARANCE IS NOT A 
FORMAL DETERMINATION BY AN ADJUDICATIVE AUTHORITY AND THEREFORE DOES NOT 
SATISFY THIS REQUIREMENT.  SAILORS IN THE FOLLOWING RATINGS WHO DO NOT MEET 
THIS REQUIREMENT ARE INELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED 
AN EXAMINATION:  AC, AE, AG, AO, AT, AWF, AWO, AWR, AWS, AWV, AZ, CTI, CTM, 
CTN, CTR, CTT, EM, EOD, ET, FC, FT, GM, HT, IC, IS, IT, ITS, LN, MA, MC, MN, 
MT, ND, OS, QM, SB, SO, STG, STS, AND YN, AS WELL AS ALL NUCLEAR-TRAINED 
PERSONNEL.

4.  EVALUATIONS USED TO COMPUTE PERFORMANCE MARK AVERAGE (PMA) MUST BE 
INCLUSIVE OF PERIOD 1 JANUARY 2009 - 31 DECEMBER 2011.  REFER TO REF D FOR USE 
OF CONCURRENT EVALUATIONS FOR SPECIFIC IA/GSA TOURS FOR PMA COMPUTATION AND 
FOR USE TO ESTABLISH ELIGIBILITY FOR EARLY PROMOTE TIR WAIVERS.  ONLY E6 
EVALUATIONS ARE TO BE USED FOR PMA COMPUTATION.  IF SERVICE MEMBER DOES NOT 
HAVE AN EVALUATION WITHIN THE PERIOD SPECIFIED, PREPARE A SPECIAL EVALUATION 
WITH 31 DECEMBER 2011 IN BLOCK 15.
