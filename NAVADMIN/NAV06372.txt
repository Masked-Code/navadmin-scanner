SUBJ/FEB 07 (CYCLE 080) NAVY-WIDE EXAMINATIONS FOR ADVANCEMENT IN RATE 
OF NAVY RESERVE PERSONNEL TO PAYGRADES E4 THROUGH E7// 
REF/A/RMG/OPNAV/191205ZOCT06// AMPN/NAVADMIN 290/06// GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  
THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THAT THE INACTIVE RESERVE EXAMINATIONS WILL BE 
ADMINISTERED FEBRUARY 2007 FOR E4/E5/E6/E7 CANDIDATES.  ALL CANDIDATES 
SERVING ON ONE YEAR RECALL, ACTIVE DUTY TRAINING, OR ACTIVE DUTY UNDER 
ANY PRESIDENTIAL RECALL AUTHORITY, WILL COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT AS 
SELECTED RESERVE INACTIVE
(USNR-INACTIVE) IN
CYCLE 080.  INDIVIDUAL READY RESERVE (IRR) PERSONNEL WHO ARE MOBILIZED 
ARE CONSIDERED UNDER THE SELECTED RESERVE (SELRES) REQUIREMENTS FOR 
ADVANCEMENT.
THIS NAVADMIN
SUPPLEMENTS BUPERSINST 1430.16E.
2.  TIMELINE OF EVENTS:
    A.  DEC 06.  INTERNET REVIEW AND MODIFICATION OF TIME IN RATE
(TIR) LIST FOR
EXAMINATION ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE.  USE NETPDTC WEB SITE AT 
HTTPS://NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL/NEAS TO REVIEW TIR LIST.  COMMAND 
EDUCATIONAL SERVICES OFFICERS WITH INTERNET ACCESS BUT WITHOUT A 
PASSWORD MAY REGISTER FOR PASSWORD ONLINE AT ABOVE WEBSITE.
    B.  JAN 07.  INTERNET REVIEW AND MODIFICATION OF TIR LIST FOR 
EXAMINATION ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE AS FOLLOWS:  RATING EXAMINATION 
ORDERING IS AVAILABLE NOW THROUGH 28 JAN 07 (14 JAN 07 FOR CRYPTOLOGIC 
TECHNICIAN (NETWORK) SCI EXAMINATIONS).  (NOTE:
CANRECS, RECALLED MOBILIZED SAILORS, AND RESCORE CANDIDATES ALL MUST 
ORDER VIA MESSAGE AS INTERNET ORDERING IS NOT AVAILABLE TO THOSE 
PERSONNEL).
    C.  JAN 07.  COMMANDS REVIEW TIR LIST AND TAKE THE FOLLOWING
ACTIONS:
          (1) ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES NOT LISTED - COMMANDS MUST ADD NAME, 
SSN, AND RATE TO THE TIR LIST.
          (2) CANDIDATES NOT ELIGIBLE OR NO LONGER RECOMMENDED FOR 
ADVANCEMENT - COMMANDS MUST CLICK NO AND SUBMIT BUTTON TO DELETE NAMES 
FROM TIR LIST.
3.  BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
    A.  SELRES CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS 
PER BUPERSINST 1430.16E, OPNAVINST 1160.7B AND THE LATEST DRILLING 
RESERVE ENLISTED CAREER MANAGEMENT OBJECTIVES (ECMO) CONTROL PROGRAM 
NOT LATER THAN 15 JAN 07.
CANDIDATES MUST MAINTAIN
COMMANDING OFFICER'S RECOMMENDATION TO REMAIN ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT.
    B.  THOSE MEMBERS WHO HAVE A PENDING RETIREMENT REQUEST OR ARE 
APPROVED FOR TRANSFER TO THE RETIRED RESERVE (WITH OR WITHOUT PAY) ON A 
DATE PRIOR TO THEIR MANDATED HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT) ARE INELIGIBLE FOR 
ADVANCEMENT.
    C.  MEMBERS WHO HAVE SUBMITTED REQUESTS FOR VOLUNTARY TRANSFER TO 
THE RETIRED RESERVE MAY BECOME ELIGIBLE FOR CHIEF PETTY OFFICER 
ADVANCEMENT BOARD CONSIDERATION PROVIDED CANCELLATION OF RETIREMENT 
AUTHORIZATION IS APPROVED PRIOR TO 1 MAY 07.
COMNAVPERSCOM
(PERS-4812) AND NETPDTC (N321) MUST BE NOTIFIED TO ENSURE THESE MEMBERS 
ARE RE-VALIDATED FOR CONSIDERATION BY THE BOARD.
    D.  LEADERSHIP CONTINUUM.  REFER TO NAVADMIN 339/06 FOR GRACE 
PERIOD AND WAIVER POLICIES FOR ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION PARTICIPATION 
AND SELECTION BOARD CONSIDERATION.
4.  EVALUATIONS USED TO COMPUTE PERFORMANCE MARK AVERAGES MUST BE 
INCLUSIVE OF THE PERIODS LISTED BELOW:
    PROSPECTIVE           EVALUATION    COMPUTATION
    PAYGRADE              PAYGRADE      PERIOD
    E4                    E3            1 JUN 06 TO 31 JAN 07
    E5                    E4            1 FEB 06 TO 31 JAN 07
    E6                    E5            1 FEB 04 TO 31 JAN 07
    E7                    E6            1 FEB 04 TO 31 DEC 06
    E7 (FTS)              E6            1 JAN 04 TO 31 DEC 06
    A.  IF A SERVICE MEMBER DOES NOT HAVE AN EVALUATION WITHIN THE 
PERIOD SPECIFIED, PREPARE A SPECIAL EVALUATION WITH BLOCK 15 REFLECTING 
31 JAN 07 FOR E4, E5, AND E6 CANDIDATES; 31 DEC 06 FOR E7 CANDIDATES.
5.  GENERAL EXAMINATION/ADVERSE INFO:
    A.  EXAMINATIONS WILL BE ADMINISTERED PER CHAPTER 6 OF BUPERSINST 
1430.16E.  DO NOT ADMINISTER ANY OTHER CYCLE 080 EXAMINATIONS PRIOR TO 
1 FEB 07 UNLESS SPECIFICALLY AUTHORIZED BY COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4812).
    B.  EXAMINATIONS FOR RECALLED RESERVISTS WILL BE ORDERED BY THE 
COMMAND HOLDING THE MEMBER'S SERVICE RECORD.  AUTHORITY IS GRANTED TO 
ADMINISTER EXAMINATIONS TO SELRES CANDIDATES ON ACTIVE DUTY WITH 
REGULAR NAVY CANDIDATES 18 JAN 07 FOR E7, 1 MAR 07 FOR E6,
8 MAR 07 FOR E5 AND 15 MAR 07 FOR E4.  THESE CANDIDATES ARE STILL 
REQUIRED TO TAKE CYCLE 080 EXAMINATION AND NOT ACTIVE DUTY EXAMINATION.
    C.  ADVANCEMENT TO E4 AND RATING ENTRY FOR NON-DESIGNATED PERSONNEL 
ARE GOVERNED BY THE LATEST ECMO.  NON-DESIGNATED MEMBERS WHO REQUIRE AN 
"A" SCHOOL WAIVER FOR ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION PARTICIPATION PER THE 
LATEST ECMO, MUST SUBMIT A REQUEST TO
PERS-4812 POSTMARKED BY 10 JAN 07.
    D.  ENSURE BLOCKS 12 AND 13 OF THE ADVANCEMENT IN RATE OR CHANGE OF 
RATING
(WORKSHEET) (NAVEDTRA 1430/2) ARE COMPLETED.  AT THE EDUCATION SERVICES 
OFFICER'S DISCRETION, WORKSHEETS MAY BE SIGNED EITHER PRIOR TO 
EXAMINATION OR AT THE EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION SITE.
    E.  LATE EXAMINATION ORDERS MUST BE HELD TO A MINIMUM TO ALLOW TIME 
FOR ADEQUATE PROCESSING AND QUOTA DETERMINATION.  REQUESTS FOR LATE 
EXAMINATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED USING SAMPLE FORMAT ON TABLE 4-3 OF 
BUPERSINST 1430.16E VIA MESSAGE TO NETPDTC (N321), WITH COMNAVPERSCOM 
MILLINGTON, TN (PERS-4812) AS INFO ADDRESSEE.
REQUESTS MUST INCLUDE
MEMBERS NAME, RATE, SSN AND JUSTIFICATION FOR MISSING REGULAR 
EXAMINATION.  NO ACTION WILL BE TAKEN ON REQUESTS RECEIVED WITHOUT 
REQUIRED INFO/JUSTIFICATION.  ENSURE "ADMIN ERROR" AND "OPERATIONAL 
COMMITMENT" ARE CLARIFIED WHEN USED AS JUSTIFICATION.  REQUESTS 
RECEIVED BY NETPDTC AFTER 2 MAR 07 WILL BE DISAPPROVED.  LATE 
EXAMINATIONS MUST BE ADMINISTERED NOT LATER THAN 12 MAR 07.  WHEN A 
CANDIDATE JUSTIFIABLY MISSES A REGULAR EXAMINATION AND EXAMINATION IS 
ON BOARD, DO NOT DESTROY EXAMINATION.
SUBMIT REQUEST AS
ABOVE FOR PERMISSION TO RETAIN THE EXAMINATION FOR LATE ADMINISTRATION.
    F.  REFER TO NAVADMIN 290/06 REGARDING ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATIONS FOR 
SAILORS ASSIGNED IN IRAQ, AFGHANISTAN, AND THE HORN OF AFRICA.
    G.  FORWARDING EXAMINATION RETURNS.  EXAMINATION GRADING REQUIRES A 
90 PERCENT RECEIPT OF RETURNS TO NETPDTC.  FORWARD EXAMINATION ANSWER 
SHEETS IN THE FOLLOWING
MANNER:
        (1) CONUS INSTALLATIONS.  MAIL EXAMINATION ANSWER SHEETS VIA 
FEDEX UNDER THE CURRENT GSA CONTRACT BY CLOSE OF BUSINESS OF THE DAY 
FOLLOWING EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION (MONDAY AFTER DRILL WEEKEND).
        (2) OVERSEAS SHORE COMMANDS.  SEND ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION 
ANSWER SHEETS VIA UNITED STATES POSTAL SERVICE (USPS) THREE-DAY EXPRESS 
MAIL SERVICE.
MAIL MAY BE
TRACKED AT WWW.USPS.GOV.
        (3) DEPLOYED SHIPS.  SEND ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION ANSWER SHEETS 
IN A REGISTERED MAIL WORKING BAG AND ADDRESSED TO THE POSTAL OFFICER 
FLEET MAIL CENTER THE SERVICING FLEET MAIL CENTER IN THE DEPLOYED AREA 
AND ENDORSED (ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION ANSWER SHEETS ENCLOSED).  IT IS 
IMPORTANT THAT THIS ENDORSEMENT IS PLACED ON THE SECOND LINE OF THE 
ADDRESS.  THE POSTAL OFFICER WILL THEN HAVE PACKAGED SENT VIA USPS 
THREE DAY EXPRESS MAIL TO NETPDTC.
    H.  EXAMINATION RESULTS ARE PROVIDED TO SUPPORTING ESO_S ONLINE.
AUTHORIZED
REPRESENTATIVES ACCESS THE OFFICIAL RESULTS, WITH A PASSWORD, THROUGH 
NETPDTC'S EXAMINATION ORDERING SITE.  CLICK ON EXAMINATION STATUS 
VERIFICATION REPORT (ESVR), RATE CHANGE AUTHORIZATION (RCA), PROFILE 
SHEETS, AND COMMAND STATISTICS.  THE ESVR AND RCA ARE THE OFFICIAL 
SOURCE FOR NAVY ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION RESULTS.
 ESO_S MUST
PROVIDE COPIES TO SUPPORTED ACTIVITIES UPON RECEIPT.  INDIVIDUAL 
SAILORS MAY ALSO VIEW/DOWNLOAD THEIR OWN PROFILE SHEET FIVE DAYS AFTER 
RESULTS ARE RELEASED, FROM HTTPS://WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL 
(SECURE SITE CLICK ON 'MY ADVANCEMENT' LINK)).
    I.  ALL COMMANDS SHOULD MONITOR THE ELECTRONIC TOTE BOARD LOCATED 
AT HTTPS//WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL TO VERIFY THAT ANSWER SHEETS 
ARE RECEIVED BY NETPDTC.
