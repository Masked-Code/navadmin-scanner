SUBJ/FY-07 FULL-TIME SUPPORT AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY// 
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE 
FY-07 FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY
(ACCP) PROGRAM AND PROVIDES ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND APPLICATION 
PROCEDURES.
2.  RETENTION OF EXPERIENCED AVIATION TRAINED OFFICERS REMAINS A TOP 
PRIORITY AND IS NECESSARY TO SUPPORT THE NAVY'S MISSION AND MAINTAIN 
COMBAT READINESS.  AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY (ACCP), FIRST 
IMPLEMENTED IN FY-00, ENCOURAGES HIGH QUALITY AVIATION OFFICERS TO 
CONTINUE THEIR SERVICE IN NAVAL AVIATION.
3.  THE FY-07 ACCP PROGRAM OFFERS SELECTED BONUSES TARGETING ELIGIBLE 
FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) PILOTS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS
(NFO) FOR DEPARTMENT HEAD (DH), OFFICERS IN CHARGE (OIC), AND COMMAND.  
ALL ELIGIBLE AVIATORS ASSIGNED TO DESIGNATED BILLETS ON OR AFTER 1 OCT 
06 MAY APPLY FOR ACCP.
4.  ANNUAL AWARD AMOUNTS ARE $15,000 AT ALL LEVELS REGARDLESS OF 
DESIGNATOR.  CONTRACT LENGTHS WILL BE BASED ON INITIAL ELIGIBILITY DATE, 
1 OCT 06 OR DATE REPORTED ONBOARD IF LATER, AND DETERMINED AS FOLLOWS:
     A.  12 MONTH DH CONTRACT FOR AVIATORS WITH SIX MONTHS TO ONE YEAR 
REMAINING UNTIL PRD.
     B.  24 MONTH DH CONTRACT FOR AVIATORS WITH AT LEAST 13 MONTHS BUT 
LESS THAN TWO YEARS REMAINING UNTIL PRD.
     C.  24 MONTH OIC CONTRACT FOR BOARD SELECTED AVIATORS.
     D.  30 MONTH DH CONTRACT AVIATORS WITH 25 MONTHS OR MORE UNTIL PRD.
     E.  36 MONTH COMMAND/MAJOR COMMAND CONTRACT FOR BOARD SELECTED 
AVIATORS.
5.  ELIGIBILITY.  THE FOLLOWING ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS APPLY:
     A.  AVIATORS MUST HAVE COMPLETED THEIR ACTIVE DUTY SERVICE 
OBLIGATION (ADSO) FOR UNDERGRADUATE FLIGHT TRAINING AND NOT BE 
OBLIGATED UNDER A CURRENT ACCP AGREEMENT.
     B.  AVIATORS MUST HAVE COMPLETED THEIR INITIAL OBLIGATION INCURRED 
WHEN ACCEPTING REDESIGNATION TO FTS.  THIS OBLIGATION IS TWO YEARS FOR 
OFFICERS WHO TRANSFER FROM THE ACTIVE-DUTY LIST AND THREE YEARS FOR 
SELECTED RESERVE OFFICERS RECALLED TO FTS.
     C.  MOBILIZED AND TEMPORARILY RECALLED RESERVE OFFICERS ARE NOT 
ELIGIBLE FOR ACCP.
6.  ALL ACCP REQUESTS MUST HAVE COMMANDING OFFICERS'S ENDORSEMENT.
FOR DETAILS, ELIGIBILITY, ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURES, AND APPLICATION 
FORMAT VISIT THE FTS AVIATION DETAILER WEBSITE AT
