RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THAT THE FY-15 NAVY ACTIVE-DUTY O-6 STAFF 
CORPS CHAPLAIN CORPS (CHC) PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD WILL CONDUCT FURTHER 
PROCEEDINGS NO EARLIER THAN 30 DAYS FROM THE DATE OF THIS MESSAGE.  DUE TO AN 
ADMINISTRATIVE ERROR UNRELATED TO THE CONDUCT OF THE BOARD, THE APPROVED 
VERSION OF THE FY-15 NAVY ACTIVE-DUTY O-6 STAFF CORPS CHC COMMUNITY BRIEF WAS 
NOT PROVIDED TO THE BOARD.  THE APPROVED VERSION WILL BE FURNISHED TO THE 
BOARD UPON REASSEMBLY AND IS AVAILABLE ON THE NPC WEBSITE AT 
HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-NPC/BOARDS/ACTIVEDUTYOFFICER/PAGES/
COMMUNITYBRIEFS.ASPX.

2.  ELIGIBILITY ZONES IN THE ORIGINAL NOTICE OF CONVENING, REF (A), REMAIN 
UNCHANGED.

3.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY COMMUNICATE WITH THE PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD IN 
ACCORDANCE WITH REF (A), CONCERNING ANY INFORMATION, PERFORMANCE, OR OTHER 
MATTERS ARISING BEFORE 4 FEBRUARY 2014.
CORRESPONDENCE CONTAINING OR ADDRESSING ANY INFORMATION, PERFORMANCE, OR 
OTHER MATTERS ARISING ON OR AFTER 4 FEBRUARY 2014 WILL BE REJECTED AND NOT 
PRESENTED FOR CONSIDERATION BY THE REASSEMBLED BOARD.  ALL CORRESPONDENCE 
PREVIOUSLY SUBMITTED WILL BE AVAILABLE TO THE BOARD.  ADDITIONAL 
CORRESPONDENCE MUST ARRIVE NLT THE DAY BEFORE THE BOARD REASSEMBLES TO 
CONDUCT FURTHER PROCEEDINGS.
THE DATE OF THE BOARD'S REASSEMBLY WILL BE PROVIDED TO ELIGIBLE OFFICERS VIA 
SEPARATE CORRESPONDENCE.

4.  THE FY-15 NAVY ACTIVE-DUTY O-6 STAFF CORPS CHC PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD 
WILL REASSEMBLE AS BOARD #15172.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS SHALL USE THIS SELECTION 
BOARD NUMBER FOR ALL ADDITIONAL CORRESPONDENCE TO THE BOARD.

5.  THIS NAVADMIN WILL BE CANCELLED FOR RECORD PURPOSES ON
30 SEPTEMBER 2015.

6.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL W. F. MORAN, N1.//
