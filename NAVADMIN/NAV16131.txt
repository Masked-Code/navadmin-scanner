RMKS/1.  THIS MESSAGE APPLIES TO ALL ACTIVE DUTY AND RESERVE NAVY PERSONNEL, 
DON CIVILLIANS AND APPLICABLE FAMILY MEMBERS. ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL 
REGIONAL COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND 
TENANT COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS FOR IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT.

2.  THE FOLLOWING GEOGRAPHIC AREA OF INTEREST (GAOI) HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED FOR 
THE SIMULATED EARTHQUAKE IN CONJUNCTION WITH AS-16: 
A.  THE STATES OF WASHINGTON AND OREGON.

3.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO:
A.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL AFFECTED NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON 
CIVILIAN PERSONNEL AND APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS IN THE GAOI. ALL MEMBERS IN THE 
AFFECTED GAOI WILL RECEIVE NOTIFICATION FROM NFAAS VIA TEXT/EMAIL TO MUSTER 
THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT SYSTEM 
(NFAAS). COMMANDING OFFICERS (COS) AND/OR COMMANDING OFFICERS REPRESENTATIVES
(CORS) WILL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION OF REQUIREMENT FOR INDIVIDUAL SPONSOR 
MUSTER IN NFAAS.  IN CONJUCTION WITH INDIVIDUAL SPONSOR MUSTER, COS/CORS WILL 
DETERMINE ACCURATE STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF THEIR PERSONNEL AND MUSTER 
(PERSONNEL NOT MUSTERED IN NFAAS 24 HOURS AFTER EARTHQUAKE) AND ACCOUNT FOR 
THEM AT HOME, WORK, TELEWORK, EVACUATED, OR DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE 
GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, 
SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER).
B.  MEMBERS, COS AND/OR CORS UTILIZE THE NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND 
ASSESSMENT SYSTEM (NFAAS) SUPPORT SITE AT
HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL TO ACCOUNT FOR EACH INDIVIDUAL REQUIRED TO BE 
REPORTED PER REFS A AND B.  COMMANDS SHALL DIRECT MEMBERS TO INDIVIDUALLY 
MUSTER IN NFAAS.
C.  IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, COMMANDS ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS OF PERSONNEL 
ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) TO THE AFFECTED REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) OR DIRECT 
CHAIN OF COMMAND.
D.  DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS COMPLETE.
REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED INCLUDING THE POSTING 
OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES AREA ON THE INDIVIDUALS ACCOUNTING LINE.

4.  ALL INDIVIDUALS (ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON CIVILLIAN PERSONNEL)
ARE EXPECTED TO:
A.  MUSTER IN IN NFAAS, HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL
B.  IF UNABLE TO ACCESS NFAAS ACTIVE, RESERVE AND DON CIVILLIAN PERSONNEL 
SHOULD CONTACT THEIR COMMAND MUSTERING OFFICER OR SUPERVISOR TO CHECK IN 
(MUSTER).
C. INDIVIDUALS WILL PROVIDE COMMAND REPRESENTATIVES WITH CURRENT RESIDENCE, 
DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, 
TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/ DESERTER, OTHER).
D. FAMILY MEMBERS CAN LOG ON TO NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL AND 
CHECK IN (MUSTER) ON THE NAVY FAMILY MEMBERS SITE. IF UNABLE TO LOG ON TO 
NFAAS, CALL THE NFAAS HELP DESK AT 866-946-9183.

5.  THERE WILL NOT BE A NEEDS ASSESSMENT CONDUCTED FOR THIS EXERCISE.
