FOR CYCLE 203// 
RMKS/1.  THE FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) E6 ADVANCEMENT LIST PUBLISHED ON

WEBSITES.

E6 ADVANCEMENT NOTIFICATIONS POSTED TO BOL, NEAS AND THE ONLINE VERSION 
OF NAVY TIMES PRIOR TO 26 MAY 2009.  THE CORRECTED RESULTS ARE LISTED 
BELOW AND WILL BE POSTED ON BOL AND NEAS WITH A BANNER THAT CLEARLY 
IDENTIFIES THEM AS CORRECTED RESULTS.
