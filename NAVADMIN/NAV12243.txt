RMKS/1.  EXERCISE-EXERCISE-EXERCISE - THIS MESSAGE APPLIES TO ALL NAVY 
PERSONNEL, UNIFORMED AND CIVILIAN.  ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL REGIONAL 
COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND TENANT 
COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS FOR IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT.

2.  THE FOLLOWING GEOGRAPHIC AREAS OF INTEREST (GAOI) HAVE BEEN ESTABLISHED 
FOR THE CITADEL RUMBLE 12 EARTHQUAKES.
    A.  CALIFORNIA GAOI.
        (1) THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES IN THE STATE OF CA:  IMPERIAL, KERN, LOS 
ANGELES, ORANGE, RIVERSIDE, SAN BERNARDINO, SAN DIEGO, AND VENTURA.

3.  PERSONNEL ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) REPORTING FOR THIS ORDER TO ACCOUNT WILL 
BEGIN AT 1600Z, 08 AUG 2012 AND WILL CONTINUE UNTIL THE PERSONNEL 
ACCOUNTABILITY OBJECTIVE HAS BEEN MET.  COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO EXECUTE PA TO 
100 PERCENT COMPLIANCE WITHIN 72 HR TIMELINE UPON PA COMMENCEMENT.  SHOULD A 
REAL WORLD CATASTROPHIC EVENT DEVELOP DURING THE EXERCISE, CITADEL RUMBLE 12 
PA WILL BE SUSPENDED TO AVOID CONFUSION.

4.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO:
    A.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL AFFECTED PERSONNEL, INCLUDING APPLICABLE 
FAMILY MEMBERS.  COMMANDING OFFICERS (COS) AND/OR COMMANDING OFFICERS 
REPRESENTATIVES (CORS) ARE TO DETERMINE ACCURATE STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF 
THEIR PERSONNEL AND MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR THEM AT CURRENT RESIDENCE, 
DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, 
TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER).  IN 
DOING SO, AN ACCURATE ACCOUNTING OF ALL PERSONNEL IN ALL ACCOUNTING CATEGORIES 
CAN BE QUICKLY DETERMINED.
    B.  COS AND/OR CORS UTILIZE THE NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT 
SYSTEM (NFAAS) SUPPORT SITE AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL TO ACCOUNT FOR EACH 
INDIVIDUAL REQUIRED TO BE REPORTED PER REFS A AND B
    C.  INDIVIDUALS OR FAMILY MEMBERS NOT ABLE TO CONTACT THEIR COMMAND, THEY 
CAN LOG ON TO NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL AND CHECK IN (MUSTER) ON 
THE NAVY FAMILY MEMBERS SITE. IF UNABLE TO LOG ON TO NFAAS, CALL THE NFAAS 
HELP DESK AT 866-946-9183.
    D.  IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, COMMANDS ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS OF PA TO 
THE AFFECTED REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) OR DIRECT CHAIN OF COMMAND.
    E.  DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS COMPLETE.  
REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED INCLUDING THE POSTING 
OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES AREA ON THE INDIVIDUAL'S ACCOUNTING LINE.

5.  COMMANDS EXECUTING EVACUATION PROCEDURES ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR ISSUING TDY 
EVACUATION ORDERS FOR THEIR PERSONNEL, MILITARY AND CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES, AND 
FAMILY MEMBERS USING THE DOD FORM 1610.
MILITARY MEMBERS DEPARTING THE EVACUATION AREA ARE ON TDY STATUS AND WILL 
RECEIVE NORMAL TDY ALLOWANCES.  COMMAND'S DAY-TO-DAY OPERATIONS FUNDING SOURCE 
IS TO BE USED PER REF D.  FOR CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES AND FAMILY MEMBERS OF 
MILITARY AND CIVILIAN PERSONNEL, THE FUNDING ACCOUNTING DATA IS OUTLINED IN 
REFS E AND F.

6.  NAVY FAMILIES WHO ARE SEVERELY IMPACTED BY THE EARTHQUAKES ARE STRONGLY 
ENCOURAGED TO LOG INTO NFAAS, HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL, TO UPDATE CONTACT 
INFORMATION AND COMPLETE A NEEDS ASSESSMENT FORM AT THEIR EARLIEST 
CONVENIENCE. FOR FURTHER ASSISTANCE AFTER A NEEDS ASSESSMENT FORM HAS BEEN 
SUBMITTED, CALL NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND EMERGENCY COORDINATION CENTER (NPC ECC) 
AT 877-414-5358 OR E-MAIL NPC.CAT.CAPTAIN(AT)NAVY.MIL.
