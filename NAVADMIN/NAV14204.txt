RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the schedule for advancement examination 
readiness reviews (AERR) from October 2014 to March 2015.

2.  Per reference (a), this NAVADMIN supersedes reference (b) and should be 
read in its entirety.  AERR events provide an opportunity for selected rating 
subject matter experts (SME) from fleet and shore-based commands to impact 
sailorization by affecting the careers of every test-taker in the Navy.  
Selected chief, senior chief, and master chief petty officers on active-duty, 
full-time support (FTS), and reservists currently on active-duty for special 
work (ADSW) may take part in the process that enables the Navy to advance the 
most qualified and rating-knowledgeable Sailors.

3.  The readiness reviews are held at Naval Education and Training 
Professional Development and Technology Center (NETPDTC) onboard Saufley 
Field, Pensacola, FL.  Temporary Additional Duty (TAD) travel in support of 
AERR events is considered mission critical and is flag approved.  All costs 
pertaining to TAD are funded by NETPDTC.

4.  The following ratings are scheduled for AERR events between October 2014 
and March 2015:  ABE, ABH, AE, AO, ATI, AWF, AWO, AWR, BU, CTT, DC, EM 
(Conventional), EO, EOD, FT, GSE, GSM, IT, LS, MM, MMW, OS, PR, SB, SW, STS, 
UT.

5.  To take part in the AERR process, visit https://wwwa.nko.navy.
mil/group/navy-advancement-center/advancement-examination-readiness-
review-aerr-.  This link provides the scheduled dates for the ratings listed 
above, eligibility and nomination package requirements, and other pertinent 
information regarding AERR events.

6.  Point of contact is Mr. Peter Pappas, NETPDTC N33A, at
(850) 473-6121/DSN 753, fax (850) 473-6124 or via e-mail at 
peter.pappas(at)navy.mil.

7.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or canceled, 
whichever occurs first.
