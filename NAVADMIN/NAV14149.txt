RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the 2014 National Naval Officers Association 
(NNOA) 42nd Annual Professional Development and Training Conference which 
will be held 9-11 July 2014 at Marine Corps Base, Quantico, Virginia.

2.  NNOA is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing professional 
development through networking, education, and mentorship of minority 
officers from all three maritime armed forces * the United States Navy, 
Marine Corps, and Coast Guard.  The conference provides educational and 
professional development workshops, seminars, and exhibits designed to 
enhance the professional knowledge of attendees while increasing overall 
awareness of issues affecting the sea services.

3.  Navy-funded participation for this conference has been approved within a 
limited scope.  Navy will sponsor 80 service members by funding their 
conference registration fees.  All Navy-funded registrations will be 
coordinated through the Navy Office of Diversity and Inclusion and will 
require participation in brief pre- and post-event surveys.

4.  Commands shall not fund registrations.  Commands are not authorized to 
expend travel funds, including lodging, transportation, meals and incidental 
expenses.  The filing of temporary additional duty or local travel claims for 
this conference is not authorized. All other participation is at the service 
member*s expense.

5.  Points of contact to obtain conference registration reservations are:
    a.  LCDR Shaletha Moran at (703) 604-5081/DSN 664 or via e-mail
at shaletha.moran(at)navy.mil.
    b.  LT Kalen Kropa at (703) 604-5075/DSN 664 or via e-mail at
kalen.kropa(at)navy.mil.

6.  More information can be found on the NNOA website at www.nnoa.org.

7.  This message is canceled for record purposes on 30 September 2014.

8.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
