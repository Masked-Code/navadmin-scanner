RMKS/1. REF A ESTABLISHES THE CHIEF OF CHAPLAINS AS PROGRAM SPONSOR FOR 
THE PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF CHAPLAIN CORPS OFFICERS AND RELIGIOUS 
PROGRAM SPECIALISTS (RP).  THE CHIEF OF CHAPLAINS CONDUCTS ANNUAL 
SHORT-COURSE TRAINING FOR CHAPLAIN CORPS OFFICERS AND RP'S TO UPDATE 
ESSENTIAL TECHNIQUES AND PRACTICES IN MILITARY MINISTRY.  THE TRAINING 
ALSO ENABLES CHAPLAINS TO OBTAIN CONTINUING EDUCATION UNITS REQUIRED TO 
MAINTAIN FAITH-GROUP CREDENTIALS.
2. FY07 PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT TRAINING CONFERENCE THEME, MINISTRY 
TOOLS FOR TIMES OF CHANGE (CDP-858U, CIN-P-5G-1303), WILL PROVIDE 
CHAPLAINS AND RPS ADDITIONAL KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS FOR MINISTRY SUPPORT 
TO SEA SERVICES AND JOINT ARENA PERSONNEL.  SUBJECT MATTER EXPERTS FROM 
BOTH MILITARY AND ACADEMIA PRODUCED THE TRAINING PACKAGE.
3. MULTIPLE TRAINING LOCATIONS HAVE BEEN ESTABLISHED TO CONSERVE TRAVEL 
FUNDS AND MINIMIZE TIME AWAY FROM HOMEPORT.  DATES AND INFORMATION FOR 
TRAINING, ALONG WITH COURSE DETAILS, CAN BE FOUND AT 
HTTPS://WWW.NKO.NAVY.MIL/PORTAL/CHAPLAINCORPS (ALL LOWER CASE).
CHAPLAINS AND RP'S SHOULD REGISTER FOR ONE OF THE FOLLOWING TRAINING
