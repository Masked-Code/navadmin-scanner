AWARD.  CATEGORIES INCLUDE:

A.  MILITARY LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR.

B.  CIVILIAN LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR.

C.  LOGISTICS TEAM (S) OF THE YEAR.

2.  THE INTENT OF THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR AWARD IS TO RECOGNIZE

NAVY MILITARY AND CIVILIAN INDIVIDUALS AND TEAMS WHOSE

CONTRIBUTIONS AND SUGGESTIONS DURING CALENDAR YEAR 2005, HAVE:

A.  MEASURABLY SUPPORTED THE LOGISTICS MISSION OF THE NAVY.

B.  PROMOTED INNOVATIVE IDEAS AND PRACTICABLE BENEFITS TO THE NAVY.

C.  ENHANCED THE LOGISTICS PROFESSION.

3.  NOMINEES MAY BE INVOLVED IN ANY AND ALL PHASES OF NAVY

OPERATIONAL

LOGISTICS AND JOINT SERVICES LOGISTICS, FROM EARLY LIFE-CYCLE

PLANNING TO IN-SERVICE LOGISTICS SUPPORT.

4.  INDIVIDUALS AND TEAMS(S) SELECTED TO RECEIVE THE ADMIRAL STAN

ARTHUR AWARDS FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2005 WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA NAVAL

MESSAGE ON OR ABOUT THE LAST WEEK OF MAY.  AWARDS WILL BE PRESENTED

IN A CEREMONY AT THE PENTAGON, ARLINGTON VA ON 27 JUNE 2006.

5.  AWARD SELECTEES RECEIVE THE FOLLOWING:

A.  PLAQUE FOR INDIVIDUAL CATEGORY AND EACH TEAM WINNER.

B.  $5,000 EACH FOR THE MILITARY AND CIVILIAN WINNERS.

C.  $10,000 FOR EACH LOGISTICS TEAM TO SHARE.

THE CASH COMPONENT OF EACH AWARD WILL BE PAID THROUGH DIRECT

DEPOSIT.

6.  NOMINATIONS MAY ONLY BE SUBMITTED BY AND VIA ECHELON TWO

COMMANDS TO DCNO (MR&L) CODE N4O.  ECHELON TWO COMMANDS MAY SUBMIT

A TOTAL OF THREE NOMINATIONS, NO MORE THAN ONE PER AWARD CATEGORY.

EMPHASIS ON SUPPORT OF THE NAVY THROUGH JOINT OPERATIONAL LOGISTICS

PROGRAMS IS HIGHLY ENCOURAGED IN THE ECHELON TWO REVIEW/NOMINATION

PROCESS.  AWARD NOMINATIONS MUST BE RECEIVED BY NO LATER THAN

14 APR 2006.
