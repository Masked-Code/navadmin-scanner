RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the Commander, Naval Air 
Systems Command Acquisition Corps assignment slating panel on
5 November 2014, or as soon as practicable thereafter, to select individuals 
for major program positions (O-6, GS-15) and major acquisition shore commands 
(O-5).

2.  Eligible military officers and civilians who desire to be considered must 
submit a signed application.  Application deadline is 1 October 2014.  
Application forms and additional slating panel information is available at: 
http://www.navair.navy.mil (select acquisition selection boards on the 
careers menu tab).

3.  The preferred method to submit an application is via encrypted e-mail.  
Applications will also be accepted via mail and fax.
    a.  Applications should be submitted via encrypted e-mail to the e-mail 
address provided in paragraph 4.
    b.  Applications submitted via mail should use the following
address:
        Commander, Naval Air Systems Command (Air-7.3.6)
        Acquisition Corps Assignment Slating Panel
        22095 Fortin Circle
        Bldg 1489 Room 206
        Patuxent River, MD 20670-1549
    c.  Applications submitted via fax should be sent to 301-757-1526/DSN 
757.

4.  The point of contact is LCDR Mark Angelo, AEDO Community Manager, at 
(301) 757-8480/DSN 757 or via e-mail at mark.angelo(at)navy.mil.

5.  This NAVADMIN will remain in effect until superseded or
30 November 2014, whichever occurs first.
