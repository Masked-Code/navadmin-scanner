SUBJ/COMBATING TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS TRAINING POLICY UPDATE// 
REF/A/DOC/DEPSECDEF MEMO/6MAR08/NOTAL// 
REF/B/MSG/CNO WASHINGTON DC/N1/301512ZJAN09// 
NARR/REF A IS DEPSECDEF MEMO, DOD POLICY ESTABLISHING CTIP AWARENESS 
TRAINING REQUIREMENTS FOR ALL NAVY PERSONNEL.  REF B IS NAVADMIN 033/09,
ANNOUNCING THE FY-09 GENERAL MILITARY TRAINING (GMT) REQUIREMENTS.// 
RMKS/1.  REF A REAFFIRMS THE DOD POSITION REGARDING THE CRIMINAL NATURE 
OF COMBATING TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS (CTIP) AND OUTLINES ACTIONS AND
RESPONSIBILITIES NECESSARY TO PREVENT, MITIGATE, OR AVOID EITHER 
PURPOSEFULLY OR UNWITTINGLY PARTICIPATING IN THOSE ACTIVITIES, 
INCLUDING SEXUAL EXPLOITATION, FORCED PROSTITUTION, INVOLUNTARY 
SERVITUDE, AND DEBT BONDAGE. 
2.  THIS NAVADMIN REAFFIRMS CTIP TRAINING REQUIREMENTS ESTABLISHED IN 
REF B. ALL NAVY PERSONNEL SHALL COMPLETE CTIP AWARENESS TRAINING BY 30 
SEPTEMBER 2009. FURTHERMORE, DOD HAS DIRECTED MANDATORY ANNUAL 
COMBATING TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS AWARENESS TRAINING FOR ALL DOD 
PERSONNEL REGARDLESS OF DEPLOYING STATUS TO INCLUDE MILITARY, CIVILIAN, 
AND CONTRACT PERSONNEL.  NAVY KNOWLEDGE ONLINE (NKO) IS THE PRIMARY 
METHOD TO COMPLETE CTIP TRAINING.  THIS COURSE IS LOCATED AT NAVY E-
LEARNING ONLINE COURSES UNDER THE LEARNING TITLE AT THE MAIN MENU.  
COMPLETION ON NKO WILL ENSURE AUTOMATIC DOCUMENTATION IN THE 
INDIVIDUAL'S ELECTRONIC TRAINING JACKET.  
3.  A CD-ROM WILL BE SENT TO ALL COMMANDS ON THE GENERAL MILITARY 
TRAINING DISTRIBUTION LIST.  THIS CD-ROM INCLUDES BOTH THE INITIAL CTIP 
AWARENESS TRAINING MODULE AND REFRESHER CTIP AWARENESS TRAINING MODULE 
AND CAN BE USED AS AN ALTERNATE METHOD TO NKO FOR COMPLETING THE 
TRAINING.  COMMANDERS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR DOCUMENTING COMPLETION FOR 
PERSONNEL WHO CONDUCT THE CTIP AWARENESS TRAINING VIA THIS METHOD.  
APPROPRIATE ENTRIES SHALL BE MANUALLY ENTERED INTO THE ELECTRONIC 
TRAINING JACKETS VIA THE FLEET TRAINING MANAGEMENT AND PLANNING SYSTEM 
(FLTMPS).
4.  COMMANDS THAT DO NOT HAVE THE CD-ROM MAY REQUEST ONE FROM THE 
DEFENSE IMAGERY MAINTENANCE OPERATION CENTER (DIMOC) BY SEARCHING FOR 
"COMBATING TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS AWARENESS TRAINING" OR USING IMI 
NUMBER "112005" UNDER LEARNING RESOURCES AT 
HTTP://WWW.DEFENSEIMAGERY.MIL.  ONCE THE SEARCH IS COMPLETE, SELECT THE 
IMI TAB TO DISPLAY RESULTS OF THE SEARCH.  IF THE STATUS OF THE SEARCH 
DISPLAYS THE CORRECT TITLE, ADD TO THE SHOPPING CART AND PROCEED TO THE 
"CHECK OUT."  FOR HELP WITH THE WEBSITE PLEASE CONTACT DIMOC CUSTOMER 
SERVICE AT 1-888-743-4662.  COMMANDS WITHOUT WEB ACCESS MAY ORDER BY 
REGULAR MAIL.  INCLUDE PRODUCT TITLE "COMBATING TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS 
AWARENESS TRAINING" AND IMI NUMBER 112005 ALONG WITH A COMMAND RETURN 
ADDRESS TO:

5.  ILLEGAL TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS IS A SIGNIFICANT CRIMINAL ACTIVITY
