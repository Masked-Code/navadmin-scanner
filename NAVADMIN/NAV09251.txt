SUBJ/LAUNCH OF NEW NAVY INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTEE (IA) WEBSITE// 
RMKS/1. NAVY INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTEES AND THEIR FAMILIES DESERVE THE BEST 
TOOLS WE CAN MUSTER. OUR IA SURVEYS INDICATE THAT CLEAR COMMUNICATION 
IS WHAT OUR IA SAILORS AND FAMILIES WANT MOST. IN ORDER TO IMPROVE THAT 
COMMUNICATION, U.S. FLEET FORCES (USFF), AS THE NAVY'S EXECUTIVE AGENT 
FOR INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTEES, LAUNCHED A NEWLY DESIGNED NAVY IA WEBSITE 
WWW.IA.NAVY.MIL. THIS WEBSITE ALIGNS SUPPORT FOR IA SAILORS AND THEIR 
FAMILIES, PROVIDES CLEAR GUIDANCE ON POLICY AND EXECUTION, AND 
INCORPORATES EVOLVING TECHNOLOGY, NETWORKING MEDIA, AND SOFTWARE 
APPLICATIONS USED BY TODAY'S SAILORS.
2. ENCOURAGEMENT AND SUPPORT FOR THE IA PROGRAM IS CRUCIAL TO ITS 
SUCCESSFUL EXECUTION AND WWW.IA.NAVY.MIL PROVIDES SAILORS AND THEIR 
FAMILIES A TREMENDOUS NEW TOOL. PASS THE WORD ABOUT THE NEW WEBSITE TO 
YOUR IA SAILORS AND THEIR FAMILIES, COMMAND IA COORDINATORS, AND 
COMMAND OMBUDSMEN. SHOULD YOU DESIRE ADDITIONAL INFORMATION, USFF 
POINTS OF CONTACT FOR THE NAVY IA WEBSITE ARE MS. KATE PERLEWITZ, 757-
836-0493, EMAIL KATE.PERLEWITZ(AT)NAVY.MIL AND CAPT DOUG KIBBEY, 757-
836-2384, EMAIL DOUG.KIBBEY(AT)NAVY.MIL.
