RMKS/1.  IAW Ref A, each year the Navy recognizes shore, sea, and overseas 
commands for outstanding community service efforts with their local 
communities by presenting awards in six categories:  USS Bainbridge (CGN 25) 
Award for Overall Excellence; Personal Excellence Partnership Flagship; 
Health, Safety, and Fitness Flagship; Campaign Drug Free Flagship; Project 
Good Neighbor Flagship; and Environmental Stewardship Flagship.

2.  As sponsor of the Environmental Stewardship Flagship, Deputy Chief of 
Naval Operations (Fleet Readiness and Logistics) (N4) salutes commands 
engaged in exemplary voluntary community service activities that promote good 
stewardship of environmental resources.

3.  For the 2016 Environmental Stewardship Flagship awards competition, 
nominations were received in the Small, Medium, and, Large Shore categories; 
Medium and Large Sea categories; and Small Overseas category.

4.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the winners and honorable 
mentions of the 2016 Navy Community Service Environmental Stewardship 
Flagship awards competition:
A. 	Small Shore
   Winner:  Naval Aviation Schools Command, Pensacola, FL
Honorable Mention:  Center for Naval Aviation Technical Training
Unit, Whidbey Island, WA
B. 	Medium Shore
   Winner:  Pacific Missile Range Facility, HI
Honorable Mention:  Afloat Training Group San Diego, CA
C. 	Large Shore
    Winner:  Naval Air Station Whidbey Island, WA
    Honorable Mention:  Naval Air Technical Training Center,
    Pensacola, FL
D. 	Medium Sea
    Winner:  Helicopter Sea Combat Squadron 23, San Diego, CA
E. 	Large Sea
    Winner:  USS Frank Cable (AS 40)
F. 	Small Overseas
    Winner:  Navy Munitions Command East Asia Division, Guam

5.  Winners will receive plaques and honorable mentions will receive 
certificates.  OPNAV N45 staff will contact commands to arrange delivery of 
the awards.

6.  This years award winners demonstrated strong commitment to their 
environmental programs and communities, and I extend my congratulations on 
all of your impressive accomplishments. Representing the Navy as dedicated 
stewards of the environment is crucial as we aspire to be good neighbors, 
afloat and ashore. Thank you for your continued efforts in supporting the 
Navy’s community service program.

7.  Released by VADM P. H. Cullom, N4.//
