2.  MANY SUPERB APPLICATION PACKAGES WENT BEFORE THE BOARD, AND 
COMPETITION WAS EXTRAORDINARILY KEEN.  THOSE SELECTED HAD THE FOLLOWING 
COMMON CHARACTERISTICS:
    A.  SUSTAINED SUPERIOR PERFORMANCE IN ALL ASSIGNMENTS WITH 
CONSISTENT TOP RANKINGS AND BREAK-OUTS IN EVALUATION REPORTS.
    B.  STEADY UPWARD PROFESSIONAL GROWTH.  SUCCESSFUL APPLICANTS TOOK 
ON DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES THAT GOT THEM OUT OF THEIR "COMFORT 
ZONES."  THEY EARNED AS MANY QUALIFICATIONS AS POSSIBLE AND COMPLETED 
CORRESPONDENCE COURSES IN OTHER RATINGS.  THEY ALSO ACHIEVED CRITICAL 
QUALIFICATIONS THAT PERTAIN TO THE DESIGNATOR APPLYING FOR:
        - WARFARE QUALIFICATION(S).
        - DCTT, ETT, REPAIR LOCKER LEADER, EOOW, PPOW, EWS, CSOOW, CSC, 
DOOW, COW, SAFE FOR FLIGHT, QUALITY ASSURANCE, MAINTENANCE 
CONTROL/PRODUCTION CONTROL, ETC.
        - JOOD/OOD UNDERWAY, OOD INPORT.
        - MASTER TRAINING SPECIALIST.
        - RECRUITER IN CHARGE (RINC).
        - SUCCESSFUL TOURS IN CHALLENGING LEADERSHIP AND TECHNICAL 
PROFICIENCY POSITIONS SUCH AS RECRUITERS, RECRUIT DIVISION COMMANDERS, 
AND INSTRUCTORS.
        - GOOD PATTERN OF SEA, SHORE, AND OVERSEAS DUTY.
INCREASINGLY CHALLENGING DIVERSE ASSIGNMENTS WERE KEY INCLUDING 
INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTATION (IA) ASSIGNMENTS IN SUPPORT OF GWOT.
        - CONTINUED EDUCATION AND PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT.

3.  TO ENSURE TOP PERFORMERS STANDOUT, REPORTING SENIORS AND ALL THOSE 
INVOLVED IN DRAFTING PERFORMANCE EVALUATIONS MUST KEEP IN MIND
THAT:
        - HARD AND SOFT BREAKOUTS ARE IMPORTANT.  BE SURE THE NARRATIVE 
CLEARLY IDENTIFIES TOP PERFORMERS, ESPECIALLY WHEN A NUMBER OF SAILORS 
ARE RANKED IN THE SAME CATEGORY, E.G.  EP .
        - ENSURE LEADERSHIP POSITIONS, DUTIES, QUALIFICATION OBTAINED, 
AND ACCOMPLISHMENTS ARE CLEARLY DESCRIBED.
        - TIMELY SUBMISSION OF EVALUATION AND FITNESS REPORTS ARE 
CRITICAL TO ENSURE APPLICANTS' RECORDS ARE COMPLETE AT SELECTION BOARDS.

4.  COMMAND ENDORSEMENTS OF LDO/CWO APPLICATION PACKAGES MUST CLEARLY 
STATE THE ENDORSER S RECOMMENDATION WITH REGARD TO SELECTION.  IF A 
NUMBER OF SAILORS ARE APPLYING FROM THE SAME COMMAND, RANK-ORDER THE 
TOP CANDIDATES TO HELP THE BOARD PICK THE VERY BEST AND MOST FULLY 
QUALIFIED APPLICANTS.
