SUBJ/NOTICE OF CONVENING OF FISCAL YEAR 2013 SUPPLY CORPS COMMANDER SELECTIVE

NARR/REF A IS NAVADMIN 048/12 - FY13 ACTIVE COMPONENT RESTRICTED LINE AND 
STAFF CORPS CAPTAIN AND COMMANDER SELECTIVE EARLY RETIRMENT BOARDS.  REF B IS 
TITLE 10, UNITED STATES CODE SECTION, SELECTIVE EARLY RETIREMENT.//

RMKS/1.  IAW REF A, THE SECRETARY OF THE NAVY APPROVED THE CONVENING OF FY-13 
SER BOARDS FOR SPECIAL DUTY OFFICER (OCEANOGRAPHY) CAPTAINS AND COMMANDERS AND 
SUPPLY CORPS COMMANDERS ON THE  ACTIVE-DUTY LIST (ADL).

2.  DUE TO THE SUFFICIENT VOLUNTARY RETIREMENT REQUESTS RECEIVED, THE 
SECRETARY OF THE NAVY HAS APPROVED CANCELLATION OF THE SPECIAL DUTY OFFICER 
(OCEANOGRAPHY) CAPTAIN AND COMMANDER SER BOARDS.

3.  THE SUPPLY CORPS SER BOARD FOR COMMANDERS WILL CONVENE ON
11 SEPTEMBER 2012.

4.  PER REF B, THE FOLLOWING SUPPLY CORPS COMMANDERS (O-5) WILL BE CONSIDERED 
FOR SER:  OFFICERS WHO HAVE FAILED OF SELECTION FOR PROMOTION TO THE GRADE OF 
CAPTAIN TWO OR MORE TIMES AND ARE WITHIN THE ZONE DEFINED IN PARA 6.

5.  THE FOLLOWING SUPPLY CORPS OFFICERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR CONSIDERATION BY 
THE SER BOARD:
    A.  OFFICERS WITH APPROVED VOLUNTARY RETIREMENT REQUESTS;
    B.  OFFICERS WITH STATUTORY RETIREMENTS IN FY-12 OR FY-13; AND
    C.  OFFICERS WHOSE NAMES ARE ON A LIST OF OFFICERS RECOMMENDED FOR 
PROMOTION.

6.  THE FOLLOWING LIST INDICATES THE NAME, ACTIVE-DUTY LIST (LINEAL) NUMBER, 
AND DATE OF RANK OF THE SENIOR AND JUNIOR OFFICERS WHO ARE ELIGIBLE FOR EARLY 
RETIREMENT.
       COMMANDER
       COMPETITIVE CATEGORY SUPPLY CORPS (31XX)
       SENIOR ELIGIBLE               CDR R. T. GERSTNER
       LINEAL/DOR                    020105-25/01 SEP 00
       JUNIOR ELIGIBLE               CDR W. W. SCOTT, JR.
       LINEAL/DOR                    021067-25/01 MAY 06

7.  OFFICERS IN THE ZONE DEFINED ABOVE WHO ARE ON ACTIVE DUTY WHEN THE BOARD 
CONVENES BUT DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS SPECIFIED IN PARA 4, OR ARE 
NOT ELIGIBLE PER PARA 5, WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED FOR EARLY RETIREMENT BY THE 
SELECTIVE EARLY RETIREMENT BOARD.

8.  PER REF B, THE NUMBER OF SUPPLY CORPS COMMANDERS SELECTED FOR RETIREMENT 
MAY NOT EXCEED 30 PERCENT OF THOSE ELIGIBLE OFFICERS CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.

9.  ONLY ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY COMMUNICATE WITH THIS SELECTION BOARD.  
CORRESPONDENCE MUST ARRIVE NOT LATER THAN 2359, 10 SEPTEMBER 2012, AND SHALL 
BE ADDRESSED TO:
    NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (NPC) CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER
    PRESIDENT ACTIVE DUTY FY-13 SUPPLY CORPS COMMANDER SELECTIVE
    EARLY RETIREMENT BOARD #311.
    5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE, MILLINGTON, TN 38055 

OR VIA E-MAIL AT CSCSELECTIONBOARD(AT)NAVY.MIL.  TO CHECK RECEIPT OF YOUR 
PACKAGE, GO TO WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/ACTIVEDUTYOFFICER OR CONTACT THE 
CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER AT (866) 827-5672.  COMMUNICATION MUST BE IN 
ACCORDANCE WITH MILPERSMAN 1420-010 AND MAY INCLUDE, AS ENCLOSURES, 
CORRESPONDENCE NOT ORIGINATED BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER, INCLUDING ENDORSEMENTS 
TO THE OFFICER'S LETTER.  LETTERS WRITTEN ON BEHALF OF THAT OFFICER MUST 
CONTAIN A WRITTEN ACKNOWLEDGEMENT BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER THAT HE OR SHE 
DESIRES SUCH CORRESPONDENCE BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD.  CORRESPONDENCE WITHOUT 
SUCH ACKNOWLEDGEMENT IS CONSIDERED THIRD PARTY CORRESPONDENCE AND WILL NOT BE 
PRESENTED TO THE BOARD.  CLASSIFIED CORRESPONDENCE WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED.

10.  OFFICERS SELECTED FOR EARLY RETIREMENT WILL BE FORMALLY NOTIFIED AS SOON 
AS POSSIBLE AFTER THE BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED BY SECNAV.  OFFICERS 
SELECTED FOR EARLY RETIREMENT ARE REQUIRED BY LAW TO BE RETIRED, IF RETIREMENT 
ELIGIBLE, NO LATER THAN THE FIRST DAY OF THE SEVENTH MONTH AFTER THE MONTH IN 
WHICH THE BOARD REPORT IS APPROVED BY SECNAV.  FOR THE FY-13 SER BOARD, THIS 
MANDATORY RETIREMENT DATE IS ANTICIPATED TO BE 1 APRIL 2013.

11.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
