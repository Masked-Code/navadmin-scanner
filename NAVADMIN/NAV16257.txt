SUBJ/2016 NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM/PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR COMMUNITY 
SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS//

RMKS/1.  Each year the Navy recognizes shore, sea and overseas commands for 
Project Good Neighbor.

2.  The Project Good Neighbor Award recognizes the best year-round volunteer 
supported program or special project that promotes outreach activities 
throughout the year to establish and restore hope to the community.

3.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2016 Navy Project Good 
Neighbor Community Service Flag Award winners:
    a.  Shore commands category:
        (1) Small - Naval Aviation Technical Training Unit, Whidbey Island
        (2) Medium - Center for Naval Aviation Technical Training Unit, North 
Island
        (3) Large - Naval Air Technical Training Center, Pensacola
    b.  Sea commands category:
        (1) Small - Littoral Combat Ship Crew 201
        (2) Medium - Helicopter Sea Combat Squadron 23
        (3) Large - Naval Mobile Construction Battalion Three
    c.  Overseas command category:
        (1) Small - Commander, Fleet Activities Chinhae
        (2) Large - Naval Computer and Telecommunications Station Far East

4.  My sincere thanks to all for your continued support of the Navy's 
Community Service Program.  Please accept my personal Bravo Zulu and thanks 
to the caring and dedicated Sailors, civilians, and families who selflessly 
volunteered and contributed in improving the quality of life within your 
communities.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral J. G. Foggo, Director, Navy Staff.//
