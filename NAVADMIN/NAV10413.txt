RMKS/1. THIS IS A COORDINATED OPNAV N2/N6, NETC, NPC, AND CYBERFOR MESSAGE.

2. REF A ANNOUNCED NEW IT NEC 2790. NEC WILL BE AWARDED TO NEW INFORMATION 
SYSTEMS TECHNICIAN OF THE FUTURE (ITOF) 'A' SCHOOL GRADUATES AND INCLUDES ALL 
CERTIFICATIONS REQUIRED BY REF B FOR INFORMATION ASSURANCE TECHNICIAN (IAT) 
LEVEL I.

3. DELTA TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION PROCESS MAY BE USED BY ALL IT PERSONNEL 
THAT HAVE COMPLETED IT 'A' SCHOOL PRIOR TO DECEMBER 2010 IN ORDER TO RECEIVE 
2790 NEC. THIS DELTA TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION PACKAGE MUST BE COMPLETED BY 
OCTOBER 2011 AND IS REQUIRED PRIOR TO ATTENDING NEW ITOF NAVY SYSTEM 
ADMINISTRATOR TRAINING FOR NEC 2791.

4.  LEGACY 2735 PERSONNEL WILL NOT BE REQUIRED TO COMPLETE 2790 DELTA 
TRAINING; THEY WILL BE REQUIRED TO COMPLETE THE 2791 DELTA TRAINING TO RECEIVE 
THE 2791 NEC.
A. LOG IN TO THE CYBERFOR PROVIDED CERTIFICATION TRAINING WEBSITE AT 
HTTPS://NAVYIACERTPREP.SKILLPORT.COM.  IF NEW USER, SELECT NEW USER TO OBTAIN 
A USER ID AND PASSWORD.
B. SELECT NEC 2790 DELTA PACKAGE.
C. SELECT ADD TO MY LEARNING PLAN.
D. COMPLETE ALL TRAINING MODULES.  ENSURE TRAINING OFFICER HAS UPLOADED 
TRAINING COMPLETION TO ELECTRONIC TRAINING JACKET OR THAT LOCAL INFORMATION 
ASSURANCE MANAGER (IAM) UPLOADS TRAINING TO TWMS INFORMATION ASSURANCE 
WORKFORCE (IAWF) MODULE VIA THE ENTER TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION MODE.
E. APPLY TO NAVY CERTIFICATION PROGRAM OFFICE VIA NAVY COOL FOR EXAM VOUCHERS 
FOR COMPUTING TECHNOLOGY INDUSTRY ASSOCIATION A PLUS (TWO
EXAMS) AND MICROSOFT MCP 70-270 EXAMS.
F. TAKE AND PASS THOSE THREE EXAMS.
G. HAVE YOUR LOCAL IAM ENTER YOUR CERTIFICATIONS INTO THE TWMS IAWF MODULE.
H. FORWARD NAVPERS 1221/6, NEC CHANGE REQUEST FORM TO CYBERFOR N132 REQUESTING 
NEW 2790 NEC.

5. PER REF C, NEC 2790 HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED AS PREREQUISITE FOR NEW NEC 2791. 
AS SUCH, LEGACY IT PERSONNEL MUST ACHIEVE 2790 NEC PRIOR TO ATTENDING NEW NEC 
2791 SYSTEM ADMINISTRATOR TRAINING WHICH IS SCHEDULED TO PILOT IN JANUARY 
2011.  DELTA REQUIREMENTS FOR THE 2791 NEC WILL BE PROMULGATED SEPCOR.

6. CONTACT CYBERFOR POC FOR ADDITIONAL INFORMATION REGARDING THIS PROCESS.  A 
DETAILED STEP BY STEP PRESENTATION TO ACCESS THE DELTA TRAINING IS AVAILABLE 
ON THE IAWF CERTIFICATION RESOURCE PAGE AT
