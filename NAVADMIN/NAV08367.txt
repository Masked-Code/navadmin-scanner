2.  THE INTENT OF THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR AWARD IS TO RECOGNIZE NAVY 
MILITARY AND CIVILIAN INDIVIDUALS AND TEAMS WHOSE CONTRIBUTIONS AND 
SUGGESTIONS DURING CALENDAR YEAR 2008, HAVE:
    A.  MEASURABLY SUPPORTED THE LOGISTICS MISSION OF THE NAVY.
    B.  PROMOTED INNOVATIVE IDEAS AND PRACTICABLE BENEFITS TO THE NAVY.
    C.  ENHANCED THE LOGISTICS PROFESSION.
3.  NOMINEES MAY BE INVOLVED IN ANY AND ALL PHASES OF NAVY ACQUISITION, 
OPERATIONAL, AND JOINT SERVICES LOGISTICS, FROM EARLY LIFE-CYCLE 
PLANNING TO IN-SERVICE LOGISTICS SUPPORT.  CONTRACTORS MAY BE INCLUDED 
ON TEAM AWARD SUBMISSIONS BUT WILL NOT RECEIVE MONETARY AWARDS.  
4.  INDIVIDUALS AND TEAM(S) SELECTED TO RECEIVE THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR 
AWARDS FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2008 WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA NAVAL MESSAGE IN 
MAY 2009.  AWARDS ARE SCHEDULED TO BE PRESENTED IN A CEREMONY AT THE 
NAVY MEMORIAL, WASHINGTON, DC ON 17 JUNE 2009.
5.  AWARD SELECTEES RECEIVE THE FOLLOWING:
    A. PLAQUE FOR INDIVIDUAL CATEGORY AND EACH TEAM WINNER.
    B. $5,000 EACH FOR THE MILITARY AND CIVILIAN WINNERS.
    C. $10,000 FOR EACH LOGISTICS TEAM TO SHARE.
6.  NOMINATIONS MAY ONLY BE SUBMITTED BY/VIA ECHELON TWO COMMANDS TO 
DEPUTY CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS (DCNO) FLEET READINESS AND LOGISTICS 
(FR&L) CODE N40.  ECHELON TWO COMMANDS MAY SUBMIT A TOTAL OF THREE 
NOMINATIONS, NO MORE THAN ONE PER AWARD CATEGORY.  AWARD NOMINATIONS 
MUST BE RECEIVED AT DCNO (FR&L) CODE N40 BY COB 31 MARCH 2009.  TO 
RECEIVE CONFIRMATION OF NOMINATION, NOMINATION PACKAGE MUST INCLUDE E-
MAIL ADDRESS AND PHONE NUMBER OF ECHELON TWO POC.
