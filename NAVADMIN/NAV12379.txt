RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN RECOGNIZES THE NAVY'S SHORE, SEA AND OVERSEAS COMMANDS 
FOR THEIR OUTSTANDING COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECTS BY PRESENTING AWARDS IN FIVE 
FLAGSHIP CATEGORIES:  PERSONAL EXCELLENCE PARTNERSHIP; HEALTH, SAFETY, AND 
FITNESS; CAMPAIGN DRUG FREE; PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR AND ENVIRONMENTAL 
STEWARDSHIP.

2.  THE PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR AWARD RECOGNIZES THE BEST YEAR-ROUND VOLUNTEER-
SUPPORTED PROGRAM OR SPECIAL PROJECT THAT PROMOTES OUTREACH ACTIVITIES 
THROUGHOUT THE YEAR TO ESTABLISH AND RESTORE HOPE TO THE COMMUNITY.

3.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE 2012 NAVY PROJECT GOOD 
NEIGHBOR COMMUNITY SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS:

        - SMALL - USS CONSTITUTION
        - MEDIUM - PCU ARLINGTON (LPD 24)
        - LARGE - NAVAL AIR STATION, PENSACOLA, FL

        - SMALL - STRIKE FORCE TRAINING PACIFIC
        - MEDIUM - PATROL SQUADRON ONE (VP 1)
        - LARGE - USS HARRY S. TRUMAN (CVN 75)
    C.  OVERSEAS COMMAND CATEGORY:
        - SMALL - NAVY MUNITIONS COMMAND, EAST ASIA DIVISION, UNIT GUAM
        - MEDIUM - NO RECOMMENDATIONS RECEIVED
        - LARGE - U.S. NAVAL HOSPITAL, YOKOSUKA

4.  IT IS NOTEWORTHY AND COMMENDABLE THAT THESE COMMANDS FOSTER A CULTURE OF 
VOLUNTEERISM THROUGH MEANINGFUL COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECTS IN OUR COMMUNITIES.
