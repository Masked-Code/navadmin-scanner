RMKS/1. Each year the Navy recognizes shore, sea and overseas commands for outstanding community 
service projects by presenting awards in five flagship categories: Personal Excellence Partnership; 
Health, Safety, and Fitness; Campaign Drug Free; Project Good Neighbor; and Environmental 
Stewardship.
2. The Project Good Neighbor Award recognizes the best year-round volunteer-supported program or 
special project that promotes outreach activities throughout the year to establish and restore hope to 
the community.
3. It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2013 Navy Project Good Neighbor Community Service 
Flag Award winners:
a.  Shore commands category:
- Small -  USS CONSTITUTION
- Medium - Navy Medicine and Professional Development Center
- Large - Joint Expeditionary Base Little Creek-Fort Story b.  Sea commands category:
- Small - Navy Region Hawaii (N00P)21st Dental Company
- Medium - USS ANTIETAM (CG 54)
- Large - USS NIMITZ (CVN 68)
c.  Overseas command category:
- Small - Commander, Fleet Activities Chinhae
- Medium - No Packages Submitted
- Large - USS FRANK CABLE (AS 40)
4. My sincere thanks to all for your continued support of the Navy's Community Service Program. Please 
accept my personal Bravo Zulu and thanks to the caring and dedicated Sailors, civilians, and families who 
selflessly volunteered and contributed in improving the quality of life within your communities.// 5. 
Released by Vice Admiral S. H. Swift, Director, Navy Staff.
