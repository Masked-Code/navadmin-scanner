RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the winners of the 2017 Community Service 
Health, Safety, and Fitness Flagship Awards.  In line with reference (a) each 
year the Navy recognizes various shore, sea, and overseas commands for 
outstanding community service projects by presenting awards in five flagship 
sponsor categories:  Personal Excellence Partnership, Health, Safety, and 
Fitness, Campaign Drug Free, Project Good Neighbor and Environmental 
Stewardship.

2.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2017 Community Service 
Health, Safety, and Fitness Flagship Award Winning Commands:

    Small Overseas Winner:              Naval Airborne Weapons
                                        Maintenance Unit ONE, Guam
    Large Overseas Winner:              U.S. Naval Hospital Naples,

    Large Overseas Honorable Mention:   U.S. Naval Hospital Guam

    Large Shore Honorable Mentions:     Joint Base Pearl Harbor-

                                        Station Patuxent River, MD

3.  Congratulations to all who participated in noteworthy efforts leading to 
this recognition.  I offer my sincere appreciation for your personal 
commitment and continued support of our Community Service Program.

4.  All award winners will receive commemorative plaques and the honorable 
mentions will receive certificates from the Flag Sponsor, Commander, Naval 
Education and Training Command, in recognition of their exceptional 
accomplishments.
