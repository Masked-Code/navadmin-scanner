RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE COALITION OF SAILORS AGAINST DESTRUCTIVE DECISIONS (CSADD) SEA AND SHORE CHAPTERS OF THE YEAR FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2011.

2.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE CSADD SEA AND SHORE CHAPTERS OF THE YEAR.  THE COMPETITION WAS FIERCE AND EACH COMMAND DID AN OUTSTANDING JOB TO PROMOTE THE IMPORTANCE OF THE CSADD PROGRAM.

3.  THE FOLLOWING CHAPTERS WERE SELECTED AS REGION SEA CHAPTERS OF THE YEAR.  FROM THESE THREE CHAPTERS THE WINNER OF SEA CHAPTER OF THE YEAR WAS SELECTED:
    A.  NAVY REGION MID-ATLANTIC - USS KEARSAGE (LHD 3).
    B.  NAVY REGION SOUTHWEST - USS DEWEY (DDG 105).
    C.  NAVY REGION JAPAN - USS GEORGE WASHINGTON (CVN 73)

4.  THE CSADD SEA CHAPTER OF THE YEAR IS THE USS KEARSAGE.  THE KEARSAGE HAS LED FROM THE FRONT BY MAKING THEIR CSADD CHAPTER THE BEST IN THE FLEET.
HONORABLE MENTION - USS GEORGE WASHINGTON.

5.  THE FOLLOWING CHAPTERS WERE SELECTED AS REGION SHORE CHAPTERS OF THE YEAR.  FROM THESE SIX CHAPTERS THE WINNER OF SHORE CHAPTER OF THE YEAR WAS
SELECTED:
    A.  NAVY REGION MID-ATLANTIC - TRANSIENT PERSONNEL UNIT NORFOLK
    B.  NAVY REGION SOUTHWEST - NAVAL MEDICAL CENTER SAN DIEGO
    C.  NAVY REGION NORTHWEST - NAVAL AIR STATION WHIDBEY ISLAND
    D.  NAVY REGION MIDWEST - NAVAL STATION GREAT LAKES
    E.  NAVY REGION EUROPE - NAVAL SUPPORT ACTIVITY BAHRAIN

6.  THE CSADD SHORE CHAPTER OF THE YEAR IS NAVAL STATION GREAT LAKES.  THE GREAT LAKES CHAPTER HAS SET A PACE FOR ALL COMMANDS TO EMULATE.
    HONORABLE MENTION - NAVAL SUPPORT ACTIVITY BAHRAIN.

7.  SOME OF THE BEST PRACTICES THAT MADE THE WINNING CHAPTERS STAND OUT
WERE:
    A.  THE CHAPTERS USE OF SOCIAL MEDIA TO SUPPORT THE PROGRAM.
    B.  ENCOURAGING SAILORS TO LEAD THROUGH CSADD.
    C.  STRONG SUPPORT FROM THE COMMAND LEADERSHIP TEAM.

8.  CONGRATULATIONS TO ALL COMMANDS THAT HAVE AN ACTIVE CSADD CHAPTER AND WE LOOK FORWARD TO THE YEAR AHEAD.

9.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
