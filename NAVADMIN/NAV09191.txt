MANAGEMENT PROGRAM// RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN SOLICITS APPLICATIONS FOR 
THE NATIONAL SECURITY MANAGEMENT PROGRAM (NSMP).  APPLICATIONS ARE DUE 
NO LATER THAN 25 SEPTEMBER 2009 FOR THE FY-10 SELECTION BOARD.
2.  NSMP IS A DOD-SPONSORED FOUR-WEEK COURSE OFFERED AT GEORGE 
WASHINGTON UNIVERSITY THAT FOCUSES ON VARIOUS ASPECTS OF NATIONAL 
SECURITY POLICY.  PARTICIPANTS WILL ATTEND THE SENIOR STUDIES COURSE IN 
NATIONAL SECURITY.  THE COURSE CONVENING MONTH HAS NOT BEEN RELEASED.  
HISTORICALLY, THE COURSE HAS BEEN OFFERED IN MARCH, APRIL, MAY, OR 
JUNE.
3.  APPLICANTS MUST BE TOP-PERFORMING UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) CAPTAIN 
OR CAPTAIN-SELECTS WHO HOLD A MASTER'S DEGREE OR PROVEN SUBSPECIALTY.  
PARTICIPANTS WILL INCUR TWO YEARS OBLIGATED SERVICE UPON COMPLETION OF 
THE COURSE.
4.  REPORTING SENIORS SHOULD SUBMIT NOMINATIONS IN THE FOLLOWING FORMAT 
TO CENTER FOR PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
(CPPD) VOLUNTARY EDUCATION DETACHMENT, ATTN:  CODE N2A2, 6490 SAUFLEY 
FIELD ROAD, PENSACOLA, FL 32509-5204:
    A.  NOMINEE RANK, NAME, SSN/DESIGNATOR 
    B.  COMMAND ADDRESS, TELEPHONE (DSN AND COMM), AND E-MAIL ADDRESS
    C.  NOMINEE LEVEL OF EDUCATION ATTAINED
    D.  NOMINEE OPERATIONAL, TECHNICAL, AND MANAGERIAL SPECIALTIES AND 
SUBSPECIALTY CODES AND A LIST OF SIGNIFICANT ASSIGNMENTS AS COMMANDING 
OFFICER, PROJECT OFFICER, OR PROVEN SUBSPECIALIST
    E.  NOMINEE FUTURE POTENTIAL, INCLUDING SUGGESTIONS FOR SPECIFIC 
CATEGORIES OF ASSIGNMENT
    F.  REPORTING SENIOR STATEMENT AFFIRMING NOMINEE'S AVAILABILITY TO 
ATTEND THE COURSE, IF SELECTED
    G.  NOMINEE STATEMENT INDICATING HOW PARTICIPATION WILL BENEFIT THE 
NAVY AND THE NOMINEE
    H.  NOMINEE ONE-PAGE BIOGRAPHY
5.  SELECTEES WILL RECEIVE FULL TUITION, ROOM AND BOARD, AND TRAVEL
