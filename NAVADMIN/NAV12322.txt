AMPN/REF A IS NAVADMIN 267/12, HUMAN RESOURCES JUNIOR OFFICER OF THE YEAR 
AWARD. 

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE WINNER OF THE HUMAN RESOURCES COMMUNITY 
OFFICER OF THE YEAR AWARD.  THIS AWARD RECOGNIZES ONE JUNIOR OFFICER, O4 AND 
BELOW, WHO HAS MADE SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE PERSONNEL READINESS OF 
THE NAVY.  

2.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE LIEUTENANT COMMANDER JAMES 
DARKENWALD AS THE FISCAL YEAR 2012 HUMAN RESOURCES COMMUNITY JUNIOR OFFICER OF 
THE YEAR FOR HIS WORK AS HEAD, ENLISTED ACCESSION PLANS AND FORCE SHAPING, 
CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS STAFF (N132).  LCDR DARKENWALD OVERSAW PERSONNEL 
PLANS AND POLICIES PERTAINING TO ACCESSIONS AND RECRUITMENT, SELECTION AND 
CLASSIFICATION, ENLISTED OCCUPATIONAL STANDARDS, RETENTION, FORCE MANAGEMENT, 
SEPARATIONS, AND RETIREMENTS.  

3.  LCDR DARKENWALD WAS SELECTED FROM A FIELD OF 28 OUTSTANDING CANDIDATES, 
REPRESENTING THE FINEST HUMAN RESOURCES JUNIOR OFFICERS SERVING ASHORE AND 
AFLOAT.  ALL NOMINEES FOR THIS AWARD ARE TO BE CONGRATULATED FOR THEIR 
ACCOMPLISHMENTS.

4.  LCDR DARKENWALD WILL RECEIVE A NAVY COMMENDATION MEDAL AND THE 2012 HUMAN 
RESOURCES COMMUNITY JUNIOR OFFICER OF THE YEAR CERTIFICATE AT AN AWARD 
CEREMONY IN ARLINGTON, VIRGINIA.   

5.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1//
