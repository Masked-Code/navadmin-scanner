RMKS/1.  THE CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS IS PLEASED TO ANNOUNCE CYBER FEDERAL 
EXECUTIVE FELLOWSHIP (CFEF) OPPORTUNITIES FOR ACADEMIC YEAR 2012-2013 .
THESE FELLOWSHIPS ARE LOCATED AT THE POTOMAC INSTITUTE FOR POLICY STUDIES 
WWW.POTOMACINSTITUTE.ORG IN ARLINGTON, VA, AND AT CARNEGIE MELLON UNIVERSITY 
WWW.CMU.EDU, IN PITTSBURGH, PA.  THESE FELLOWSHIPS WILL PROVIDE INFORMATION 
DOMINANCE CORPS (IDC) OFFICERS (1800, 1810, 1820, AND 1830
DESIGNATORS) THE
UNIQUE OPPORTUNITY TO INCREASE THEIR UNDERSTANDING OF CYBERSPACE POLICY 
DEVELOPMENT AND DECISION MAKING AT THE HIGHEST LEVELS OF GOVERNMENT WHILE 
INTEGRATING CONCERNS OF NATIONAL SECURITY, GOVERNANCE, AND INFORMATION 
TECHNOLOGY.

2.  ELIGIBILITY FOR THE PROGRAM IS AS FOLLOWS:  ACTIVE DUTY IDC OFFICERS IN 
PAYGRADES O4-O5, WITH BALANCED OPERATIONAL/STAFF EXPERIENCE AND EITHER A 
GRADUATE DEGREE OR SIGNIFICANT EXPERIENCE IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY, 
INFORMATION WARFARE OR INTELLIGENCE APPLICATION, POLICY, AND STRATEGY .

3.  APPLICATIONS ARE DUE TO NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-47) NO LATER THAN 17 
SEPTEMBER 2011.  APPLICATION FORMAT IN REF A.  REF A CAN BE OBTAINED ON THE 
DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY ISSUANCES WEBSITE HTTP://DONI.DAPS.DLA.MIL OR FROM THE 
OPNAV N2/N6 PROGRAM POC (PARA 5).  APPLICATIONS SHOULD BE SENT
TO:  NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND, ATTN:  PERS-440C, 5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE, 
MILLINGTON, TN 38055-4400.  ADDITIONALLY, E-MAIL A COPY OF THE COMPLETED 
APPLICATION TO THE OPNAV N2/N6 POC (PARA 5) AND YOUR DETAILER.

4.  OFFICERS COMPLETING THE CFEF PROGRAM SUBSEQUENTLY WILL BE AVAILABLE TO 
FILL BILLETS RELATED TO CYBERSPACE IN STRATEGY, PLANNING AND OPERATIONS TO 
LEVERAGE THEIR EXPERTISE.  OFFICERS WHO COMPLETE THIS PROGRAM CAN EXPECT TO BE 
DETAILED TO BILLETS AT ECHELON ONE/HEADQUARTERS STAFFS.  THIS SELECTION BOARD 
WILL CHOOSE ONE PRIMARY CANDIDATE FOR EACH CFEF OPPORTUNITY.  UP TO THREE 
ALTERNATE CANDIDATES WILL BE CHOSEN TO FILL THE POSITION IF EITHER PRIMARY 
CANDIDATE IS UNABLE TO ATTEND.
