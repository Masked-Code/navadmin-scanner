RMKS/1. APPLICATIONS FOR ACADEMIC YEAR 2013-2014 SAMUEL ELIOT MORISON NAVAL HISTORY 
SUPPLEMENTAL SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM ARE DUE NO LATER THAN 15 APRIL 2013.

2. THE SCHOLARSHIP OF $5,000 IS AWARDED TO ONE ACTIVE DUTY NAVY OR MARINE CORPS OFFICER 
WITH DEMONSTRATED LEADERSHIP POTENTIAL AND HIGH ACADEMIC QUALIFICATIONS ALREADY 
PURSUING GRADUATE STUDY IN HISTORY, INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS, OR A RELATED FIELD. THE 
AWARD IS TO HELP PAY FOR EXPENSES RELATED TO RESEARCH, TRAVEL, AND THE PURCHASE OF 
EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS.

3. ANY OFFICER DESIRING TO COMPETE FOR THIS SCHOLARSHIP SHOULD SUBMIT A LETTER OF 
APPLICATION VIA THEIR COMMANDING OFFICER TO THE SENIOR HISTORIAN, NAVAL HISTORY AND 
HERITAGE COMMAND (NHHC), 805 KIDDER BREESE STREET SE, WASHINGTON D.C. 20374-5060. 
CANDIDATES ARE REMINDED TO MAIL APPLICATIONS WITH ADEQUATE TIME FOR DELIVERY OR TO USE 
A COMMERCIAL EXPRESS DELIVERY SERVICE. APPLICANTS ON DEPLOYMENT WITHOUT REGULAR MAIL 
ACCESS MAY SUBMIT APPLICATIONS VIA ROUTINE MESSAGE OR FAX TO DSN: 288-3593, COMM: (202) 
433-3593 OR EMAIL TO MICHAEL.CRAWFORD(AT)NAVY.MIL. APPLICATION PACKAGES SHOULD PROVIDE 
THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION:
A. UNIVERSITY PRESENTLY ATTENDING OR TO WHICH APPLICATION HAS BEEN FORWARDED AND THE 
CURRENT STATUS OF THAT APPLICATION.
B. A STATEMENT FROM THE APPLICANT THAT INCLUDES AN OUTLINE OF THE PROPOSED EDUCATION 
PROGRAM, TITLE, AND DEGREE OBJECTIVE.
C. BIOGRAPHY
D. COPY OF ALL OFFICIAL COLLEGE/UNIVERSITY GRADUATE AND POSTGRADUATE TRANSCRIPTS.
E. A STATEMENT NOT TO EXCEED 500 WORDS CONCERNING THE RELATIONSHIP OF THE MORISON 
SCHOLARSHIP TO THE APPLICANT'S AREA OF STUDY AND THE VALUE TO BE DERIVED BOTH BY THE 
INDIVIDUAL AND THE NAVY.
F. COMMANDING OFFICERS ENDORSEMENT.

4. THE DIRECTOR OF NHHC WILL MAKE FINAL SELECTION OF THE MORISON SCHOLARSHIP RECIPIENT 
AND AN ALTERNATE BY 15 MAY 2013.

5. THE DIRECTOR NHHC WILL NOTIFY THE SELECTED APPLICANT VIA LETTER.

6. POINTS OF CONTACT: DR. MICHAEL CRAWFORD, DSN: 288-6936,
COMM: (202) 433-6936, EMAIL: MICHAEL.CRAWFORD(AT)NAVY.MIL.

7. RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL R. W. HUNT, DIRECTOR, NAVY STAFF.
