RMKS/1.  THIS ORDER TO ACCOUNT WILL COMMENCE AFTER STORM PASSAGE/ CLOSEST 
POINT OF APPROACH(CPA)AND APPLIES TO ALL NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON 
CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES, NAF EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES AND APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS 
WITHIN THE ESTABLISHED GEOGRAPHIC AREA OF INTEREST (GAOI)  DESIGNATED IN 
PARAGRAPH 2.  ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL REGIONAL COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE 
WIDEST DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND TENANT COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS 
FOR IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT.

2. THE FOLLOWING GAOI(S) HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED FOR HURRICANE IRMA A.  THE 
FOLLOWING COUNTIES IN THE STATE OF FLORIDA: PASCO, HERNANDO, CITRUS, LAKE, 
LEVY,MARION, VOLUSIA, ALACHUA, PUTNAM, FLAGLER, BRADFORD, CLAY, SAINT JOHNS, 
BAKER, DUVAL, SUMTER, SEMINOLE, GILCHRIST, SUWANEE, COLUMBIA, UNION, NASSAU, 
WAKULLA, LEON, MADISON, JEFFERSON, HAMILTON, DIXIE, LAFAYETTE, GADSDEN, 
LIBERTY, GULF, FRANKLIN, JACKSON, CALHOUN, BAY, HOLMES, WASHINGTON, WALTON, 
OKALOOSA, SANTA ROSA, TAYLOR, AND ESCAMBIA.
B.  THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES IN THE STATE OF GEORGIA: CHARLTON, CAMDEN 
BRANTLEY, ECHOLS, CLINCH, WARE, PIERCE, WAYNE, LONG, MCINTOSH, LIBERTY, 
CHATHAM, BRYAN, EFFINGHAM, GLYNN, AND LOWNDES.
(ALL DESIGNATED PERSONNEL AND OR DEPENDENTS IN THE GAOI NEED TO BE ACCOUNTED 
FOR).

3.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO:
A.  COMMANDING OFFICERS(COS) AND/OR COMMANDING OFFICER
REPRESENTATIVES(CORS) WILL ENSURE THAT THEIR COMMANDS NAVY FAMILIY 
ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT SYSTEM(NFAAS) DATABASE IS UP TO DATE, TO 
INCLUDE PHONE NUMBERS AND ADDRESSES. COS AND/OR CORS ENSURE ANY RECENT CHECK-
INS TO YOUR COMMAND WHO HAVE NOT HAD THIER CONTACT INFORMATION UPDATED IN 
NFAAS COMMUNICATE RECALL INFORMATION TO THEIR RESPECTIVE CHAIN OF COMMAND.
B.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON CIVILIAN 
EMPLOYEES, NAF EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES AND APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS IN THE GAOI 
AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA.  ALL CORS/SPONSORS IN THE AFFECTED GAOIWILL RECEIVE 
NOTIFICATION FROM NFAAS VIA TEXT/EMAIL TO MUSTER THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN 
NFAAS AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA. COS AND/OR CORS WILL ENSURE WIDEST 
DISSEMINATION OFREQUIREMENT FOR ACCURATE AND TIMELY ACCOUNTABILITY VIA NFAAS.
COS/CORS WILL DETERMINE ACCURATE STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF THEIR PERSONNEL 
AND MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR THEM AT HOME, WORK, TELEWORK, EVACUATED,OR 
DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, 
TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER).
C.  COS AND/OR CORS UTILIZE THE NFAAS SUPPORT SITE AT 
HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL TO ACCOUNT FOR EACH INDIVIDUAL REQUIRED TO BE 
MUSTERED PER REFS A AND B.
D. COMMANDS SHALL DIRECT CORS/SPONSORS TO MUSTER IN NFAAS AFTER STORM 
PASSAGE/CPA UPON NOTIFICATION.
E.  IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, COMMANDS/MEMBERS ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS OF 
PERSONNEL ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) TO THE AFFECTED REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) 
OR DIRECT CHAIN OF COMMAND.
F. DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS COMPLETE.  
REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED INCLUDING THE POSTING 
OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES  AREA ON THE INDIVIDUAL'S ACCOUNTING LINE.

4.  ALL CORS/SPONSORS ARE EXPECTED TO:
A.  ACCOUNT FOR THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN NFAAS AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA 
AND NOTIFICATION VIA HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL.
B.  IF UNABLE TO ACCESS NFAAS SPONSORS SHOULD CONTACT THEIR COR,  COMMAND 
MUSTERING OFFICER OR SUPERVISOR TO CHECK IN (MUSTER).
C.  SPONSORS WILL PROVIDE COMMAND REPRESENTATIVES WITH CURRENT 
RESIDENCE,DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE 
(DEPLOYED,TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, 
OTHER).
D.  FAMILY MEMBERS CAN LOG ON TO NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL AND 
CHECK IN (MUSTER) ON THE NAVY FAMILY MEMBERS SITE.  IF UNABLE TO LOG ON TO 
NFAAS, CALL THE NFAAS HELP DESK AT 866-946-9183 (FOR TECHNICAL SUPPORT ONLY).

5.  A NEEDS ASSESSMENT HAS BEEN INITIATED FOR HURRICANE IRMA.
