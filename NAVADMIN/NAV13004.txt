RMKS/1.  PER REF A, NOMINATIONS ARE REQUESTED FOR THE ANNUAL ADMIRAL STAN 
ARTHUR AWARDS.  SUBMIT ALL NOMINATIONS, NOT TO EXCEED TWO PAGES, USING OPNAV 
5305/8 (REV JUL 2011), AT HTTP://NAVALFORMS.DAPS.DLA.MIL. CATEGORIES INCLUDE:
    A.  MILITARY LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR
    B.  CIVILIAN LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR
    C.  LOGISTICS TEAM(S) OF THE YEAR

2.  THE INTENT OF THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR AWARD IS TO RECOGNIZE NAVY MILITARY 
AND CIVILIAN INDIVIDUALS AND TEAM(S) WHOSE CONTRIBUTIONS AND SUGGESTIONS 
DURING CALENDAR YEAR 2012 HAVE:
    A.  MEASURABLY SUPPORTED THE LOGISTICS MISSION OF THE NAVY
    B.  PROMOTED INNOVATIVE IDEAS AND PRACTICABLE BENEFITS TO THE
        NAVY
    C.  ENHANCED THE LOGISTICS PROFESSION
    D.  CONTRIBUTED TO THE NAVY'S AUDITABILITY EFFORT

3.  NOMINEES MAY BE INVOLVED IN ANY AND ALL PHASES OF NAVY ACQUISITION, 
OPERATIONAL LOGISTICS, AND JOINT SERVICES LOGISTICS, FROM EARLY LIFE-CYCLE 
PLANNING TO IN-SERVICE LOGISTICS SUPPORT.  NOMINEES CAN COME FROM A VARIETY OF 
COMMUNITIES, TO INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO:  CIVIL ENGINEERING, SUPPLY, 
MEDICAL, AND MAINTENANCE. CONTRACTORS MAY BE INCLUDED ON TEAM AWARD 
SUBMISSIONS, BUT WILL NOT RECEIVE MONETARY AWARDS.

4.  INDIVIDUALS AND TEAM(S) SELECTED TO RECEIVE THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR AWARDS 
FOR CALENDER YEAR 2012 WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA NAVAL MESSAGE.  THE AWARD 
CEREMONY IS SCHEDULED FOR TUESDAY, 18 JUNE 2013, 1500-1630, AT THE NAVY 
MEMORIAL, WASHINGTON, DC.

5.  AWARD SELECTEES RECEIVE THE FOLLOWING:
    A.  PLAQUE FOR INDIVIDUAL CATEGORY AND TEAM(S)
    B.  $5,000 EACH FOR THE MILITARY AND CIVILIAN WINNERS
    C.  $10,000 FOR WINNING TEAM TO SHARE AMONG ITS CIV/MIL MEMBERS

6.  SUBMIT NOMINATIONS, VIA ECHELON TWO COMMANDS, TO DIRECTOR, LOGISTICS 
PROGRAMS AND BUSINESS OPERATIONS DIVISION (OPNAV N41) NLT COB TUESDAY, 9 APRIL 
2013.  ECHELON TWO COMMANDS MAY SUBMIT A TOTAL OF THREE NOMINATIONS, NO MORE 
THAN ONE PER AWARD CATEGORY.  TO RECEIVE CONFIRMATION, NOMINATION PACKAGE MUST 
INCLUDE E-MAIL ADDRESS AND PHONE NUMBER OF ECHELON TWO POC.

7.  RELEASED BY VADM P. H. CULLOM, N4.//
