BE ELIGIBLE AND THEIR RECORDS WILL NOT BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD FOR 
CONSIDERATION.

4.  THE FOLLOWING OFFICERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE AND THEIR RECORDS WILL NOT 
BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD FOR CONSIDERATION:  ANY OFFICER WHO HAS AN
