SUBJ/NAVY CASUALTY AND TRAINING CONFERENCE//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE 2011 NAVY CASUALTY AND TRAINING 
CONFERENCE WILL BE HELD AT THE MID-SOUTH CONFERENCE CENTER, 5700 ATTU 
STREET, MILLINGTON, TN 38053, FROM 15-17 MARCH 2011.  PARTICIPATION BY 
NAVY REGIONAL CASUALTY COORDINATORS, QUALIFIED OR PROSPECTIVE CASUALTY 
ASSISTANCE CALLS OFFICERS (CACO), DECEDENT AFFAIRS OFFICERS, COMMANDING 
OFFICERS, EXECUTIVE OFFICERS, COMMAND MASTER CHIEFS, SENIOR ENLISTED 
ADVISORS, AND COMMAND-LEVEL LEADERSHIP ASSOCIATED WITH CASUALTY 
ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS IS STRONGLY ENCOURAGED.   NO REGISTRATION FEE WILL 
BE ASSESSED.  NAVY CASUALTY WILL NOT FUND TRAVEL, LODGING, OR OTHER 
EXPENSES FOR THOSE WHO ATTEND THE CONFERENCE.

2.  THE CONFERENCE GOALS ARE TO PROVIDE CACO TRAINING AND TO BRIEF ALL 
ATTENDEES ON THE CURRENT STATE OF NAVY CASUALTY AND NEW OR PENDING 
POLICIES AND PROGRAMS.

3.  EARLY REGISTRATION IS REQUIRED AND CAN BE ACCOMPLISHED VIA THE 
FOLLOWING WEBSITE:
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/COMMANDSUPPORT/CASUALTYASSISTANCE.

4.  PARTICIPANTS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR ARRANGING THEIR OWN TRAVEL AND 
LODGING.  REMEMBER, TO BE REIMBURSED FOR LODGING EXPENSES, THOSE 
MILITARY PERSONNEL WHO DO NOT STAY ON THE NSA MID-SOUTH BASE MUST OBTAIN 
A CERTIFICATE OF NON-AVAILABILITY TO DOCUMENT THAT GOVERNMENT LODGING 
WAS NOT AVAILABLE.

5.  FOR ON BASE LODGING RESERVATIONS, CONTACT NAVY GATEWAY INNS AND 
SUITES AT 1-877-628-9233 OR ONLINE AT WWW.DODLODGING.NET.

6.  UNIFORMS FOR THIS EVENT:    
NAVY E6 AND BELOW:  SERVICE UNIFORM;
NAVY E7 AND ABOVE: SERVICE KHAKI; 
OTHER MILITARY SERVICES:  SERVICE EQUIVALENT;
CIVILIANS:  BUSINESS CASUAL.

7.  POINT OF CONTACT IN THE NAVY CASUALTY ASSISTANCE OFFICE IS LSC 
BUTTS, OPNAV (N135C), (901)874-4370/DSN 882 OR VIA E-MAIL: 
DONNA.BUTTS(AT)NAVY.MIL.

8.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
