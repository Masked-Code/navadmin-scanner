SUBJ/SEPTEMBER 2007 S-3B SUNDOWN TRANSITION SELECTION BOARD// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE CONVENING OF THE FINAL S-3B 
SUNDOWN TRANSITION BOARD ON 20 SEPTEMBER 2007.  ALL REMAINING S-3 
AVIATORS IN YEAR GROUPS 2001 TO 2004 WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY CONSIDERED 
REGARDLESS OF THEIR PROJECTED ROTATION DATE.  A LIST OF ELIGIBLE 
OFFICERS, TRANSITION PREFERENCE FORMS, AND FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS 
CAN BE FOUND AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/AVIATION/S3SUNDOWNTRANSITION.
2.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS WHO DESIRE CONSIDERATION FOR A SPECIFIC PLATFORM 
MUST SUBMIT A TRANSITION PREFERENCE FORM TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-433H).  
ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY ALSO SUBMIT CORRESPONDENCE TO THE BOARD, 
INCLUDING LETTERS OF RECOMMENDATION.  ALL CORRESPONDENCE MUST BE 
RECEIVED BY 17 SEPTEMBER 2007.  TRANSITION PREFERENCE FORMS AND OTHER 
CORRESPONDENCE MAY BE SUBMITTED VIA FAX TO (901) 874- 2756/DSN 882, 
EMAIL TO POINT OF CONTACT LISTED IN PARA 5, OR VIA MAIL TO:
     COMMANDER NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND
     (PERS-433H)
     5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE
     MILLINGTON TN  38055-4300
3.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS WHO DO NOT DESIRE TO TRANSITION MUST INDICATE 
THEIR INTENTIONS VIA THE TRANSITION PREFERENCE FORM BEFORE THE BOARD 
CONVENES.  DECLINING TRANSITION RENDERS AN OFFICER INELIGIBLE FOR 
FUTURE CONSIDERATION ON ANY ADDITIONAL SUNDOWN TRANSITION BOARDS 
(SHOULD THEY BE REQUIRED) AND AS AN AVIATION OPERATIONAL DEPARTMENT
HEAD.
4.  OFFICERS THAT DESIRE TRANSITION MUST ACCEPT THE PLATFORM THEY ARE 
OFFERED; ACCEPTANCE OF A TRANSITION OFFER INCURS A FOLLOW-ON OBLIGATION 
(30 MONTHS FOLLOWING COMPLETION OF FLEET REPLACEMENT SQUADRON TRAINING) 
REGARDLESS OF THE OFFICER'S ORIGINAL MINIMUM SERVICE REQUIREMENT.  
AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY ELIGIBILITY IS NOT AFFECTED BY A 
TRANSITION OBLIGATION.
5.  POINT OF CONTACT:  LCDR P. CARELLI AT (901) 874-2288/DSN 882 OR 
EMAIL AT PAUL.CARELLI(AT)NAVY.MIL.
6.  RELEASED BY VADM J. C. HARVEY, JR., N1.//
