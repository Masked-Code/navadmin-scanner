SUBJ/2017 NAVAL SHORE ACTIVITIES SAILOR OF THE YEAR//

RMKS/1.  I am pleased to announce the selection of CTR1(IW/SW/SG/EXW) Jared 
D. Mollette as the 2017 Naval Shore Activities Sailor of the Year.

2.  CTR1(IW/SW/SG/EXW) Jared D. Mollette was nominated by the Commander,U.S. 
Fleet Cyber Command/U.S. TENTH Fleet and selected from a field of 20 
nominations representing some of the finest Petty Officers currently serving 
in our Navy.
Congratulations to him and the other four exceptional finalists:
    a.  HM1(SW) Jane Lynn V. Borja representing Chief, Bureau of
    Medicine and Surgery.
    b.  HM1(EXW/FMF/DV/PJ) Brandon S. Elrod representing Commander,
    Naval Sea Systems Command.
    c.  ABH1(AW/SW) Joshua L. Schmeck representing Commander, Navy
    Instillations Command.
    d.  SO1(SEAL) Aaron K. Smith representing Commander, Naval
    Special Warfare Command.

3.  The competition was robust and reflected the high caliber of Sailors 
serving at shore activities throughout the world.  All of the finalists 
represented their commands and the entire enlisted community with 
distinction.

4.  Petty Officer Mollette will next compete during the 2017 Navy Shore 
Sailor of the Year competition in April.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral K. M. Donegan, Director, Navy Staff.//
