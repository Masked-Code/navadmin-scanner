RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN PROVIDES ADDITIONAL GUIDANCE FOR THE ASSOCIATION OF 
NAVAL SERVICES OFFICERS (ANSO) LEADERSHIP, PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND 
TRAINING GENERAL CONVENTION BEING HELD 6-10 AUGUST 2012 AT THE MANCHESTER 
GRAND HYATT, SAN DIEGO, CA.

2.  OSD HAS GIVEN APPROVAL FOR NAVY TO PARTICIPATE IN THE 2012 ANSO 
CONVENTION; GUIDANCE BELOW GOVERNS PARTICIPATION AND FUNDING FOR NAVY 
PERSONNEL.

3.  COMMANDING OFFICERS ARE AUTHORIZED TO SUPPORT ATTENDANCE.
COMMANDING OFFICERS MAY AUTHORIZE AND ISSUE TAD ORDERS, TO INCLUDE 
REGISTRATION, TRANSPORTATION, LODGING AND PER DIEM IAW REF A.  PER REF B, 
TRAVEL ORDERS AND ASSOCIATED CLAIMS MUST REFLECT WHEN THE REGISTRATION FEE 
INCLUDES CHARGES FOR MEALS AND THE DATES ON WHICH THE MEALS ARE FURNISHED.

4.  IN ORDER TO ACHIEVE THE MOST COST-EFFECTIVE TRANSPORTATION AVAILABLE, 
AGGREGATION OF CONVENTION TRANSPORTATION REQUIREMENTS IS ENCOURAGED.  USE OF 
NAVY OPERATIONAL SUPPORT AIRLIFT AND NAVY UNIQUE FLEET ESSENTIAL AIRCRAFT MAY 
BE REQUESTED, IAW REF C.

5.  PER REF D, SELECTED RESERVE PERSONNEL ARE AUTHORIZED TO PARTICIPATE AT THE 
ANSO GENERAL CONVENTION IN A PAID DRILL STATUS.

6.  REGISTRATION, TRANSPORTATION, AND LODGING INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND ON THE 
ANSO WEBSITE AT WWW.ANSOMIL.ORG.  REGISTRATION FEES FOR THE ENTIRE CONVENTION, 
INCLUDING THE AWARDS BANQUET, ARE $375 FOR MEMBERS, $450 FOR NON-MEMBERS AND 
$150 FOR MIDSHIPMEN AND CADETS.
DAILY REGISTRATION FEES ARE $150 FOR MEMBERS AND $175 FOR NON-MEMBERS.

7.  THE CHIEF OF NAVAL PERSONNEL WILL FUND THE FIRST 100 JUNIOR OFFICERS 
(LIEUTENANT COMMANDER AND BELOW).  PERSONNEL PLANNING TO ATTEND SHOULD 
REGISTER AS SOON AS POSSIBLE FOR THE CONVENTION AND THE HOTEL TO ENSURE 
RESERVATIONS ARE AVAILABLE.

8.  POINTS OF CONTACT ARE:
    A.  FOR OFFICERS AND CIVILIANS, CONTACT CDR NORA M. PEREZ, USN AT (619) 
846-6940 OR VIA E-MAIL AT NORA.PEREZ(AT)AMEDD.ARMY.MIL.
    B.  FOR ENLISTED PERSONNEL, CONTACT PS1 LUIS GALVEZ-MERAZ, USN AT (804) 
765-4060 OR VIA E-MAIL AT ANSO.NAVY.ENL(AT)GMAIL.COM.
