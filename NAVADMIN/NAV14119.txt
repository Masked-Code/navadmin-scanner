RMKS/1.  THE ARLEIGH BURKE FLEET TROPHY IS AWARDED ANNUALLY TO ONE  SHIP OR 
AIRCRAFT SQUADRON FROM EACH COAST FOR ACHIEVING THE GREATEST  IMPROVEMENT IN 
BATTLE EFFICIENCY BASED ON THE BATTLE EFFICIENCY COMPETITION.

2.  IT IS AN HONOR TO ANNOUNCE THE CALENDAR YEAR 2013 ARLEIGH BURKE FLEET 
TROPHY AWARD RECIPIENTS FOR THEIR SUPERIOR PERFORMANCE AND IMPROVEMENT IN 
BATTLE EFFICIENCY:
  A.  ATLANTIC FLEET:  USS WYOMING (SSBN 742) (GOLD)
  B.  PACIFIC FLEET:  ELECTRONIC ATTACK SQUADRON ONE FOUR TWO
 (VAQ-142)

3.  SELECTION FOR THIS AWARD IS A REFLECTION OF THE QUALITY AND TENACITY OF 
THESE COMMANDS.
  A.  USS WYOMING (SSBN 742) (GOLD):  THEIR PERFORMANCE DEMONSTRATED 
UNPARALLELED SUCCESS IN SUPPORTING THE STRATEGIC MISSION AND SUBSTANTIALLY 
IMPROVED IN NUMEROUS BATTLE EFFICIENCY AREAS.
  B.  ELECTRONIC ATTACK SQUADRON ONE FOUR TWO (VAQ-142):  DURING  THEIR FIRST 
CARRIER-BASED DEPLOYMENT IN 21 YEARS, THEY FLEW AN IMPRESSIVE 1,723.3  HOURS 
AND 820 SORTIES, INCLUDING 637.4 HOURS AND 100 PERCENT COMBAT SORTIE 
COMPLETION RATE IN SUPPORT OF OPERATION ENDURING FREEDOM.

4.  CONGRATULATIONS TO USS WYOMING (SSBN 742) (GOLD) AND ELECTRONIC ATTACK 
SQUADRON ONE FOUR TWO (VAQ-142) FOR THEIR CONTINUED DEDICATION TO DUTY AND 
COMMITMENT TO EXCELLENCE.

5.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. H. SWIFT, DIRECTOR, NAVY STAFF.//
