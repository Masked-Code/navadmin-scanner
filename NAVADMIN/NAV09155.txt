RMKS/1.  THE ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP SELECTION BOARD IS SCHEDULED 
TO CONVENE ON 21 SEPTEMBER 2009.  ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP IS A 
PREREQUISITE FOR ASSIGNMENT TO ONE OF APPROXIMATELY 700 DEPARTMENT OF 
THE NAVY (DON) CRITICALLY CODED ACQUISITION BILLETS.  
2.  POLICY AND GUIDANCE ON ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP PROCEDURES FOR 
DON OFFICERS MAY BE FOUND AT THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE:  
HTTP://WWW.ACQUISITION.NAVY.MIL/RDA/HOME/CAREER_MANAGEMENT/
ACQUISITION_CORPS.
3.  APPLICATIONS (NO ENDORSEMENTS REQUIRED) MUST BE SUBMITTED FOR 
CONSIDERATION BY THE BOARD NOT LATER THAN 21 AUGUST 2009.  THE REQUIRED 
APPLICATION (INCLUDING COVER LETTER AND DATA SHEET) ARE AVAILABLE FROM 
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/ACQUISITION/.  ALL APPLICANTS ARE 
REQUIRED TO PROVIDE INFORMATION AS DELINEATED IN THE SAMPLE DATA SHEET. 
4.  THE PREFERRED METHOD OF SUBMITTING APPLICATIONS IS BY MAIL.  
APPLICATIONS WILL ALSO BE ACCEPTED VIA FAX OR EMAIL (SIGNED COPIES
ONLY) FOR THOSE OFFICERS DEPLOYED ABOARD SHIPS AND AT REMOTE SITES.
