SUBJ/RESTORATION OF PAPER-BASED TESTING ASHORE//
RMKS/1.  EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY, NAVY ASHORE DANTES TESTING FACILITIES 
WILL RESUME THE ADMINISTRATION OF PAPER-BASED EXAMS ON SHORE 
INSTALLATIONS ONLY.  THIS INCLUDES CLEP, DANTES STANDARDIZED SUBJECT 
TESTS, EXCELSIOR COLLEGE EXAMS, LSAT, AND PRAXIS.  
2.  CRITICAL TO THE RESUMPTION OF TESTING AT SEA WILL BE OUR ABILITY TO 
STRICTLY ADHERE TO THE ADMINISTRATION AND SAFEGUARDING OF EXAMS. 
TO ENSURE INCREASED SECURITY, NAVY WILL MAKE SIGNIFICANT CHANGES TO 
BOTH POLICY AND GUIDELINES.  THESE CHANGES WILL ESTABLISH CLEAR DUTIES 
AND RESPONSIBILITIES FOR THE EDUCATION SERVICES OFFICERS AND DANTES 
TEST CONTROL OFFICERS, AND WILL IDENTIFY CONSEQUENCES IF THE SECURITY 
OF THE EXAMS IS COMPROMISED.  
3.  I WILL CONTINUE TO KEEP YOU INFORMED AS WE MOVE TOWARD FULL 
RESTORATION OF ACADEMIC TESTING CAPABILITIES FOR OUR SAILORS.
4.  POINT OF CONTACT:  ANN HUNTER, OPNAV N128, AT (703) 695-3905/DSN 
295, OR EMAIL AT ANN.HUNTER(AT)NAVY.MIL.
