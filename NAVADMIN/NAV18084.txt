AMPN/REF A IS COMMANDER NAVY RESERVE FORCE NOTICE 5400, FISCAL YEAR 2019

ASSIGNMENT PROCEDURES.//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the fiscal year (FY) 2019 Navy 
Reserve National Command and Senior Officer (O5/O6) Non-Command Billet 
Screening and Assignment (APPLY) Board on 13 August 2018.  All Selected 
Reserve officer communities will utilize the APPLY Board process for senior 
officer and command assignments.

2.  Eligible officers desiring a billet assignment are required to complete 
all applicable sections of the APPLY application. Applications must be 
submitted via the Reserve Forces Manpower Tools (RFMT) website available at
https://private.navyreserve.navy.mil/apps/rfmtweb/.  Officers are encouraged 
to place the maximum number of billet choices on their dreamsheet to maximize 
the opportunity for an assignment.  Officers are highly encouraged to state 
their willingness to travel for billets not on their dreamsheet, which will 
enable the board to select the best qualified applicants to vacancies and 
other assignment opportunities.

3.  Applications must be finalized and billet preferences made via RFMT no 
later than 25 June 2018.

4.  With the exception of the most recent O-6 periodic fitness report 
(FITREP) (addressed in paragraph 5 below), all correspondence to the board 
must be received no later than 25 June 2018. Submission methods are detailed 
in paragraph 6 below.  Late correspondence will not be considered by the 
board.

5.  The July 2018 O-6 FITREPS will be accepted until 2359 CST 6 August 2018 
and should be submitted as an enclosure to a letter addressed to the APPLY 
Board President.  Enclosures, other than the July 2018 O-6 FITREP, submitted 
after the regular correspondence deadline in line with paragraph 4 will be 
removed from the correspondence submission and will not be considered by the 
board.

7.  Selected Reserve (SELRES) Officers must be qualified in line with 
reference (a) to be considered for an assignment.  Qualified officers must 
complete a dreamsheet application in RFMT to be considered by the APPLY Board 
for a billet.

8.  Officers who are not affiliated with the Reserve Component in a drilling 
status (e.g., SELRES on active-duty orders, active status pool/voluntary 
participation unit, mobilization, etc.) are also required to complete a 
dreamsheet to participate in APPLY.  Officers experiencing difficulty 
completing a dreamsheet in RFMT should contact Commander, Navy Reserve Force 
Command (COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12)) at cnrfc_apply_mailbox@navy.mil for 
assistance.

9.  APPLY Board assignments are assumed to be accepted by selected officers, 
meaning no further action is required.  Officers who desire to decline 
awarded billets must do so within 14 days following release of official 
results via the RFMT website.

10.  Point of contact:  All APPLY questions or concerns should be addressed 
to COMNAVRESFORCOM (N122) APPLY team via e-mail at 
cnrfc_apply_mailbox(at)navy.mil.
