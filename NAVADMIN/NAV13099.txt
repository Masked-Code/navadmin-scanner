RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES A CHANGE TO BASIC ALLOWANCE TO HOUSING (BAH) 
ENTITLEMENTS FOR DUAL MILITARY COUPLES WHEN ONE OR BOTH ARE ON SEA DUTY.  PER 
REF A, A SERVICE MEMBER MARRIED TO ANOTHER SERVICE MEMBER, IN PAYGRADE E5 OR 
BELOW, ASSIGNED TO PERMANENT SEA DUTY IS AUTHORIZED BASIC ALLOWANCE FOR 
HOUSING.

2.  REFS B AND C ARE CURRENTLY BEING UPDATED TO REFLECT THIS CHANGE.
REF D IS NOT AFFECTED BY THIS ENTITLEMENT CHANGE.  THIS CHANGE IS FOR A MEMBER 
ON SEA DUTY ONLY AND DOES NOT AFFECT A SAILOR ON SHORE DUTY OR A NEW 
ACCESSION.
