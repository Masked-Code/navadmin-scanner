SUBJ/2018 NAVY SHORE SAILOR OF THE YEAR//

RMKS/1.  I am pleased to announce the selection of LS1(AW/SW) Sindy Johnson 
as the 2018 Navy Shore Sailor of the Year.

2.  Petty Officer Johnson was the Pacific Fleet Sailor of the Year and was 
selected from a field of five outstanding finalists representing the finest 
Petty Officers serving ashore.  Congratulations to her and the other 
finalists:
    a.  LN1(AW) Oneka Stegall, representing Naval Shore Activities
    b.  FCA1(SW) Rose Houle, representing Manpower, Personnel, Training and 
Education
    c.  EM1(SW) Steffi Gutierrezlara, representing U.S. Fleet Forces Command
    d.  CTR1(SW/AW/IW) Jeremy Thompson, representing U.S. Naval Forces 
Europe, U.S. Naval Forces Africa, U.S. SIXTH Fleet

3.  The competition was extremely close and reflected the highest caliber of 
Sailors serving ashore throughout the world.  Each of the finalists 
represented their commands and the entire enlisted community with 
distinction.

4.  Petty Officer Johnson will be meritoriously advanced to Chief Petty 
Officer in May 2019.

5.  Released by  Ms. Steffanie B. Easter, Director, Navy Staff.//
