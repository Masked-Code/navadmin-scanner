SUBJ/APPLICATIONS SOUGHT FOR FY-09 INACTIVE DUTY NAVY RESERVE LIMITED 
DUTY OFFICER AND CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT PROGRAM// 
RMKS/1.  THE NAVY RESERVE IS SEEKING APPLICATIONS FROM HIGHLY QUALIFIED 
E-6 THROUGH E-9 PERSONNEL FOR THE FY-09 LIMITED DUTY OFFICER
(LDO) AND CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER (CWO) PROGRAMS.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE 
RECEIVED NO LATER THAN 31 OCT 07 AND ADDENDUMS TO APPLICATIONS MUST BE 
RECEIVED BY 1 DEC 07.
2.  LIMITED DUTY OFFICERS PROVIDE BROAD TECHNICAL MANAGEMENT SKILLS AND 
CHIEF WARRANT OFFICERS PROVIDE SPECIFIC TECHNICAL EXPERTISE AND 
LEADERSHIP SKILLS IN SUPPORT OF THE UNRESTRICTED AND RESTRICTED LINE 
AND STAFF COMMUNITIES.
3.  TIME IN SERVICE ELIGIBILITY FOR THE FY-09 BOARD IS CALCULATED TO
1 OCT 08.  COMMANDS SHOULD ENSURE COPIES OF THE MOST RECENT PERIODIC 
FITREP OR EVAL FOR THE APPLICANTS ARE SUBMITTED IN THE ORIGINAL 
APPLICATION OR IN AN ADDENDUM.
4.  ELIGIBILE SAILORS ARE ENCOURAGED TO APPLY FOR THE DESIGNATOR WHICH 
THEY ARE MOST QUALIFIED.  REFER TO OPNAVINST 1420.1A AND OPNAVINST
1120.12 FOR ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND APPLICATION FORMAT.  DETAILED 
INFORMATION REGARDING THE FY-09 USNR LDO/CWO OPEN DESIGNATORS, 
APPLICATION PROCEDURES, FREQUENT APPLICATION ERRORS, ELIGIBILITY 
REQUIREMENTS, AND BOARD MEMBERSHIP APPLICATIONS CAN BE REVIEWED AT THE 
USNR LDO/CWO WEBPAGE: 
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/CAREERINFO/RESERVEPERSONNELMANAGEMENT/OFFICERS/
LDO_CWO_COMMUNITY/LDOCWOPROCUREMENTBOARD.HTM
