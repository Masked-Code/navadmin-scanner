RMKS/1.  THE PURPOSE OF THIS NAVADMIN IS TO ANNOUNCE THE HUMAN RESOURCES
(HR) COMMUNITY JUNIOR OFFICER OF THE YEAR AWARD (JOOY).

2.  EACH YEAR THE HUMAN RESOURCES COMMUNITY WILL RECOGNIZE ONE JUNIOR OFFICER, 
O-4 AND BELOW, WHO HAS MADE SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE READINESS OF THE 
NAVY.

3.  ALL HR JUNIOR OFFICERS ARE ELIGIBLE (1200, 1205, 1207).

4.  NOMINATIONS SHALL BE SUBMITTED IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF A.

5.  ALL NOMINATIONS SHALL BE SUBMITTED BY THE MEMBER'S COMMANDING OFFICER OR 
OFFICER-IN-CHARGE.

6.  ENDORSED NOMINATIONS WILL BE SUBMITTED TO:  DIRECTOR, HR CENTER OF 
EXCELLENCE VIA E-MAIL AT GSPBBHRCOE(AT)NPS.EDU OR BY MAIL TO:
    DIRECTOR
    HR CENTER OF EXCELLENCE
    GRADUATE SCHOOL OF BUSINESS AND PUBLIC POLICY NPS
    555 DYER ROAD, IN-307
    MONTEREY, CA 93943

7.  THE NOMINATION DEADLINE FOR THE INAUGURAL HR JOOY AWARD IS 15 AUGUST 2010.

8.  THIS IS A FISCAL YEAR AWARD AND WILL RECOGNIZE CONTRIBUTIONS FROM THE 
CURRENT FISCAL YEAR.

9.  NOMINATIONS WILL BE EVALUATED BY A BOARD OF SIX VOTING MEMBERS (EQUALLY 
SPLIT BETWEEN 1200 AND 1205/1207
OFFICERS) AND TWO RECORDERS.

10.  AWARD WINNER WILL BE RECOGNIZED WITH:
    A.  NAVY COMMENDATION MEDAL;
    B.  FUNDED TRAVEL FOR WINNER TO WASHINGTON, DC, FOR AWARD PRESENTATION; 
AND
    C.  POSTING OF THE AWARD WINNER'S CONTRIBUTIONS ON THE HUMAN RESOURCES 
WEBSITE.

11.  THE COORDINATOR FOR THE AWARD IS THE HUMAN RESOURCES CENTER OF 
EXCELLENCE, GSPBBHRCOE(AT)NPS.EDU, DSN 756-3174 OR COMMERCIAL 831-656-3174.

12.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
