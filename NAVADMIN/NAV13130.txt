GENTEXT/RMKS/1.  Congratulations to the following personnel who have been 
selected for advancement to senior and master chief petty officer by the FY-14 
Navy Reserve E8 and E9 Selection Boards.  Strong competition between qualified 
professionals is one of the strengths of our Navy and your selection speaks 
highly of your abilities.  This NAVADMIN contains the names of all selectees 
to include those who are no longer in a hold status.

2.  This NAVADMIN is not an authorization for advancement.  Specific dates of 
advancement will be published by Commanding Officer, Naval Education and 
Training Professional Development Training Center Pensacola, FL.  Personnel 
listed below may be frocked IAW ref (a) if qualified.  

3.  Selectees are listed in alphabetical order by rate within competitive 
category.  Read each line from left to right, name, and numerical order of 
advancement.  Categories of advancement are Selected Reservist and Full Time 
Support.
