SUBJ/FY-07 INACTIVE DUTY NAVAL RESERVE LIMITED DUTY OFFICER AND
/CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT PROGRAM//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE LIMITED DUTY OFFICER AND CHIEF WARRANT
OFFICER PROGRAMS PROVIDE TECHNICAL MANAGERS AND SPECIALISTS WHO
EXERCISE LEADERSHIP IN KEY POSITIONS THROUGHOUT THE NAVY.  THROUGH
THESE PROGRAMS, OUTSTANDING SENIOR ENLISTED PERSONNEL MAY COMPETE
FOR A COMMISSION WITHOUT A COLLEGE DEGREE.
2.  THE FY-07 INACTIVE DUTY LIMITED DUTY OFFICER/CHIEF WARRANT
OFFICER IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT BOARD CONVENES ON 9 JAN 06.
APPLICATIONS ARE DUE TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4922A) BY 31 OCT 05 PER
OPNAVINST 1420.1A.  SELECTEES WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA NAVADMIN IN
FEB 06.
3.  TOP NOTCH INACTIVE NAVAL RESERVE FIRST CLASS PETTY OFFICERS WITH
8 TO 16 YEARS OF TOTAL QUALIFYING FEDERAL YEARS OF SERVICE AS OF 1
OCT 05 ARE ENCOURAGED TO APPLY FOR THE LIMITED DUTY OFFICER PROGRAM.
TOTAL QUALIFYING FEDERAL YEARS OF SERVICE IS DEFINED IN 10 U.S.C. AS
ANY TIME SPENT IN A NAVY COMPONENT AND THE SERVICE MEMBER EARNS AT
LEAST 50 POINTS.  HARD-CHARGING SENIOR CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS AND
CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS ON INACTIVE DUTY ARE ENCOURAGED TO APPLY
FOR BOTH THE LIMITED DUTY OFFICER AND CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER
PROGRAMS.  INACTIVE DUTY MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS ARE HIGHLY
ENCOURAGED TO APPLY FOR THE CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER PROGRAM.
4.  FOR DETAILED INFORMATION ON THE FY-07 UNITED STATES NAVAL
RESERVE LIMITED DUTY OFFICER/CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER OPEN DESIGNATORS,
APPLICATION PROCEDURES, AND ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS, VISIT THE
UNITED STATES NAVAL RESERVE LIMITED DUTY OFFICER/CHIEF WARRANT
OFFICER WEBPAGE AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/CAREERINFO/
RESERVEPERSONNELMANAGEMENT/SELRESOFFICER/LDO_CWO_COMMUNITY/
LDOCWOPROCUREMENTBOARD.HTM.
