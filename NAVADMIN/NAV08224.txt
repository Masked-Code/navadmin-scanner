REF A, ALL AFLOAT MORALE, WELFARE, AND RECREATION (MWR) VEHICLES 
PURCHASED WITH NON-APPROPRIATED FUNDS (NAF) ARE REQUIRED TO BE 
REGISTERED AND LICENSED THROUGH COMMANDER, NAVY INSTALLATIONS COMMAND 
(CNIC).
2. BEGINNING 1 OCTOBER 2008, CNIC WILL ISSUE NEW LICENSE PLATES FOR ALL 
SHIPBOARD/AFLOAT MWR VEHICLES PER GENERAL SERVICE ADMINISTRATIONS' 
OFFICE OF GOVERNMENT-WIDE POLICY.  EACH LICENSE PLATE SET (2) WILL BE 
VALID FOR A PERIOD OF FIVE YEARS AND REMAIN WITH THE DESIGNATED VEHICLE.
3. COMMANDS SPONSORING VEHICLES ARE REQUESTED TO SUBMIT (VIA E-MAIL TO 
LARRY.ZUMWALT@NAVY.MIL): MAKE, MODEL, COLOR, YEAR OF MANUFACTURE, VIN, 
CURRENT USN NUMBER FOR EACH VEHICLE, AND COMMAND NAME AND POC (NAME, 
TELEPHONE AND E-MAIL).  SUBMISSIONS WILL BE REQUIRED NO LATER THAN 1 
OCTOBER 2008.
4. FOR CONUS TRAVEL, ALL NAF MWR VEHICLES WILL BE REQUIRED TO DISPLAY 
NEW VEHICLE PLATES NO LATER THAN 31 DECEMBER 2009. VEHICLES THAT DO NOT 
HAVE NEW PLATES AND REGISTRATIONS BY THIS DATE WILL BE CONSIDERED IN 
VIOLATION OF STATE AND LOCAL VEHICLE LAWS. OCONUS NAF MWR VEHICLES WILL 
REQUIRE THE SAME REGISTRATION, BUT DISPLAY LICENSE PLATES PER LOCAL 
SOFA AGREEMENT, ETC. 
5. REQ ACTION: ADDRESSEES FORWARD TO SUBORDINATE SHIPS.
