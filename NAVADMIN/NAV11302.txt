3.  THIS INSTRUCTION HAS CRITICAL UPDATES.  MAJOR CHANGES INCLUDE  UPDATES TO 
MOTORCYCLE MISHAP RELATED OPREP-3 REPORTING CRITERIA,  VOICE MESSAGE REPORT 
CONTACT INFORMATION, AND UPDATES TO SEXUAL  ASSAULT REPORTING REQUIREMENTS.

A.  INITIAL SEXUAL ASSAULT OPREP/SITREP REPORTS WILL FOLLOW THE SAME STANDARD 
DATA FIELD REQUIREMENTS AS OTHER PERSONNEL INCIDENT REPORTS AS OUTLINED IN 
APPENDIX B.
B.  GENTEXT/INCIDENT IDENTIFICATION AND DETAILS SECTION OF THE FINAL SECTION

INCLUDE, AS APPLICABLE, (1) STATUS OF MILITARY PROTECTIVE ORDER(S) (MPO'S) 
(E.G., ISSUANCE, DURATION, TERMINATION, VIOLATION, ETC.).  (2) STATUS OF 
TRANSFER/REASSIGNMENT (PERMANENT AND/OR TEMPORARY) OF VICTIM(S) AND/OR 
OFFENDER(S).  (3) STATUS OF VICTIM'S COLLATERAL MISCONDUCT INVESTIGATION 
AND/OR DISPOSITION (IF APPLICABLE).  (4) INFORMATION PERTINENT TO THE ONGOING 
INCIDENT RESPONSE.

REVISED AND AUTOMATED AND IS NOW A ONE-TIME REPORT REQUIRED WITHIN TEN DAYS OF

HTTPS://NAVALFORMS.DAPS.DLA.MIL/ THAT THE REPORTING COMMAND WILL COMPLETE AND
