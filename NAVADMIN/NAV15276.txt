SUBJ/2015 NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM/PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR COMMUNITY 
SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS//

RMKS/1.  Each year the Navy recognizes shore, sea and overseas commands for 
Project Good Neighbor.

2.  The Project Good Neighbor Award recognizes the best year-round, volunteer 
supported program or special project that promotes outreach activities to 
establish and restore hope to the community.

3.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2015 Navy Project Good 
Neighbor Community Service Flag Award winners:
    a.  Shore commands category:
        (1) Small - Surface Combat System Center
  (2) Medium - Navy Medicine Professional Development Center
	  (3) Large - Naval Air Technical Training Center
    b.  Sea commands category:
        (1) Medium - Helicopter Sea Combat Squadron 23
        (2) Large - Naval Mobile Construction Battalion Eleven
    c.  Overseas command category:
        (1) Small - Fleet Activities Chinhae
        (2) Large - Naval Air Station Sigonella

4.  My sincere thanks to all for your continued support of the Navy's 
Community Service Program.  Please accept my personal Bravo Zulu and thanks 
to the caring and dedicated Sailors, civilians, and families who selflessly 
volunteered and contributed in improving the quality of life within your 
communities.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral R. L. Thomas, Director, Navy Staff.//
