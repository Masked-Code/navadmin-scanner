RMKS/1.  This message announces the 2016 planner level review of the NSF
MPV-P MMWG.  The review, hosted by OPNAV N462, will be held 19-21 April 2016
(0800-1600) at the Washington Navy Yard, building 111, room 307B-Shore
Support Team.

2.  Background and purpose.  The MPV-P is the CNO model used to determine 
posts required to meet protection requirements, associated staffing, and
resource options ref (a).  The current version of MPV-P is 3.1.  This MMWG
will verify existing metrics and approve new metrics.

3.  All participants are required to send the following information to the
above listed POC: rank/name, duty position, unit command, email address,
and telephone number.  The MMWG planner level review is limited to 25
participants.

4.  Uniform for participants at the MPV-P review is as follows: service khaki 
(active duty) or business casual for civilian.  Attendees are responsible for 
hotel reservations and transportation.  The current per diem rate for the 
Washington Metropolitan area can be found at www.gsa.gov. Washington Reagan 
National Airport is the closest to the Washington Navy Yard.  The Navy 
Commercial Lodging Program can be reached at 1-877-NAVY-BED (1-877-628-9233) 
which provides reservations for hotels convenient to the Washington Navy 
Yard.

5.  Released by VADM P.H. Cullom, N4.//
