SUBJ/FY11 NATIONAL SECURITY MANAGEMENT PROGRAM//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN SOLICITS APPLICATIONS FOR THE NATIONAL SECURITY 
MANAGEMENT PROGRAM (NSMP).  APPLICATIONS ARE DUE NO LATER THAN 24 SEPTEMBER 
2010 FOR THE FY11 SELECTION BOARD.

2.  NSMP IS A DOD-SPONSORED FOUR-WEEK COURSE OFFERED AT GEORGE WASHINGTON 
UNIVERSITY FOCUSING ON VARIOUS ASPECTS OF NATIONAL SECURITY POLICY.  
PARTICIPANTS WILL ATTEND THE SENIOR MANAGER COURSE IN NATIONAL SECURITY.  THE 
COURSE CONVENING MONTH HAS NOT BEEN RELEASED.  HISTORICALLY, THE COURSE HAS 
BEEN OFFERED IN MARCH/APRIL OR JUNE.

3.  APPLICANTS MUST BE TOP-PERFORMING ACTIVE-DUTY, OR FULL TIME SUPPORT ON THE 
ACTIVE DUTY LIST, UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) CAPTAINS OR CAPTAIN-SELECTS WHO HOLD 
A MASTER'S DEGREE OR PROVEN SUBSPECIALTY.  PARTICIPANTS WILL INCUR TWO YEARS 
OBLIGATED SERVICE UPON COMPLETION OF THE COURSE.

4.  REPORTING SENIORS SHOULD SUBMIT NOMINATIONS IN THE FOLLOWING FORMAT TO 
CENTER FOR PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT (CPPD) VOLUNTARY EDUCATION 
DETACHMENT, ATTN:  CODE N2A2NSMP 6490 SAUFLEY FIELD ROAD, PENSACOLA, FL 32509-
5204:
    A.  NOMINEE RANK, NAME, SSN/DESIGNATOR;
    B.  COMMAND ADDRESS, TELEPHONE (DSN AND COMM), E-MAIL ADDRESS, AND AN 
ALTERNATE E-MAIL ADDRESS AND TELEPHONE NUMBER FOR THE NOMINEE FOR CONTACT 
PURPOSES;
    C.  NOMINEE LEVEL OF EDUCATION ATTAINED;
    D.  NOMINEE OPERATIONAL, TECHNICAL, AND MANAGERIAL SPECIALTIES AND 
SUBSPECIALTY CODES, AND A LIST OF SIGNIFICANT ASSIGNMENTS AS COMMANDING 
OFFICER, PROJECT OFFICER, OR PROVEN SUBSPECIALIST;
    E.  NOMINEE FUTURE POTENTIAL, INCLUDING SUGGESTIONS FOR SPECIFIC 
CATEGORIES OF ASSIGNMENT;
    F.  REPORTING SENIOR STATEMENT AFFIRMING NOMINEE'S AVAILABILITY TO ATTEND 
THE COURSE, IF SELECTED;
    G.  NOMINEE STATEMENT INDICATING HOW PARTICIPATION WILL BENEFIT THE NAVY 
AND THE NOMINEE; AND
    H.  NOMINEE ONE-PAGE BIOGRAPHY.

5.  SELECTEES WILL RECEIVE FULL TUITION, ROOM AND BOARD, AND TRAVEL FUNDING.

6.  POINT OF CONTACT:  MS. BILLIE COLONNA, CPPD N2A2, AT (850) 452- 1001, MENU 
OPTION 3, EXT 2219/DSN 922 OR VIA E-MAIL AT BILLIE.COLONNA(AT)NAVY.MIL.

7.  RELEASED BY MS. STEFFANIE EASTER, DEPUTY N1.//
