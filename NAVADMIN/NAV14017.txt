RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the winners of the 2013 Community Service 
Health, Safety, and Fitness Flagship Awards.  Each year the Navy recognizes 
various shore, sea, and overseas commands for outstanding community service 
projects by presenting awards in five flagship sponsor categories:  Personal 
Excellence Partnership; Health, Safety, and Fitness; Project Good Neighbor; 
Campaign Drug Free; and Environmental Stewardship.

2.  It is with great pleasure that I announce the 2013 Community Service 
Health, Safety, and Fitness Flagship Award Winning Commands:
    a.  Small Overseas Winner:  Fleet Activities Chinhae.
    b.  Small Overseas Honorable Mention:  Navy Munitions Command Guam.
    c.  Large Overseas Winner:  U.S. Naval Hospital Guam.
    d.  Small Sea Winner:  21st Dental Company Kaneohe Bay, HI.
    e.  Large Sea Winner:  USS GEORGE H. W. BUSH (CVN 77).
    f.  Small Shore Winner:  Navy Operational Support Center Battle Creek, 
Augusta, MI.
    g.  Small Shore Honorable Mention:  Afloat Training Group Middle Pacific, 
Pearl Harbor, HI.
    h.  Small Shore Honorable Mention:  Surface Combat Systems Detachment, 
Wallops Island, VA.
    i.  Medium Shore Winner:  Center for Naval Aviation Technical Training 
Unit Keesler, Keesler AFB, MS.
    j.  Medium Shore Honorable Mention:  Navy and Marine Corps Intelligence 
Training Center, Virginia Beach, VA.
    k.  Medium Shore Honorable Mention:  Navy Medicine Professional 
Development Center, Bethesda, MD.
    l.  Large Shore Winner:  Naval Air Station, Jacksonville, FL.
    m.  Large Shore Honorable Mention:  Naval Base, San Diego, CA.
    n.  Large Shore Honorable Mention:  Training Support Center, Great Lakes, 
IL.

3.  Congratulations to all who participated in noteworthy efforts, leading to 
this recognition.  I offer my sincere appreciation for your personal 
commitment and continued support of our Navy's Community Service Program.

4.  All award winners and honorable mentions will receive certificates from 
the flag sponsor, Commander, Naval Education and Training Command, in 
recognition of their exceptional accomplishments.

5.  Point of contact is Mr. Keith A. Washington, NETPDTC N7, at
(850) 473-6377/DSN 753 or via e-mail at keith.a.washington1(at)navy.mil.

6.  This NAVADMIN will remain in effect until superseded or 31 December 2014, 
whichever occurs first.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
