SUBJ/APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR NUCLEAR QUALIFIED SAILORS FOR THE FY14 
NUCLEAR LIMITED DUTY OFFICER (LDO) IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT BOARD//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE REQUIREMENT FOR ALL NUCLEAR TRAINED 
PERSONNEL INTENDING TO SUBMIT APPLICATIONS FOR THE FY14 LDO IN-SERVICE 
PROCUREMENT BOARD TO STATE THEIR INTENTIONS USING NAVPERS FORM 1306/7 
(ENLISTED PERSONNEL ACTION REQUEST) TO THE NUCLEAR OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGER 
(OPNAV N133C) VIA THEIR CHAIN OF COMMAND NO LATER THAN 30 JUNE 2012.  THE 
PURPOSE OF THIS REQUIREMENT IS TO ENSURE THAT THE COMMUNITY MANAGER IS AWARE 
OF THE NUMBER OF NUCLEAR QUALIFIED SAILORS SUBMITTING LDO APPLICATIONS AND CAN 
ADEQUATELY PLAN FOR SUBSEQUENT PERSONNEL MANNING PROFILES AS THE 1 OCTOBER IN-
SERVICE PROCUREMENT BOARD SUBMISSION DEADLINE IS APPROACHED.  

2.  1306/7 SUBMISSIONS SHOULD INCLUDE ALL REQUIRED INFORMATION EXCEPT 
"MILITARY SPOUSE DATA", "FAMILY MEMBER DATA", AND "INDIVIDUAL'S OFFICIAL 
DATA".  COMMENTS IN THE "COMMAND ENDORSEMENT" SECTION ARE NOT REQUIRED; 
HOWEVER, COMMANDING OFFICER SIGNATURE IS REQUIRED.  THE "REQUESTED ACTION" 
SECTION SHOULD STATE "INTEND TO SUBMIT NUCLEAR LDO APPLICATION FOR THE FY14 
IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT 
BOARD".

3.  COMMANDS SHOULD SUBMIT AN ENDORSED 1306/7 VIA EMAIL TO 
NXAG_N133C(AT)NAVY.MIL OR VIA REGULAR MAIL TO:  
 
    OPNAV N133 
    701 S. COURTHOUSE RD 
    ARLINGTON, VA 22204-2472

4.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR T. J. NETHERCOTT, N133C, (703) 604-5490 OR VIA  
E-MAIL AT NXAG_N133C(AT)NAVY.MIL.

5.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
