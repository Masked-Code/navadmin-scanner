SUBJ/EXTENSION OF ENLISTED SUPERVISOR RETENTION PAY (ESRP) PROGRAM
/TO ZONE D//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE ENLISTED SUPERVISOR RETENTION PAY PROGRAM IS
NOW EXPANDED TO INCLUDE SUPERVISORS FROM SELECT RATINGS IN ZONE D
(16-20 YEARS OF SERVICE (YOS)).
2.  ZONE D SAILORS WHO HOLD ESRP-DESIGNATED NEC S AND HAVE OBLIGATED
SERVICE LESS THAN 20 YOS, MAY BE ELIGIBLE FOR COMPENSATION FOR ZONE
D REENLISTMENT.  ZONE D REENLISTMENTS MUST EXTEND OBLIGATED SERVICE
A MINIMUM OF TWO YEARS PAST CURRENT END OF ACTIVE OBLIGATED SERVICE
(EAOS); HOWEVER, AWARD CALCULATION WILL BE BASED SOLELY ON CONTRACT
TIME UP TO THE 20TH YOS ANNIVERSARY DATE.
3.  THIS MESSAGE SUPERSEDES THE AWARD LEVELS PROMULGATED IN NAVADMIN
195/05, AND ESTABLISHES NEW ESRP AWARD LEVELS EFFECTIVE THE DATE OF
THIS NAVADMIN FOR SAILORS IN ZONES C AND D WHO HOLD ESRP-DESIGNATED
NEC S.  FUTURE AWARD LEVEL CHANGES WILL CONTINUE TO BE EFFECTIVE ON
THE DATE OF THE NAVADMIN RELEASE, INCLUDING ANY DECREASES IN AWARD
LEVELS.  ESRP-ELIGIBLE SAILORS AND CAREER COUNSELORS ARE ENCOURAGED
TO REVIEW THE REVISED CNO (N13) ESRP POLICY MEMORANDUM OF 1 MAR
06, WHICH CAN BE FOUND ON THE NUCLEAR ENLISTED COMMUNITY MANAGER'S
BUPERS WEBSITE AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/ENLISTED/COMMUNITYMANAGERS/
NUCLEAR/ESRP_SRB.HTM.
4.  ESRP ELIGIBLE PERSONNEL WHO HAVE PRIOR APPROVED ESRP
REENLISTMENT AUTHORITY, AS OF THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF THIS NAVADMIN,
WILL BE AUTHORIZED TO REENLIST UNDER THE CURRENT APPROVAL MESSAGE.
PERSONNEL WHO HAVE NOT YET REENLISTED, AND WHO WOULD BENEFIT FROM
REENLISTMENT UNDER THE NEW ESRP AWARD LEVELS, MUST CANCEL THEIR
EXISTING ESRP REQUEST AND RESUBMIT A NEW ESRP REENLISTMENT REQUEST.
5.  ESRP REENLISTMENT REQUESTS SHOULD BE SUBMITTED 35-120 DAYS IN
ADVANCE OF THE MEMBERS DESIRED REENLISTMENT DATE, TO ENSURE THE
APPROVAL MESSAGE REACHES THE MEMBER'S COMMAND IN A TIMELY MANNER.
    A.  THE OFFICER PERSONNEL INFORMATION SYSTEM (OPINS) IS
PROGRAMMED TO PROCESS ESRP REENLISTMENT REQUESTS, AND EMPLOYS THE
SAME PROCESS USED TO REQUEST AND APPROVE SRB REENLISTMENT REQUESTS.
THE ESRP REQUEST TYPE IS 3AA FOR SAILORS ON ACTIVE DUTY, AND 3BB FOR
BROKEN SERVICE REQUESTS.
    B.  A SAILOR MUST BE PRE-APPROVED FOR AN ESRP REENLISTMENT.
PAYMENTS SHOULD POST TO THE SAILOR'S ACCOUNT WITHIN FOURTEEN DAYS
FOLLOWING THE DAY THE ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER RECEIVES NOTIFICATION OF
REENLISTMENT.  THE ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER COORDINATES WITH DEFENSE
FINANCE AND ACCOUNTING SERVICE (DFAS) FOR EACH MEMBER'S PAYMENT.
PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS MUST CONTACT THE ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER
AS THE POC, VICE DFAS, TO RESOLVE ANY PAYMENT ISSUES.  INITIAL
PAYMENT WILL BE AUTHORIZED AS SOON AS THE CONTRACT EXECUTION IS
REFLECTED IN THE SAILORS ENLISTED MASTER FILE (EMF).  IN ORDER TO
ENSURE PROMPT PAYMENT, IT IS IMPERATIVE THAT NEW CONTRACTS ARE
POSTED TO THE EMF IN A TIMELY MANNER.
    C.  A SAILOR WITH AN APPROVED PRE-CERTIFICATION, WHO DOES NOT
REENLIST ON THE DATE SPECIFIED IN THE PRE-CERTIFICATION MESSAGE,
MUST RESUBMIT A REQUEST TO REVISE THE REENLISTMENT DATE AND AWARD
AMOUNT.  A REVISED APPROVAL MESSAGE WILL BE SENT TO THE SAILOR'S
COMMAND.

NOTES.
(1) PERSONNEL IN ZONES A OR B ARE CURRENTLY INELIGIBLE FOR ESRP, AND

(2) PERSONNEL IN ZONES C OR D WHO DO NOT HOLD A SUPERVISOR NEC, OR
