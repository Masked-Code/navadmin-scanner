NAVADMIN 102/15

SUBJ/SUBMARINE ACQUISITION CORPS ELIGIBLE SELECTION BOARD//

MSGID/GENADMIN/CNO WASHINGTON DC/N1/APR//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the next Submarine Acquisition 
Corps (AC) eligible selection board the week of 31 August 2015.  AC eligible 
opportunities are available for command screened officers in year groups 94, 
95, 96 and 97.

2.  AC eligible candidates must submit their applications via e-mail to the 
AC community manager no later than 15 July 2015.

3.  Application guidance and AC candidate information sheets may be obtained 
by contacting the AC community manager.

4.  Point of contact is CAPT Doug Adams, AC Community Manager, at (202) 781-
4907 or via e-mail at douglas.j.adams(at)navy.mil.

5.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or 30 September 2015, 
whichever comes first.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
