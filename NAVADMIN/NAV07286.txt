RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN OUTLINES THE RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE MTT HOST 
SITES AND PROVIDES THE FY-08 MTT AND RESIDENT TRAINING AREA SCHEDULE 
WITH POINTS OF CONTACT.  TO MAXIMIZE THE AVAILABILITY OF TRAINING 
OPPORTUNITIES FOR SITES LOCATED MORE THAN 50 MILES FROM A RESIDENT 
TRAINING SITE, THE CMEO MANAGER COURSE IS OFFERED VIA MTT AND THE CTTI 
COURSE IS OFFERED VIA MTT AND VIDEO TELE-TRAINING.  QUOTAS FOR THE CMEO 
MANAGER AND CTTI COURSES SHALL BE OBTAINED THROUGH THE ENTERPRISE NAVAL 
TRAINING RESERVATION SYSTEM AT HTTPS://ENTRS.CHAMB.DISA.MIL AND REQUEST 
CLASS QUOTA USING THE COURSE DATA PROCESSING (CDP) CODES LISTED IN PARA 
4.  COMMANDS CAN ALSO VIEW FY-08 SCHEDULE VIA THE CATALOG OF NAVY 
TRAINING COURSES (CANTRAC).  REQUESTS FOR ADDITIONAL TRAINING OUTSIDE 
DATES LISTED BELOW SHOULD BE DIRECTED TO THE POINT OF CONTACT AT THE 
SPECIFIC LEARNING SITE. 
FUTURE CMEO/CTTI MTT AND RESIDENT TRAINING SCHEDULES WILL NOT BE 
DISSEMINATED VIA NAVADMIN, BUT WILL, AS ALWAYS, BE AVAILABLE IN CANTRAC.
2.  PER REF A, ALL COMMANDS ARE NOW REQUIRED TO ESTABLISH A COMMAND 
TRAINING TEAM (CTT).  REQUIRED TRAINING FOR THE CTT CAN BE OBTAINED 
THROUGH THE CENTER FOR PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT (CPPD) 
CTTI COURSES LISTED BELOW.
3.  SITES HOSTING A CMEO MANAGER/CTTI MTT SHALL PROVIDE THE FOLLOWING 
SUPPORT (IF HOST SITES ARE UNABLE TO MEET THESE ACCOMMODATIONS, THEY 
SHALL NOTIFY THE LEARNING SITE PROVIDING THE TRAINING AT LEAST TWO 
WEEKS PRIOR TO THE SCHEDULED MTT):
    A.  STORAGE AREA FOR COURSE REFERENCE MATERIALS
    B.  COMPUTER WORKSTATION WITH CAPABILITY TO PROJECT POWER POINT 
SLIDES
    C.  TWO EASELS WITH CHARTPACK PAPER AND MARKERS
    D.  VHS PLAYER WITH MONITOR
    E.  WALL PROJECTOR SCREEN
    F.  115V AC POWER OUTLET/VOLTAGE CONVERTER
    G.  APPROXIMATELY 1,000 SQ FT DEDICATED CLASSROOM; CHAIRS FOR 25
