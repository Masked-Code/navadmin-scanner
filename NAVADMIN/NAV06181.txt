GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN SOLICITS APPLICATIONS FOR THE
NATIONAL SECURITY MANAGEMENT PROGRAM (NSMP).  APPLICATIONS ARE DUE
NLT 30 SEP 06 FOR THE FY07 SELECTION BOARD.
2.  NSMP IS A DOD-SPONSORED SIX-WEEK COURSE OFFERED AT THE MAXWELL
SCHOOL OF SYRACUSE UNIVERSITY THAT FOCUSES ON VARIOUS ASPECTS OF
NATIONAL SECURITY POLICY.  PARTICIPANTS EXAMINE ELEMENTS OF POLITICS,
GLOBAL ECONOMICS, ETHICS AND LAW, INTERAGENCY COORDINATION, AND
COLLECTIVE DEFENSE ARRANGEMENTS.  PARTICIPANTS WILL ATTEND THE COURSE
DURING THE MAR-MAY 07 TIMEFRAME.  MORE INFORMATION ABOUT THE COURSE
CAN BE FOUND AT THE NATIONAL SECURITY STUDIES WEBSITE AT WWW.NSS.EDU.
3.  APPLICANTS MUST BE TOP PERFORMING UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL)
CAPTAINS AND CAPTAINS SELECT WHO HOLD A MASTER'S DEGREE OR PROVEN
SUBSPECIALTY.  PARTICIPANTS WILL INCUR TWO YEARS OBLIGATED SERVICE
UPON COMPLETION OF THE COURSE.
4.  REPORTING SENIORS SHOULD SUBMIT NOMINATIONS IN THE FOLLOWING
FORMAT TO:  COMMANDING OFFICER, NAVAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER, ATTN:  CODE N2A2,
6490 SAUFLEY FIELD ROAD, PENSACOLA, FL 32509-5204:
    A.  RANK, NAME, SSN/DESIGNATOR OF NOMINEE.
    B.  COMMAND ADDRESS, TELEPHONE (DSN AND COMM), AND E-MAIL
ADDRESS.
    C.  LEVEL OF EDUCATION ATTAINED.
    D.  OPERATIONAL, TECHNICAL, AND MANAGERIAL SPECIALTIES AND
SUBSPECIALTY CODES, AND A LIST OF SIGNIFICANT ASSIGNMENTS AS
COMMANDING OFFICER, PROJECT OFFICER, OR PROVEN SUBSPECIALIST.
    E.  FUTURE POTENTIAL, INCLUDING SUGGESTIONS FOR SPECIFIC
CATEGORIES OF ASSIGNMENT.
    F.  STATEMENT AFFIRMING THE NOMINEE WILL BE AVAILABLE TO ATTEND
THE COURSE, IF SELECTED.
    G.  STATEMENT FROM NOMINEE INDICATING HOW HIS/HER PARTICIPATION
WILL BENEFIT THE NAVY AND THE NOMINEE.
5.  SELECTEES WILL RECEIVE FULL TUITION, ROOM AND BOARD, AND TRAVEL
FUNDING.
6.  RELEASED BY VADM J. K. MORAN, COMMANDER, NAVAL EDUCATION AND
TRAINING COMMAND.//
