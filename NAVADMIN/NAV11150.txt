SUBJ/2010 NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM (NCSP)/PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR 
COMMUNITY SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS//

RMKS/1.  EACH YEAR THE NAVY RECOGNIZES SHORE, SEA AND OVERSEAS COMMANDS FOR 
OUTSTANDING COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECTS BY PRESENTING AWARDS IN FIVE FLAGSHIP 
CATEGORIES:  PERSONAL EXCELLENCE PARTNERSHIP; HEALTH, SAFETY, AND FITNESS; 
CAMPAIGN DRUG FREE; PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR; AND ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP.

2.  THE PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR AWARD RECOGNIZES THE BEST YEAR-ROUND VOLUNTEER-
SUPPORTED PROGRAM OR SPECIAL PROJECT THAT PROMOTES OUTREACH ACTIVITIES 
THROUGHOUT THE YEAR TO ESTABLISH AND RESTORE HOPE TO THE COMMUNITY.

3.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE TO ANNOUNCE THE 2010 NAVY PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR 
COMMUNITY SERVICE FLAG AWARD WINNERS:
    A.  SHORE COMMANDS CATEGORY:
- SMALL - CENTER FOR SEABEES AND FACILITIES ENGINEERING DETACHMENT, SHEPPARD 
AIR FORCE BASE, TEXAS
- MEDIUM - NAVAL AIR STATION, JOINT RESERVE BASE, WILLOW GROVE, PA
- LARGE - NAVAL SUBMARINE BASE, KINGS BAY, GA
    B.  SEA COMMANDS CATEGORY:
- SMALL - USS MARYLAND (SSBN 738) BLUE
- MEDIUM - USS COWPENS (CG 63)
- LARGE - USS BLUE RIDGE (CC 19)
    C.  OVERSEAS COMMANDS CATEGORY:
- LOGISTICS GROUP WESTERN PACIFIC, SINGAPORE

4.  MY SINCEREST THANKS TO ALL FOR THEIR CONTINUED SUPPORT OF THE NAVY'S 
COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM.  PLEASE ACCEPT MY PERSONAL BRAVO ZULU AND THANKS TO 
THE CARING AND DEDICATED SAILORS, CIVILIANS, AND FAMILIES WHO SELFLESSLY 
VOLUNTEERED AND CONTRIBUTED IN IMPROVING THE QUALITY LIFE IN THEIR 
COMMUNITIES.

5.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
