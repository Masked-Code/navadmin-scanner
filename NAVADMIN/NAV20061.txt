RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces a modification to paragraph 3 of reference 
(a) and the Fiscal Year 2021 Active-Duty Officer Continuation Plan.  The 
modification expands the continuation plan for the nurse corps (NC) to allow 
all 2X failure of selection (FOS) lieutenants (LT) regardless of their 
subspecialty to be continued on Active Duty until retirement eligibility or 
for a period of 3 years.

2.  Paragraph 3 is corrected to read:  LT Unrestricted Line (URL)
(1310 and 1320 with primary additional qualification designators
(AQD) of DA5/DA7/DB2/DB5/DB6 (VFA), DD1/DD2 (VAQ), DL3 (VAW) and DS2 (VRC)), 
chaplain corps (CHC), public affairs officers (PAO), cyber warfare engineers 
(CWE), cryptologic warfare (CW), information professionals (IP), intelligence 
(INTEL), engineering duty officer (EDO), medical service corps (MSC) and NC:
    a.  2XFOS URL (1310 and 1320 with primary AQDs of
DA5/DA7/DB2/DB5/DB6 (VFA), DD1/DD2 (VAQ), DL3 (VAW) and DS2 (VRC)), CHC, PAO, 
CWE, CW, IP, INTEL, EDO, MSC and NC LTs with less than 18 years of Active 
service (YOAS).  Officers selected for continuation will be continued for a 
period of 3 years, but not beyond retirement eligibility at 20 YOAS.
    b.  2XFOS URL (1310 and 1320 with primary AQDs of
DA5/DA7/DB2/DB5/DB6 (VFA), DD1/DD2 (VAQ), DL3 (VAW), and DS2 (VRC)), CHC, 
PAO, CWE, CW, IP, INTEL, EDO, MSC and NC LTs with 18 or more YOAS, as 
provided by law, will not be considered for continuation but will be retained 
on Active Duty until retirement eligibility at 20 YOAS.
    c.  In no case will any URL (1310 and 1320 with primary AQDs of
DA5/DA7/DB2/DB5/DB6 (VFA), DD1/DD2 (VAQ), DL3 (VAW), and DS2 (VRC)), CHC, 
PAO, CWE, CW, IP, INTEL, EDO, MSC or NC LTs be continued or retained beyond 
the last day of the month in which the officer becomes eligible for 
retirement under section 8323 of title 10, U.S. Code, or the last day of the 
month in which the officer becomes 62 years of age, whichever is earlier.

3.  All other provisions of reference (a) remain in effect.

4.  This NAVADMIN will be canceled for record purposes on 30 September 2020.

5.  Released by Vice Admiral John B. Nowell, Jr, N1.//
