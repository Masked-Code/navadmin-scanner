RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces that continuation of authority granted in 
references (a) through (f) and provides continuation of references (g) 
through (l) for FY-15.  For FY-15, references (a) through (l) remain 
unchanged.

2.  Submission for FY-15 special pays may begin upon release of this NAVADMIN 
in accordance with reference (f).  Officers will have 30 days from the 
release date of this NAVADMIN to submit requests for special pays back to 1 
October 2014.

3.  Medical Corps (MC), Dental Corps (DC), Multi-year Special Pay
(MSP) and Incentive Special Pay (ISP) requests must be routed through the 
privileging authority for endorsement.  Waivers for MC and DC MSP, ISP, and 
dental officer multi-year retention bonus, where applicable, must be endorsed 
by the respective corps chief and approved by Bureau of Medicine and Surgery 
(BUMED).

4.  Specific special pays implementation guidance, administrative procedures 
and BUMED special pays point of contact information are available at 
http://www.med.navy.mil/bumed/special_pay/pages/default.aspx.
All personnel eligible for medical department officer special pays are 
strongly encouraged to review their corps* specific guidance located at the 
aforementioned link.

5.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or canceled, 
whichever occurs first.
