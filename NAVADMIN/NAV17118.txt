RMKS/1.  THIS MESSAGE APPLIES TO ALL ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE AND DON CIVILIAN 
PERSONNEL.  ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL REGIONAL COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE 
WIDEST DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND TENANT COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS 
FOR IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT.

2.  THE FOLLOWING GEOGRAPHIC AREA OF INTEREST (GAOI) HAS BEEN ESTABLISHED FOR 
THE SIMULATED DISASTER IN CONJUNCTION WITH HURREX-CG 17/HURRICANE BRADY:

A. THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES WITHIN THE STATE OF NORTH CAROLINA:
BEAUFORT, BERTIE, BLADEN, BRUNSWICK, CARTERET, CHOWAN, COLUMBUS, CRAVEN, 
DUPLIN, GATES, GREEN, HERTFORD, JONES, LENOIR, MARTIN, NEW HANOVER, ONSLOW, 
PAMLICO, PENDER, PITT, TYRRELL, AND WASHINGTON.
B. THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES WITHIN THE STATE OF VIRGINIA:
ACCOMACK, ARLINGTON, CHESAPEAKE, FAIRFAX, GLOUCESTER, HAMPTON, ISLE OF WIGHT, 
JAMES CITY, KING GEORGE, MATHEWS, MIDDLESEX, NEWPORT NEWS, NORFOLK, 
NORTHAMPTON, POQUOSON, PORTSMOUTH, PRINCE GEORGE, SOUTHAMPTON, STAFFORD, 
SUFFOLK, SURRY, SUSSEX, VIRGINIA BEACH, AND YORK.
C. THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA.
D. THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES WITHIN THE STATE OF MARYLAND:
ANNE ARUNDEL, BALTIMORE, BALTIMORE CITY, CALVERT, CAROLINE, CECIL, CHARLES, 
DORCHESTER, HARFORD, KENT, QUEEN ANNE, SOMERSET, ST.MARY'S, TALBOT, WICOMICO, 
AND WORCESTER.
E. THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES WITHIN THE STATE OF DELAWARE: 
KENT, NEW CASTLE, AND SUSSEX.
F. THE FOLLOWING COUNTIES WITHIN THE STATE OF NEW JERSEY: 
ATLANTIC, BERGEN, BURLINGTON, CAPE MAY, CUMBERLAND, ESSEX, HUDSON, MIDDLESEX, 
MONMOUTH, OCEAN, PASSAIC, SALEM, SUSSEX, AND UNION.

3.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO:

A.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL AFFECTED NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON 
CIVILIAN PERSONNEL AND APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS IN THE GAOI AFTER STORM 
PASSAGE/CPA.  ALL MEMBERS IN THE AFFECTED GAOI WILL RECEIVE NOTIFICATION FROM 
NFAAS VIA TEXT/EMAIL TO MUSTER THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN NAVY FAMILIY 
ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT SYSTEM (NFAAS) AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA. 
COMMANDING OFFICERS (COS) AND/OR COMMANDING OFFICERS REPRESENTATIVES (CORS) 
WILL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION OF REQUIREMENT FOR INDIVIDUAL SPONSOR MUSTER 
IN NFAAS. IN CONJUCTION WITH INDIVIDUAL SPONSOR MUSTER, COS/CORS WILL 
DETERMINE ACCURATE STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF THEIR PERSONNEL AND MUSTER 
(PERSONNEL NOT MUSTERED IN NFAAS 24 HOURS AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA) AND 
ACCOUNT FOR THEM AT HOME, WORK, TELEWORK, EVACUATED, OR DISPLACED LOCATION, 
OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, TDY/TAD/IA, ON 
LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER).
B.  MEMBERS, COS AND/OR CORS UTILIZE THE NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND 
ASSESSMENT SYSTEM (NFAAS) SUPPORT SITE AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL TO 
ACCOUNT FOR EACH INDIVIDUAL REQUIRED TO BE REPORTED PER REFS A AND B. 
COMMANDS SHALL DIRECT MEMBERS TO INDIVIDUALLY MUSTER IN NFAAS.
C.  IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, COMMANDS ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS OF PERSONNEL 
ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) TO THE AFFECTED REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) OR DIRECT 
CHAIN OF COMMAND.
D.  DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS COMPLETE.  
REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED INCLUDING THE POSTING 
OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES AREA ON THE INDIVIDUALS ACCOUNTING LINE.

4.  ALL INDIVIDUALS(ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON CIVILIAN PERSONNEL) ARE 
EXPECTED TO:
A.  MUSTER THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN NFAAS AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA VIA 
NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL
B.  IF UNABLE TO ACCESS NFAAS; ACTIVE, RESERVE AND DON CIVILIAN PERSONNEL 
SHOULD CONTACT THEIR COMMAND MUSTERING OFFICER OR SUPERVISOR TO CHECK IN 
(MUSTER).
C.  INDIVIDUALS WILL PROVIDE COMMAND REPRESENTATIVES WITH CURRENT RESIDENCE, 
DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE (DEPLOYED, 
TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, OTHER).
D.  FAMILY MEMBERS CAN LOG ON TO NFAAS AT HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL AND 
CHECK IN (MUSTER) ON THE NAVY FAMILY MEMBERS SITE.  IF UNABLE TO LOG ON TO 
NFAAS, CALL THE NFAAS HELP DESK AT 866-946-9183.

5.  THERE WILL NOT BE A NEEDS ASSESSMENT CONDUCTED FOR THIS EXERCISE.
