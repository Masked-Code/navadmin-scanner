SUBJ/NAVY KNOWLEDGE ONLINE SUNSET//

RMKS/1.  With the successful launch of My Navy Portal (MNP), the Navy will 
sunset Navy Knowledge Online (NKO) on 14 April 2017 and all NKO users will be 
redirected to MNP at https://my.navy.mil for logon.

2.  Navy e-Learning courses (also known as NKO courses) can now be found by 
going to the MNP top navigation menu, hovering over Professional Resources 
and clicking Navy e-Learning/Online Courses.  Electronic Training Jacket will 
be found by hovering over My Record.  Navy e-Library resources are found at
https://mwrdigitallibrary.navy.mil/.

3.  Communities of Practice (COP), including Navy Advancement Center, General 
Skills Training, Personnel Qualification Standards, Career Management, and 
Learning and Development Roadmaps (LaDRs), are accessed by hovering over 
Professional Resources or Organizations.  Users will also find a COP sitemap 
by hovering over Organizations, selecting Communities of Practice and then 
clicking Sitemap.

4.  Navy organizations offering hyperlinks to NKO need to update and point 
users to MNP at https://my.navy.mil.  Commands currently maintaining a COP 
are directed to ensure their content is current and begin conducting all 
relevant business in MNP.  Training and other helpful resources for COP site 
administrators is available on MNP.  Hover over Support, select MNP Help, and 
then click Administrator Tutorials.  Site administrators may also contact 
MNP_Helpdesk(at)navy.mil for assistance.

5.  More information about MNP can be found on the MNP help tab and on the 
Navy Personnel Command Career Toolbox website at 
http://www.public.navy.mil/bupers-npc/career/toolbox/Pages/default2.aspx

6.  Point of contact is LCDR Eric Smith, OPNAV (N16), who can be reached at 
(703) 604-5747/DSN 664 or via e-mail at eric.s.smith(at)navy.mil.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral, R. P. Burke, N1.//
