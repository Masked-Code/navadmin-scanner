COMMANDER/COMMANDING OFFICER.

COMMAND AND IS THE VEHICLE TO SUPPORT OMBUDSMEN.  OMBUDSMAN ASSEMBLIES 
FUNCTION ONLY UNDER THE SUPERVISION AND GUIDANCE OF THE SPONSORING
