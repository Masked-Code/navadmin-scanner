AMPN/REF A IS FISCAL YEAR 2021 NATIONAL COMMAND AND SENIOR OFFICER COMMANDER 
OR CAPTAIN NON-COMMAND BILLET SCREENING AND ASSIGNMENT PROCEDURES.//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the convening of the Fiscal Year
(FY) 2021 Navy Reserve National Command and Senior Officer (O5/O6) Non-
Command Billet Screening and Assignment (APPLY) Board on 7 December 2020.  
All Selected Reserve officer communities will utilize the APPLY Board process 
for senior officer and command assignments.

2.  Eligible officers desiring a billet assignment are required to complete 
and submit all applicable sections of the APPLY application.  Applications 
must be submitted via the Reserve Forces Manpower Tools (RFMT) website 
available at https://private.navyreserve.navy.mil/apps/rfmtweb/.  Officers 
are encouraged to place the maximum number of billet choices on their dream 
sheet to maximize the opportunity for an assignment.  In addition, officers 
are highly encouraged to note on their applications, their willingness to 
travel for billets not on their dream sheet.  In doing so, will enable the 
board to select the best qualified applicants.

3.  Applications must be finalized with billet preferences made via RFMT and 
submitted no later than (NLT) 26 October 2020.

4.  All correspondence to the board must be received NLT
2 December 2020.  Submission methods are detailed in paragraph 5 below.  Late 
correspondence will not be considered by the board.

5.  Board correspondence can be submitted via one of the following
methods:
    a.  Encrypted e-mail (preferred) to cscselboard(at)navy.mil.
Subject line of email should read: 

        FY-21 Navy Reserve National Command and Senior Officer
        (O5/O6) Non-Command Billet Screening and Assignment (APPLY)
        Board (#391) ICO (Rank, First Name, Last Name, Designator). 

    b.  Standard (US Postal Service) mail (if encrypted e-mail is not 
available) to: 

        Navy Personnel Command Customer Service Center
        FY-21 Navy Reserve National Command and Senior Officer
        (O5/O6) Non-Command Billet Screening and Assignment (APPLY)
        Board (#391)
        5720 Integrity Drive
        Bldg 768, Room E302
        Millington, TN 38055-9200 

    c.  Overnight/Express Mail requiring signature: 

        Navy Personnel Command Customer Service Center
        FY-21 Navy Reserve National Command and Senior Officer
        (O5/O6) Non-Command Billet Screening and Assignment (APPLY)
        Board (#391)
        5640 Ticonderoga Loop
        Millington, TN 38055-9200 

    d.  Members may contact MyNavy Career Center to confirm receipt of their 
package and ask questions concerning supplemental/additional correspondence 
at 833-330-MNCC/833-330-6622, 901-874-MNCC/901-874-6622 or via e-mail at 
askmncc(at)navy.mil.

6.  Selected Reserve Officers must be qualified per reference (a) to be 
considered for an assignment.  Qualified officers must complete and submit a 
dream sheet with at least one billet on their application in RFMT to be 
considered by the APPLY Board for a billet.

7.  APPLY eligible officers who are not affiliated with the Reserve Component 
in a drilling status (e.g. Active Status Pool/Voluntary Participation Unit, 
Recall, Mobilization) are still required to complete and submit a dream sheet 
to participate in APPLY and to be eligible for an Interim Fill assignment 
opportunity.  Officers experiencing difficulty submitting a dream sheet in 
RFMT should contact COMNAVRESFORCOM (N122) at cnrfc_apply_mailbox(at)navy.mil 
for assistance.

8.  APPLY Board assignments are assumed to be accepted by selected officers, 
whereby no further action is required.  Officers who desire to decline 
awarded billets must do so within 7 days following release of official 
results via the RFMT website.

9.  Point of contact:  All APPLY questions or concerns should be addressed to 
COMNAVRESFORCOM (N122) APPLY team via e-mail at 
cnrfc_apply_mailbox(at)navy.mil.

10.  This NAVADMIN will remain in effect until superseded or canceled, 
whichever occurs first.
