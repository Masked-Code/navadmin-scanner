SUBJ/2006 SEA SERVICE LEADERSHIP ASSOCIATION SYMPOSIUM//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE SEA SERVICE LEADERSHIP ASSOCIATION (SSLA),
FORMERLY KNOWN AS THE WOMEN OFFICERS PROFESSIONAL ASSOCIATION, IN
CONJUNCTION WITH THE COAST GUARD WOMENS LEADERSHIP ASSOCIATION, WILL
HOST THEIR 19TH ANNUAL SYMPOSIUM 21-22 JUN 06, AT THE NATIONAL
DEFENSE UNIVERSITY, FORT LESLIE J. MCNAIR, WASHINGTON, DC.  THE
THEME THIS YEAR IS "TRANSFORMATION - A LEADER'S ROLE."  THE
CONFERENCE WILL INCLUDE EDUCATIONAL, PROFESSIONAL, AND TRAINING
DEVELOPMENT WORKSHOPS TO INCLUDE MENTORSHIP, LEADERSHIP DURING THE
GLOBAL WAR ON TERRORISM, DISASTER RESPONSE, AND THE CURRENT NATIONAL
SECURITY PERSONNEL SYSTEM TRANSITION.
2.  THE SSLA IS A PROFESSIONAL ORGANIZATION THAT FOSTERS EDUCATIONAL
AND CAREER DEVELOPMENT AND PROVIDES MENTORING AND NETWORKING
OPPORTUNITIES FOR ACTIVE, RESERVE, CIVILIAN, AND RETIRED MEMBERS OF
THE SEA SERVICES.
3.  ATTENDANCE IS OPEN TO ALL.  COMMANDING OFFICERS ARE ENCOURAGED
TO SUPPORT ATTENDANCE IN ORDER TO MAINTAIN AND PROMOTE PROFESSIONAL
DEVELOPMENT OF COMMAND PERSONNEL.  ISSUANCE OF TAD ORDERS, TO
INCLUDE REGISTRATION, TRANSPORTATION, AND PER DIEM IS ENCOURAGED.
4.  DRESS FOR THE SYMPOSIUM IS SUMMER WHITE OR KHAKI.
5.  FOR ADDITIONAL INFORMATION AND REGISTRATION, VISIT
WWW.SEALEADER.ORG OR WWW.CGWLA.ORG.
6.  POC:  LT DAWN FRANK AT (301) 757-7002/DSN 757 OR EMAIL AT
DAWN.FRANK@NAVY.MIL.
7.  RELEASED BY W. SCOTT SLOCUM, N1/NTB.//
