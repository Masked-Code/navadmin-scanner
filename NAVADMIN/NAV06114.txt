SUBJ/FY-07 MEDICAL SERVICE CORPS (MSC) IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT PROGRAM
/(IPP) SELECTION BOARD//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE FY-07 MEDICAL SERVICE CORPS (MSC) IN-SERVICE
PROCUREMENT PROGRAM (IPP) SELECTION BOARD WILL CONVENE 16 OCTOBER
2006.  MSC IPP PROVIDES A PATHWAY FOR CAREER MOTIVATED ACTIVE DUTY
ENLISTED PERSONNEL WHO MEET ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA TO OBTAIN A
COMMISSION AS AN MSC OFFICER IN HEALTH CARE ADMINISTRATION,
ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH, INDUSTRIAL HYGIENE, ENTOMOLOGY, PHARMACY,
PHYSICIAN ASSISTANT, OR RADIATION HEALTH.
2.  THE IPP PROGRAM IS OPEN TO ACTIVE DUTY ENLISTED SAILORS AND
MARINES, TO INCLUDE FULL-TIME SUPPORT (FTS) AND ACTIVE DUTY
RESERVISTS IN ANY RATING OR MILITARY OCCUPATIONAL SPECIALTY (MOS),
IN PAYGRADES E-5 THROUGH E-9 AT THE TIME OF APPLICATION.  RESERVISTS
IN THE INDIVIDUAL READY RESERVE (IRR) OR SERVING ON ACTIVE DUTY FOR
SPECIAL WORK (ADSW), ACTIVE DUTY FOR TRAINING (ADT), OR 1-3 YEAR
RECALLS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR THIS PROGRAM.
3.  APPLICATION PROCEDURES AND EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS ARE LOCATED IN
OPNAVINST 1420.1A (CHAPTERS 1, 2, AND 6).  APPLICANTS MUST NOT HAVE
REACHED THEIR 42ND BIRTHDAY BY TIME OF INITIAL APPOINTMENT AND
COMMISSIONING.  NO AGE WAIVERS WILL BE GRANTED.
4.  SCHOLASTIC APTITUDE TEST (SAT) OR AMERICAN COLLEGE TEST (ACT)
SCORES ARE REQUIRED FOR ALL APPLICANTS REQUESTING TO COMPLETE A
BACHELOR'S DEGREE USING MILITARY FUNDING, WITH A MINIMUM ACCEPTABLE
SAT SCORE OF 1000 (MINIMUM MATH, 460) OR MINIMUM ACT SCORE OF
42.  GRADUATE RECORD EXAMINATION (GRE) OR GRADUATE MANAGEMENT
ADMINISTATION TEST (GMAT) SCORES ARE REQUIRED FOR ALL OTHER
NON-MASTERS DEGREE PREPARED APPLICANTS, EXCLUDING PHARMACY, WITH A
MINIMUM ACCEPTABLE GRE SCORE OF 1050 OR A GMAT SCORE OF 525.
PHARMACY APPLICANTS MUST SUBMIT PHARMACY COLLEGE ADMISSION TEST
(PCAT) SCORES.
5.  APPLICANTS FOR THE PHYSICIAN ASSISTANT PROGRAM MUST SUBMIT THEIR
OFFICIAL TRANSCRIPTS AND A COMPLETE LIST OF ALL SCHOOLS ATTENDED TO
NAVAL MEDICAL EDUCATION TRAINING COMMAND NOT LATER THAN 7 JUL 2006
FOR DETERMINATION OF ACADEMIC ELIGIBILITY BY THE DEGREE GRANTING
INSTITUTION PRIOR TO THE SUBMISSION OF THEIR APPLICATION.
APPLICANTS WILL BE NOTIFIED OF ELIGIBILITY WELL BEFORE THE
APPLICATION DEADLINE.
6.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE POSTMARKED NOT LATER THAN 25 AUG 2006.
ADDITIONAL OR AMPLIFYING DOCUMENTS MUST BE POSTMARKED NOT LATER THAN
15 SEPT 2006.  SAT/ACT/GRE/GMAT/PCAT OR OTHER SCORES MUST BE
POSTMARKED NOT LATER THAN THE BOARD CONVENING DATE.  DETAILED
APPLICATION INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND AT
HTTP:(SLASH-SLASH)NSHS.MED.NAVY.MIL/MSCIPP/MSCIPP.HTM.
