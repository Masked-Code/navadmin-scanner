RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces the issuance of reference (a) and directs 
the implementation of Tactical Combat Casualty Care (TCCC) Tier 1 All Service 
Member (ASM) training as part of unit pre- deployment training and 
certification.  U.S. Fleet Forces (USFF) and U.S. Pacific Fleet (PACFLT), 
through the type commanders (TYCOM), will ensure their units receive TCCC 
Tier 1 ASM training prior to deployment in line with references (a), (b) and 
(c).

2.  Responsibilities
    a.  USFF and PACFLT:
        (1) Ensure deploying units meet the standardized TCCC Tier 1 ASM 
training certification requirements in line with references (a) and (d).
        (2) Ensure individual training certification completion for TCCC Tier 
1 is documented in the Fleet Training Management and Planning System 
(FLTMPS).
        (3) Maintain primary responsibility for the implementation and 
sustainment of TCCC Tier 1 ASM training.
        (4) Coordinate with Bureau of Medicine and Surgery (BUMED) for 
support with TCCC Tier 1 ASM training needs and requirements.
        (5) In coordination with Commander, Naval Reserve Forces (CNRC), 
provide TCCC Tier 1 ASM training for Reserve Individual Augmentee and 
deploying Reserve Component personnel.
    b.  BUMED:
        (1) Ensure all BUMED trauma skills curriculum development and 
delivery meets current standardized Defense Health Agency TCCC Tier 1 
standards in line with references (a) and (d).
        (2) Provide support for TCCC Tier 1 ASM training via cognizant Navy 
medicine readiness and training commands or units.
        (3) Support USFF and PACFLT in identifying and developing training 
needed for TCCC Tier 1 implementation.

3.  Individual, command training departments/training officers, or learning 
sites are responsible for recording locally completed training in FLTMPS.  
The following course identification number will be used to document 
individual completion of TCCC Tier 1:
B-300-2010 TCCC Tier 1 All Service Member (ASM).

4.  USFF and PACFLT will report completion of TCCC Tier 1 implementation to 
Navy Education, Strategy, Policy and Training Branch (OPNAV N13M7) via the 
point of contact (POC) in paragraph 5.

5.  POC.  LCDR Jonathan Greenberg, OPNAV N13M7, email at 
jonathan.d.greenberg(at)navy.mil.

6.  This NAVADMIN will remain in effect until superseded or cancelled, 
whichever occurs first.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral John B. Nowell, Jr, N1.//
