RMKS/1.  THE CAREER RECRUITER FORCE (CRF) WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1978 TO DEVELOP A 
CADRE OF EXCEPTIONAL RECRUITING MANAGERS TO PROVIDE CONSISTENCY AND LEADERSHIP 
TO THE RECRUITING EFFORT.  THE CRF PROGRAM IS MADE UP OF PROVEN, VOLUNTEER 
RECRUITERS WHO FILL KEY MIDDLE MANAGEMENT BILLETS.  THE SELECTION CRITERIA 
FOCUSES ON PROVEN RECRUITING PERFORMANCE WITH AN EMPHASIS ON EFFECTIVE 
LEADERSHIP, MANAGEMENT, COUNSELING AND TRAINING ABILITIES.

2.  THE CRF PROGRAM IS OPEN TO PERSONNEL WHO ARE CURRENTLY ASSIGNED OR HAVE 
SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED A TOUR IN RECRUITING DUTY WITH NEC CODES 9585, 9586, OR 
9587.  FURTHER ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND APPLICATION PROCEDURES ARE LISTED IN 
REF A.

3.  THE CONVENING DATES FOR THE NEXT THREE SELECTION BOARDS ARE AS
FOLLOWS:
     A.  17 FEBRUARY 2011
     B.  19 MAY 2011
     C.  2 AUGUST 2011

4.  ALL APPLICATIONS ARE REQUIRED TO BE RECEIVED NOT LATER THAN THREE WEEKS 
PRIOR TO THE CONVENING DATE OF A SCHEDULED SELECTION BOARD.  FORWARD PACKAGES 
TO COMMANDER, NAVY RECRUITING COMMAND, 5722 INTEGRITY DRIVE, BLDG 784, 
MILLINGTON, TN 38054-5057 (ATTN:
ASSISTANT CRF PROGRAM MANAGER, N1121A) OR SCAN AND E-MAIL DIRECTLY TO MR. 
RICHARD THOMPSON AT RICHARD.E.THOMPSON2(AT)NAVY.MIL.

5.  ALL CANDIDATES WILL BE NOTIFIED VIA OFFICIAL MESSAGE OF THEIR SELECTION OR 
NON-SELECTION.

6.  SELECTEES CURRENTLY ON RECRUITING DUTY WILL BE CONVERTED IMMEDIATELY TO 
NAVY COUNSELOR (RECRUITER) (NEC-2186).  SELECTEES NOT ON RECRUITING DUTY WILL 
BE IMMEDIATELY ASSIGNED TEMPORARY NEC 2199.
FLEET PERSONNEL SELECTED WILL TAKE CURRENT RATING NAVY-WIDE ADVANCEMENT 
EXAMINATION UNTIL SUCCESSFULLY GRADUATING FROM THE CRF ACADEMY.  ALL SELECTEES 
WILL ATTEND THE CAREER RECRUITER FORCE ACADEMY IN PENSACOLA, FL IN CONJUNCTION 
WITH PERMANENT CHANGE OF STATION (PCS) ORDERS AT THEIR CURRENT PROJECTED 
ROTATION DATE (PRD).

7.  POINT OF CONTACT IS MR. RICHARD THOMPSON, ASSISTANT CRF PROGRAM MANAGER AT 
COM (901)874-9292/DSN 882.

8.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
