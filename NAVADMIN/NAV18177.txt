SUBJ/2018 MRS. SYBIL STOCKDALE OMBUDSMAN OF THE YEAR AWARD RECIPIENTS//

RMKS/1.  I am pleased to announce the selection of five outstanding Ombudsmen 
as the 2018 Mrs. Sybil Stockdale Ombudsman of the Year Award recipients.  
These volunteers exemplify the spirit of Mrs. Sybil Stockdale who supported 
the families of Prisoners of War during her husband, Vice Admiral James Bond 
Stockdale's internment in Southeast Asia.  This award recognizes those who 
serve as inspirational Ombudsmen and embody the ideals of volunteer service 
and commitment to Navy Sailors and their families.

2.  The following Ombudsmen were nominated by their commanding officers for 
serving their command and families with extraordinary, selfless dedication 
and commitment to family readiness.  Congratulations to:

    a.  Tiffany Croshaw, Ombudsman for Coastal Riverine Squadron FOUR (CRS-
4), selected from all sea commands under Commander, U.S. Fleet Forces 
Command.

    b.  Mr. Garrett Zopfi and Mrs. Kumi Bergstraser, Ombudsmen for USS 
FITZGERALD (DDG 62), selected from all sea commands under Commander, U.S. 
Pacific Fleet.

    c.  Mrs. Johnna Johnson, Ombudsman for Commander, U.S. Naval Forces 
Korea/Navy Region Korea selected from all naval shore activities in the Navy.

    d.  Mrs. Barbara Schumaker, Ombudsman for Navy Operational Support Center 
Detroit selected from all Reserve commands under Commander, Navy Reserve 
Force.

3.  The Stockdale Ombudsman of the Year Awards will be presented at the Naval 
District Washington Ombudsman Appreciation Luncheon on 16 October 2018 at the 
Joint Base Anacostia-Bolling Club.

4.  Thank you to all Command Ombudsmen who maintain the highest standards of 
professionalism and create a healthy sense of community by being positive 
role models for command members and their families.

5.  Commander, Navy Installations Command point of contact is Ms. Carrie 
Mast, Ombudsman Program Coordinator, (202) 433-4701 (DSN 288), or email 
carrie.mast1(at)navy.mil.

6.  Released by Mrs. Steffanie B. Easter, Director, Navy Staff.//
