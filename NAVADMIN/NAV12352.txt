RMKS/1.  PER REF A, EACH YEAR THE NAVY RECOGNIZES SHORE, SEA, AND  OVERSEAS 
COMMANDS FOR OUTSTANDING COMMUNITY SERVICE BY PRESENTING  AWARDS IN FIVE 
FLAGSHIP CATEGORIES:  PERSONAL EXCELLENCE  PARTNERSHIP; HEALTH, SAFETY, AND 
FITNESS; CAMPAIGN DRUG FREE;  PROJECT GOOD NEIGHBOR; AND ENVIRONMENTAL 
STEWARDSHIP.  AS SPONSOR OF  THE ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP FLAGSHIP, THE 
DEPUTY CHIEF OF NAVAL  OPERATIONS (FLEET READINESS AND LOGISTICS) (N4) SALUTES 
COMMANDS  ENGAGED IN EXEMPLARY VOLUNTARY COMMUNITY SERVICE ACTIVITIES THAT  
PROMOTE GOOD STEWARDSHIP OF ENVIRONMENTAL RESOURCES.

2.  FOR THE 2012 NAVY ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP FLAGSHIP AWARDS  COMPETITION, 
NOMINATIONS WERE RECEIVED IN THE SMALL, MEDIUM, AND  LARGE SHORE CATEGORIES, 
MEDIUM AND LARGE SEA CATEGORIES, AND SMALL  AND LARGE OVERSEAS CATEGORIES.

3.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE WINNERS AND  HONORABLE 
MENTIONS OF THE 2012 NAVY COMMUNITY SERVICE ENVIRONMENTAL  STEWARDSHIP 
FLAGSHIP AWARDS COMPETITION:
A.  SMALL SHORE
    WINNER:  NAVAL MAGAZINE INDIAN ISLAND, PORT HADLOCK, WA
    HONORABLE MENTION:  NAVY MANPOWER ANALYSIS CENTER, MILLINGTON, TN B.  
MEDIUM SHORE
    WINNER:  PRECOMMISSIONING UNIT ARLINGTON (LPD 24), NORFOLK, VA
    HONORABLE MENTION: NAVAL SUPPORT ACTIVITY PANAMA CITY, FL C.  LARGE SHORE
    WINNER:  NAVAL AIR STATION WHIDBEY ISLAND, OAK HARBOR, WA
    HONORABLE MENTION:  NAVAL AIR STATION PENSACOLA, FL D.  MEDIUM SEA
    WINNER:  USS ANTIETAM (CG 54)
E.  LARGE SEA
    WINNER:  USS HARRY S. TRUMAN (CVN 75) F.  SMALL OVERSEAS
    WINNER:  NAVY MUNITIONS COMMAND, EAST ASIA DIVISION, UNIT GUAM G.  LARGE 
OVERSEAS
    WINNER:  USS FRANK CABLE (AS 40)

4.  WINNERS WILL RECEIVE PLAQUES AND HONORABLE MENTIONS WILL RECEIVE  
CERTIFICATES IN RECOGNITION OF THEIR EXCEPTIONAL COMMITMENT TO OUR  
ENVIRONMENT.  OPNAV N45 STAFF WILL CONTACT COMMANDS TO ARRANGE  DELIVERY OF 
THE AWARDS.

5.  CONGRATULATIONS TO THE AWARD WINNERS, HONORABLE MENTIONS, AND ALL  WHO 
PARTICIPATED IN THESE NOTEWORTHY ENDEAVORS.  YOUR DEDICATED  SERVICE HAS MADE 
A POSITIVE DIFFERENCE, STRENGTHENED COMMUNITY TIES,  AND ENHANCED OUR 
ENVIRONMENT.  PLEASE ACCEPT MY PERSONAL BRAVO ZULU  AND THANKS FOR YOUR 
CONTINUED COMMITMENT AND SUPPORT OF OUR NAVY'S  COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM.
