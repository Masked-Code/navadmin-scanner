GENTEXT/REMARKS/ALL NAVY 2008 CHESS TOURNAMENT.// 
RMKS/1. THE INTER-SERVICE CHESS TOURNAMENT WILL BE HELD 8-14 JUNE 2008 
AT DAVIS MONTHAN AIR FORCE BASE IN TUCSON, AZ. THE ALL-NAVY TEAM WILL 
BE COMPRISED OF SIX TEAM MEMBERS AND WILL BE SELECTED FROM THE 
APPLICATIONS.
2. THE INTERNATIONAL MILITARY CHESS TOURNAMENT WILL BE HELD IN BRUSSELS, 
BELGIUM IN LATE AUGUST OR SEPTEMBER 2008, DATES TO BE DETERMINED. THE 
U.S. TEAM WILL CONSIST OF SIX PLAYERS DETERMINED BY PLACEMENT AT THE 
INTER-SERVICE CHESS TOURNAMENT.
3. ACTIVE DUTY NAVY PERSONNEL INTERESTED IN BEING SELECTED MUST SUBMIT 
AN APPLICATION. APPLICATIONS MUST INCLUDE THE UNITED STATES CHESS 
FEDERATION (USCF) MEMBERSHIP CARD, IDENTIFICATION NUMBER, STATE OF 
REGISTRATION, USCF RATING, DATE OF RATING AND RECORD OF SIGNIFICANT 
CHESS ACTIVITY DURING THE PAST TWO YEARS (PROVIDE DATE, PLACE, EVENT 
AND STANDING). APPLICATION FORMS ARE AVAILABLE ON THE MORALE, WELFARE, 
AND RECREATION (MWR) WEBSITE AT HTTP://WWW.MWR.NAVY.MIL, OR BY 
CONTACTING COMMANDER, NAVY INSTALLATIONS COMMAND (CNIC) NAVY RECREATION 
BRANCH, FLEET AND FAMILY READINESS DIVISION (CNIC-N923), MILLINGTON 
DETACHMENT AT COMM: (901) 874-6838 OR DSN: 882.
4. PACKAGES MUST CONTAIN COMMAND ENDORSEMENT STATING THAT THE 
INDIVIDUAL WILL BE AVAILABLE TO ATTEND ALL EVENTS. ALL APPLICATIONS 
MUST BE SUBMITTED DIRECTLY TO CNIC, MILLINGTON DETACHMENT, (CNIC-N923), 
5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE, BLDG 457, MILLINGTON, TN 38055-6550, OR VIA FAX 
COMM: 901-874-6838 OR DSN: 882 NO LATER THAN 25 APRIL 2008.
5. INDIVIDUALS SELECTED FOR THE 2008 NAVY CHESS TEAM WILL BE NOTIFIED 
VIA PHONE CALL OR EMAIL NO LATER THAN 11 MAY 2008. THE SELECTION WILL 
ALSO BE POSTED ON THE MWR WEBSITE. ALL PERSONNEL SELECTED WILL HAVE 
REQUESTED NO COST TO THE GOVERNMENT TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY ORDERS. 
ALL TRAVEL ARRANGEMENTS WILL BE COORDINATED BY CNIC-N923.
6. RELEASED BY VADM J. STUFFLEBEEM, DIRECTOR, NAVY STAFF.//
