RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the Acquisition Corps Membership Selection Board 
on 10 October 2013.  Acquisition Corps Membership is a prerequisite for assignment to one of 
approximately
700 Department of the Navy (DoN) critically-coded acquisition billets.

2.  Policy and guidance on Acquisition Corps membership procedures for DON officers may be found at 
the following website: 
http://acquisition.navy.mil/content/view/full/5689

3.  In order to be considered for membership, perspective applicants must meet all of the prerequisites 
for Defense Acquisition Corps membership, of chapter 9 of ref (a), prior to the application due date.

4.  Applications (no endorsements required) must be received no later than 16 September 2013 to be 
considered by the board.  Application should include a copy of the Defense Acquisition University (DAU) 
transcript, Electronic Director Acquisition Career Manager (eDACM) Dashboard (from individual's 
eDACM account) and college transcript (copies of the college transcripts are acceptable) to document 
compliance with the business academic requirement.  To ensure submitted package is accepted, all 
necessary documentation must be included.  

5.  The preferred method of submitting applications is by mail.  
Submission of application from multiple methods is strongly discouraged.  
    a.  Mail applications to:  
        Navy Personnel Command 
        FY-15 Acquisition Corps Selection Board (Board #190)
        Customer Service Center
        5720 Integrity Drive  
        Millington, TN 38055-4470
    b.  Applications sent via FEDEX/UPS/DHL should use the following 
        address:  
        Navy Personnel Command
        FY-15 Acquisition Corps Selection Board (Board #190)
        5640 Ticonderoga Loop Bldg 768 RM E203
        Millington TN 38055-3400
Applications will also be accepted via encrypted e-mail (signed copies only) for those officers deployed 
aboard ships or stationed at remote sites where regular mail could be disrupted.  Email packages to 
cscselboard(at)navy.mil.  Applications received after 16 September
2013 will not be accepted and will be returned to applicant or destroyed.  

6.  E-mail questions or concerns to pers-447_mail(at)navy.mil.  You may also access the Navy Personnel 
Command Acquisition Workforce Management Branch website at http://www.public.navy.mil/BUPERS-
NPC/OFFICER/DETAILING/ACQUISITION/Pages/default2.aspx for additional information pertaining to the 
Defense Acquisition Corps.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
