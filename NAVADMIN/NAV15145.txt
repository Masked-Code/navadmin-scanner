RMKS/1.  It is an honor to announce USS PHILIPPINE SEA (CG 58) as the 
calendar years 2013 and 2014 USS ARIZONA Memorial Trophy winner.

2.  The USS ARIZONA Memorial Trophy is awarded biennially to the ship 
achieving the greatest combat readiness in strike warfare, surface fire 
support, and anti-surface warfare.

3.  USS PHILIPPINE SEA's (CG 58) accomplishments are a testament to the 
professionalism and dedication of her crew and set a combat readiness 
benchmark for all to emulate.  Your hard work and commitment to excellence 
embody the fighting spirit of USS ARIZONA and are deserving of this 
prestigious award.

4.  Released by Vice Admiral J. F. Caldwell, Jr., Director, Navy Staff.//
