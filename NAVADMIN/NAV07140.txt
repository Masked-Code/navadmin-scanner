GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES REVISED SDAP LEVELS FOR THE 
REGULAR COMPONENT AND RESERVE COMPONENT FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) SAILORS 
AND SUPERSEDES NAVADMIN 004/06.  THE SDAP PROGRAM ENHANCES THE NAVY'S 
ABILITY TO SIZE, SHAPE, AND STABILIZE THE FORCE BY USING A MONETARY 
INCENTIVE TO ENCOURAGE SAILORS WITH SPECIFIC DESIGNATED SKILLS TO APPLY 
THEIR KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE TO UNIQUELY CHALLENGING ASSIGNMENTS AND 
TO STAY NAVY.
2.  NEW STARTS AND INCREASES TO PAY LEVELS ARE EFFECTIVE 1 JUN 07, AND 
ARE SUMMARIZED IN PARA 8 FOR EASE OF REFERENCE.  REDUCTIONS IN OR 
TERMINATIONS OF PREVIOUS LEVELS ARE EFFECTIVE 1 OCT 07, AND ARE 
SUMMARIZED IN PARA 9.  ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ON SDAP IS LOCATED AT 
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/CAREERINFO/PAYANDBENEFITS/N130/.
3.  THE RECERTIFICATION MESSAGE NORMALLY PROMULGATED SEPARATELY HAS 
BEEN INCORPORATED INTO PARA 10 FOR EASE OF REFERENCE.  TO RECEIVE SDAP, 
SAILORS MUST BE FILLING A SDAP AUTHORIZED BILLET WITH THE SPECIAL SKILL 
LISTED IN PARA 7.  UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED, SAILORS MAY NOT RECEIVE THIS 
PAY WHILE ASSIGNED IN EXCESS OF AUTHORIZED BILLETS.  COMMANDS MUST 
REVIEW THEIR ACTIVITY MANPOWER DOCUMENT AND ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION 
VERIFICATION REPORT (EDVR) TO ENSURE THAT THE NUMBER OF SAILORS 
RECEIVING THIS SPECIAL PAY DOES NOT EXCEED THE NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED 
BILLETS FOR THAT SKILL.  SAILORS ARE ONLY AUTHORIZED TO RECEIVE ONE 
SDAP ENTITLEMENT.
4.  THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT REGULATION, VOLUME 
7A AND REF A GOVERN THE POLICIES AND PROCEDURES FOR THE ADMINISTRATION 
AND PAYMENT OF SDAP.  COMMANDS AND SERVICING PERSONNEL SUPPORT 
DETACHMENTS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR ADMINISTRATING ALL APPLICABLE CHANGES 
TO SDAP PER THIS NAVADMIN AND THE ABOVE- LISTED DIRECTIVES.
5.  COMMANDS SHOULD ENSURE COMPLIANCE WITH MILPERSMAN 1306-1200 THROUGH 
MILPERSMAN 1306-1210 FOR SAILORS IN A LIMITED DUTY STATUS.  
SEE REF A, PARA 7.D.(8) FOR PATIENT STATUS GUIDANCE AS IT PERTAINS TO 
SDAP.
6.  SKILL SPECIFIC QUESTIONS MAY BE DIRECTED TO THE APPROPRIATE 
ENLISTED COMMUNITY MANAGER, AS LISTED IN PARA 7.  TO LOCATE THE PHONE 
NUMBER OF THE APPROPRIATE COMMUNITY MANAGER, REFER TO THE PHONE LIST IN 
THE LINK-PERSPECTIVE MAGAZINE.  ADDITIONALLY, CONTACT INFO MAY BE 
ACCESSED AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/ENLISTED/COMMUNITYMANAGERS.  YOU MUST LOG 
ONTO BUPERS ONLINE TO ACCESS THIS LISTING.
