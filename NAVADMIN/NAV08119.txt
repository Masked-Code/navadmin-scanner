PROGRAM.// RMKS/1.  EFFECTIVE 1 MAY 2008, SHORE-BASED DEFENSE ACTIVITY 
FOR NON-TRADITIONAL EDUCATION SUPPORT (DANTES) TEST SITES MAY RESUME 
THE ADMINISTRATION OF PAPER-BASED ACT, SAT, AND GED EXAMS.  SAILORS 
APPLYING FOR THE SEAMAN-TO-ADMIRAL (STA-21) PROGRAM SHOULD CONTACT 
THEIR LOCAL NAVY COLLEGE OFFICE IMMEDIATELY TO SCHEDULE THE ACT OR SAT 
EXAMS.  ALL OTHER PAPER-BASED DANTES SPONSORED ACADEMIC EXAMS REMAIN 
SUSPENDED FOR NAVY TESTING SITES.
2.  DANTES CONTINUES TO WORK CLOSELY WITH NAVY AND THE TESTING AGENCIES 
TO RESTORE THE FULL CAPABILITY OF TESTING SERVICES BOTH ASHORE AND AT 
SEA.  CRITICAL TO THIS NEXT STEP IS OUR STRICT ADHERENCE TO THE 
ADMINISTRATION AND SAFEGUARDING OF EXAMS AS 
DIRECTED BY REF B AND IN THE DANTES TESTING HANDBOOK.   
3.  NATIONAL TESTING CENTERS CONTINUE TO PROVIDE ELECTRONIC T ESTING 
AND ARE AN ATTRACTIVE ALTERNATIVE TO PAPER-BASED TESTING.  
AT NATIONAL TESTING CENTERS, SAILORS RECEIVE THEIR TEST RESULTS 
IMMEDIATELY AS OPPOSED TO THE TIME DELAY FOR PAPER-BASED TESTS.  
NAVY HAS AN AGGRESSIVE PLAN TO ESTABLISH ADDITIONAL NATIONAL TEST 
CENTERS AND EXPECTS TO HAVE 41 CENTERS OPERATING BY THE END OF FY08.
