RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN PROVIDES PROGRAM SPECIFICS AND SOLICITS 
APPLICATIONS FOR THE FY-08 NAVY WASHINGTON DC INTERN PROGRAM.
2.  THIS PROGRAM OFFERS A UNIQUE CAREER ENHANCING OPPORTUNITY FOR UP 
TO SIX OFFICERS TO PARTICIPATE IN A REWARDING AND CHALLENGING 12 MONTH 
EXPERIENCE.  THE PROGRAM COMBINES A FULLY-FUNDED MASTER OF ARTS 
DEGREE IN ORGANIZATIONAL MANAGEMENT FROM GEORGE WASHINGTON 
UNIVERSITY (GWU) WITH THE OPPORTUNITY TO SERVE WITHIN SENIOR 
WASHINGTON DC AREA STAFFS (OSD, JCS, SECNAV, OPNAV) AND GOVERNMENT 
AGENCIES (STATE, DHS, ETC).  TUITION, BOOKS AND ALL ACADEMIC FEES ARE 
FUNDED BY THE NAVY.
3.  THE PROGRAM CONSISTS OF TWO PHASES:
    A.  PHASE I (JUL08):  IN-PROCESSING AND PROGRAM ORIENTATION.
    B.  PHASE II (AUG08-JUN09):  TWO SIX-MONTH INTERNSHIP ASSIGNMENTS 
WITH SENIOR WASHINGTON STAFFS/AGENCIES.  OFFICERS WILL ALSO ATTEND 
GWU ON A PART-TIME BASIS TO COMPLETE GRADUATE STUDIES.  OFFICERS WILL 
SPEND TWO DAYS A WEEK ON ACADEMIC PREPARATION AND AFTER HOUR 
CLASSES PAGE 03 RUCOMFB8669 UNCLAS AND WILL PARTICIPATE IN THEIR 
INTERNSHIPS THREE DAYS A WEEK.
4.  ELIGIBILITY IS LIMITED TO O-4/O-5 URL OFFICERS WHO:
    A.  HAVE DEMONSTRATED SUPERIOR PERFORMANCE, UPWARD CAREER MOBILITY, 
AND COMMAND POTENTIAL.
    B.  ARE ROTATING OR OTHERWISE ELIGIBLE FOR A 12 MONTH (MINIMUM) TOUR 
OF SHORE DUTY.
    C.  HAVE NOT PREVIOUSLY COMPLETED AN IN-RESIDENCE DEPARTMENT OF 
DEFENSE FUNDED MASTER'S DEGREE.
    D.  HAVE COMPLETED JPME PHASE I.
5.  SELECTED OFFICERS ARE REQUIRED TO TAKE THE GRADUATE RECORD EXAM
(GRE) AND SUBMIT AN APPLICATION, INCLUDING BACCALAUREATE TRANSCRIPT 
AND GRE SCORES, TO GWU PRIOR TO STARTING THE PROGRAM.  AFTER GWU 
REVIEW OF TRANSCRIPTS AND GRE SCORES, OFFICERS WHO MEET GWU 
ADMISSION STANDARDS WILL BE ADMITTED TO DEGREE STATUS.
6.  PER REFS A AND B, ACTIVE DUTY OBLIGATION INCURRED IS:
    A.  PROGRAM PARTICIPANTS WILL INCUR AN ACTIVE DUTY SERVICE OBLIGATION 
THREE TIMES THE LENGTH OF THE PROGRAM CAPPED AT 36 MONTHS.
THIS OBLIGATION WILL BE SERVED CONCURRENTLY WITH ANY OTHER SERVICE 
OBLIGATION TO BEGIN UPON COMPLETION OF OR WITHDRAWAL FROM THE PAGE 
04 RUCOMFB8669 UNCLAS PROGRAM.  THIS AGREEMENT DOES NOT OBLIGATE THE 
NAVY TO RETAIN THE MEMBER ON ACTIVE DUTY.
    B.  REQUESTS FOR WAIVER OF SERVICE OBLIGATION RESULTING FROM 
GRADUATE EDUCATION PROGRAMS WILL NOT NORMALLY BE APPROVED.  ONLY 
WHEN NOT PRESCRIBED BY LAW AND WHEN EXTENUATING CIRCUMSTANCES 
SUPPORT, WILL A SERVICE OBLIGATION BE WAIVED.  SUCH WAIVERS MUST BE 
APPROVED BY COMMANDER, NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND.  WHEN A SERVICE 
OBLIGATION IS WAIVED, REPAYMENT OF UNEARNED PORTIONS OF EDUCATIONAL 
BENEFITS IS REQUIRED.
