RMKS/1. IN SUPPORT OF THE OFFICE OF PERSONNEL MANAGEMENT'S (OPM) 3RD
ANNUAL "FEDS FEED FAMILIES" (FFF) FOOD DRIVE, THE DEPUTY SECRETARY
OF DEFENSE HAS REQUESTED DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE (DOD) SUPPORT.  THIS
YEAR'S THEME IS "FEEDING FAMILIES ONE FED AT A TIME" AND IS A DIRECT
RESPONSE TO THE "UNITED WE SERVE ACT" SIGNED BY PRESIDENT OBAMA IN
APRIL 2009 WHICH CALLS ON ALL AMERICANS TO CONTRIBUTE TO THE NATION'S
ECONOMIC RECOVERY BY SERVING THEIR COMMUNITIES.  REF A REFERS.

2. COMMANDER, NAVY INSTALLATIONS COMMAND (CNIC) IS DESIGNATED AS THE
NAVY'S LEAD ORGANIZATION AND SUPPORTED COMMANDER FOR THIS INITIATIVE.
THE FFF FOOD DRIVE IS SCHEDULED TO RUN THROUGH 30 AUG 2011 AND IS AN
EXCELLENT OPPORTUNITY FOR OUR SAILORS AND CIVILIANS TO SUPPORT THE
COMMUNITIES THAT SUPPORT THEM.

3. CONCEPT OF OPERATIONS:
A. NAVY COMMANDS ARE TO DESIGNATE A FFF COMMAND REP TO COORDINATE
THIS INITIATIVE WITH THE KEY CNIC REPRESENTATIVES - INSTALLATION
CHAPLAINS/PAOS.
B. FFF DROP OFF/COLLECTION POINTS HAVE BEEN STAGED THROUGHOUT EACH
NAVY INSTALLATION.
C. EACH MON, INSTALLATION FFF REPS WILL COLLECT WEEKLY REPORTS FROM
THEIR TENANT COMMAND FFF REPS AND REPORT CONSOLIDATED NUMBERS
(LBS) TO REGION FFF REPS NLT COB TUES.  WEEKLY REPORTS WILL INCLUDE
THE FOLLOWING INFO:
(1) WEIGHT OF FOOD COLLECTED AND RECIPIENT OF GOODS.
(2) RECEIVING CHARITIES CAN INCLUDE ORGANIZATIONS SUCH AS COMMUNITY
FOOD BANKS, LOCAL RED CROSS PANTRIES, SALVATION ARMY FOOD KITCHENS
AND THE LIKE.
D. BY 1700 EST EACH WED, REGION FFF REPS FORWARD CONSOLIDATED WEEKLY
REPORTS TO CNIC, WHO WILL THEN REPORT/UPLOAD DATA INTO THE DON/AA
SHAREPOINT SITE.
E. NAVY HAS SET A STRETCH GOAL TO COLLECT 200,000 LBS OF
NON-PERISHABLE FOOD BY 31 AUG 2011.  TO DATE, APPROXIMATELY 153,000
LBS HAVE BEEN COLLECTED.  BASED ON A PER CAPITA FORMULA OF
ACTIVE DUTY PERSONNEL, CURRENT REGIONAL COLLECTION GOALS ARE AS
FOLLOWS:
(1) CNREURAFSWA - 16,000 LBS
(2) CNRH - 4,800 LBS
(3) CNRJ - 9,600 LBS
(4) CNRK - 1,600 LBS
(5) CNRMA - 33,600 LBS
(6) CNRMW - 3,200 LBS
(7) NDW - 16,000 LBS
(8) CNRNW - 12,800 LBS
(9) CNRSE - 33,600 LBS
(10) CNRSW - 27,200 LBS
(11) JRM - 3,200 LBS

4. CNIC POCS ARE THE REGION COMMANDERS LISTED ABOVE. ADDITIONAL
DETAILS ARE AVAILABLE THROUGH THE FOLLOWING LINK:
WWW.FEDSFEEDFAMILIES.GOV.
