SUBJ/STAYNAVY SPONSOR ASSIGNMENT AID (SAA)//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE STAYNAVY SPONSOR ASSIGNMENT AID (SAA) IS AN
INTERACTIVE, WEB-BASED RESOURCE TOOL THAT ALLOWS TRANSFERRING
SAILORS OF ALL PAYGRADES TO COMMUNICATE WITH A GAINING COMMAND AND
REQUEST THAT A SPONSOR BE ASSIGNED TO ASSIST WITH THEIR TRANSFER.
SAA CAN BE ACCESSED THROUGH THE STAYNAVY HOMEPAGE ON THE OFFICIAL



PAGE 02 RUEWMFU7715 UNCLAS
NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND WEBSITE AT
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/CAREERINFO/STAYNAVYTOOLS/CAREERTOOLS/.
2.  SAILORS RECEIVING ORDERS ENTER PERTINENT INFORMATION INCLUDING
CURRENT AND FUTURE UNIT IDENTIFICATION CODE, DETACHING AND REPORTING
DATE, AND PERSONAL INFORMATION (NAME, PAYGRADE, DEPENDENT AND
CONTACT INFORMATION).  THE REQUEST IS SENT TO THE GAINING COMMAND
AND THE TRANSFERRING SAILOR RECEIVES A TRACKING NUMBER THAT HELPS
MONITOR THEIR REQUEST.
3.  ACTIVE PARTICIPATION IS REQUIRED BY EVERY NAVY COMMAND IN ORDER
TO MAINTAIN AND IMPROVE UPON THIS VALUABLE TOOL.  THE COMMAND
SPONSOR COORDINATOR CONTACT INFORMATION MUST REMAIN ACCURATE.  THIS
INFORMATION CAN BE UPDATED BY ACCESSING THE "SPONSOR COORDINATOR
UPDATE" LINK LOCATED ON THE STAYNAVY TOOLS PAGE.  WITHOUT CURRENT
INFORMATION, THE TRANSFERRING SAILOR SPONSOR REQUEST WILL NOT BE
RECEIVED.
4.  SAA DIRECTLY SUPPORTS OUR SEA WARRIOR PHILOSOPHY, IMPROVES THE
QUALITY AND CONSISTENCY OF SPONSOR PROGRAMS, AND ENSURES ALL SAILORS
EXPERIENCE A SMOOTH TRANSFER FROM COMMAND TO COMMAND.
5.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LT DOMINIC FORMICA AT (901) 874-4004/DSN 882.
6.  RELEASED BY VADM G. L. HOEWING, N1NT.//
