RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE CONVENING OF THE ACTIVE AND RESERVE 
CAREER RECRUITER FORCE (CRF) SELECTION BOARDS.  CRF WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1978 TO 
DEVELOP A CADRE OF EXCEPTIONAL RECRUITING MANAGERS TO PROVIDE CONSISTENCY AND 
LEADERSHIP TO THE RECRUITING EFFORT.  THE CRF PROGRAM IS MADE UP OF PROVEN, 
VOLUNTEER RECRUITERS WHO FILL KEY MIDDLE MANAGEMENT BILLETS.  THE SELECTION 
CRITERIA FOCUSES ON PROVEN RECRUITING PERFORMANCE WITH AN EMPHASIS ON FFECTIVE 
LEADERSHIP, MANAGEMENT, COUNSELING AND TRAINING ABILITIES.

2.  THE CRF PROGRAM IS OPEN TO PERSONNEL WHO ARE CURRENTLY ASSIGNED OR HAVE 
SUCCESSFULLY COMPLETED A TOUR IN RECRUITING DUTY WITH NEC CODES 9585, 9586 OR 
9587.  FURTHER ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND APPLICATION PROCEDURES ARE OUTLINED IN 
REF A.

3.  THE CONVENING DATES FOR THE NEXT FOUR SELECTION BOARDS ARE AS FOLLOWS:
     A. 1 NOVEMBER 2012
     B. 7 FEBRUARY 2013
     C. 16 MAY 2013
     D. 6 AUGUST 2013

4.  ALL APPLICATIONS ARE REQUIRED TO BE RECEIVED NO LATER THAN THREE WEEKS 
PRIOR TO THE CONVENING DATE OF EACH SCHEDULED SELECTION BOARD.  FORWARD 
PACKAGES TO:
    COMMANDER, NAVY RECRUITING COMMAND
    ATTN:  ASSISTANT CRF PROGRAM MANAGER, (N11151)
    5722 INTEGRITY DRIVE, BLDG 784
    MILLINGTON, TN 38054-5057

OR SCAN AND E-MAIL DIRECTLY TO MR. RICHARD THOMPSON AT 
RICHARD.E.THOMPSON2(AT)NAVY.MIL.

5.  ALL CANDIDATES WILL BE NOTIFIED VIA OFFICIAL MESSAGE OF THEIR SELECTION OR 
NON-SELECTION.

6.  SELECTEES CURRENTLY ON RECRUITING DUTY WILL BE CONVERTED IMMEDIATELY TO 
NAVY COUNSELOR (RECRUITER) (NEC-2186).  SELECTEES NOT ON RECRUITING DUTY WILL 
BE IMMEDIATELY ASSIGNED TEMPORARY NEC 2199.
FLEET PERSONNEL SELECTED WILL TAKE CURRENT RATING NAVY-WIDE ADVANCEMENT 
EXAMINATION UNTIL SUCCESSFULLY GRADUATING FROM THE CRF ACADEMY.  ALL SELECTEES 
WILL ATTEND THE CRF ACADEMY IN PENSACOLA, FL IN CONJUNCTION WITH PERMANENT 
CHANGE OF STATION ORDERS AT THEIR CURRENT PROJECTED ROTATION DATE.

7.  POINT OF CONTACT IS MR. RICHARD THOMPSON, ASSISTANT CRF PROGRAM MANAGER AT 
(901) 874-9292/DSN 882 OR VIA E-MAIL AT RICHARD.E.THOMPSON2(AT)NAVY.MIL.
