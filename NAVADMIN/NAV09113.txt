FLAGS BEING PRESENTED TO FAMILY MEMBERS UNDER REF A.  MILPERSMAN 
ARTICLE 1770-275, WHICH DELINEATES POLICY AND ELIGIBLE FAMILY MEMBERS, 
WILL BE PUBLISHED THIS MONTH.

FLAG:
    A.  PERSON AUTHORIZED TO DIRECT DISPOSITION (PADD).
    B.  SURVIVING SPOUSE (REGARDLESS OF WHETHER OR NOT THE SURVIVING 
SPOUSE REMARRIES AFTER THE DECEDENT'S DEATH).
    C.  EACH CHILD OF THE DECEDENT.
    D.  PARENT OR PARENTS.

EMERGENCY DATA (NAVPERS 1070/602) AS CHANGES OCCUR.
