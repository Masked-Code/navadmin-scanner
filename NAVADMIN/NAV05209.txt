2. BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
     A.  SELRES CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE BASIC ELIGIBILITY
REQUIREMENTS PER BUPERSINST 1430.16E, OPNAVINST 1160.7B, ALNAVRESFOR
013/04 AND NAVADMIN 060/05 NOT LATER THAN 1 JAN 06.  CANDIDATES MUST
MAINTAIN A COMMANDING OFFICER'S RECOMMENDATION TO REMAIN ELIGIBLE
FOR ADVANCEMENT.
     B.  PERSONNEL WHO WILL BE OVER HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT) ON THE
FIRST DAY OF THE ADVANCEMENT CYCLE (1 SEP 06) ARE INELIGIBLE FOR
PARTICIPATION IN THE NAVY WIDE ADVANCEMENT EXAM FOR E7 AND SELECTION
BOARD CONSIDERATION UNLESS THEY RECEIVE A HYT WAIVER THAT EXTENDS
THEIR HYT BEYOND 1 SEP 06.  SELECTED RESERVE E7 CANDIDATES WHO MEET
OR EXCEED HYT FOR THEIR CURRENT PAYGRADE DUE TO AN APPROVED HYT
WAIVER, WILL BE CONSIDERED FOR ADVANCEMENT TO THE NEXT HIGHER
PAYGRADE, IF THEY ARE STILL ON ACTIVE DUTY THE DAY THE ENLISTED
SELECTION BOARD ADJOURNS.  THOSE MEMBERS WHO SUBMIT RETIREMENT
REQUESTS, HAVE RETIREMENT REQUESTS PENDING, OR ARE APPROVED FOR
TRANSFER TO THE RETIRED RESERVE WITH OR WITHOUT PAY ON A DATE PRIOR

SELECTION BOARD CONSIDERATION PROVIDED CANCELLATION OF RETIREMENT
AUTHORIZATION IS APPROVED PRIOR TO 1 MAY 06.  COMNAVPERSCOM
(PERS-4812) AND NETPDTC (N321) MUST BE NOTIFIED BY MESSAGE TO ENSURE

     D.  PER NAVADMIN 069/04, SUCCESSFUL COMPLETION OF THE PREVIOUS

1430.16E REMAIN IN EFFECT FOR TAKING PART IN THE ADVANCEMENT EXAM
FOR CPO.  WAIVERS FOR ATTENDANCE REQUIREMENTS WILL NO LONGER BE
AUTHORIZED FOR BOARD PARTICIPATION OR TAKING PART IN ADVANCEMENT
