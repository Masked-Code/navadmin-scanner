ADVOCATE GENERAL OF THE NAVY SELECTION BOARD// 

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE NOTICE OF CONVENING DATE FOR 
SUBJECT SELECTION BOARD AS 5 DECEMBER 2012.  THE FOLLOWING LIST INDICATES THE 
ELIGIBLE CAPTAIN'S NAME, ACTIVE-DUTY LIST NUMBER, AND DATE OF RANK OF THE 
SENIOR AND JUNIOR OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE DUTY LIST IN THE COMPETITIVE CATEGORY 
OF NAVY JUDGE ADVOCATE GENERAL'S CORPS, WHO ARE ELIGIBLE FOR CONSIDERATION AS 
OF THE DATE OF THIS NAVADMIN.
SENIOR ELIGIBLE - MODZELEWSKI, M D.   010214-50    1 JUN 2003
JUNIOR ELIGIBLE - REISMEIER, M. S.    011599-00    1 NOV 2009

2.  ONLY ELIGIBLE OFFICERS MAY COMMUNICATE DIRECTLY WITH A SELECTION BOARD.
ALL CORRESPONDENCE MUST ARRIVE NOT LATER THAN THE DAY BEFORE THE CONVENING 
DATE OF THE BOARD AND SHALL BE ADDRESSED TO:

NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (NPC) CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER 
PRESIDENT, FY-13 ACTIVE DUTY ASSISTANT JUDGE ADVOCATE GENERAL OF THE NAVY 
SELECTION BOARD #106 
5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE MILLINGTON, TN 38055 

THE WRITTEN COMMUNICATION MAY CALL TO THE ATTENTION OF THE BOARD INFORMATION 
THE OFFICER CONSIDERS RELEVANT.  THE WRITTEN COMMUNICATION MAY INCLUDE, AS 
ENCLOSURES, CORRESPONDENCE FROM ANY INDIVIDUAL CONCERNING THE ELIGIBLE 
OFFICER.  
CORRESPONDENCE NOT ORIGINATED BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER, INCLUDING ENDORSEMENTS 
TO THE OFFICER'S LETTER AND LETTERS WRITTEN ON BEHALF OF THAT OFFICER, MUST 
CONTAIN A WRITTEN ACKNOWLEDGEMENT BY THE ELIGIBLE OFFICER THAT HE OR SHE 
DESIRES SUCH CORRESPONDENCE BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD.  CORRESPONDENCE WITHOUT 
SUCH ACKNOWLEDGEMENT IS CONSIDERED THIRD PARTY CORRESPONDENCE AND WILL BE 
RETURNED. CLASSIFIED CORRESPONDENCE WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED.  THE CUSTOMER 
SERVICE CENTER HAS ESTABLISHED A NEW FUNCTIONAL E-MAIL ACCOUNT THAT ALLOWS 
ELIGIBLES TO SUBMIT LETTERS TO THE BOARD AND CORRESPONDENCE THROUGH ENCRYPTED 
E-MAIL TO COMPLY WITH DOD PII POLICY.  SEND THE E-MAIL ENCRYPTED TO 
CSCSELBOARD(AT)NAVY.MIL.

3.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS NOT WANTING TO BE CONSIDERED BY THIS BOARD MAY SUBMIT A 
REQUEST INDICATING THE DESIRE THAT THEIR RECORD NOT BE CONSIDERED.  OFFICERS 
SUBMITTING REQUESTS NOT TO BE CONSIDERED WILL NOT BE ELIGIBLE AND THEIR 
RECORDS WILL NOT BE PRESENTED TO THE BOARD FOR CONSIDERATION.

4.  THE FOLLOWING OFFICERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE AND THEIR RECORDS WILL NOT BE 
PRESENTED TO THE BOARD FOR CONSIDERATION:  ANY OFFICER WHO HAS AN APPROVED 
SEPARATION/RETIREMENT DATE AS OF THE CONVENING DATE OF THE BOARD; OR WAS 
RECOMMENDED BY A PREVIOUS SELECTION BOARD FOR APPOINTMENT AS ASSISTANT JUDGE 
ADVOCATE GENERAL OF THE NAVY OR LEGAL COUNSEL TO THE CHAIRMAN OF THE JOINT 
CHIEFS OF STAFF (PURSUANT TO SECTION 156 OF TITLE 10 U.S. CODE).

5.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
