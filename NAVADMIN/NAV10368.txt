RMKS/1. THIS MESSAGE ANNOUNCES THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NEW GENADMIN
MESSAGE SERIES CALLED "NAVSHORE," RELEASED BY COMMANDER, NAVY
INSTALLATIONS COMMAND (CNIC). THE PURPOSE OF NAVSHORE MESSAGES IS TO
COMMUNICATE AND INCREASE TRANSPARENCY OF SHORE-RELATED INFORMATION
SUCH AS THE IMPLEMENTATION OF NEW AND/OR CHANGING POLICY,
INITIATIVES, PROCEDURAL CHANGES, AND PROGRAM ROLLOUTS ASSOCIATED
WITH CNIC'S 31 PROGRAMS AND 122 PRODUCTS THAT COMPRISE NAVY'S SHORE
SERVICES.

2. NAVSHORE MESSAGE RECIPIENTS WILL INCLUDE THE NAVY INSTALLATION
COMMAND ENTERPRISE AND ECHELON TWO COMMANDS, INCLUDING THE NUMBERED
FLEETS. FLEET COMMANDERS WILL FORWARD THESE MESSAGES, AS
APPROPRIATE, TO THEIR SUBORDINATE COMMANDS SINCE THE INFORMATION IN
THE NAVSHORE MESSAGES WILL BE GERMANE TO SAILORS AND THEIR FAMILIES.
