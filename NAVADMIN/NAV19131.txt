SUBJ/SUBMARINE ACQUISITION CORPS ELIGIBLE SELECTION BOARD//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the next Submarine Acquisition 
Corps (AC) eligible selection board the week of
23 September 2019.  Submarine AC eligible opportunities are available for

mail to the Submarine AC community manager no later than 9 August 2019.

3.  Application guidance and Submarine AC candidate information sheets may be 
obtained by contacting the Submarine AC community manager.

4.  Point of contact is CAPT Chris Polk, Submarine AC Community Manager, at 
(202) 781-4457 or via e-mail at christopher.j.polk1(at)navy.mil.

5.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or 30 September 2019, 
whichever comes first.
