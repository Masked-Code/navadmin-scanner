SUBJ/MERGER OF THE INFORMATION SYSTEMS TECHNICIAN (IT) AND
CRYPTOLOGIC TECHNICIAN (COMMUNICATIONS) (CTO) RATINGS//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  CNO HAS APPROVED THE MERGER OF THE INFORMATION
SYSTEMS TECHNICIAN (IT) AND CRYPTOLOGIC TECHNICIAN (COMMUNICATIONS)
(CTO) RATINGS INTO THE IT RATING.  THIS MERGER WILL APPLY TO ACTIVE
DUTY, FULL-TIME SUPPORT, AND SELECTED RESERVE SAILORS IN THE IT AND
CTO RATINGS.  AS AN ELEMENT OF THE NAVY'S TOTAL FORCE STRATEGY, THIS
MERGER WILL CAPITALIZE ON THE KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES FOUND
IN THE TWO RATINGS AND APPLY THEM TOWARD A UNIFIED MISSION.
2.  MERGING THE IT AND CTO RATINGS WILL IMPROVE EFFICIENCY, SUPPORT
OPTIMAL MANNING, AND BETTER ALIGN THE COMBINED RATING WITH
COMMERCIAL INDUSTRY PRACTICES.  REDUNDANCY OF FUNCTIONS AND TRAINING
WILL ALSO BE REDUCED.  ANTICIPATED BENEFITS ARE REDUCED
INFRASTRUCTURE COSTS AND IMPROVED CUSTOMER SERVICE AND QUALITY OF
LIFE FOR SAILORS.
3.  OFFICIAL CONVERSION FROM CTO TO IT FOR RATED SAILORS WILL BE
AUTOMATIC AND WILL OCCUR ON 1 MAR 06 FOR ACTIVE DUTY E1-E5, 1 JUN 06
FOR RESERVE E1-E5 AND 1 OCT 06 FOR BOTH ACTIVE AND RESERVE E6 AND
ABOVE PERSONNEL.  NO INDIVIDUAL PERSONNEL ACTION BY COMMANDS OR
MEMBERS WILL BE REQUIRED.
4.  THE IT RATING BADGE WILL BE UTILIZED FOR THE NEW COMBINED
RATING.  ALL CTO PERSONNEL E1-E5 SHALL REPLACE UNIFORM RATING BADGES
FROM CTO TO THE IT RATING BADGE ON 1 MAR 06.  ALL CTO PERSONNEL
E6-E9 SHALL CONVERT UNIFORM RATING BADGES TO THE IT RATING BADGE ON
1 OCT 06.  THE FOLLOWING EXCEPTION APPLIES:
CRYPTOLOGIC TECHNICIAN (COMMUNICATIONS) WHO TRANSFER TO THE FLEET
RESERVE OR THE RETIRED LIST BETWEEN 1 MAR 06 AND 31 DEC 07 FOR E5,
OR BETWEEN 1 OCT 06 AND 30 SEP 08 FOR E6-E9 MAY CHOOSE TO RETIRE IN
THEIR NEW OR PREVIOUS RATING.  THE ENLISTED MASTER FILE, HOWEVER,
WILL REFLECT ALL PERSONNEL IN THE IT RATING EFFECTIVE 1 MAR 06,
1 JUN 06, AND 1 OCT 06 RESPECTIVELY.
