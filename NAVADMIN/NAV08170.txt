SUBJ/OVERSEAS CITIZENS VOTERS WEEK// 
RMKS/1. THE SECRETARY OF DEFENSE HAS DESIGNATED JUNE 28 THROUGH JULY 7 
AS OVERSEAS CITIZENS VOTERS WEEK. ALL LOCAL AND INSTALLATION VOTING 
ASSISTANCE OFFICERS ARE ENCOURAGED TO HOLD ACTIVE PROMOTIONS TO 
ENCOURAGE NAVY CITIZENS TO TAKE PART IN THE ELECTION PROCESS BY 
REGISTERING TO VOTE. 
SUGGESTED ACTIVITIES INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO: HOLDING 
REGISTRATION DRIVES DURING JULY 4TH CELEBRATIONS, ADVERTISING IN LOCAL 
MILITARY MEDIA, SETTING UP AWARENESS AND REGISTRATION BOOTHS IN COMMON 
AREAS, HOLDING UNIT TRAINING, AND PLAN OF THE DAY OR WEEK NOTICES.
2. REGION VOTING ASSISTANCE OFFICERS ARE ENCOURAGED TO PROVIDE 
ASSISTANCE AND ENCOURAGEMENT TO SUBORDINATE UNITS AS NEEDED IN ADDITION 
TO CONDUCTING PROMOTIONS WITHIN THEIR UNIT.
3. COMMANDER, NAVY INSTALLATIONS COMMAND (CNIC) NAVY VOTING ASSISTANCE 
PROGRAM
(NVAP) STANDS READY TO ASSIST VOTING ASSISTANCE OFFICERS AT ALL LEVELS 
IN THEIR EFFORTS. INFORMATION, ASSISTANCE, AND RESOURCES MAY BE 
OBTAINED BY CONTACTING THE NAVY VOTING ACTION OFFICER, LT JASON WATKINS 
AT VOTE(AT)NAVY.MIL, TOLL FREE 1-866-827-5672 OPTION 6, COMMERCIAL 901-
874-6647, DSN 882-6647, AND ON THE WEB AT 
HTTP:/WWW.CNI.NAVY.MIL/NAVYVOTE/.
4. RELEASED BY VADM J. C. HARVEY, JR., DIRECTOR, NAVY STAFF.//
