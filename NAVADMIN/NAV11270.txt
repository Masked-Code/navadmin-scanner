RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN PROVIDES DETAILS ON THE GRADUATE EDUCATION VOUCHER 
(GEV) PROGRAM AND GUIDANCE FOR FY-12 PROGRAM APPLICATION.
ELIGIBILITY, OBLIGATED SERVICE REQUIREMENTS, SUBSPECIALTY UTILIZATION TOUR, 
AND AUTHORIZED PROGRAM BENEFITS ARE PRESCRIBED IN REF A.

2.  GEV FUNDING IS AUTHORIZED ONLY FOR OFF-DUTY STUDY IN MASTER DEGREE 
PROGRAMS WHICH MEET THE REQUIREMENTS OF AT LEAST ONE APPROVED NAVY 
SUBSPECIALTY CODE AS VERIFIED BY NAVAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL (NPS).  FOR FY-12, 
115 GEV QUOTAS ARE PLANNED, SUBJECT TO FUNDING AVAILABILITY.  QUOTAS ARE 
APPORTIONED AS FOLLOWS:
AVIATION OFFICERS - 40
SUBMARINE OFFICERS - 30
SURFACE WARFARE OFFICERS - 40
SPECWAR/SPECOPS OFFICERS - 5
FY-12 QUOTAS BY AREA OF STUDY, SUBSPECIALTY FOR AREA OF STUDY, AND WARFARE 
AREA ARE:
CHEMISTRY (4201P/G)        AV  1  SUB   1  SURFACE  1  SPECWAR/OPS 0
COMPUTER SCIENCE (6203P/G) AV  5  SUB   1  SURFACE  5  SPECWAR/OPS 0
ENGINEERING (5000P/G)      AV  4  SUB   8  SURFACE  4  SPECWAR/OPS 0
ENGLISH (4301P/G)          AV  1  SUB   1  SURFACE  1  SPECWAR/OPS 0
FINANCIAL MGMT (31XXP/G)   AV 13  SUB   8  SURFACE 13  SPECWAR/OPS 3
HISTORY (4302P/G)          AV  1  SUB   1  SURFACE  1  SPECWAR/OPS 0
MATHEMATICS (4100P/G)      AV  2  SUB   1  SURFACE  2  SPECWAR/OPS 0
OPS ANALYSIS (3211P/G)     AV  3  SUB   3  SURFACE  3  SPECWAR/OPS 0
OPS RESEARCH (3211P/G)     AV  5  SUB   3  SURFACE  5  SPECWAR/OPS 0
REGIONAL STUDIES (2000P/G) AV  5  SUB   3  SURFACE  5  SPECWAR/OPS 2

3.  UNRESTRICTED LINE OFFICERS INTERESTED IN THE FY-12 GEV PROGRAM SHOULD 
SUBMIT A WRITTEN REQUEST TO THEIR DETAILER USING THE FORMAT PROVIDED IN ENCL 
(1) OF REF A.  FOR SHIPS AT SEA, APPLICATIONS VIA NAVAL MESSAGE CONTAINING THE 
REQUISITE INFORMATION WILL BE ACCEPTED.

4.  OFFICERS COMPLETING A DEGREE USING GEV BENEFITS MUST FORWARD A FINAL 
TRANSCRIPT WITH DEGREE CONFERRED TO THE CENTER OF PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT VOLUNTARY EDUCATION DETACHMENT, PENSACOLA, FL 32509 AND SHOULD 
EXPECT TO SERVE ONE TOUR IN A SUBSPECIALTY BILLET NOT LATER THAN THE SECOND 
TOUR FOLLOWING GRADUATION.
