SUBJ/OPNAVINST 7220.11D//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE PUBLICATION OF OPNAVINST 7220.11D, 
NUCLEAR OFFICER INCENTIVE PAY (NOIP) PROGRAM.

2.  THE UPDATED INSTRUCTION CAN BE DOWNLOADED AT 
HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-NPC.

3.  SUMMARY OF CHANGES:  THIS REVISION UPDATES THE NOIP LEGISLATIVE AUTHORITY 
FROM TITLE 37 U.S.C. SECTION 312 TO TITLE 37 U.S.C. SECTION 333.  THESE 
CHANGES INCREASE THE FLEXIBILITY OF OFFICERS TO RENEGOTIATE COPAY CONTRACTS IN 
SOME SITUATIONS AND REDEFINE THOSE NUCLEAR LIMITED DUTY OFFICER (LDO) AND 
CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER (CWO) BILLETS WHICH MAY QUALIFY FOR THE ANNUAL INCENTIVE 
BONUS (AIB).

4.  CONTRACT RATES AND INCENTIVE PAYMENT AMOUNTS HAVE NOT CHANGED.
EXISTING CONTRACTS SIGNED UNDER OPNAVINST 7220.11C WILL CONTINUE TO BE 
GOVERNED BY THE EXISTING 37 USC 312 AUTHORITIES.  NEW AND RENEGOTIATED 
CONTRACTS WILL BE EXECUTED UNDER THE NEW 37 U.S.C. SECTION 333 AUTHORITY.  
NUCLEAR TRAINED OFFICERS WITH SPECIFIC CONTRACT OR ELIGIBILITY QUESTIONS 
SHOULD CONTACT THEIR DETAILERS.

5.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR TODD NETHERCOTT, (N133C), AT (703) 604-5490 OR 
VIA E-MAIL AT NXAG_N133C(AT)NAVY.MIL.

6.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
