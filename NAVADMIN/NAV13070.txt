RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE REQUIREMENT FOR NUCLEAR-TRAINED SAILORS ASSIGNED 
TO A NUCLEAR TRAINING COMMAND OR HOLDING A NUCLEAR NAVY ENLISTED CLASSIFICATION (NEC) 
(335X, 336X, 338X, 339X) APPLYING TO AN OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAM TO OBTAIN 
CONDITIONAL RELEASE FROM NUCLEAR FIELD DUTY.

2.  TO OBTAIN A CONDITIONAL RELEASE, A SAILOR MUST SUBMIT A NAVPERS 1306/7, ENLISTED 
PERSONNEL ACTION REQUEST TO THE NUCLEAR PROPULSION PROGRAM MANAGEMENT BRANCH, 
OPNAV (N133), VIA THEIR DETAILER (PERS-403).  NUCLEAR-TRAINED SAILORS WITH 18 MONTHS OR LESS 
ON THEIR ENLISTMENT CONTRACT WILL BE GRANTED CONDITIONAL RELEASE.  WAIVERS FOR NUCLEAR-
TRAINED SAILORS WITH GREATER THAN 18 MONTHS REMAINING ON THEIR CONTRACT WILL BE 
CONSIDERED ON A CASE-BY-CASE BASIS WITH CONSIDERATIONS GIVEN TO COMMUNITY HEALTH.

3.  APPLICANTS MUST OBTAIN CONDITIONAL RELEASE PRIOR TO SUBMITTING THEIR APPLICATION FOR 
AN OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAM.

4.  OFFICER ACCESSION BOARDS ARE ONLY TO CONSIDER NUCLEAR ENLISTED CANDIDATES WITH 
CONDITIONAL RELEASE INCLUDED IN THEIR PROGRAM APPLICATION.

5.  ALL OTHER REQUIREMENTS FOR APPLICATION TO OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS ARE LISTED 
IN REF A.  AFFECTED OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS INCLUDE THE U.S. NAVAL ACADEMY AND 
NAVAL ACADEMY PREPARATORY SCHOOL PROGRAMS, OFFICER CANDIDATE SCHOOL PROGRAM, 
MEDICAL ENLISTED COMMISSIONING PROGRAM, MEDICAL SERVICE CORPS IN- SERVICE PROCUREMENT 
PROGRAM, LIMITED DUTY OFFICER AND CHIEF WARRANT OFFICER PROGRAMS, AND SEAMAN TO 
ADMIRAL-21 PROGRAM.

6.  POINT OF CONTACT IS CDR JACOB FORET, NUCLEAR ENLISTED PROGRAM MANAGER, N133D, AT (703) 
604-5491/5492/DSN 664 OR VIA E-MAIL AT NXAG_N133D(AT)NAVY.MIL.

7.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
