RMKS/1.  Reference (a) announced the convening of the Senior Enlisted 
Continuation Board (SECB) on 3 August 2015.  The board convening date has 
been adjusted to 7 December 2015.

2.  All eligibility requirements contained in reference (a) remain in effect.

3.  The deadline for submitting letters to the board (LTB) has been adjusted 
to 16 November 2015.  All LTBs currently received by Navy Personnel Command 
(NPC) Customer Service Center (CSC) will be presented to the board.  Senior 
chief petty officer and chief petty officer September 2015 periodic 
evaluations are expected to be either in the members Official Military 
Personnel File or submitted by the member via a LTB.  For LTB submission 
procedures and guidance visit http://www.npc.navy.mil/bupers-
npc/boards/activedutyenlisted/
pages/generalinformation.aspx.

4.  Point of contact for SECB execution and retirement programs is the NPC 
CSC at uasknpc(at)navy.mil or 1-866-U-ASK-NPC (1(866)827-5672).

5.  This NAVADMIN will remain in effect until superseded or cancelled.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
