GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  COMMAND MASTER CHIEFS AND COMMAND SENIOR
CHIEFS (CMC/CSC) ARE THE SENIOR ENLISTED LEADERS CHARGED WITH
ENFORCING STANDARDS OF PROFESSIONALISM, DEVELOPING SAILORS
AND FOSTERING BETTER COMMUNICATIONS AT ALL LEVELS OF THE
CHAIN OF COMMAND.  THEY SERVE AS SENIOR ENLISTED LEADERS TO
COMMANDING OFFICERS AND ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR STRENGTHENING THE

CHAIN OF COMMAND
2.  ALL HARD CHARGING, SQUARED AWAY AND HIGHLY MOTIVATED COMMAND
MASTER CHIEFS AND SENIOR CHIEFS ARE ELIGIBLE FOR AND ENCOURAGED
TO APPLY FOR THIS PROGRAM.  THE FY-07 SELECTION BOARDS HAVE BEEN
COMBINED AND THE DEADLINE TO APPLY FOR THE JAN 07 SELECTION BOARD
IS 15 NOV 06.
3.  APPLICANTS MUST POSSESS AND MAINTAIN THE FOLLOWING QUALITIES:
    A.  SUPERIOR LEADERSHIP AND BROAD MANAGEMENT EXPERIENCE.
    B.  GOOD COMMUNICATION AND ADMINISTRATIVE SKILLS.
    C.  PERSONNEL COUNSELING SKILLS.
    D.  SHARP MILITARY APPEARANCE, DEMEANOR AND MILITARY BEARING,
AND MUST MEET ALL HEALTH AND PHYSICAL READINESS STANDARDS.
    E.  OUTSTANDING RECORD OF PERFORMANCE.
    F.  BE A HIGHLY MOTIVATED ROLE MODEL.
    G.  ACTIVELY INVOLVED IN QUALITY OF LIFE INITIATIVES AND
PROGRAMS.
    H.  STRONG OVERALL CMC/CSC POTENTIAL.
    I.  NO MARK BELOW 3.0 ON FITNESS REPORTS FOR THE LAST FIVE
YEARS.  MUST MAINTAIN THESE STANDARDS WHILE ASSIGNED AS A CMC OR
CSC.

    J.  WORK EFFECTIVELY WITH ALL LEVELS OF THE CHA
IN OF COMMAND.
4.  VOLUNTEERS FOR PRIMARY DUTY CMC OR CSC MUST REQUEST ASSIGNMENT
TO THE PROGRAM PER REF A USING NAVPERS 1306/7 (ENLISTED PERSONNEL
ACTION REQUEST) SIGNED BY THEIR COMMANDING OFFICER (BY DIRECTION
IS NOT AUTHORIZED).
5.  COMMANDING OFFICERS SHALL CONDUCT FORMAL SCREENING (LOCAL ORAL
BOARD RECOMMENDED) AND CERTIFY IN WRITING THAT ALL QUALIFICATION
FACTORS FROM SECTION C OF REF A ARE MET.
6.  FORWARD ORIGINAL REQUESTS, WITH ENDORSEMENTS AND ENCLOSURES,
TO COMMANDER, NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-40FF), VIA THE IMMEDIATE
SUPERIOR IN COMMAND AND TYPE COMMANDER (FLEET, FORCE, OR
CNO-DIRECTED CMC) FOR FINAL ENDORSEMENT.  REQUESTS FROM NUCLEAR
TRAINED PERSONNEL SHALL BE FORWARDED VIA OPNAV (N133) FOR AN
ADDITIONAL ENDORSEMENT.  CMC AND CSC PROGRAM REQUESTS SHALL BE
FORMULATED IAW REF A AND THIS NAVADMIN INCLUDED AS ENCLOSURES:
    A.  ENCLOSURE (1) - A RECENT 5 BY 7 INCH FRONT AND PROFILE
PHOTO TAKEN IN SERVICE KHAKI.  PHOTOGRAPHS SHALL INCLUDE A DATA
BOARD INDICATING NAME, SSN, RANK/RATE, WARFARE QUALIFICATION, AND
DATE.

    B.  ENCLOSURE (2) - COPY OF PRIMS DATA FOR THE LAST SIX
PHYSICAL FITNESS ASSESSMENTS. FOR MISSING ASSESSMENTS, INCLUDE A
MEMO OF EXPLANATION SIGNED BY THE COMMAND MASTER CHIEF.
    C.  ENCLOSURE (3) - A SIGNED NAVPERS 1070/613 (ADMINISTRATIVE
REMARKS) PAGE 13 STATING, "I DESIRE TO SERVE AS A COMMAND MASTER

CMCS.
7.  INCOMPLETE REQUESTS WILL BE RETURNED WITHOUT BEING PRESENTED TO
THE SELECTION BOARD.
8.  THE BOARD WILL REVIEW AND SELECT THE BEST QUALIFIED APPLICANTS
FOR ASSIGNMENT TO THE CMC/CSC PROGRAM WITHOUT REGARD TO SOURCE
RATING OR COMMUNITY MANNING.  DESIGNATED CMCS ARE CLOSED-LOOP
DETAILED FOR THE REMAINDER OF THEIR CAREERS.
