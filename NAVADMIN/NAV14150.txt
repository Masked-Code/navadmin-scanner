RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN SOLICITS NOMINATION PACKAGES FOR THE FY-14 HUMAN 
RESOURCES (HR) COMMUNITY JUNIOR OFFICER OF THE YEAR (JOOY) AWARD.  THE CHIEF 
OF NAVAL PERSONNEL IS THE SPONSOR FOR THE ANNUAL HR JOOY AWARD.
    A.  THE HR COMMUNITY RECOGNIZES ONE JUNIOR OFFICER (LCDR OR
BELOW) EACH YEAR WHO HAS MADE SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE READINESS OF 
THE NAVY.
    B.  ALL HR JUNIOR OFFICERS ARE ELIGIBLE (1200, 1205 AND 1207).
    C.  THIS IS A FISCAL YEAR AWARD AND WILL BE BASED ON CONTRIBUTIONS MADE 
DURING FY-14.

2.  REF (A) PROVIDES BACKGROUND, DISCUSSION, AND GUIDANCE FOR COMMANDING 
OFFICERS AND/OR OFFICERS IN CHARGE TO NOMINATE ELIGIBLE OFFICERS.

3.  NOMINATIONS SHALL BE SUBMITTED PER REF (A) WITH THE FOLLOWING ADDITIONAL 
INFORMATION/MODIFICATIONS:
    A.  NOMINATION DEADLINE IS 17 SEPTEMBER 2014.
    B.  BLOCK 2 OF OPNAV 1650/3, PERSONAL AWARD RECOMMENDATION, SHOULD READ 
CHIEF OF NAVAL PERSONNEL, 701 S. COURTHOUSE ROAD, ARLINGTON, VA 22204.  BLOCK 
2A, UIC, SHOULD READ 45997.
    C.  COMPLETED NOMINATION PACKAGES SHOULD BE SUBMITTED IN PDF FORMAT (WITH 
THE NAVY AND MARINE CORPS COMMENDATION MEDAL CITATION SUBMITTED IN MICROSOFT 
WORD FORMAT) TO THE DIRECTOR, HUMAN RESOURCES CENTER OF EXCELLENCE VIA THE 
SAFE ACCESS FILE EXCHANGE AT HTTPS://SAFE.AMRDEC.ARMY.MIL/SAFE, NOTING 
HRCOE(AT)NPS.EDU AS THE RECIPIENT ADDRESS.  DO NOT SEND ORIGINALS VIA REGULAR 
MAIL.

4.  THE AWARD WINNER WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA NAVADMIN AND RECOGNIZED WITH A 
NAVY AND MARINE CORPS COMMENDATION MEDAL.

5.  POINT OF CONTACT IS THE HUMAN RESOURCES CENTER OF EXCELLENCE, AT 
COMMERCIAL (831) 656-2554/DSN 756 OR VIA E-MAIL AT HRCOE(AT)NPS.EDU.

6.  THIS MESSAGE WILL REMAIN IN EFFECT UNTIL SUPERSEDED OR CANCELED, 
WHICHEVER OCCURS FIRST.

7.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL W. F. MORAN, N1.//
