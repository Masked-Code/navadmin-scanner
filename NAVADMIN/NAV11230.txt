RMKS/1.  RECOGNIZING SUPERIOR PERFORMANCE IN INTRA-TYPE BATTLE EFFICIENCY, IT 
IS AN HONOR TO ANNOUNCE THE CALENDAR YEAR 2010 MARJORIE STERRETT BATTLESHIP 
FUND AWARD WINNERS:
A.  ATLANTIC FLEET:  PATROL COASTAL CREW LIMA B.  PACIFIC FLEET:  USS PATRIOT 
(MCM 7) 

2.  SELECTION FOR THIS AWARD REFLECTS THE EXTRAORDINARY PROFESSIONALISM AND 
DEDICATION OF THESE TWO UNITS.
A.  PATROL COASTAL CREW LIMA'S UNPARALLELED COMMITMENT TO COMPREHENSIVE 
MATERIAL AND PROFESSIONAL READINESS ENABLED THEM TO RAPIDLY RETURN USS CHINOOK 
(PC 9) TO FULL OPERATIONAL CAPABILITY BY COMPLETING A DRY DOCK PHASED 
MAINTENANCE AVAILABILITY COMPRISED OF MORE THAN 100 JOBS ON TIME.
B.  PATRIOT'S EXCEPTIONAL EXECUTION OF THEATER SECURITY COOPERATION ACTIVITIES 
WHILE FORWARD-DEPLOYED IN SEVENTH FLEET MEASURABLY ADVANCED MEANINGFUL 
RELATIONSHIPS WITH KEY ALLIES AND PARTNERS WHILE ENHANCING THEIR OPERATIONAL 
READINESS.

3.  CONGRATULATIONS TO PATROL COASTAL CREW LIMA AND THE CREW OF USS PATRIOT 
(MCM 7).  YOUR UNMATCHED DEDICATION AND PROFESSIONALISM EXEMPLIFIES THE 
HIGHEST IDEALS AND SPIRIT REPRESENTED BY THIS PRESTIGIOUS AWARD.

4.  RELEASED BY ADMIRAL G. ROUGHEAD, CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS.//
