RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE CANCELLATION OF THE FISCAL YEAR
(FY)-12 FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) COMMANDER SELECTIVE EARLY RELEASE FROM ACTIVE 
DUTY (SERAD) BOARD AND THE CONVENING OF THE FY-12 FTS CAPTAIN SELECTIVE 
RETENTION BOARD.

2.  BASED UPON UPDATED FTS COMMANDER INVENTORY PROJECTIONS, THE FTS COMMANDER 
SERAD BOARD HAS BEEN CANCELLED.

3.  THE FTS CAPTAIN SELECTIVE RETENTION (SELRET) BOARD IS DESIGNED TO BRING 
FTS CAPTAIN INVENTORY IN LINE WITH AUTHORIZED END STRENGTH, ALLOW FOR 
REASONABLE PROMOTION OPPORTUNITY, AND PERMIT SUFFICIENT JUNIOR OFFICER 
ACCESSIONS TO MEET FTS OFFICER OPERATIONAL REQUIREMENTS.

4.  AS REQUIRED BY REF A, THE FTS CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD WILL CONVENE ON 13 
SEPTEMBER 2011 TO CONSIDER FTS CAPTAINS FOR CONTINUATION BEYOND THEIR 
RESPECTIVE GATES.  THIS BOARD WILL CONSIDER FIRST GATE CAPTAINS WHO WERE 
PROMOTED DURING FY-10, AND SECOND GATE CAPTAINS WHO WERE PROMOTED DURING FY-
07.

5.  FIRST GATE FTS CAPTAINS WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT NUMBERS TO MAINTAIN 
FTS CAPTAIN END STRENGTH.  SECOND GATE CAPTAINS WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT 
NUMBERS TO FILL SPECIFIC SENIOR MANAGEMENT BILLETS.

6.  FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION WILL BE FORMALLY NOTIFIED AS SOON 
AS THE BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED.  PER REF A, FIRST GATE FTS CAPTAINS NOT 
SELECTED FOR RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED FROM ACTIVE DUTY NO LATER THAN THE 
DATE THEY ACHIEVE THREE YEARS TIME IN GRADE.
SECOND GATE FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED FROM 
ACTIVE DUTY NO LATER THAN 1 SEPTEMBER 2012.

7.  AMPLIFYING INFORMATION WILL BE POSTED ON THE PERS-9 AND FTS COMMUNITY 
MANAGER WEB SITES AS IT BECOMES AVAILABLE.  THE EXPECTED NUMBER OF 
CONTINUATIONS AND THE EFFECTIVE CONTINUATION RATE WILL BE POSTED NLT 2 
SEPTEMBER 2011.  THE FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT WEBPAGE IS FOUND AT:  
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/COMMUNITYMANAGERS/RESERVEBRANCH/FULLTIMESUPPOR
T/.

8.  COMMUNICATION TO THE CAPT SELRET BOARD SHALL FOLLOW THE SAME PROCEDURES 
AND POLICIES AS THAT FOR STATUTORY PROMOTION BOARDS.
ADDRESS ALL CORRESPONDENCE TO THE PRESIDENT, FTS CAPT SELRET BOARD #416.  FOR 
ADDITIONAL GUIDANCE, REFER TO THE GENERAL BOARD INFORMATION PAGE ON THE NPC 
WEB SITE AT:  HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/GENERALBOARDINFO/.

9.  POINTS OF CONTACT:

OR VIA E-MAIL AT DARREN.ANDING(AT)NAVY.MIL.
