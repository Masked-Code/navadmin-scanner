3.  ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
    A.  CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS OUTLINED IN REF B, 
CHAPTER 2, PRIOR TO EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION DATE FOR THEIR PAY GRADE.
REFER TO PARAGRAPH 1 OF THIS NAVADMIN FOR EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION DATES.
CANDIDATES WHO DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS ARE INELIGIBLE FOR 
ADVANCEMENT AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED A CYCLE 220 ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION.
    B.  TERMINAL ELIGIBILITY DATE (TED):  THE TED FOR CYCLE 220 USED TO 
COMPUTE TIR IS 1 JANUARY 2014.  E6 PERSONNEL WITH A TIR DATE OF 1 JANUARY
2011 OR EARLIER;  E5 WITH A TIR DATE OF 1 JANUARY 2013 OR EARLIER;  AND E4 
WITH A TIR DATE OF 1 JULY 2013 OR EARLIER ARE ELIGIBLE.  REFER TO REF B, TABLE 
2-2, FOR TIR REQUIREMENTS.
    C.  HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT):  PER REF C, CANDIDATES WHO REACH HYT ON OR 
AFTER 1 JANUARY 2014, OR WHO HAVE RECEIVED A HYT WAIVER TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE 
DUTY ON OR AFTER 1 JANUARY 2014, ARE ELIGIBLE TO PARTICIPATE IN THE 
ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION.  CANDIDATES WITH A HYT DATE PRIOR TO 1 JANUARY 2014 
ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED AN 
EXAMINATION.
    D.  PERFORM TO SERVE (PTS):  SAILORS WHO HAVE RECEIVED FINAL PTS DENIAL 
(DENIED FINAL - ACTIVE) AS OF THE JUNE 2013 APPLICATION RACK AND STACK, FOR 
WHICH RESULTS ARE AVAILABLE IN JULY, ARE INELIGIBLE AND SHALL NOT BE 
ADMINISTERED CYCLE 220 NWAE.
    E.  NON-DESIGNATED SAILORS:  COMMANDS ARE REQUIRED TO USE THE FLEET RATING 
IDENTIFICATION ENGINE (FLEET RIDE) TOOL TO VALIDATE RATING QUALIFICATIONS AND 
REQUEST RATING ENTRY VIA ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION QQQQ PARTICIPATION FOR NON-
DESIGNATED SAILORS.  COMPLETE FLEET RIDE APPLICATIONS IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF 
D.  ONLY FLEET RIDE APPLICATIONS WHICH HAVE BEEN SUBMITTED AND APPROVED DURING 
A MARCH 2013 THROUGH AUGUST 2013  RACK/STACK ARE VALID FOR CYCLE 220 
EXAMINATIONS.  NON-DESIGNATED SAILORS WHO DO NOT MEET THIS REQUIREMENT ARE NOT 
ELIGIBLE FOR RATING ENTRY VIA CYCLE 220 AND SHALL NOT BE ADMINISTERED AN 
EXAMINATION.
