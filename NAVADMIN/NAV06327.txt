REF/A/DOC/OPNAVINST 1524.2 (GRADUATE EDUCATION PLUS TEACHING PROGRAM)
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES APPLICATION PROCEDURES FOR 
THE FY-07 GRADUATE EDUCATION PLUS TEACHING (GET) PROGRAM.  THE UNITED 
STATES NAVAL ACADEMY (USNA) IS SEEKING MOTIVATED UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) 
OFFICERS WHO ARE QUALIFIED ACADEMICALLY AND PROFESSIONALLY TO APPLY FOR 
THE FY-07 GET PROGRAM TO SERVE AS BOTH TEACHERS  AND ROLE MODELS FOR 
OUR FUTURE NAVAL OFFICERS.
2.  THE GET PROGRAM PROVIDES APPROXIMATELY 12 MONTHS OF GRADUATE 
EDUCATION AT EITHER A SELECTED CIVILIAN INSTITUTION IN THE BALTIMORE, 
MD/WASHINGTON, DC AREA OR THE NAVAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL (NPS), DIRECTLY 
FOLLOWED BY A 2-ACADEMIC-YEAR TEACHING ASSIGNMENT AT USNA AS AN 
OFFICER-INSTRUCTOR, RESULTING IN A MAXIMUM 36 MONTH COMBINED TOUR.
3.  REF A PROVIDES SPECIFIC INFORMATION REGARDING AVAILABLE DISCIPLINES 
AND GRADUATE SCHOOLS, AS WELL AS APPLICATION GUIDANCE.
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION CAN BE FOUND ON THE USNA WEBSITE AT 
HTTP://WWW.USNA.EDU/ACDEAN/OFFPOS/GET.HTML.
4.  COMMUNITY SPECIFIC ELIGIBLE YEAR GROUPS AND ADDITIONAL ELIGIBILITY 
REQUIREMENTS.
    A.  SUBMARINE:
        (1) ELIGIBLE YG:  YG03
        (2) SATISFACTORY PERS-42 EVALUATION OF SOAC GATE AT 7 YCS
    B.  SURFACE:
        (1) ELIGIBLE YG:  YG03 - YG04 AND EARLY ROLLERS ON A CASE BY 
CASE BASIS DETERMINED BY PERS-41.
        (2) SWOCP CONTRACT MUST BE SUBMITTED IMMEDIATELY UPON SELECTION 
FOR THE GET PROGRAM.
5.  TIMELINE:
    A.  23 FEB 07:  APPLICATIONS DUE TO COMMANDER, NAVY PERSONNEL 
COMMAND (PERS-440F).
    B.  9 MAR 07:  APPLICANTS INFORMED OF SELECTION/NON SELECTION.
    C.  JUN - AUG 07:  SELECTEES REPORT TO GRADUATE SCHOOL BASED UPON 
CURRICULUM START DATES.
