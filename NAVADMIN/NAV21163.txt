RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces a modification to paragraphs 8.a and 10.e of reference (a).  The 
modification adds the convening date for the Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 Active Duty Navy O-7 Line and Staff 
Promotion Selection Boards, as well as the letter to the board (LTB) submission deadline for the FY-22 
Active Duty Navy Expanded Continuation Selection Board.

2.  Paragraph 8.a is modified to read:  The FY-23 Active-Duty Navy
O-7 Line and Staff Promotion Selection Boards convene 21 September 2021.  The FY-22 Active Duty Navy 
Line and Staff Corps O-6 Expanded Continuation Selection Boards will follow these boards.

3.  Paragraph 10.e is modified to read:  LTBs must be received by Navy Personnel Command not later 
than 2359 (Central Standard Time)
(CST) 10 calendar days prior to the convening date of the respective board, as noted below.  Submitters 
are cautioned to not wait until the deadline date to submit LTBs via electronic submission of selection 
board documents or other available avenues.
Continuation For          Convening Date     Due Date / Time
URL/RL/Staff Corps CAPT   21 SEP 21          10 SEP 21 / 2359 CST

4.  All other provisions of reference (a), remain in effect.

5.  This NAVADMIN will be canceled for record purposes on 30 September 2021.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral John B. Nowell, Jr, N1.//
