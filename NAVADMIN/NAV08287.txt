2.  THE FOLLOWING GUIDANCE IS PROVIDED:  THIS SELECTION BOARD WILL 
RECOMMEND ONE OFFICER FOR APPOINTMENT AS LEGAL COUNSEL TO THE CJCS.  
THE BOARD WILL CONSIDER JUDGE ADVOCATES FROM THE ARMY, NAVY, AIR FORCE 
AND MARINE CORPS.  NO WEIGHT WILL BE GIVEN TO BRANCH OF SERVICE OR 
SOURCE OF COMMISSION.  THE OFFICER SELECTED MUST HAVE SIGNIFICANT JOINT 
EXPERIENCE, DEFINED AS: 
HAVING COMPLETED AT LEAST ONE YEAR IN THE GRADE OF O-5/O-6 ON THE JOINT 
STAFF, A COMBATANT COMMAND, THE OFFICE OF SECRETARY OF DEFENSE, OR A 
JOINT TASK FORCE (JTF).  OTHER SIGNIFICANT JOINT DUTY WHILE ASSIGNED TO 
A JTF MAY BE CALCULATED CUMULATIVELY, INCLUDING ASSIGNMENTS TO JOINT 
INDIVIDUAL AUGMENTATION (IA) BILLETS.  UPON APPROVAL BY THE PRESIDENT 
OF THE UNITED STATES, THE OFFICER SELECTED WILL BE APPOINTED TO THE 
TEMPORARY GRADE OF BRIGADIER GENERAL OR REAR ADMIRAL (LOWER HALF).
