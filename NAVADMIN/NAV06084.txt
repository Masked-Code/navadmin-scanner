SUBJ/ANNUAL NAVY COUNSELORS ASSOCIATION (NCA) SYMPOSIUM//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE NCA WILL CONDUCT ITS 18TH ANNUAL SYMPOSIUM AT
THE COOK CONVENTION CENTER IN MEMPHIS, TN, FROM 10-14 JUL 06.  THIS
YEARS THEME IS:  "CAREER DEVELOPMENT:  SAILORS TAKING CARE OF
SAILORS."
2.  THE SYMPOSIUM PROVIDES AN OPPORTUNITY TO DISCUSS A BROAD RANGE
OF CAREER ISSUES AND CONCERNS, ALLOWS MEMBERS TO ADDRESS AND WORK
TOWARD INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS TO CURRENT AND FUTURE RETENTION,
ATTRITION, AND CAREER DEVELOPMENT ISSUES.  COMMAND CAREER
COUNSELORS, COMMAND MASTER CHIEFS, CAREER DEVELOPMENT TEAM MEMBERS,
RECRUITERS, DETAILERS, AND OTHER MILITARY AND CIVILIAN PERSONNEL
INVOLVED IN THE PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF SAILORS ARE ENCOURAGED
TO ATTEND.
3.  TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY ORDERS ARE REQUIRED TO COVER EXPENSES,
INCLUDING THE REGISTRATION FEE OF $125.00.  THERE IS NO GOVERNMENT
MESSING AVAILABLE IN THE SYMPOSIUM AREA.  HOTEL RESERVATION
INFORMATION IS AVAILABLE AT WWW.USNCA.ORG.
4.  EACH YEAR DURING THE SYMPOSIUM, THE NCA RECOGNIZES TWO CAREER
COUNSELORS OF THE YEAR, ONE FROM SEA DUTY (TYPE 2,3, OR 4) AND ONE
FROM SHORE DUTY (TYPE 1 OR 6).  COMMANDS MAY NOMINATE ASSIGNED
CAREER COUNSELORS (E6 AND BELOW) USING THE FORMAT IN THE NCA BY-LAWS
AVAILABLE AT WWW.USNCA.ORG.  NOMINATIONS SHOULD ADDRESS
SPECIFIC ACCOMPLISHMENTS AND CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE CAREER COUNSELOR
COMMUNITY DURING THE PAST YEAR.  PACKAGES MUST REACH THE BELOW
