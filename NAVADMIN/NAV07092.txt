SUBJ/AUG 07 (CYCLE 081) NAVY-WIDE EXAMINATIONS FOR ADVANCEMENT IN RATE 
OF NAVY RESERVE PERSONNEL TO PAYGRADES E4 THROUGH E6// 
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THAT THE INACTIVE RESERVE 
ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATIONS WILL BE ADMINISTERED IN AUGUST 2007 FOR
E4/E5/E6 CANDIDATES.  ALL CANDIDATES SERVING ON VOLUNTARY RECALL,
ACTIVE DUTY TRAINING, OR ACTIVE DUTY UNDER ANY PRESIDENTIAL RECALL 
AUTHORITY,INCLUDING MOBILIZATION, WILL COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT AS 
SELECTED RESERVE INACTIVE (USNR-INACTIVE) IN CYCLE 081.  INDIVIDUAL 
READY RESERVE (IRR) PERSONNEL WHO ARE MOBILIZED ARE CONSIDERED UNDER 
THE SELECTED RESERVE (SELRES) REQUIREMENTS FOR ADVANCEMENT.
MOBILIZED IRR AND SELRES PERSONNEL WHO ARE HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT) FOR 
THEIR CURRENT PAYGRADE MUST SATISFY ADVANCEMENT ELIGIBILITY AS OUTLINED 
IN MILPERSMAN 1160-130 PARAGRAPH 7.  REFER TO NAVADMIN
290/06 REGARDING ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATIONS FOR SAILORS ASSIGNED IN IRAQ, 
AFGHANISTAN, AND THE HORN OF AFRICA.
THIS NAVADMIN IS A SUPPLEMENT TO BUPERSINST 1430.16E.
2.  TIMELINE OF EVENTS.
    A.  MAY 07:  INTERNET REVIEW AND MODIFICATION OF TIME IN RATE
(TIR) LISTS FOR EXAM ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE.  USE NETPDTC WEB SITE 
AT HTTPS://NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL/NEAS TO REVIEW TIR LIST.
COMMAND EDUCATIONAL SERVICES OFFICERS (ESO) WITH INTERNET ACCESS MAY 
REGISTER FOR ACCESS AT ABOVE WEBSITE.
    B.  MAY THROUGH JUN 07:  RATING EXAMINATION ORDERING WILL BE 
AVAILABLE AS FOLLOWS: COMMANDS WITH CRYPTOLOGIC TECHNICIANS
(COLLECTION) OR CRYPTOLOGIC TECHNICIAN (NETWORK) CANDIDATES MUST
ORDER SCI EXAMINATIONS BETWEEN 7 MAY THROUGH 28 JUN 07.  OTHER RATING 
EXAMINATIONS ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE BETWEEN 28 MAY THROUGH 24 JUN 
07.  SUPPLEMENTAL EXAMINATION ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE BETWEEN 25 JUN 
- 29 JUL 07.  (NOTE:  CANRECS, RECALLED MOBILIZED SAILORS AND RESCORE 
CANDIDATES ALL MUST ORDER VIA MESSAGE AS INTERNET ORDERING IS NOT 
AVAILABLE TO THOSE PERSONNEL).
    C.  MAY THROUGH JUN 07:  COMMANDS REVIEW TIR LISTS AND TAKE THE 
FOLLOWING ACTIONS:
        (1) ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES NOT LISTED - COMMANDS MUST ADD NAME, 
SSN, AND RATE TO THE TIR LIST.
        (2) CANDIDATES NOT ELIGIBLE OR NO LONGER RECOMMENDED FOR 
ADVANCEMENT - COMMANDS MUST CLICK NO AND SUBMIT BUTTON TO DELETE NAMES 
FROM TIR LIST.
        (3) TO RECOMMEND FOR ADVANCEMENT, COMMANDS MUST ESTABLISH 
PERFORMANCE MARK AVERAGE AS WELL AS VERIFY CANDIDATES HAVE AN 
EVALUATION IN CURRENT PAYGRADE.
3.  BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
    A.  SELRES CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS 
PER BUPERSINST 1430.16E, OPNAVINST 1160.7B AND THE LATEST DRILLING 
RESERVE ENLISTED CAREER MANAGEMENT OBJECTIVES CONTROL PROGRAM (ECMO) 
NOT LATER THAN 1 JUL 07.  CANDIDATES MUST MAINTAIN COMMANDING OFFICER'S 
RECOMMENDATION TO REMAIN ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT.
    B.  LEADERSHIP CONTINUUM:  REFER TO NAVADMIN 339/06 FOR GRACE 
PERIOD AND WAIVER POLICIES FOR ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION PARTICIPATION.
 MOBILIZED IRR SAILORS ARE EXEMPT FROM NLDP REQUIREMENTS.
4.  EVALUATIONS USED TO COMPUTE PERFORMANCE MARK AVERAGES MUST BE 
INCLUSIVE OF THE PERIODS LISTED BELOW:
    PROSPECTIVE           EVALUATION    COMPUTATION
    PAYGRADE              PAYGRADE      PERIOD
    E4                    E3            1 AUG 05 TO 31 JUL 07
    E5                    E4            1 JUL 05 TO 31 JUL 07
    E6                    E5            1 APR 05 TO 31 JUL 07
    A.  IF A SERVICE MEMBER DOES NOT HAVE AN EVALUATION WITHIN THE 
PERIOD SPECIFIED, PREPARE A SPECIAL EVALUATION WITH BLOCK 15 REFLECTING 
31 JUL 07 FOR E4, E5, AND E6 CANDIDATES.
5.  GENERAL EXAMINATION/ADVERSE INFO:
    A.  EXAMINATIONS WILL BE ADMINISTERED PER CHAPTER 6 OF BUPERSINST 
1430.16E.  DO NOT ADMINISTER ANY OTHER CYCLE 081 EXAMINATIONS PRIOR TO 
1 AUG 07 UNLESS SPECIFICALLY AUTHORIZED BY COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4812).
    B.  EXAMINATIONS FOR RECALLED RESERVISTS WILL BE ORDERED BY THE 
COMMAND HOLDING THE MEMBER'S SERVICE RECORD.  AUTHORITY IS GRANTED TO 
ADMINISTER EXAMINATIONS TO SELECTED RESERVE CANDIDATES ON ACTIVE DUTY 
WITH REGULAR NAVY CANDIDATES 6 SEP 07 FOR E6, 13 SEP 07 FOR E5, AND 20 
SEP 07 FOR E4.  THESE CANDIDATES ARE STILL REQUIRED TO TAKE THE CYCLE 
081 RESERVE EXAMINATION AND NOT THE ACTIVE DUTY EXAMINATION.
    C.  ADVANCEMENT TO E4 AND RATING ENTRY FOR NON-DESIGNATED PERSONNEL 
ARE GOVERNED BY THE LATEST ECMO.  NON-DESIGNATED MEMBERS WHO REQUIRE AN 
"A" SCHOOL WAIVER FOR ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION PARTICIPATION PER THE 
LATEST ECMO MUST SUBMIT A REQUEST TO PERS-4812 POSTMARKED BY 15 JUN 07.
    D.  ENSURE BLOCKS 12 AND 13 OF THE ADVANCEMENT IN RATE OR CHANGE OF 
RATING (WORKSHEET) (NAVEDTRA 1430/2) ARE COMPLETED.  AT THE EDUCATION 
SERVICES OFFICER'S DISCRETION, WORKSHEETS MAY BE SIGNED EITHER PRIOR TO 
EXAMINATION OR AT THE EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION SITE.
    E.  LATE EXAMINATION ORDERS MUST BE HELD TO A MINIMUM TO ALLOW TIME 
FOR ADEQUATE PROCESSING AND QUOTA DETERMINATION.  REQUESTS FOR LATE 
EXAMINATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED USING SAMPLE FORMAT ON TABLE 4-3 OF 
BUPERSINST 1430.16E VIA MESSAGE TO NETPDTC (N321), WITH COMNAVPERSCOM 
MILLINGTON, TN (PERS-4812) AS INFO ADDRESSEE.  REQUESTS MUST INCLUDE 
MEMBER'S NAME, RATE, LAST FOUR OF THE SSN, AND JUSTIFICATION FOR 
MISSING REGULAR EXAMINATION.  NO ACTION WILL BE TAKEN ON REQUESTS 
RECEIVED WITHOUT REQUIRED INFO/JUSTIFICATION.
ENSURE "ADMIN ERROR" AND "OPERATIONAL COMMITMENT" ARE CLARIFIED WHEN 
USED AS JUSTIFICATION.  REQUESTS RECEIVED BY NETPDTC AFTER 14 SEP 07 
WILL BE DISAPPROVED.  LATE EXAMINATIONS MUST BE ADMINISTERED NLT 21 SEP 
07.  WHEN A CANDIDATE JUSTIFIABLY MISSES A REGULAR EXAMINATION AND 
EXAMINATION IS ONBOARD, DO NOT DESTROY EXAMINATION.  SUBMIT A REQUEST 
AS ABOVE FOR PERMISSION TO RETAIN THE EXAMINATION FOR LATE 
ADMINISTRATION.
    F.  FORWARDING EXAMINATION RETURNS.  EXAMINATION GRADING REQUIRES A 
90 PERCENT RECEIPT OF RETURNS TO NETPDTC.  FORWARD RATE OF NAVY RESERVE 
PERSONNEL TO PAYGRADES E4 THROUGH E6 EXAMINATION ANSWER SHEETS IN THE 
FOLLOWING MANNER:
        (1) CONUS INSTALLATIONS.  MAIL EXAMINATION ANSWER SHEETS VIA 
FEDEX UNDER THE CURRENT GSA CONTRACT BY CLOSE OF BUSINESS OF THE DAY 
FOLLOWING EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION (MONDAY AFTER DRILL WEEKEND).

THE POSTAL OFFICER WILL THEN HAVE THE PACKAGE SENT VIA USPS THREE DAY 
EXPRESS MAIL TO NETPDTC.
    G.  EXAMINATION RESULTS ARE PROVIDED TO SUPPORTING ESO_S ONLINE.  
AUTHORIZED REPRESENTATIVES ACCESS THE OFFICIAL RESULTS, WITH A PASSWORD, 
THROUGH NETPDTC'S EXAMINATION ORDERING SITE.  CLICK ON EXAMINATION 
STATUS VERIFICATION REPORT (ESVR), RATE CHANGE AUTHORIZATION (RCA), 
PROFILE SHEETS, AND COMMAND STATISTICS.  THE ESVR AND RCA ARE THE 
OFFICIAL SOURCE FOR NAVY ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION RESULTS.  ESO_S MUST 
PROVIDE COPIES TO SUPPORTED ACTIVITIES UPON RECEIPT.  INDIVIDUAL 
SAILORS MAY ALSO VIEW/DOWNLOAD THEIR OWN PROFILE SHEET FIVE DAYS AFTER 
RESULTS ARE RELEASED, FROM HTTPS://WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL 
(SECURE SITE CLICK ON _MY ADVANCEMENT LINK).
    H.  ALL COMMANDS SHOULD MONITOR THE ELECTRONIC TOTE BOARD LOCATED 
AT HTTPS://WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL TO VERIFY THAT ANSWER SHEETS 
ARE RECEIVED BY NETPDTC.
6.  EXAMINATION DISCREPANCIES.  TO CLEAR EXAMINATION DISCREPANCIES 
CREATED BY INCORRECT DATA, SUBMIT THE CORRECTED INFO AS REQUIRED BY 
TABLE 7-3 OF BUPERSINST 1430.16E TO NETPDTC (N321) AND MAKE APPROPRIATE 
NSIPS ENTRIES. A LIST OF OUTSTANDING DISCREPANCIES WILL BE UPDATED 
DAILY AND VIEWED AT HTTPS://WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL.
7.  AEROGRAPHER_S MATE (AG) THIRD CLASS PETTY OFFICER CANDIDATES WILL 
TAKE A MULTIMEDIA EXAMINATION.  SHORE FACILITIES WILL USE DESIGNATED 
EXISTING COMPUTER ASSETS.  AFLOAT ACTIVITIES WILL USE DESIGNATED 
EXISTING COMPUTER ASSETS UNLESS PRIOR ARRANGEMENTS ARE MADE FOR 
COMPUTERS TO BE PROVIDED BY NETPDTC.  COMMANDS WITH AG3 CANDIDATES WILL 
RECEIVE ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FROM NETPDTC.
8.  POC:  PSC M. M. MEISER AT (901) 874-4500/DSN 882 OR EMAIL AT 
MARGARET.MEISER(AT)NAVY.MIL.
