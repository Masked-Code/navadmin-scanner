RMKS/1.  THE U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL SELECTION BOARD IS SCHEDULED 
TO CONVENE 20 FEB 08, PER REF A.  THIS BOARD WILL SELECT HIGHLY 
QUALIFIED NAVAL AVIATORS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS FOR THE U.S. NAVAL 
TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USNTPS), AIR FORCE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY/U.S. 
NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL (AFIT/USNTPS) COOPERATIVE PROGRAM (COOP), AND 
THE U.S. AIR FORCE TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USAFTPS).
2.  ELIGIBILITY:
    A.  UNRESTRICTED LINE (13XX) AVIATORS MUST BE DUE COURSE JUNIOR 
OFFICERS (O-2 OR O-3) WITH FORECASTED TIMING TO COMPLETE THE SELECTED 
TEST PILOT SCHOOL (TPS) PROGRAM, A MINIMUM 24-MONTH TEST TOUR AND 
RETURN FOR SQUADRON DEPT HEAD TOUR NOT LATER THAN SCREEN GROUP PLUS
11 YEARS. 
    B.  AEROSPACE ENGINEERING DUTY OFFICER (1510) AVIATORS, THROUGH THE 
GRADE OF LIEUTENANT COMMANDER, ARE ELIGIBLE TO APPLY IF ABLE TO MEET 
CAREER MILESTONES AND ABLE TO SERVE A MINIMUM 24-MONTH FLIGHT TEST TOUR 
PRIOR TO FORECASTED IN-ZONE ELIGIBILITY FOR PROMOTION TO COMMANDER. 
3.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION, A COMPLETE APPLICATION 
LETTER, PER REF A, AND COMMAND ENDORSEMENT MUST BE RECEIVED AT THE NPC 
CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER (CSC) NOT LATER THAN 11 JAN 08.  THE 
ENDORSEMENT SHOULD CONFIRM THE APPLICANT, IF SELECTED, IS AVAILABLE TO 
REPORT FOR INITIAL TRAINING AS EARLY AS APR 08.  THE SERVICE MEMBER?S 
EMAIL ADDRESS(ES), PHONE NUMBER(S), COLLEGE TRANSCRIPTS AND POST-TPS 
TEST SQUADRON ASSIGNMENT PREFERENCE MUST BE INCLUDED IN THE APPLICATION.
