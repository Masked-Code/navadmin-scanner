2.  SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS FOR NUCLEAR TRAINED PERSONNEL:
    A.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E9, NUCLEAR SUBMARINE SAILORS MUST 
HOLD A SUPERVISORY NEC (3363, 3364, 3365, 3366).
PERSONNEL IN SUBMARINE NUCLEAR SUPERVISORY RATINGS (NEC 3363, 3364, 3365, 
3366) MUST ALSO HOLD SECONDARY NEC 3302 AND HAVE SERVED OR BE CURRENTLY 
SERVING AS AN ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT MASTER CHIEF (EDMC) OF A SUBMARINE.  
SAILORS WITHOUT EDMC SERVICE DOCUMENTED IN THEIR OFFICIAL MILITARY PERSONNEL 
FILE (OMPF) MUST SUBMIT DOCUMENTATION IN THEIR LTB WHICH MUST BE RECEIVED BY 2 
APRIL 2012.  ACCEPTABLE DOCUMENTATION OF EDMC SERVICE FOR REVIEW BY THE 
ADVANCEMENT BOARD INCLUDES AN EVALUATION WITH "EDMC" IN BLOCK 29 OR A LETTER 
FROM THE COMMANDING OFFICER VIA THE MEMBER.  DOCUMENTATION MUST BE SUBMITTED 
WITH THEIR LTB PROVIDING DATES OF EDMC SERVICE.
    B.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E9, NUCLEAR SURFACE SAILORS MUST 
HOLD A SUPERVISORY NEC (3393, 3394, 3395, 3396), AND MUST HAVE SERVED, OR BE 
CURRENTLY SERVING AS, A DIVISION LEADING CHIEF PETTY OFFICER OF A NUCLEAR 
DIVISION AT SEA (REACTOR MECHANICAL, REACTOR ELECTRICAL, REACTOR CONTROLS, 
REACTOR PROPULSION, MECHANICAL, OR REACTOR LABORATORIES), REACTOR TRAINING 
DIVISION OFFICER AT SEA, OR LEADING CREW CHIEF (LCC) OF A PROTOTYPE.
SAILORS WITHOUT DLCPO, RT DIVO, OR LCC SERVICE DOCUMENTED IN THEIR OMPF MUST 
SUBMIT DOCUMENTATION IN THEIR LTB WHICH MUST BE RECEIVED BY 2 APRIL 2012.  
ACCEPTABLE DOCUMENTATION OF LCPO, REACTOR TRAINING OFFICER, OR LCC SERVICE FOR 
REVIEW BY THE ADVANCEMENT BOARD INCLUDES
(1) AN EVALUATION WITH "DLCPO," "RT DIVO," OR "LCC" IN BLOCK 29 OR
(2) LETTER FROM THE COMMANDING OFFICER VIA THE MEMBER PROVIDING DATES OF 
DLCPO, RT DIVO, OR LCC SERVICE.  DOCUMENTATION MUST BE SUBMITTED WITH THEIR 
LTB PROVIDING DATES OF DLCPO, RT DIVO, OR LCC SERVICE.
    C.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT TO E8, NUCLEAR SAILORS MUST HOLD A 
SUPERVISORY NEC (3363, 3364, 3365, 3366 FOR SUBMARINE SAILORS; 3393, 3394, 
3395, 3396 FOR SURFACE SAILORS).  PERSONNEL IN SUBMARINE NUCLEAR RATINGS (NEC 
3363, 3364, 3365, 3366) MUST ALSO HAVE QUALIFIED AS ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT 
MASTER CHIEF (EDMC) AND HOLD SECONDARY NEC 3302.  A NAVPERS 1221 REQUESTING A 
SECONDARY NEC OF 3302 MUST BE SUBMITTED TO OPNAV N133 BY 13 APRIL 2012.  EMAIL 
NAVPERS 1221 AND COPY OF QUALIFICATION CARD TO NXAG_N133D2(AT)NAVY.MIL.
    D.  TO BE SELECTION BOARD ELIGIBLE FOR E7, PERSONNEL IN NUCLEAR RATINGS 
MUST HAVE QUALIFIED AS ENGINEERING WATCH SUPERVISOR (EWS) OR PROPULSION PLANT 
WATCH SUPERVISOR (PPWS).  IF A MEMBER'S OMPF DOES NOT ALREADY INCLUDE 
DOCUMENTATION OF EWS OR PPWS QUALIFICATION, THIS DOCUMENTATION MUST BE 
RECEIVED BY 4 JUNE 2012.  ACCEPTABLE DOCUMENTATION OF EWS OR PPWS 
QUALIFICATION INCLUDES AN EVALUATION WITH "EWS" OR "PPWS" IN BLOCK 29, OR A 
LETTER FROM THE COMMANDING OFFICER VIA THE MEMBER WHICH CERTIFIES EWS OR PPWS 
QUALIFICATION.
DOCUMENTATION MUST BE SUBMITTED WITH THEIR LTB WHICH CERTIFIES EWS OR PPWS 
QUALIFICATION.
