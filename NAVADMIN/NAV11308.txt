RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES CHANGES TO THE ENLISTED SUPERVISOR RETENTION 
PAY (ESRP) PROGRAM.  EFFECTIVE 1 JANUARY 2012, REFS B AND C ARE CANCELLED.  
ALL CHANGES ANNOUNCED IN THIS NAVADMIN WILL BE EFFECTIVE ON 1 JANUARY 2012.  
ESRP-ELIGIBLE SAILORS AND COMMAND CAREER COUNSELORS SHOULD REVIEW REF A, WHICH 
PROVIDES FURTHER PROGRAM DETAILS.  REF A CAN BE FOUND ON THE NUCLEAR ENLISTED 
COMMUNITY MANAGER WEBSITE AT:  WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-
NPC/ENLISTED/COMMUNITY/NUCLEAR/.

2.  SUMMARY.  TO ENCOURAGE SERVICE IN THE NUCLEAR ENLISTED COMMUNITY'S MOST 
CHALLENGING BILLETS AND TO PROVIDE INCENTIVES THAT PROMOTE CAREER LONGEVITY, 
THE CHIEF OF NAVAL PERSONNEL HAS RESTRUCTURED ESRP TO BE AVAILABLE FROM 10 
THROUGH 23 YEARS OF  SERVICE.  THIS CHANGE INVOLVES RESTRUCTURING ZONES C AND 
D TO CREATE ZONES 1, 2, AND 3.  ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS FOR THESE NEW ZONES 
ARE DETAILED IN PARAGRAPHS 6, 7 AND 8 BELOW.  THESE NEW  ZONES AND ELIGIBILITY 
REQUIREMENTS ESTABLISH A CLEAR CAREER PATH FOR  NUCLEAR SAILORS TO ACHIEVE THE 
PINNACLE OF NUCLEAR SERVICE:  DUTY AS  A SUBMARINE ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT 
MASTER CHIEF (EDMC), CVN DIVISION  LEADING CHIEF PETTY OFFICER (LCPO), OR CVN 
REACTOR DEPARTMENT MASTER  CHIEF (RDMC).

3.  ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER.  DEPUTY CHIEF OF NAVAL OPERATIONS, (MANPOWER, 
PERSONNEL, TRAINING AND EDUCATION) POLICY DIVISION (N133D) IS DESIGNATED AS 
THE ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER.

4.  ALL ESRP REENLISTMENT REQUESTS FOR 1 JANUARY 2012 OR LATER WILL BE 
PROCESSED UNDER THE RESTRUCTURED PROGRAM.  ANY ESRP REQUESTS ENTERED IN THE 
OFFICER PERSONNEL INFORMATION SYSTEM (OPINS) PRIOR TO 1 JANUARY 2012 FOR A 
REENLISTMENT ON OR AFTER 1 JANUARY 2012 WILL NOT BE PROCESSED UNTIL THE ESRP 
PROGRAM MANAGER MANUALLY CHANGES THE REQUEST TO THE NEW PROGRAM FORMAT.

5.  PROGRAM OVERVIEW.  ESRP USES THE CRITICAL SKILLS RETENTION BONUS
 (CSRB) LEGISLATIVE AUTHORITY TO PROVIDE A RETENTION INCENTIVE FOR MEMBERS 
ASSIGNED TO NUCLEAR NAVY ENLISTED CLASSIFICATION (NEC) CODES.
    A.  ESRP PROGRAM EXECUTION WILL BE SIMILAR TO THE SELECTIVE REENLISTMENT 
BONUS (SRB) PROGRAM.
    B.  THE ESRP PAYMENT STRUCTURE IS IDENTICAL TO THE SRB PROGRAM, IN WHICH 
50 PERCENT OF THE CONTRACT IS PAID UPON CONTRACT EXECUTION AND THE REMAINDER 
IS PAID IN EQUAL INSTALLMENTS ON 1 OCTOBER FOR EACH REMAINING YEAR OF THE ESRP 
REENLISTMENT CONTRACT.
    C.  INDIVIDUAL ESRP REENLISTMENT CONTRACTS ARE LIMITED TO 100K.
    D.  SAILORS IN ZONES A AND B ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR ESRP, BUT CONTINUE TO BE 
ELIGIBLE FOR REENLISTMENT INCENTIVES UNDER THE SRB PROGRAM.  SAILORS WHO ARE 
CURRENTLY IN ZONE B AND WITHIN 120 DAYS OF ENTERING ZONE 1 ARE ELIGIBLE TO 
REQUEST ZONE 1 ESRP FOR A PENDING ZONE 1 REENLISTMENT.
    E.  ESRP REQUESTS FOR PERSONNEL BEING PROCESSED FOR NEC REMOVAL OR 
RESTORATION WILL BE HELD IN ABEYANCE PENDING FINAL RESOLUTION.

6.  ESRP ZONE 1 ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS ARE IN ADDITION TO THE REQUIREMENTS 
OF PARAGRAPH 5 OF REF A.
    A.  COMPLETION OF 10 YEARS OF SERVICE THROUGH COMPLETION OF 14 YEARS OF 
SERVICE.
    B.  ADDITIONAL OBLIGATED SERVICE (AOS) THROUGH COMPLETION OF 16 YEARS OF 
SERVICE MAY BE USED IN THE AWARD CALCULATION.

7.  ESRP ZONE 2 ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS ARE IN ADDITION TO THE REQUIREMENTS 
OF PARAGRAPH 5 OF REF A.
    A.  COMPLETION OF 14 YEARS OF SERVICE THROUGH COMPLETION OF 18 YEARS OF 
SERVICE.
    B.  MUST BE A FROCKED CPO OR IN PAYGRADE E7, E8, OR E9 ON THE DATE OF THE 
ESRP ZONE 2 REENLISTMENT.
    C.  AWARD CALCULATION IS ONLY PAYABLE THROUGH COMPLETION OF 18 YEARS OF 
SERVICE.

8.  ESRP ZONE 3 ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS ARE IN ADDITION TO THE REQUIREMENTS 
OF PARAGRAPH
5 OF REF A.
    A.  COMPLETION OF 18 YEARS OF SERVICE THROUGH COMPLETION OF 23 YEARS OF 
SERVICE.
    B.  MUST BE A FROCKED SCPO OR IN PAYGRADE E8 OR E9 ON THE DATE OF THE ESRP 
ZONE 3 REENLISTMENT.
    C.  MUST HAVE PREVIOUSLY SERVED AS, BE CURRENTLY SERVING AS, OR HAVE 
ACCEPTED ORDERS AS A SUBMARINE EDMC, CVN LCPO, OR CVN RDMC.
    D.  AWARD CALCULATION IS ONLY PAYABLE THROUGH COMPLETION OF 23 YEARS OF 
SERVICE.

10.  APPLICATION PROCESS:  ESRP REENLISTMENT REQUESTS WILL BE SUBMITTED 35-120 
DAYS IN ADVANCE OF THE MEMBER'S DESIRED REENLISTMENT DATE TO ENSURE THE 
APPROVAL MESSAGE CAN BE RELEASED IN A TIMELY MANNER.  EXCEPTIONS TO THIS 
POLICY WILL BE APPROVED BY THE ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER ON AN INDIVIDUAL BASIS.
    A.  OPINS FORCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (FORMAN) IS PROGRAMMED TO PROCESS ESRP 
REENLISTMENT REQUESTS AND EMPLOYS THE SAME PROCESS USED TO REQUEST AND APPROVE 
SRB REENLISTMENT REQUESTS.
CAREER COUNSELORS AND PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS ARE FAMILIAR WITH THIS 
PROGRAM AND SHOULD USE IT AS THE PRIMARY METHOD OF ESRP REQUEST SUBMISSION.  
THE ESRP REQUEST TYPE IS 3AA FOR SAILORS ON ACTIVE DUTY AND 3BB FOR BROKEN 
SERVICE REQUESTS.
    B.  IN THE EVENT OPINS IS NOT AVAILABLE, THE COMMAND CAN FAX, EMAIL 
(SCANNED COPY OF REQUEST), OR MAIL THE REQUEST DIRECTLY TO THE ESRP PROGRAM 
MANAGER UTILIZING THE FORMAT FOUND IN THE ESRP POLICY MEMORANDUM (REF A).
 IF OPERATIONAL COMMITMENTS LIMIT THE ABILITY TO SUBMIT ESRP APPLICATIONS VIA 
THIS MANNER, COMMANDS SHOULD SUBMIT THE ESRP REQUEST VIA MESSAGE TO THEIR 
IMMEDIATE SUPERIOR IN CHARGE (ISIC),  WHO CAN SUBMIT THE OPINS REQUEST.  ESRP 
REQUESTS SENT DIRECTLY TO THE ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER BY MESSAGE OR MAIL MAY 
RESULT IN A PROCESSING DELAY.
    C.  ZONE 1 SAILORS MUST HAVE A VALID APPLICATION ENTERED IN THE PERFORM TO 
SERVE (PTS) SYSTEM TO BE ELIGIBLE TO REENLIST.  PTS SUBMISSION CAN BE MADE IN 
PARALLEL WITH THE OPINS REQUEST FOR ESRP.
    D.  A SAILOR MUST BE PREAPPROVED FOR AN ESRP REENLISTMENT.  ESRP APPROVAL 
WILL NOT CURRENTLY NOTIFY THE DEFENSE JOINT MILITARY PAY SYSTEM (DJMS) OF THE 
SAILOR'S INTENT TO REENLIST.  UNTIL DJMS CAN BE PROGRAMMED TO ACCEPT ESRP 
REENLISTMENT PAYMENTS, MANUAL PROCESSING IS REQUIRED.  PAYMENTS SHOULD POST TO 
THE SAILOR'S ACCOUNT WITHIN 14 DAYS FOLLOWING THE DAY THE ESRP PROGRAM MANAGER 
RECEIVES NOTIFICATION OF REENLISTMENT.  INITIAL PAYMENT WILL BE AUTHORIZED AS 
SOON AS THE CONTRACT EXECUTION IS REFLECTED IN THE ENLISTED MASTER FILE (EMF).  
IN ORDER TO ENSURE PROMPT PAYMENT, NEW CONTRACTS MUST BE POSTED TO THE EMF IN 
A TIMELY MANNER.
    E.  PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS AND COMMANDS SHOULD CONTACT THE ESRP 
PROGRAM MANAGER (N133D) VICE DFAS TO RESOLVE ANY PAYMENT ISSUES.
    F.  A SAILOR WITH AN APPROVED PRE-CERTIFICATION, WHO DOES NOT REENLIST ON 
THE DATE SPECIFIED IN THE PRE-CERTIFICATION MESSAGE,  MUST RESUBMIT A REQUEST 
TO REVISE THE REENLISTMENT DATE AND ESRP  AWARD AMOUNT.
