PROVIDERS.  REF E IS TITLE 37 U.S.C., 302A, SPECIAL PAY: OPTOMETRISTS.  REF F IS OPNAVINST 7220.17, 
SPECIAL PAYS FOR MEDICAL CORPS, DENTAL CORPS, MEDICAL SERVICE CORPS, AND NURSE CORPS 
OFFICERS.  REF G IS NAVADMIN 350/12, FY-13 MEDICAL DEPARTMENT OFFICER SPECIAL PAYS.  REF H IS 
NAVADMIN 240/11, IMPLEMENTATION OF GENERAL DENTIST SPECIAL PAYS.  REF I IS NAVADMIN 
371/10, FY-11 NURSE OFFICER SPECIAL PAY PLAN.  REF J IS NAVADMIN 372/10, PHARMACY OFFICER

MEDICAL SERVICE CORPS.  REF L IS NAVADMIN 172/03, OPTOMETRY RETENTION BONUS.  

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN establishes the authority granted in refs (a) through (f) and provides 
continuation of refs (g) through (l) for FY-14.  For FY-14, refs (a) through (l) remain unchanged.

2.  Officers will have 30 days from 1 October 2013 to submit requests for FY-14 special pays as per this 
NAVADMIN and ref (f).  

3.  Medical Corps (MC) and Dental Corps (DC) Multi-year Special Pay
(MSP) and Incentive Special Pay (ISP) requests must be routed through the privileging authority for 
endorsement.  Waivers for MC and DC MSP, ISP, and Dental Officer Multi-year Retention Bonus 
(DODMRB), where applicable, must be endorsed by the respective corps chief and approved by BUMED 
(M1).

4.  Specific special pays implementation guidance, administrative procedures and BUMED special pays 
point of contact information are available at 
http://www.med.navy.mil/bumed/special_pay/pages/default.aspx.
All personnel eligible for Medical Department special pays are strongly encouraged to review their 
corps' specific guidance located at the aforementioned link.
