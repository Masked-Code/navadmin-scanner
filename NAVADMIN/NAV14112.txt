RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES CONVENING OF THE FY-15 NAVY RESERVE NATIONAL 
COMMAND AND SENIOR OFFICER (O5/O6) NON-COMMAND BILLET SCREENING AND 
ASSIGNMENT "APPLY" BOARD ON 12 AUGUST 2014 OR AS SOON AS PRACTICABLE 
THEREAFTER.  ALL SELECTED RESERVE OFFICER COMMUNITIES WILL UTILIZE THE 
"APPLY" BOARD PROCESS FOR SENIOR OFFICER AND COMMAND ASSIGNMENTS.

2.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS DESIRING A BILLET ASSIGNMENT ARE REQUIRED TO COMPLETE 
ALL APPLICABLE SECTIONS OF THE "APPLY" APPLICATION.
APPLICATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED VIA THE "APPLY" WEBSITE AVAILABLE ON THE NAVY 
RESERVE HOMEPORT AT HTTPS://PRIVATE.NAVYRESERVE.NAVY.MIL/PAGES/DEFAULT.ASPX.  
OFFICERS SHOULD INCLUDE BILLET PREFERENCES WITHIN THEIR DESIGNATOR TO MEET 
COMMUNITY REQUIREMENTS.  ALTERNATE BILLET SELECTIONS MAY ALSO BE INCLUDED TO 
INCREASE THE OPPORTUNITIES FOR ASSIGNMENT.  MEMBERS ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED 
TO STATE THEIR WILLINGNESS TO TRAVEL FOR BILLETS NOT ON THEIR DREAM SHEET TO 
ENABLE THE BOARD TO SELECT THEM FOR UNADVERTISED VACANCIES.

3.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE FINALIZED AND BILLET PREFERENCES MADE NLT 16 JULY 
2014.

4.  WITH THE EXCEPTION OF THE MOST RECENT O-6 PERIODIC FITNESS REPORT
(FITREP) (ADDRESSED IN PARAGRAPH 5 BELOW), ALL CORRESPONDENCE TO THE BOARD 
MUST BE RECEIVED NLT 16 JULY 2014.  SUBMISSION METHODS ARE DETAILED IN 
PARAGRAPH 6 BELOW.  LATE CORRESPONDENCE WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.

5.  THE JULY 2014 O-6 PERIODIC FITREP WILL BE ACCEPTED UNTIL 11 AUGUST 2014 
AND SHOULD BE SUBMITTED AS AN ENCLOSURE TO A LETTER ADDRESSED TO THE "APPLY" 
BOARD PRESIDENT.  ENCLOSURES, OTHER THAN THE JULY 2014 O-6 FITREP, SUBMITTED 
AFTER THE REGULAR CORRESPONDENCE DEADLINE PER PARAGRAPH 4 WILL BE REMOVED 
FROM THE CORRESPONDENCE SUBMISSION AND WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.

6.  BOARD CORRESPONDENCE CAN BE SUBMITTED VIA ONE OF THE FOLLOWING
METHODS:
    A.  ENCRYPTED E-MAIL (PREFERRED) TO CSCSELBOARD(AT)NAVY.MIL.

SUBJECT LINE OF E-MAIL SHOULD READ:
    "FY-15 NAVY RESERVE NATIONAL COMMAND AND SENIOR OFFICER (O5/O6)
    NON-COMMAND BILLET SCREENING AND ASSIGNMENT (APPLY) BOARD (#391)
    ICO (RANK FIRST NAME LAST NAME, DESIGNATOR)".
    B.  STANDARD (US POSTAL SERVICE) MAIL (IF ENCRYPTED E-MAIL IS NOT 
AVAILABLE) TO:

7.  OFFICERS WHO ARE NOT AFFILIATED WITH THE RESERVE COMPONENT IN A DRILL 
STATUS MUST CONTACT COMMANDER, NAVY RESERVE FORCES COMMAND (COMNAVRESFORCOM) 
(N12) VIA E-MAIL AT CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL TO REQUEST AN ACCOUNT.

8.  OFFICERS WHO ARE MOBILIZED AND UNABLE TO REGISTER IN "APPLY" DUE TO 
LIMITED INTERNET CONNECTIVITY SHOULD SEEK ASSISTANCE THROUGH THEIR 
OPERATIONAL SUPPORT OFFICER OR CONTACT COMNAVRESFORCOM (N12) FOR ASSISTANCE 
VIA E-MAIL AT CNRFC_APPLY(AT)NAVY.MIL.

9.  OFFICERS MUST BE QUALIFIED PER REF (A), TO BE CONSIDERED FOR AN 
ASSIGNMENT.

10.  INTERESTED OFFICERS NOT BEING CONSIDERED FOR BILLET ASSIGNMENT ARE 
ENCOURAGED TO SERVE AS A BOARD MEMBER OR BOARD RECORDER.

11.  "APPLY" BOARD ASSIGNMENTS ARE ASSUMED TO BE ACCEPTED BY SELECTED 
OFFICERS.  OFFICERS WHO DESIRE TO DECLINE AWARDED BILLETS MUST DO SO WITHIN 
14 DAYS FOLLOWING RELEASE OF OFFICIAL RESULTS VIA THE "APPLY" WEBSITE.

12.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR SEAN DUBBS, "APPLY"/OFFICER  ASSIGNMENTS,
N122 AT (757) 322-2259/DSN 262 OR VIA E-MAIL AT SEAN.DUBBS(AT)NAVY.MI L.

13.  THIS MESSAGE WILL REMAIN IN EFFECT UNTIL SUPERSEDED OR CANCELED, 
WHICHEVER OCCURS FIRST.
