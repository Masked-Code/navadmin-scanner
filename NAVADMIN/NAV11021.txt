RMKS/1.  PER REF A, APPLICATIONS FROM QUALIFIED ACTIVE-DUTY AND SELECTED 
RESERVE (SELRES) OFFICERS ARE NOW BEING ACCEPTED FOR THE SPRING FY-11 
FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) OFFICER TRANSFER AND REDESIGNATION BOARD 
CONVENING ON 15 MARCH 2011. THIS BOARD WILL ALSO ACCEPT APPLICATIONS 
FROM EXISTING FTS OFFICERS REQUESTING REDESIGNATION INTO ANOTHER FTS 
OFFICER COMMUNITY.

2.  APPLICATIONS MUST BE RECEIVED NO LATER THAN 1600 CENTRAL STANDARD 
TIME (CST), 24 FEBRUARY 2011.  THE PREFERRED METHOD OF APPLICATION 
SUBMISSION IS TO SCAN AND E-MAIL THE COMPLETED APPLICATION WITH 
SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION TO PERS-9_FTSBOARD(AT)NAVY.MIL.  HARDCOPY 
APPLICATIONS, COMMAND ENDORSEMENTS, AND SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION MAY BE 
SENT TO THE BELOW ADDRESS IF E-MAIL SERVICES ARE UNAVAILABLE:

3.  TO PREVENT THE RECEIPT OF MULTIPLE COPIES OF AN APPLICATION, 
APPLICANTS ARE ASKED TO SUBMIT THEIR APPLICATION VIA A SINGLE METHOD AND 
CONFIRM RECEIPT BEFORE SUBMITTING THEIR APPLICATION VIA A SECONDARY 
METHOD.  APPLICATIONS, AS WELL AS SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS (TO INCLUDE 
COMMAND ENDORSEMENTS), CAN ONLY BE ACCEPTED DIRECTLY FROM THE APPLICANT. 
APPLICANTS CAN REVIEW THEIR SERVICE RECORD ON BUPERS ONLINE (BOL) VIA 
THE WEB-ENABLED RECORD REVIEW (WERR) APPLICATION LINK. APPLICANTS ARE 
REMINDED TO ONLY SEND SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION (FITREPS, AWARDS, ETC.) 
THAT IS NOT PRESENT IN WERR.

OFFICERS TO TRANSITION INTO THE FOLLOWING FTS OFFICER COMMUNITIES TO 
MEET IMMEDIATE MANNING NEEDS  IN THE YEAR GROUPS (YG)
INDICATED:

    A.  IN ADDITION TO THE ABOVE YG RANGES, THE BOARD IS ACCEPTING 
HELICOPTER SEA COMBAT (HSC) APPLICATIONS IN YEAR GROUPS 94-96 AND 99-04, 
AS WELL AS VAW NFO APPLICATIONS IN YEAR GROUPS 97, 99-04.
    B.  LIMITED QUOTAS ARE AVAILABLE FOR LIEUTENANT COMMANDERS (O4) IN 
THE 1520 AND 6330 DESIGNATORS INTERESTED IN REDESIGNATING INTO THE FTS 
AVIATION  MAINTENANCE DUTY OFFICER (AMDO) COMMUNITY (1527 DESIGNATOR).

5.  YG ELIGIBILITY MAY BE ADJUSTED BASED UPON COMMUNITY REQUIREMENTS UP 
UNTIL THE APPLICATION DUE DATE.  THE MOST UP-TO-DATE YG ELIGIBILITY 
MATRIX WILL BE POSTED ON THE FTS REDESIGNATION WEBSITE (SEE PARAGRAPH 
15).

6.  FROM THE UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) COMMUNITIES, THE FTS OFFICER 
PROGRAM IS INTERESTED IN RECEIVING APPLICATIONS FROM ACTIVE DUTY AND 
RESERVE OFFICERS TO APPLY TO THESE FTS URL COMMUNITIES: SWO, SUB, 
SPECWAR, EOD AND AVIATION.

7.  THE FTS HR COMMUNITY IS INTERESTED IN RECEIVING APPLICATIONS FROM  
ACTIVE DUTY AND RESERVE OFFICERS FROM ANY DESIGNATOR THAT POSSESS 
SUBSPECIALTIES, SIGNIFICANT  EXPERIENCE, AND/OR EDUCATION IN INFORMATION 
TECHNOLOGY OR INFORMATION MANAGEMENT.

8.  ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (BOTH GENERAL AND SPECIFIC FTS OFFICER 
COMMUNITY REQUIREMENTS), YG RESTRICTIONS BY COMMUNITY, INFORMATION ON 
APPLICATION PROCEDURES, APPLICATION MODEL, AND SAMPLE LETTERS OF 
RECOMMENDATION ARE POSTED ON THE FTS REDESIGNATION BOARD WEBSITE.    
APPLICATIONS WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED FROM OFFICERS WHO DO NOT MEET ALL 
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA.  THE FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGER (OCM) WEBSITE 
CONTAINS VALUABLE INFORMATION ABOUT THE FTS PROGRAM AS WELL AS 
INFORMATION ABOUT THE VARIOUS FTS OFFICER COMMUNITIES.

9.  RESERVE DIRECT COMMISSION OFFICERS (DCO) ARE ELIGIBLE FOR THE FTS 
PROGRAM.  ALL OFFICERS APPLYING TO THE FTS OFFICER PROGRAM MUST POSSESS 
A 4-YEAR BACCALUAREATE DEGREE.

10.  SELRES OFFICERS CURRENTLY SERVING ON ORDERS AS CANVASSER  
RECRUITERS OR RESERVE MANAGEMENT TEMPORARY RECALLS ARE ENCOURAGED TO 
APPLY IF THEY MEET ALL OTHER  APPLICABLE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS.

11.  ACTIVE DUTY OFFICERS WITH A PENDING OR APPROVED RESIGNATION ARE 
ELIGIBLE AND STRONGLY QQQQ ENCOURAGED TO APPLY.  APPLICANTS WHO HAVE 
SUBMITTED THEIR RESIGNATION ARE HIGHLY ENCOURAGED TO REVIEW THE 
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS SECTION ON THE FTS  REDESIGNATION BOARD 
WEBSITE.

12.  APPLICANTS ARE REQUIRED TO HAVE A CURRENT PHOTO IN THEIR OFFICIAL 
MILITARY PERSONNEL FILE.

13.  APPLICANTS MUST SUBMIT PROOF OF APPLICABLE MEDICAL QUALIFICATION 
(E.G., CURRENT PREVENTIVE HEALTH ASSESSMENT) AS AN ENCLOSURE TO THEIR 
APPLICATION.

14.  THE RESULTS OF THE BOARD WILL BE POSTED ON THE FTS OCM WEBSITE ONCE 
THE BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED.  APPLICANTS CAN EXPECT TO SEE THE 
RESULTS POSTED WITHIN 30 DAYS OF BOARD ADJOURNMENT.
