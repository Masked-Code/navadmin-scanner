SUBJ/FY-09 ENLISTED SELECTION BOARDS FOR NAVY RESERVE AND ACTIVE DUTY 
MASTER AND SENIOR CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS CYCLE 197// 
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  THE FY-09 E8/E9 SELECTION BOARD FOR NAVY RESERVE 
PERSONNEL WILL CONVENE ON 3 MAR 08 AND WILL CONSIDER PERSONNEL SERVING 
ON ACTIVE DUTY AS FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) IN THE ACTIVE/GUARD RESERVE, 
AND SELECTED RESERVE (SELRES) PERSONNEL TO INCLUDE THOSE SERVING ON 
VOLUNTARY AND INVOLUNTARY RECALL AND MOBILIZATION ORDERS.  INDIVIDUAL 
READY RESERVE (IRR) PERSONNEL WHO ARE MOBILIZED ARE CONSIDERED UNDER 
THE SELRES REQUIREMENTS FOR ADVANCEMENT.  THE FY-09 E9 AND E8 SELECTION 
BOARDS FOR ACTIVE DUTY PERSONNEL WILL CONVENE 31 MAR 08 AND 21 APR 08, 
RESPECTIVELY.  THIS NAVADMIN IS A SUPPLEMENT TO BUPERSINST 1430.16E.
2.  TIMELINE OF EVENTS
     A.  OCT 07:  COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-811/812) COMPILES AND SUBMITS 
INITIAL ELIGIBILITY LIST OF CANDIDATES WHO ARE TIME IN RATE (TIR) 
ELIGIBLE TO NETPDTC (N321).
     B.  NOV 07:  NETPDTC (N321) FORWARDS TO COMMANDS/SERVICING PSD 
LISTINGS OF E7/E8 TIR ELIGIBLES VIA THE EXAMINATION STATUS VERIFICATION 
REPORT (ESVR).  COMMAND DESIGNATED PERSONNEL CAN ALSO ACCESS THEIR ESVR 
VIA INTERNET AT HTTPS://NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL/NEAS.
    C.  NOV 07 - MAR 08:  COMMANDS MUST VERIFY ESVR AND TAKE THE 
FOLLOWING ACTIONS:
        (1) ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES NOT LISTED - COMMANDS MUST MAIL A 
COMPLETED ANSWER SHEET (NETPDTC 1430/2) WITH LETTER OF TRANSMITTAL
TO NETPDTC (N322) FOR ALL PERSONNEL NOT LISTED.  REFER TO BUPERSINST 
1430.16E, CHAPTER 6 FOR FURTHER INFORMATION.
        (2) CANDIDATES NOT ELIGIBLE OR NO LONGER RECOMMENDED FOR 
ADVANCEMENT - COMMANDS MUST SUBMIT A MESSAGE TO NETPDTC (N321), INFO
PERS-811/812 REQUESTING INVALIDATION.  WITHDRAWAL OF ADVANCEMENT 
RECOMMENDATION MUST BE DOCUMENTED PER BUPERSINST 1430.16E, PARA 721B.
        (3) CANDIDATES VALIDATED IN WRONG COMPETITIVE CATEGORY - 
COMMANDS MUST SUBMIT A MESSAGE TO PERS-811/812, INFO NETPDTC (N321) 
DETAILING DISCREPANCY.  PERS-811/812 WILL RESEARCH AND COMMUNICATE 
RESOLUTION.
    D.  MAR 08 - APR 08:  SELECTION BOARDS CONVENE.
3.  ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:  CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE ELIGIBILITY 
REQUIREMENTS OUTLINED IN BUPERSINST 1430.16E CHAPTER 2 NOT LATER THAN 1 
NOV 07.  A CANDIDATE WHO DOES NOT HAVE A RECOMMENDATION FOR ADVANCEMENT 
ON THEIR MOST RECENT FITNESS REPORT SHALL NOT BE VALIDATED.
     A.  TIR:  TERMINAL ELIGIBILITY DATE FOR CYCLE 197 IS 1 OCT 08.
PERSONNEL WITH A TIR DATE OF 1 OCT 05 OR EARLIER WILL BE TIR ELIGIBLE 
FOR THIS SELECTION BOARD CYCLE.  REFER TO BUPERSINST 1430.16E, PARA 216 
TO COMPUTE TIR.
     B.  COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS:  CANDIDATES WHO HAVE BEEN SELECTED FOR 
SEAMAN TO ADMIRAL, MEDICAL ENLISTED COMMISSIONING, AND MEDICAL SERVICE 
CORPS IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT PROGRAMS REMAIN ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT 
CONSIDERATION TO SENIOR CHIEF AND MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICER.  
SELECTION TO ANY OTHER OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS RENDER CANDIDATES 
INELIGIBLE AND THEY MUST BE INVALIDATED BY THEIR COMMANDS.
    C.  HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT):  CANDIDATES WHO REACH HYT FOR THEIR 
PRESENT PAYGRADE ON OR AFTER THE FIRST DAY OF THE ADVANCEMENT CYCLE
(1 JUL 08) OR HAVE RECEIVED A HYT WAIVER TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY (OR 
REMAIN IN SELRES STATUS) BEYOND 1 JUL 08 ARE ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT 
AND WILL BE CONSIDERED BY THE SELECTION BOARD.  THOSE CANDIDATES WITH A 
HYT DATE PRIOR TO 1 JUL 08 ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT 
UNLESS THEY RECEIVE A HYT WAIVER.  HYT WAIVERS MUST BE APPROVED AND 
CANDIDATES VALIDATED BY THEIR COMMANDS PRIOR TO THE CONVENING OF THE 
BOARD.  SELRES PERSONNEL WHO ARE MOBILIZED AND REACH HYT WHILE 
MOBILIZED HAVE A COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-
913) APPROVED HYT WAIVER THROUGH THE ENDING DATE OF THEIR MOBILIZATION 
ORDERS.  PERSONNEL WHO EXCEED THE HYT LIMIT FOR THE NEXT PAYGRADE DO 
NOT MEET THE CRITERIA TO SERVE IN THE NEXT PAYGRADE VACANCY AND 
THEREFORE ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT.
     D.  REQUESTS FOR TRANSFER TO THE FLEET RESERVE/RETIRED LIST:
CANDIDATES WHO SUBMIT INVOLUNTARY REQUESTS DUE TO HYT TO TRANSFER AT OR 
BEYOND HYT LIMITS ARE ELIGIBLE FOR CONSIDERATION AS LONG AS MEMBER IS 
PROJECTED TO REMAIN IN AN ELIGIBLE STATUS AS OF 1 JUL 08 OR LATER.  
VOLUNTARY REQUESTS AS OUTLINED IN BUPERSINST 1430.16E, CHAPTER 2 RENDER 
CANDIDATES INELIGIBLE FOR SELECTION BOARD CONSIDERATION ONCE THE 
REQUEST IS GENERATED AT THE COMMAND LEVEL.
THE COMMANDS MUST COUNSEL THOSE MEMBERS AS REQUIRED IN BUPERSINST 
1430.16E, PARA 209.A.  AN APPROVED OR PENDING FLEET RESERVE/RETIRED 
LIST APPLICATION MUST BE SUCCESSFULLY CANCELED NOT LATER THAN 1 FEB
08 FOR A CANDIDATE TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION.
     E.  LEADERSHIP CONTINUUM:  PER NAVADMIN 056/06, SUCCESSFUL 
COMPLETION OF THE PREVIOUS CHIEF PETTY OFFICER LTC (OR NEW LCPOLC) WILL 
BE REQUIRED BY 1 NOV 07 TO PARTICIPATE IN THE SELECTION BOARDS FOR 
ADVANCEMENT TO SENIOR CHIEF PETTY OFFICER.
