3.  Per reference (a), CNIC is responsible for administering the Navy 
Community Service Awards Program and coordinating the efforts of Flagship 
sponsors and Region coordinators.

4.  Each Flagship sponsor will conduct an awards board with judges 
representing Active duty, Reservists, and community-based organizations.  The 
awards board will select Navy-wide winners in each category and Flagship.  
CNIC's Flagship awards board will also consider nominations for the USS 
BAINBRIDGE (CGN 25) Award for Overall Excellence.

5.  Guidance on Submitting Nomination Packages:
    a.  All Navy commands are eligible to compete for the 2018 Navy Community 
Service Awards program.
    b.  Deadline for commands to submit nominations to the Navy Community 
Service Program Geographic Region Coordinator (GRC) is 31 July 2018.  
Submissions must be in the format provided in reference (b).  A fillable PDF 
submission data sheet and guidelines are available through the Region 
coordinator.
    c.  Nominations cannot exceed 10 pages in length.  The maximum is five 
pages for nomination information and five pages for supporting documents, 
such as news clippings, letters of appreciation, and local community awards.
    d.  Only the GRC is authorized to submit nominations directly to Flagship 
sponsors and CNIC for the USS BAINBRIDGE (CGN 25) Award for Overall 
Excellence.

6.  Timeline For Processing Of Nominations:
    a.  31 July 2018:  Command nomination packages due to GRC.
    b.  30 September 2018:  GRC nominations due to Flagship sponsors.
    c.  1-27 October 2018:  Nominations processed through Flagship sponsor 
awards boards.
    d.  31 October 2018:  Awards boards finalize nominations.
    e.  30 November 2018:  Flagship sponsors release NAVADMIN to announce 
awardees.
    f.  31 December 2018:  Awards plaques and certificates distributed to 
awardees.
