RMKS/1.  EACH YEAR, THE NAVY RECOGNIZES VARIOUS SEA, SHORE, AND
OVERSEAS COMMANDS FOR OUTSTANDING COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECTS BY
PRESENTING AWARDS IN FIVE FLAGSHIP SPONSOR CATEGORIES:  PERSONAL
EXCELLENCE PARTNERSHIP; HEALTH, SAFETY, AND FITNESS; PROJECT GOOD
NEIGHBOR; CAMPAIGN DRUG FREE; AND ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP.
2.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE 2005 COMMUNITY
SERVICE HEALTH, SAFETY, AND FITNESS FLAGSHIP AWARD WINNING
COMMANDS:
    A.  SMALL SEA WINNER:  MOBILE DIVING AND SALVAGE UNIT ONE.
    B.  MEDIUM SEA WINNER:  USS ANZIO (CG 68).  HONORABLE MENTIONS:
SPECIAL PROJECTS PATROL SQUADRON TWO AND SEA CONTROL SQUADRON THREE
ONE.
    C.  SMALL OVERSEAS WINNER:  U.S. NAVAL COMPUTER AND
TELECOMMUNICATIONS STATION, GUAM.
    D.  LARGE OVERSEAS WINNER:  U.S. NAVAL HOSPITAL, GUAM.
    E.  SMALL SHORE WINNER:  FLEET AREA CONTROL AND SURVEILLANCE
FACILITY, PEARL HARBOR.  HONORABLE MENTIONS:  CENTER FOR SURFACE
COMBAT SYSTEMS DETACHMENT, WALLOPS ISLAND.
    F.  MEDIUM SHORE WINNER:  NAVAL AVIATION SCHOOLS COMMAND,
PENSACOLA.  HONORABLE MENTIONS:  FLEET AND INDUSTRIAL SUPPLY
CENTER, PEARL HARBOR AND SURFACE WARFARE OFFICERS SCHOOL COMMAND,
NEWPORT.
    G.  LARGE SHORE WINNER:  NAVAL SUBMARINE BASE, KINGS BAY.
HONORABLE MENTIONS:  TRAINING SUPPORT CENTER, GREAT LAKES AND
NAVAL SECURITY GROUP, PEARL HARBOR.
3.  CONGRATULATIONS TO ALL WHO PARTICIPATED IN THESE NOTEWORTHY
EFFORTS.  MY SINCERE APPRECIATION FOR YOUR PERSONAL COMMITMENT AND
CONTINUED SUPPORT OF OUR NAVY'S COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM.
4.  ALL AWARD WINNERS WILL RECEIVE COMMEMORATIVE PLAQUES AND THE
HONORABLE MENTIONS WILL RECEIVE CERTIFICATES FROM THE FLAG SPONSOR
IN RECOGNITION OF THEIR EXCEPTIONAL ACCOMPLISHMENTS.
