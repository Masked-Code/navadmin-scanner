RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN provides details on the Graduate Education Voucher 
(GEV) program and guidance for FY-15 program application.
Eligibility, obligated service requirements, subspecialty utilization tour, 
and authorized program benefits are prescribed in ref (a).

2.  GEV funding is authorized only for off-duty study in master*s degree 
programs which meet the requirements of at least one approved Navy 
subspecialty code as verified by Naval Postgraduate School (NPS).  For FY-15, 
120 GEV quotas for select unrestricted line officers are planned, subject to 
funding availability.
    a.  Quotas are apportioned as follows:
        Aviation officers - 49
        Submarine officers - 27
        Surface Warfare officers * 42
        SPECWAR/EOD officers * 2
    b.  FY-15 Quotas by area of study, subspecialty for area of study, and 
warfare area are:
Chemistry (4201P/G)            AV 1  SUB  0 SURFACE 1  SPECWAR/EOD 0
Computer Science (6203P/G)     AV 4  SUB  3 SURFACE 4  SPECWAR/EOD 0
Engineering (5XXXP/G)          AV 12 SUB  5 SURFACE 8  SPECWAR/EOD 0
English (4301P/G)              AV 1  SUB  1 SURFACE 0  SPECWAR/EOD 0
Financial Mgmt. (3105P/G)      AV 18 SUB  8 SURFACE 16 SPECWAR/EOD 2
History (4302P/G)              AV 1  SUB  0 SURFACE 1  SPECWAR/EOD 0
Mathematics (4100P/G)          AV 1  SUB  1 SURFACE 1  SPECWAR/EOD 0
Ops Analysis/Research (3211G)  AV 4  SUB  4 SURFACE 4  SPECWAR/EOD 0
Regional Studies (2XXXP/G)     AV 7  SUB  5 SURFACE 7  SPECWAR/EOD 0

3.  Application and program specifics may be found in ref (a) or at 
https://www.navycollege.navy.mil/gev/gev_home.aspx.

4.  Points of contact:
