GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  ENSURING THE FLEET IS PROVIDING THE RIGHT
SAILORS, WITH THE RIGHT SKILLS, IN THE RIGHT JOBS IS A PRINCIPAL
GOAL OF THE NAVY'S HUMAN CAPITAL STRATEGY.  AS THE REQUIREMENTS OF
THE FORCE CHANGE, MANPOWER REQUIREMENTS SHIFT AND CAREER
OPPORTUNITIES MUST ADJUST TO ENSURE THE NEEDS OF THE FLEET ARE MET.



PAGE 02 RUEWMFU5804 UNCLAS
THIS NAVADMIN PROVIDES THE INFORMATION REQUIRED FOR SAILORS TO MAKE
INFORMED CAREER DECISIONS, INCLUDING RATING AVAILABILITY FOR
NON-DESIGNATED STRIKERS, CREO CATEGORIES, CRITICAL SKILLS LISTS, AND
PERFORM TO SERVE CHANGES.  ALSO INCLUDED IS THE MOST RECENT
ADVANCEMENT OPPORTUNITIES FOR ACTIVE DUTY AND FULL TIME SUPPORT
(FTS).  THIS SUPERCEDES NAVADMIN 283/04.
2.  PERFORM TO SERVE (PTS).  CHANGES TO THE CREO CATEGORY OF A
RATING, FOR THE PURPOSE OF PTS, BECOME EFFECTIVE 31 AUGUST 2005.
SAILORS IN RATINGS NEWLY REQUIRED TO APPLY TO PTS DUE TO A CREO
CATEGORY CHANGE FROM CREO 1 TO CREO 2 OR CREO 3, SHOULD BEGIN
SUBMITTING PTS APPLICATIONS IN JULY 2005.  COMMANDS ARE STRONGLY
ENCOURAGED TO HAVE COMMAND CAREER COUNSELORS SUBMIT PTS APPLICATIONS
VIA FLEET RIDE TO ENSURE AWARENESS OF ALL POTENTIAL CONVERSION
OPPORTUNITIES.  THOSE SAILORS THAT RECEIVED A SEPARATION MESSAGE VIA
PTS IN THE TIME PERIOD OF JAN 05 - JUN 05 AND WHOSE CREO GROUP HAS
CHANGED FROM 2 TO 1 MAY REQUEST TO BE RESET IN PTS FOR ANOTHER
OPPORTUNITY.  PLEASE CONTACT THE PTS DESK ASAP TO BE RESET.
3.  ADVANCEMENT EXAMS.  THE DEADLINE FOR ORDERING REGULAR PETTY
OFFICER THIRD CLASS EXAMS HAS BEEN EXTENDED UNTIL 20 AUG 05.  EARLY
PROMOTE E5 AND E6, INCLUDING THOSE SAILORS RANKED 1 OF 1 ON PERIODIC

EVALUATIONS WHO MEET THE REQUIREMENTS OF THE ADVANCEMENT MANUAL ARE
ELIGIBLE FOR TIME-IN-RATE WAIVERS AT THE DISCRETION OF THE
COMMANDING OFFICER.  TIME-IN-RATE WAIVERS APPLY ONLY TO ADVANCEMENT
EXAMINATION PARTICIPATION AND IS NOT TO BE APPLIED TO THE COMMAND
ADVANCEMENT PROGRAM.
4.  RATING CONVERSIONS.  CONVERSIONS WILL BE CONSIDERED ONLY FOR
THOSE IN CREO 2 OR CREO 3 RATINGS WHO SEEK ENTRY INTO CREO 1
RATINGS.  WHILE PREVIOUS CREO MESSAGES HAVE GIVEN A 6 MONTH
PLANNING HORIZON THIS MESSAGE DEPICTS A LONGER TERM PROJECTION FOR
EACH COMMUNITIES' MANNING STATUS.

(NON-DESIGNATED STRIKER STATUS, ADVANCEMENT OPPORTUNITY, AND CREO
