RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES REQUIREMENTS AND SOLICITS OFFICER
APPLICATIONS FOR ONE ADMINISTRATIVE/EXECUTIVE OFFICER FOR THE NAVY
FLIGHT DEMONSTRATION SQUADRON (BLUE ANGELS) 2009-2010 SHOW SEASONS.
INTERESTED OFFICERS SHOULD SUBMIT APPLICATIONS NLT 30 NOVEMBER 2007.
FINAL SELECTION WILL BE MADE IN DECEMBER 2007 WITH A REPORT DATE OF
SEPTEMBER 2008.
2.  QUALIFICATIONS.  ADMINISTRATIVE/EXECUTIVE OFFICERS SHOULD BE
CAREER ORIENTED, REGULAR NAVY DESIGNATED, LDO/CWO ADMIN OFFICERS
(641X/741X) (LCDR/LT/CWO4/CWO3), AND BE IN, OR HAVE COMPLETED, AN
OPERATIONAL FLEET TOUR. 
3.  SELECTEE WILL BE APPROVED BY BUPERS PRIOR TO PUBLIC NOTIFICATION.
4.  REF A DELINEATES APPLICATION PROCEDURES.  SUBMIT APPLICATIONS TO
COMMANDING OFFICER, NAVY FLIGHT DEMONSTRATION SQUADRON, 390 SAN
CARLOS RD, STE A, ATTN:  LCDR WALLEY, PENSACOLA, FL 32508-5508 VIA
CURRENT COMMANDING OFFICER.  SUBMIT A COPY OF THE APPLICATION TO THE
RESPECTIVE COMMUNITY DETAILER AT NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND.
5.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR ANTHONY WALLEY (BLUE ANGELS APPLICANTS
OFFICER) AT (850) 452-2583 EXT 3112/DSN 922 OR VISIT THE BLUE ANGELS
WEBSITE AT WWW.BLUEANGELS.NAVY.MIL.
6.  RELEASED BY VADM J. C. HARVEY, JR., N1.//
