SUBJ/AUG 06 (CYCLE 079) NAVY-WIDE EXAMINATIONS FOR ADVANCEMENT IN
RATE OF NAVY RESERVE PERSONNEL TO PAYGRADES E4 THROUGH E6//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  INACTIVE RESERVE EXAMINATIONS WILL BE
ADMINISTERED DURING AUG 06.  ALL CANDIDATES SERVING ON ACTIVE DUTY
FOR SPECIAL WORK/ONE YEAR RECALL (ADSW/OYR), OR ACTIVE DUTY UNDER
ANY PRESIDENTIAL RECALL AUTHORITY, WILL COMPETE FOR ADVANCEMENT AS
SELECTED RESERVE INACTIVE (USNR-INACTIVE) IN CYCLE 079.  INDIVIDUAL
READY RESERVE PERSONNEL WHO ARE MOBILIZED ARE ELIGIBLE UNDER THE
SELECTED RESERVE (SELRES) REQUIREMENTS FOR ADVANCEMENT.
2.  BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
    A.  SELRES CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE BASIC ELIGIBILITY
REQUIREMENTS PER BUPERSINST 1430.16E, OPNAVINST 1160.7B, AND THE
LATEST DRILLING RESERVE ENLISTED CAREER MANAGEMENT OBJECTIVES
CONTROL PROGRAM (ECMO) NOT LATER THAN 1 JUL 06.  CANDIDATES MUST
MAINTAIN COMMANDING OFFICER'S RECOMMENDATION TO REMAIN ELIGIBLE FOR
ADVANCEMENT.
    B.  INTERNET REVIEW AND MODIFICATION OF TIME-IN-RATE (TIR) LIST
FOR EXAMINATION ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE ON THE FOLLOWING DATES:
COMMANDS WITH CRYPTOLOGIC TECHNICIANS (COLLECTIONS) OR CRYPTOLOGIC
TECHNICIAN (NETWORK) CANDIDATES MUST ORDER SCI EXAMINATIONS BETWEEN
22 MAY 06 THROUGH 5 JUN 06; OTHER RATING EXAMINATIONS ORDERING WILL
BE AVAILABLE BETWEEN 30 MAY 06 THROUGH 25 JUN 06.  SUPPLEMENTAL
EXAMINATION ORDERING WILL BE AVAILABLE BETWEEN 26 JUN 06 THROUGH 30
JUL 06.  USE NETPDTC WEB SITE AT HTTPS:NEASOS.CNET.NAVY.MIL/CGI-BIN/
TO REVIEW TIR LIST.  COMMAND EDUCATIONAL SERVICE OFFICERS (ESO) WITH
INTERNET ACCESS BUT WITHOUT PASSWORD MAY REGISTER FOR PASSWORD
ONLINE AT ABOVE WEBSITE.  (NOTE:  CANRECS, RECALLED MOBILIZED
SAILORS AND RESCORE CANDIDATES ALL MUST ORDER VIA MESSAGE AS
INTERNET ORDERING IS NOT AVAILABLE TO THOSE PERSONNEL).
    C.  REFER TO NAVADMIN 033/06 ON NAVY LEADERSHIP CONTINUUM
REQUIREMENTS.
3.  EVALUATIONS USED TO COMPUTE PERFORMANCE MARK AVERAGES MUST BE
INCLUSIVE OF THE PERIODS LISTED BELOW:
    PROSPECTIVE           EVALUATION    COMPUTATION
    PAYGRADE              PAYGRADE      PERIOD
    E4                    E3            1 AUG 04 TO 31 JUL 06
    E5                    E4            1 JUL 04 TO 31 JUL 06
    E6                    E5            1 APR 04 TO 31 JUL 06
    A.  IF A SERVICE MEMBER DOES NOT HAVE AN EVALUATION WITHIN THE
PERIOD SPECIFIED, PREPARE A SPECIAL EVALUATION WITH BLOCK 15
REFLECTING 31 JUL 06 FOR E4, E5, AND E6 CANDIDATES.
4.  GENERAL EXAMINATION/ADVERSE INFO:
    A.  EXAMINATIONS WILL BE ADMINISTERED PER CHAPTER 6 OF
BUPERSINST 1430.16E.  DO NOT ADMINISTER ANY OTHER CYCLE 079
EXAMINATIONS PRIOR TO 1 AUG 06 UNLESS SPECIFICALLY AUTHORIZED BY
COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4812).
    B.  EXAMINATIONS FOR RECALLED RESERVISTS WILL BE ORDERED BY THE
COMMAND HOLDING THE MEMBER'S SERVICE RECORD.  AUTHORITY IS GRANTED
TO ADMINISTER EXAMINATIONS TO SELECTED RESERVE CANDIDATES ON ACTIVE
DUTY WITH REGULAR NAVY CANDIDATES 7 SEP 06 FOR E6, 14 SEP 06 FOR E5,
AND 21 SEP 06 FOR E4.  THESE CANDIDATES ARE STILL REQUIRED TO TAKE
CYCLE 079 EXAMINATION AND NOT ACTIVE DUTY EXAMINATION.
    C.  ADVANCEMENT TO E4 AND RATING ENTRY FOR NON-DESIGNATED
PERSONNEL ARE GOVERNED BY THE LATEST ECMO.  NON-DESIGNATED MEMBERS
WHO REQUIRE AN "A" SCHOOL WAIVER FOR ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION
PARTICIPATION PER THE LATEST ECMO, MUST SUBMIT A REQUEST TO
PERS-4812 POSTMARKED BY 15 JUN 06.
    D.  ENSURE BLOCKS 12 AND 13 OF THE ADVANCEMENT IN RATE OR
CHANGE OF RATING (WORKSHEET) (NAVEDTRA 1430/2) ARE COMPLETED.  AT
THE ESO'S DISCRETION, WORKSHEETS MAY BE SIGNED EITHER PRIOR TO
EXAMINATION OR AT THE EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION SITE.
    E.  LATE EXAMINATION ORDERS MUST BE HELD TO A MINIMUM TO ALLOW
TIME FOR ADEQUATE PROCESSING AND QUOTA DETERMINATION.  IMPORTANCE OF
TIMELY EXAMINATION ORDERING AND ADMINISTRATION CANNOT BE OVER-
EMPHASIZED.  REQUESTS FOR LATE EXAMINATIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED
USING SAMPLE FORMAT ON TABLE 4-3 OF BUPERSINST 1430.16E VIA MESSAGE
TO NETPDTC (N321), WITH COMNAVPERSCOM MILLINGTON, TN (PERS-4812) AS
INFO ADDRESSEE.  REQUESTS MUST INCLUDE MEMBER'S NAME, RATE, SSN, AND
JUSTIFICATION FOR MISSING REGULAR EXAMINATION.  NO ACTION WILL BE
TAKEN ON REQUESTS RECEIVED WITHOUT REQUIRED INFO/JUSTIFICATION.
ENSURE "ADMIN ERROR" AND "OPERATIONAL COMMITMENT" ARE CLARIFIED WHEN
USED AS JUSTIFICATION.  REQUESTS RECEIVED BY NETPDTC AFTER 8 SEP 06
WILL BE DISAPPROVED.  LATE EXAMINATIONS MUST BE ADMINISTERED NOT
LATER THAN 25 SEP 06.  WHEN A CANDIDATE JUSTIFIABLY MISSES A REGULAR
EXAMINATION AND EXAMINATION IS ONBOARD, DO NOT DESTROY EXAMINATION.
SUBMIT REQUEST AS ABOVE FOR PERMISSION TO RETAIN THE EXAMINATION FOR
LATE ADMINISTRATION.
    F.  FORWARDING EXAMINATION RETURNS.  EXAMINATION GRADING
REQUIRES A 90 PERCENT RECEIPT OF RETURNS TO NETPDTC.  FORWARD
EXAMINATION ANSWER SHEETS IN THE FOLLOWING MANNER:
        (1) CONUS INSTALLATIONS.  MAIL EXAMINATION ANSWER SHEETS VIA
FEDEX UNDER THE CURRENT GSA CONTRACT BY CLOSE OF BUSINESS OF THE DAY
FOLLOWING EXAMINATION ADMINISTRATION (MONDAY AFTER DRILL WEEKEND).
FEDEX PACKAGES MAY BE TRACKED AT WWW.FEDEX.COM.
        (2) OVERSEAS SHORE COMMANDS.  SEND ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION
ANSWER SHEETS VIA UNITED STATES POSTAL SERVICE (USPS) THREE-DAY
EXPRESS MAIL SERVICE.  MAIL MAY BE TRACKED AT WWW.USPS.GOV.
        (3) DEPLOYED SHIPS.  SEND ADVANCEMENT EXAMINATION ANSWER
SHEETS VIA REGISTERED MAIL TO THE SERVICING FLEET MAIL CENTER IN THE
DEPLOYED AREA.
    G.  EXAMINATION RESULTS ARE PROVIDED TO SUPPORTING ESOS
ONLINE.  AUTHORIZED REPRESENTATIVES ACCESS THE OFFICIAL RESULTS,
WITH A PASSWORD, THROUGH NETPDTC'S EXAMINATION ORDERING SITE.  CLICK
ON EXAMINATION STATUS VERIFICATION REPORT (ESVR), RATE CHANGE
AUTHORIZATION (RCA), PROFILE SHEETS, AND COMMAND STATISTICS.  THE
ESVR AND RCA ARE THE OFFICIAL SOURCE FOR NAVY ADVANCEMENT
EXAMINATION RESULTS.  ESOS MUST PROVIDE COPIES TO SUPPORTED
ACTIVITIES UPON RECEIPT.  INDIVIDUAL SAILORS MAY ALSO VIEW/DOWNLOAD
THEIR OWN PROFILE SHEET FIVE DAYS AFTER RESULTS ARE RELEASED FROM
HTTPS:WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL (SECURE SITE) UNDER 'MY
ADVANCEMENT' LINK.
    H.  ALL COMMANDS SHOULD MONITOR THE ELECTRONIC TOTE BOARD
LOCATED AT HTTPS:WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL TO VERIFY THAT ANSWER
SHEETS ARE RECEIVED BY NETPDTC.
5.  EXAMINATION  DISCREPANCIES.  TO CLEAR ALL EXAMINATION
DISCREPANCIES CREATED BY INCORRECT DATA, SUBMIT THE CORRECTED INFO
AS REQUIRED BY TABLE 7-3 OF BUPERSINST 1430.16E TO NETPDTC (N321)
AND MAKE APPROPRIATE NSIPS ENTRIES.  A LIST OF OUTSTANDING
DISCREPANCIES WILL BE UPDATED DAILY AND VIEWED AT
HTTPS:WWW.ADVANCEMENT.CNET.NAVY.MIL.
6.  POC:  PSC M. M. MEISER AT (901)874-4500/DSN 882, OR EMAIL AT
MARGARET.MEISER@NAVY.MIL.
