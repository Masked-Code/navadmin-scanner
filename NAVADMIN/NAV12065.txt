RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE PERMANENT MILITARY PROFESSOR (PMP) 
SELECTION BOARD FOR OFFICERS MATRICULATING IN GRADUATE SCHOOL IN AUGUST 2013.  
PMPS CONTINUE TO PERFORM CRUCIAL ROLES AT THE U.S. NAVAL ACADEMY (USNA), NAVAL 
POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL (NPS), AND NAVAL WAR COLLEGE IN MEETING OFFICER-INSTRUCTOR 
REQUIREMENTS.  PMPS PROVIDE STAFFING CONTINUITY AS WELL AS RECRUITING, 
MENTORING, AND OUTPLACEMENT COUNSELING FOR MILITARY INSTRUCTORS ON ROTATIONAL 
ASSIGNMENTS.  ADDITIONALLY, PMPS MAINTAIN AND ENHANCE LINKS WITH THE REST OF 
THE NAVY FOR RESEARCH OPPORTUNITIES, FACULTY DEVELOPMENT, AND CURRICULUM 
FEEDBACK.  SELECTION
FOR ASSIGNMENT TO THE PMP COMMUNITY IS MADE BY A SCREENING BOARD, WHICH WILL 
MAKE SELECTIONS FROM AMONG OFFICERS WHO HAVE SUBMITTED AN APPLICATION FOR THE 
COMMUNITY.

2.  REF A PROVIDES SPECIFIC APPLICATION GUIDANCE.  APPLICATIONS ARE DUE TO 
COMMANDER, NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND (PERS-440F) NLT 1 MAY 2012.
 MAIL
APPLICATIONS TO:
    DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY
    NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND, (PERS-440)
    5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE
    MILLINGTON, TN 38055-4400
THOSE SELECTED FROM THIS YEAR'S BOARD WILL ULTIMATELY BE ASSIGNED TO USNA OR 
NPS.  SELECTEES NOT ALREADY POSSESSING A PHD WILL ATTEND THE NAVAL 
POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL OR DESIGNATED CIVILIAN INSTITUTIONS TO OBTAIN A PHD BEFORE 
ASSIGNMENT AS FACULTY TO USNA OR NPS.
    A.  THOSE ELIGIBLE TO APPLY ARE WARFARE QUALIFIED ACTIVE DUTY NAVAL 
OFFICERS IN ANY DESIGNATOR WHO ARE SELECTED FOR OR SERVING IN PAYGRADE O5.
WARFARE QUALIFIED IS DEFINED AS BEING CURRENTLY ELIGIBLE TO WEAR THE WARFARE 
INSIGNIA OF AN UNRESTRICTED LINE OR INFORMATION DOMINANCE CORPS COMMUNITY 
(1110, 1120, 1130, 1140, 1310, 1320, 18XX).  REF A PROVIDES GUIDANCE FOR 
REQUESTS TO WAIVE THIS REQUIREMENT.  THE PRESIDENT OF THE SELECTION BOARD IS 
THE WAIVER APPROVAL AUTHORITY.
    B.  COMMANDERS AND THOSE SELECTED FOR COMMANDER MUST POSSESS A PHD OR BE 
ACADEMICALLY ELIGIBLE TO PURSUE A NAVY-SPONSORED DOCTORAL DEGREE.
CAPTAINS AND CAPTAIN SELECTS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO APPLY TO THE FY13 PMP BOARD.
    C.  FULL-TIME SUPPORT (FTS) OFFICERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO APPLY FOR THE 
FY13 PMP BOARD.  SELECTED RESERVE OFFICERS, OTHER MILITARY SERVICES, AND 
RETIRED OFFICERS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE.
    D.  APPLICANTS MUST HAVE COMPLETED AT LEAST ONE YEAR AT THEIR CURRENT 
COMMAND BY JULY 2013 TO COMMENCE A PHD PROGRAM OR BEGIN FULL TIME TEACHING AS 
A PMP.  OFFICERS SERVING IN A DESIGNATED JOINT BILLET MUST HAVE COMPLETED AT 
LEAST 22 MONTHS BY JULY 2013 IN THEIR JOINT COMMAND TOUR.
OFFICERS WHO ARE COMMITTED TO A SPECIFIC COMMUNITY THROUGH A  CONTINUOUS PAY 
AGREEMENT (E.G., NUCLEAR OFFICER INCENTIVE PAY, AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION 
PAY, SURFACE WARFARE CRITICAL SKILLS RETENTION BONUS) OR  A SIMILAR SERVICE 
AGREEMENT MUST BE WITHIN ONE YEAR OF COMPLETION OF  THE AGREED UPON SERVICE 
OBLIGATION BY THE AUGUST 2012 CONVENING OF THE  PMP SELECTION BOARD.  OFFICERS 
WHO HAVE RECEIVED FUNDED GRADUATE EDUCATION FROM THEIR CURRENT COMMUNITY MUST 
COMPLETE THE ASSOCIATED OBLIGATED SERVICE IDENTIFIED IN REF B PRIOR TO 
MATRICULATING INTO GRADUATE SCHOOL.
    E.  SELECTEES WILL BE REDESIGNATED INTO THE PMP COMMUNITY, 1230 
DESIGNATOR, ESTABLISHED BY REF C.  UPON REDESIGNATION, OFFICERS ARE OBLIGATED 
TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY THROUGH STATUTORY RETIREMENT:  28 YEARS OF 
COMMISSIONED SERVICE FOR COMMANDERS AND 30 YEARS FOR CAPTAINS.

3.  THE FY13 PMP SELECTION BOARD MAY SELECT UP TO A TOTAL OF SEVEN PMPS FOR 
ULTIMATE ASSIGNMENT TO USNA FROM THE FOLLOWING EIGHT FIELDS (DOCTOR  OF 
PHILOSOPHY DEGREE STUDY LOCATION - DISCIPLINE):
    A.  NPS - CONTROL SYSTEMS ENGINEERING; QQQQ
    B.  CIVINS - CHEMISTRY;
    C.  CIVINS - NAVAL ARCHITECTURE OR OCEAN ENGINEERING;
    D.  NPS - MATHEMATICS (OPERATIONS RESEARCH);
    E.  NPS - COMPUTER SCIENCE/INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY;
    F.  NPS - PHYSICS;
    G.  NPS - OCEANOGRAPHY;
    H.  CIVINS - LEADERSHIP (SOCIOLOGY).
REF A PROVIDES SPECIFIC GUIDANCE CONCERNING QUALIFYING CIVINS FOR CHEMISTRY, 
NAVAL ARCHITECTURE OR OCEAN ENGINEERING, AND LEADERSHIP.

4.  THE FY13 PMP SELECTION BOARD MAY SELECT ONE PMP FOR ENROLLMENT IN DOCTORAL 
STUDIES AT NPS IN COMPUTER SCIENCE, WITH ULTIMATE ASSIGNMENT TO NPS.

5.  TO ENSURE AN ADEQUATE RETURN ON THE EDUCATIONAL INVESTMENT IN PMP 
OFFICERS, THE FOLLOWING YEAR GROUP (YG) RESTRICTIONS APPLY.  YG ELIGIBILITY IS 
NOT WAIVERABLE:
    A.  YG-95 AND JUNIOR FOR CDR OR CDR SELECT WITHOUT PHD.
    B.  YG-94 CDR APPLICANTS WITHOUT PHD COMING OUT OF OPERATIONAL COMMAND BY 
JULY 2013 MAY SEEK A WAIVER OF YEAR GROUP CONSTRAINTS.
    C.  YG-91 AND JUNIOR FOR CDR OR CDR SELECT WITH PHD.

6.  OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGERS WILL IDENTIFY DESIGNATORS AND YEAR GROUPS THAT 
ARE ELIGIBLE TO TRANSFER A LIMITED NUMBER OF ACTIVE DUTY OFFICERS FROM THEIR 
CURRENT COMMUNITY TO PMP.  OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGERS WILL ALSO REVIEW ALL PMP 
APPLICATION SUBMISSIONS TO ENSURE ELIGIBILITY GUIDELINES HAVE BEEN MET PRIOR 
TO RELEASING THE APPLICATIONS TO THE BOARD.

7.  THE FOLLOWING DRAFT POD/POW NOTE IS PROVIDED FOR USE IN THE
 FLEET:  "THERE
IS A CONTINUING NEED FOR WARFARE QUALIFIED NAVAL OFFICERS TO SERVE AS LONG-
TERM OFFICER-INSTRUCTORS AT THE U.S. NAVAL ACADEMY (USNA) AND NAVAL 
POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL (NPS).  THE PMP COMMUNITY PROVIDES DOCTORAL DEGREE

THE DEADLINE FOR APPLICATIONS FOR THE FY13 PMP COMMUNITY IS 1 MAY 2012 WITH
