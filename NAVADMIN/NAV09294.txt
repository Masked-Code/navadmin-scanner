SUBJ/FLEET RESERVE ASSOCIATION 82ND NATIONAL CONVENTION//

RMKS/1.  THE FLEET RESERVE ASSOCIATION (FRA) 82ND NATIONAL CONVENTION IS 
SCHEDULED AT THE CAVALIER HOTEL, 42ND STREET AND ATLANTIC AVENUE, VIRGINIA 
BEACH, VA FROM 21 - 24 OCTOBER 2009.

2.  ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE AND RETIRED NAVY, MARINE CORPS, AND COAST GUARD 
PERSONNEL ARE INVITED TO ATTEND THE OPENING CEREMONIES, LEADERSHIP FORUM AND 
SPECIAL SESSIONS WORKSHOPS.  SPECIAL SESSIONS AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT 
WORKSHOPS INCLUDE:  A NAVY, MARINE CORPS AND COAST GUARD SENIOR ENLISTED 
LEADERSHIP PANEL, PRESENTATIONS BY SEA SERVICE RESERVE SENIOR ENLISTED LEADERS 
AND NAVY RECRUITING COMMAND, AND WORKSHOPS ON DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE HEALTH 
CARE AND DENTAL BENEFITS, VETERANS BENEFITS, THE LEGISLATIVE ADVOCACY PROCESS 
AND OTHER SESSIONS FOCUSED ON BENEFITS AND QUALITY OF LIFE PROGRAMS.  COMMANDS 
ARE AUTHORIZED TO ISSUE PERMISSIVE TRAVEL ORDERS WITH THE UNDERSTANDING THAT 
SERVICE MEMBERS WILL NOT BE ENTITLED TO REIMBURSEMENT FOR ANY TRAVEL, 
TRANSPORTATION, PER DIEM, OR MISCELLANEOUS EXPENSES IN CONNECTION WITH THEIR 
EXECUTION.  PERMISSIVE TRAVEL ORDERS MAY BE ISSUED IAW MILPERSMAN 1320-080 
(PARA 1E) FOR THOSE WHO DESIRE TO ATTEND.

3.  ADDITIONAL INFORMATION IS AVAILABLE ON THE FRA WEBSITE AT WWW.FRA.ORG OR 
BY CALLING 1-800-FRA-1924.

4.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1.//
