SUBJ/SUBMARINE ACQUISITION CORPS ELIGIBLE SELECTION BOARD//

RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the next Submarine Acquisition 
Corps (AC) eligible selection board the week of 29 August 2016.  AC eligible 
opportunities are available for command screened officers in year groups 94, 
96, 97 and 98.

AC community manager no later than 8 July 2016.

3.  Application guidance and AC candidate information sheets may be obtained 
by contacting the AC community manager.

4.  Point of contact is CAPT Douglas Adams, AC Community Manager, at
(202) 781-4907/DSN 664-4907 or via e-mail at douglas.j.adams(at)navy.mil.

5.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or 30 September 2016, 
whichever comes first.
