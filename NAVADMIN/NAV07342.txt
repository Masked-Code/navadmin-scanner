SUBJ/FY-08 FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) AVIATION CAREER CONTINUATION PAY 
(ACCP)// 
RMKS/1.  A VITAL PART OF DEVELOPING A TOTAL FORCE STRATEGY AND 
MAINTAINING COMBAT READINESS IS TO PROVIDE APPROPRIATE INCENTIVES TO 
RETAIN SKILLED PERSONNEL FOR CRITICAL NAVAL AVIATION ENTERPRISE BILLETS.  
THE FTS ACCP PROGRAM PROVIDES SELECTED BONUSES AS AN INCENTIVE TO 
ELIGIBLE PILOTS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS THROUGH DEPARTMENT HEAD AND 
COMMAND BILLETS.
2.  THE FY-08 FTS ACCP PROGRAM CONTINUES THE TIERED BONUS SYSTEM OF 
PREVIOUS YEARS THAT IS TIED DIRECTLY TO FORCE STRUCTURE AND TARGETS 
INITIAL ELIGIBLE AVIATORS AND COMMAND BILLETS.  ANNUAL RATES ARE 15,000 
DOLLARS FOR ALL BONUSES. 
3.  FY-08 FTS ACCP APPLICATIONS MUST BE RECEIVED PRIOR TO 26 SEPTEMBER 
2008.
4.  FOR SPECIFIC DETAILS, ELIGIBILITY, ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURES AND 
APPLICATION LETTER FORMAT VISIT THE FTS AVIATION OFFICER COMMUNITY 
MANAGER WEBSITE AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/PERS44/ 
FULLTIMESUPPORTMOBILIZED/AVIATORS.
