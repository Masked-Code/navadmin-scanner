RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE FY09-2 TEST PILOT SCHOOLS 
SELECTION BOARD (BOARD #380), SCHEDULED TO CONVENE 21 JULY 2009.  THIS 
BOARD WILL SELECT HIGHLY QUALIFIED NAVAL AVIATORS AND NAVAL FLIGHT 
OFFICERS FOR THE U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USNTPS), THE NAVAL 
POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL/U.S. NAVAL TEST PILOT SCHOOL (NSTPS) COOPERATIVE
PROGRAM, THE U.S. AIR FORCE TEST PILOT SCHOOL (USAFTPS), AND THE 
BRITISH EMPIRE TEST PILOT SCHOOL (ETPS).
2.  TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION, A COMPLETE APPLICATION 
LETTER, AS DESCRIBED IN REF A, AND A COMMAND ENDORSEMENT, MUST BE 
RECEIVED AT THE NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND(NPC) CUSTOMER SERVICE CENTER 
(CSC) NOT LATER THAN 19 JUNE 2009.  THE ENDORSEMENT SHOULD CONSIDER 
WHETHER THE APPLICANT, IF SELECTED, IS AVAILABLE TO REPORT FOR INITIAL 
TRAINING AS EARLY AS OCTOBER 2009.  THE SERVICE MEMBER'S EMAIL 
ADDRESS(ES), PHONE NUMBER(S), COLLEGE TRANSCRIPTS AND POST-TPS TEST 
SQUADRON ASSIGNMENT PREFERENCE MUST BE INCLUDED IN THE APPLICATION AND 
ALSO RECEIVED BY 19 JUNE 2009.

5.  ELIGIBILITY:
    A.  DESIGNATED NAVAL AVIATORS AND NAVAL FLIGHT OFFICERS THROUGH THE
GRADE OF LIEUTENANT COMMANDER MAY BE CONSIDERED. 
    B.  UNRESTRICTED LINE (13XX) AVIATORS SHOULD BE DUE COURSE JUNIOR
OFFICERS (O-2 OR O-3) WITH FORECASTED TIMING TO COMPLETE THE SELECTED 
TPS PROGRAM AND A FOLLOW-ON 24-MONTH TEST TOUR PRIOR TO RETURNING TO 
SQUADRON DEPARTMENT HEAD TOUR.  INTERESTED CANDIDATES SHOULD CONSULT 
THEIR COMMUNITY ASSIGNMENTS OFFICER TO CONFIRM CAREER TIMING.  
    C.  AEROSPACE ENGINEERING DUTY OFFICERS (AEDO) (1510) THROUGH THE 
GRADE OF LIEUTENANT COMMANDER ARE ELIGIBLE TO APPLY IF ABLE TO SERVE A 
MINIMUM 24-MONTH FLIGHT TEST TOUR FOLLOWING TPS WHILE MEETING AEDO 
CAREER MILESTONES.  INTERESTED AEDOS SHOULD CONTACT THE AEDO 
ASSIGNMENTS OFFICER TO CONFIRM CAREER TIMING.
