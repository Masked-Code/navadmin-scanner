RMKS/1.  IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I ANNOUNCE THE SELECTION OF NAVAL 
STATION NORFOLK (NAVSTA NORVA) AS THE NAVY'S WINNER OF THE 2014 COMMANDER IN 
CHIEF'S ANNUAL AWARD FOR INSTALLATION EXCELLENCE. IN ACCORDANCE WITH REF A, 
THIS AWARD RECOGNIZES ONE INSTALLATION FROM EACH SERVICE THAT HAS MADE BEST 
USE OF AVAILABLE RESOURCES TO ACCOMPLISH ITS ASSIGNED MISSION, AND HAS 
FOCUSED ON IMAGINATIVE AND INNOVATIVE MANAGEMENT ACTIONS THAT HAVE INCREASED 
WORK FORCE PRODUCTIVITY WHILE ENHANCING THE QUALITY OF LIFE FOR ITS 
PERSONNEL. THIS OUTSTANDING NAVY INSTALLATION, REPRESENTED BY CAPTAIN D. A. 
CULLER (CO), WILL COMPETE FOR THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE'S 2014 COMMANDER IN 
CHIEF'S INSTALLATION EXCELLENCE AWARD.

2.  THE SELECTION WAS HIGHLY COMPETITIVE WITH THE WINNER CHOSEN FROM A FIELD 
OF TEN (10) LARGE INSTALLATIONS AND TEN (10) SMALL INSTALLATIONS.  THE TOP 
THREE NOMINEES IN EACH SIZE CATEGORY WERE  EXTREMELY CLOSE, AND I EXTEND A 
SPECIAL "WELL DONE" TO THE FIRST  PLACE SMALL CATEGORY WINNER WEAPONS STATION 
EARLE, CAPTAIN RICHARD  C. VALENTINE (CO).  SECOND PLACE WINNERS ARE: 
COMMANDER FLEET ACTIVITIES SASEBO, CAPTAIN CHARLES W. ROCK (CO) (LARGE 
CATEGORY), AND COMMANDER FLEET ACTIVITIES CHINHAE, COMMANDER QUINN SKINNER 
(CO) (SMALL CATEGORY).  THIRD PLACE WINNERS ARE NAVAL AIR STATION 
JACKSONVILLE, CAPTAIN ROY UNDERSANDER (CO) (LARGE CATEGORY), AND U.S. NAVAL 
SUPPORT FACILITY DIEGO GARCIA, CAPTAIN MATTHEW R. VANDERSLUIS (CO) (SMALL 
CATEGORY).  THESE TOP NAVY CATEGORY WINNERS WILL RECEIVE A NAVY MONETARY 
AWARD FOR THEIR SELECTION.
