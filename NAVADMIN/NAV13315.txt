SUBJ/NOTICE OF CONVENING FY-15 OFFICER CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARDS AND 
ANNOUNCEMENT OF CONTINUATION POLICY//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE ACTIVE-DUTY OFFICER CONTINUATION 
SELECTION BOARDS.  THE ACTIVE-DUTY OFFICER CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARDS WILL 
CONVENE, WHEN REQUIRED, IMMEDIATELY FOLLOWING THE CORRESPONDING FY-15 
PROMOTION SELECTION BOARDS.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE DUTY LIST WHO 
ARE FULLY QUALIFIED TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE DUTY AND MEET THE REQUIREMENTS IN 
PARAGRAPHS 2 THROUGH 4 WILL BE CONSIDERED FOR CONTINUATION.  WHERE 
APPLICABLE, CONTINUATION PERIOD IS BASED ON YEARS OF ACTIVE SERVICE (YOAS) 
CALCULATED FROM THE ACTIVE-DUTY BASE DATE TO APPROXIMATELY SEVEN MONTHS AFTER 
APPROVAL OF THE PROMOTION BOARD RESULTS.

2.  LIEUTENANT COMMANDER UNRESTRICTED LINE, RESTRICTED LINE, AND STAFF CORPS 
(LIMITED DUTY OFFICERS ARE DISCUSSED IN PARAGRAPH 3):
    A.  TWICE FAILURE OF SELECTION (2XFOS) LCDRS WITH 14-18 YOAS.
OFFICERS SELECTED FOR CONTINUATION CAN CONTINUE UNTIL ELIGIBLE FOR RETIREMENT 
AT THE COMPLETION OF 20 YOAS.
    B.  2XFOS LCDRS WITH LESS THAN 14 YOAS.  OFFICERS SELECTED FOR 
CONTINUATION CAN BE CONTINUED IN THREE YEAR INCREMENTS OR UNTIL RETIREMENT 
ELIGIBLE AT THE COMPLETION OF 20 YOAS.
    C.  2XFOS LCDRS WITH 18 OR MORE YOAS, AS PROVIDED BY LAW, WILL NOT BE 
CONSIDERED FOR CONTINUATION, BUT WILL BE RETAINED ON ACTIVE DUTY UNTIL 
RETIREMENT ELIGIBLE AT YOAS.

3.  COMMANDER, LIEUTENANT COMMANDER, AND LIEUTENANT LIMITED DUTY OFFICERS 
(LDO):
    A.  ALL PERMANENT LDOS SELECTED FOR PROMOTION IN FY-15 WHO ARE

SUBJECT TO STATUTORY RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THEIR SCHEDULED PROMOTION 
DATES AND/OR PRIOR TO COMPLETING THE TIME-IN-GRADE NECESSARY TO RETIRE IN THE 
NEXT HIGHER GRADE AFTER PROMOTION WILL BE CONSIDERED FOR CONTINUATION.  THESE 
OFFICERS WILL BE CONTINUED FOR A PERIOD SUFFICIENT TO ALLOW THEM TO ACCEPT 
PROMOTION AND, AS REQUIRED, ATTAIN SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT THE 
HIGHER GRADE.
    B.  ALL PERMANENT LDOS WHO ARE ON ACTIVE DUTY AT THE TIME WHEN THE 
PROMOTION BOARD MEETS AND ARE CONSIDERED IN ZONE FOR PROMOTION AND WHO ARE 
SUBJECT TO STATUTORY RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THE END OF FY-14 WILL BE 
OFFERED CONTINUATION FOR A PERIOD SUFFICIENT TO ALLOW THEM TO ACCEPT 
PROMOTION AND, AS REQUIRED, ATTAIN SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT THE 
HIGHER GRADE (IF SELECTED FOR PROMOTION) OR UNTIL 30 SEPTEMBER 2014 (IF NOT 
SELECTED FOR PROMOTION).
    C.  2XFOS LDO LCDRS SELECTED FOR CONTINUATION WILL BE CONTINUED TO 
RETIREMENT ELIGIBILITY AT THE COMPLETION OF 20 YOAS.

4.  LIEUTENANT CHAPLAIN CORPS.  2XFOS OFFICERS SELECTED FOR CONTINUATION CAN 
BE CONTINUED FOR AN INCREMENT OF THREE YEARS.

5.  CHIEF WARRANT OFFICERS (CWO):
    A.  ALL CWOS SELECTED FOR PROMOTION IN FY-15 WHO ARE SUBJECT TO STATUTORY 
RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THEIR SCHEDULED PROMOTION DATES AND/OR PRIOR TO 
COMPLETING THE TIME-IN-GRADE NECESSARY TO RETIRE IN THE NEXT HIGHER GRADE 
AFTER PROMOTION WILL BE CONSIDERED FOR CONTINUATION.  THESE OFFICERS WILL BE 
CONTINUED FOR A PERIOD SUFFICIENT TO ALLOW THEM TO ACCEPT PROMOTION AND, AS 
REQUIRED, ATTAIN SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT THE HIGHER GRADE.
    B.  ALL CWOS WHO ARE ON ACTIVE DUTY AT THE TIME THE PROMOTION BOARD MEETS 
AND ARE CONSIDERED IN ZONE FOR PROMOTION AND WHO ARE

SUBJECT TO STATUTORY RETIREMENT FOR YOAS PRIOR TO THE END OF FY-14 WILL BE 
CONTINUED FOR A PERIOD SUFFICIENT TO ALLOW THEM TO ACCEPT PROMOTION AND 
ATTAIN SUFFICIENT TIME-IN-GRADE TO RETIRE AT THE HIGHER GRADE (IF SELECTED 
FOR PROMOTION) OR UNTIL 30 SEPTEMBER 2014 (IF NOT SELECTED FOR PROMOTION).

6.  OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE-DUTY LIST WHO MEET ANY OF THE CRITERIA IN 
PARAGRAPHS 2 THROUGH 4 SHALL CHECK BUPERS ONLINE, HTTPS://WWW.BOL.NAVY.MIL/, 
AFTER RELEASE OF THE RESPECTIVE SELECTION ALNAV MESSAGE, TO DETERMINE IF THEY 
WERE SELECTED FOR CONTINUATION.
ONCE LOGGED INTO BUPERS ONLINE, CLICK ON ADVANCEMENT, AND THEN ON THE 
APPROPRIATE OFFICER (PROMOTION/CONTINUATION) BOARD TO DETERMINE THE RESULTS.

7.  PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD CONVENING DATES ARE AS FOLLOWS:
    A.  ACTIVE O6 LINE PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD CONVENES 14 JANUARY 2014.  
THE ACTIVE O5 LDO LINE CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    B.  ACTIVE O6 STAFF CORPS PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD CONVENES 4 FEBRUARY 
2014.
    C.  ACTIVE O5 LINE PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD CONVENES 11 FEBRUARY 2014.  
THE ACTIVE O4 LINE CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    D.  ACTIVE O5 STAFF CORPS PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD CONVENES 25 MARCH 
2014.  THE ACTIVE O4 STAFF CORPS CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARD WILL FOLLOW 
THIS BOARD.
    E.  ACTIVE O4 LINE PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD CONVENES 7 APRIL 2014.  THE 
ACTIVE O3 LDO CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    F.  ACTIVE O4 STAFF CORPS PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD CONVENES 13 MAY 2014.  
THE ACTIVE O3 LDO STAFF CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARD WILL FOLLOW THIS BOARD.
    G.  ACTIVE CWO3/4/5 PROMOTION SELECTION BOARDS CONVENE 16 JUNE 2014.  THE 
ACTIVE CWO2/3/4 CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARDS WILL FOLLOW THESE BOARDS.

8.  CORRESPONDENCE ADDRESSED TO THE PROMOTION SELECTION BOARD WILL NOT BE 
FORWARDED TO A SUBSEQUENT CONTINUATION SELECTION BOARD FOR CONSIDERATION.  
OFFICERS MUST COMMUNICATE WITH EACH BOARD SEPARATELY.
