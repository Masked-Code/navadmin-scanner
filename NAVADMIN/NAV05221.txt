GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  DUE TO HURRICANE KATRINA AND THE EVACUATION OF
SEVERAL COMMANDS, THE FOLLOWING GUIDANCE AND PROCEDURES FOR THE
ORDERING AND ADMINISTRATION OF CYCLE 188 SUBSTITUTE EXAMINATIONS

(SUBEXAMS) IS PROVIDED.
2.  THE DEADLINE FOR ORDERING SUBEXAMS FOR SAILORS AFFECTED OR
EVACUATED DUE TO HURRICANE KATRINA HAS BEEN EXTENDED TO 14 OCT 05.
WILL EVALUATE THE NEED FOR FURTHER EXTENSION BASED ON THE STATUS OF
HURRICANE KATRINA RECOVERY EFFORTS.
3.  TO ACCOMMODATE HURRICANE EVACUEES, EDUCATION SERVICES OFFICES
(ESO) RECEIVING SERVICE MEMBERS FROM COMMANDS OTHER THAN THOSE
ASSIGNED TO THEM SHALL ADMINISTER EXAMINATIONS.  SAILORS WHO WERE
REQUIRED TO EVACUATE AND DO NOT HAVE THE UNIFORM OF THE DAY ARE
AUTHORIZED TO PARTICIPATE IN THE EXAMINATION.  IF AVAILABILITY OF
EXAMINATIONS ARE LIMITED, ESO SHALL SPLIT EXAMINATIONS FOR ALL
CANDIDATES.  IF SAILORS REPORT WITHOUT WORKSHEETS, ESO SHALL
ADMINISTER EXAMINATIONS AND CONTACT THE NAVAL EDUCATION TRAINING
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT TECHNOLOGY CENTER (NETPDTC) N321.  NETPDTC
WILL CREATE THE WORKSHEETS.
4.  SUBMIT SUBEXAM REQUESTS PER TABLE 4-3 OF THE ADVANCEMENT MANUAL
(BUPERSINST 1430.16E) TO NETPDTC (N321), INFO COMNAVPERSCOM
(PERS-4811/4812).
