the promotion and not be tendered the promoting appointment.  The delay 
authority will immediately forward the notification of delay with supporting 
documentation to COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-83), copy to COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-806).

promoting are mentally, physically, morally and professionally qualified in 
accordance with title 10, U.S. Code 624.

the most recent Physical Fitness Assessment.  COs shall verify the promoting 
officer's information in the Physical Readiness Information Management System 
to ensure compliance with reference (a), as modified by reference (b), and 
delay the officers promotion in accordance with paragraph 3 of this NAVADMIN, 
if required.
