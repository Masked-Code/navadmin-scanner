RMKS/1.  This NAVADMIN announces convening of the Defense Acquisition Corps 
Selection Board on 3 December 2015.  Defense Acquisition Corps membership is 
a prerequisite for assignment to one of the approximately
1,560 Department of the Navy (DON) critically-coded officer acquisition 
billets throughout the force.

2.  Policy and guidance on Defense Acquisition Corps membership procedures 
for DON active-duty officers may be found at the following
website:
http://www.secnav.navy.mil/rda/workforce/Pages/StrategyPolicy.aspx.

3.  In order to be considered for membership, prospective applicants must 
meet all the prerequisites for Defense Acquisition Corps membership listed in 
reference (a) prior to board convening date.  Applications (no endorsements 
required) must be received by 30 October 2015, to be considered by the board.

4.  Applications should include:
    a.  Defense Acquisition Corps Board Application Data Sheet (NAVPERS 
1301/86), found at the following website:
http://www.npc.navy.mil/bupers-npc/reference/forms/NAVPERS/Pages/
default.aspx
    b.  Copy of eDACM transcript.
    c.  All college transcripts (copies are acceptable)
    d.  Applicable FITREPs to validate requested non-acquisition coded 
experience.
    e.  Additional pertinent information for consideration by selection 
board.  To ensure submitted packages are accepted, all necessary 
documentation must be included.  The preferred method of submitting 
applications is by mail.  Submission of applications from multiple methods is 
strongly discouraged.
    f.  Mail applications to:
	  Navy Personnel Command
	  FY-17 Defense Acquisition Corps Selection Board
	  Customer Service Center
	  5720 Integrity Drive
	  Millington TN 38055-4470
    g.  Applications sent via FEDEX/UPS/DHL should use the following
address:
	  Navy Personnel Command
	  FY-17 Defense Acquisition Corps Selection Board
	  5640 Ticonderoga Loop Bldg 768 RM E203
	  Millington TN 38055-3400
Applications will also be accepted via encrypted e-mail (signed and scanned 
copies only) for those officers deployed aboard ships or stationed at remote 
sites where regular mail could be disrupted.
E-mail packages to:  cscselboard(at)navy.mil.  Applications received after 30 
October 2015 will not be accepted and will be destroyed.

5.  E-mail questions or concerns to pers-447_mail(at)navy.mil.  You may also 
access the Navy Personnel Command Acquisition Workforce Management Branch 
website at http://www.npc.navy.mil/bupers-npc/
officer/Detailing/acquisition/Pages/default2.aspx for additional information 
pertaining to the Defense Acquisition Corps.

6.  This message will remain in effect until superseded or canceled, 
whichever comes first.

7.  Released by Vice Admiral W. F. Moran, N1.//
