SUBJ/TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS (TIP) POLICY UPDATE//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  SECNAV HAS DIRECTED MANDATORY AWARENESS TRAINING
REGARDING COMBATING TIP FOR DON PERSONNEL DEPLOYING OVERSEAS,
INCLUDING MILITARY, CIVILIAN, AND CONTRACT PERSONNEL.
2.  FOR CLARIFICATION, DEPLOYING OVERSEAS IS CONSIDERED ANY TRAVEL
OCONUS, REGARDLESS OF TYPE OR DURATION.
3.  TO COMPLETE TIP TRAINING, ONLINE REMAINS THE PRIMARY VENUE.  IF
COMPLETED ON NKO, TRAINING WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY DOCUMENTED IN THE
MEMBER'S ELECTRONIC TRAINING JACKET.  FOR INDIVIDUALS WITHOUT AN
ELECTRONIC TRAINING JACKET, PROOF OF COMPLETION IS STILL REQUIRED.
COMMANDERS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR DOCUMENTING COMPLETION FOR PERSONNEL
WHO RECEIVE THIS TRAINING.  MANUAL INPUT TO ELECTRONIC TRAINING
JACKETS VIA FLEET TRAINING MANAGEMENT AND PLANNING SYSTEM (FLTMPS)
OR NAVY TRAINING MANAGEMENT AND PLANNING SYSTEM (NTMPS) ARE ALSO
OPTIONS.
4.  A CD-ROM IS CURRENTLY BEING MAILED TO ALL COMMANDS ON THE
GENERAL MILITARY TRAINING DISTRIBUTION LIST, ATTN ADMIN OFFICERS.
THIS CD-ROM INCLUDES BOTH THE TIP INITIAL TRAINING MODULE AND TIP
REFRESHER TRAINING MODULE AND CAN BE USED AS AN ALTERNATE METHOD OF
COMPLETING THE TRAINING.
5.  COMMENCING MAR 06, THE DEFENSE INSTRUCTIONAL TECHNOLOGY
INFORMATION SYSTEM (DITIS) WILL BEGIN STOCKING THE CD-ROMS.
