SECRETARY OF DEFENSE FIELD-LEVEL MAINTENANCE AWARDS.  I WOULD LIKE TO EXTEND

THROUGHOUT THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE.

MAINTENANCE UNITS IN THREE CATEGORIES (SMALL, MEDIUM, LARGE) THROUGHOUT THE

HELICOPTER MARITIME STRIKE SQUADRON SEVEN SEVEN (HSM-77).  HSM-77 IS 
HOMEPORTED IN SAN DIEGO, CA AND IS ASSIGNED TO CARRIER AIR WING TWO.

2012 IN CONJUNCTION WITH THE 2012 DOD MAINTENANCE SYMPOSIUM AND EXHIBITION.
