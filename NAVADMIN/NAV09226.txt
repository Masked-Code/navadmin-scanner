SUBJ/NUCLEAR OFFICER INCENTIVE PAY// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES CHANGES TO NUCLEAR OFFICER INCENTIVE
PAY (NOIP).  NAVY LEADERSHIP REMAINS COMMITTED TO RETAINING THE RIGHT 
QUALITY AND QUANITITY OF NUCLEAR TRAINED OFFICERS.  THE EFFECTIVE NOIP 
INSTRUCTION IS OPNAVINST 7220.11C AND CANCELS OPNAVINST 7220.11B CH-1.
2.  THE FOLLOWING CHANGES ARE EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY:
    A.  CONTINUATION PAY (COPAY) CONTRACT LENGTHS ARE NOW AUTHORIZED 
TO BE SIGNED BY ELIGIBLE OFFICERS FOR LENGTHS OF THREE, FOUR, FIVE, 
SIX, OR SEVEN YEARS.  
    B.  THE RECOUPMENT POLICY HAS BEEN MODIFIED TO CONFORM TO UNDER 
SECRETARY OF DEFENSE RECOUPMENT MEMORANDUM DATED 21 MAY 2008. 
3.  QUESTIONS REGARDING NOIP SHOULD BE DIRECTED TO COMNAVPERSCOM 
(PERS-421D), AT (901) 874-3933/DSN 882. 
4.  RELEASED BY VADM MARK FERGUSON, N1//
