3.  IF A SELECTED OFFICER DOES NOT DECLINE HIS OR HER PROMOTION IN

HIS OR HER PROMOTION MUST SUBMIT THE DECLINATION IN WRITING TO 
COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-831), PRIOR TO THE EFFECTIVE DATE INDICATED 
ABOVE.
4.  THE COMMANDING OFFICER, THE SECRETARY OF THE NAVY OR HIS 
DESIGNEE, HAVE THE AUTHORITY TO DELAY PROJECTED APPOINTMENTS.  UPON 
THE EXERCISE OF A DELAY, THE OFFICER SHALL BE NOTIFIED IN WRITING 
BEFORE THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF THE APPOINTMENT, AND NOT BE TENDERED THE 
APPOINTMENT.  THE DELAY AUTHORITY WILL IMMEDIATELY FORWARD THE 
RECOMMENDATION TO DELAY WITH SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION TO 
COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-83), COPY TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-831).
