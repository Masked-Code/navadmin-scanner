RMKS/1. PER REF A, NOMINATIONS ARE REQUESTED FOR THE ANNUAL ADMIRAL STAN 
ARTHUR AWARDS.
CATEGORIES INCLUDE:
    A.  MILITARY LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR
    B.  CIVILIAN LOGISTICIAN OF THE YEAR
    C.  LOGISTICS TEAM(S) OF THE YEAR

2.  THE INTENT OF THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR AWARD IS TO RECOGNIZE NAVY MILITARY 
AND CIVILIAN INDIVIDUALS AND TEAMS WHOSE CONTRIBUTIONS AND SUGGESTIONS DURING 
CALENDAR YEAR 2010 HAVE:
    A.  MEASURABLY SUPPORTED THE LOGISTICS MISSION OF THE NAVY
    B.  PROMOTED INNOVATIVE IDEAS AND PRACTICABLE BENEFITS TO THE
    NAVY
    C.  ENHANCED THE LOGISTICS PROFESSION

3.  NOMINEES MAY BE INVOLVED IN ANY AND ALL PHASES OF NAVY ACQUISITION, 
OPERATIONAL LOGISTICS, AND JOINT SERVICES LOGISTICS, FROM EARLY LIFE-CYCLE 
PLANNING TO IN-SERVICE LOGISTICS SUPPORT.  CONTRACTORS MAY BE INCLUDED ON TEAM 
AWARD SUBMISSIONS, BUT WILL NOT RECEIVE MONETARY AWARDS.

4.  INDIVIDUALS AND TEAM(S) SELECTED TO RECEIVE THE ADMIRAL STAN ARTHUR AWARDS 
FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2010 WILL BE ANNOUNCED VIA NAVAL MESSAGE. THE AWARD CEREMONY 
IS SCHEDULED FOR WEDNESDAY, 8 JUN 2011, AT THE NAVY MEMORIAL, WASHINGTON, DC.

5.  AWARD SELECTEES RECEIVE THE FOLLOWING:
    A. PLAQUE FOR INDIVIDUAL CATEGORY AND EACH TEAM WINNER
    B. $5,000 EACH FOR THE MILITARY AND CIVILIAN WINNERS
    C. $10,000 FOR EACH LOGISTICS TEAM TO SHARE

6.  SUBMIT NOMINATIONS VIA ECHELON TWO COMMANDS TO DEPUTY CHIEF OF NAVAL 
OPERATIONS, FLEET READINESS AND LOGISTICS (N40).  ECHELON TWO COMMANDS MAY 
SUBMIT A TOTAL OF THREE NOMINATIONS, NO MORE THAN ONE PER AWARD CATEGORY. 
AWARD NOMINATIONS MUST BE RECEIVED BY OPNAV N40 BY COB 01 APRIL 2011. TO 
RECEIVE CONFIRMATION OF NOMINATION, NOMINATION PACKAGE MUST INCLUDE E-MAIL 
ADDRESS AND PHONE NUMBER OF ECHELON TWO POC.

7.  RELEASED BY J.A. DECKER, N4B.//
