NARR/REF A IS DOD MEMORANDUM, TRAINING AND RECORDS SCHEDULING TO IMPROVE DOD 
RECORDS MANAGEMENT AND CONTROLLED UNCLASSIFIED INFORMATION HANDLING.   
REF B IS NAVADMIN 201/22, FISCAL YEAR 2023 GENERAL MILITARY TRAINING 
REQUIREMENTS//.

RMKS/1.  As directed in reference (a), all personnel must complete records 
management and controlled unclassified information (CUI) training no later 
than 15 January 2023.  Commands may provide this training online or in 
person.  Specific training required:
    a.  Records Management in the DON:  Everyones Responsibility
(CIN:  DOR-RM-010-1.2).
    b.  DoD Mandatory Controlled Unclassified (CUI) Training (CIN:
TWMS-686564 or JS-US082).

2.  Training is available on the MyNavy Portal General Military Training 
website at https://www.mnp.navy.mil/group/general-skills-
training/gmt/.

3.  Commands will ensure completed training is documented in Fleet Training 
Management and Planning System.

4.  Point of contact is LCDR Jonathan Greenberg, Education and Training 
Branch (OPNAV N13M7T), jonathan.d.greenberg.mil(at)us.navy.mil.

5.  This NAVADMIN supersedes the records management and CUI training 
requirement of reference (b) and will remain in effect until superseded or 30 
September 2023, whichever occurs first.

6.  Released by Vice Admiral Richard J. Cheeseman, Jr., N1.//
