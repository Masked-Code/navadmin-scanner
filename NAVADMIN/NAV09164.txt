RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN SERVES AS NOTICE OF COMMANDER, NAVAL PERSONNEL 
COMMAND'S INTENTION TO CONVENE THE FTS CDR SERAD AND FTS CAPT SELECTIVE 
RETENTION BOARDS FOR FY-10.  THESE ACTIONS ARE NECESSARY TO BRING FTS 
CAPTAIN AND COMMANDER INVENTORY IN LINE WITH AUTHORIZED END STRENGTH, 
ALLOW FOR REASONABLE PROMOTION OPPORTUNITY, AND PERMIT SUFFICIENT 
JUNIOR OFFICER ACCESSIONS TO MEET FTS OFFICER OPERATIONAL REQUIREMENTS.

2.  AS AUTHORIZED BY SECNAVINST 1920.6C, A FULL TIME SUPPORT
(FTS) COMMANDER SELECTIVE EARLY RELEASE FROM ACTIVE DUTY
(SERAD) BOARD WILL CONVENE ON 10 AUGUST 2009 FOR THE PURPOSE OF 
CONSIDERING FTS COMMANDERS FOR EARLY RELEASE FROM ACTIVE DUTY.

HAVE A CDR DATE OF RANK WITHIN FY-05 AND FY-06.  THE FOLLOWING

4.  ELIGIBLE OFFICERS SHALL BE CONSIDERED FOR SERAD ONLY AMONG THOSE IN 
THE SAME COMPETITIVE CATEGORY AND PROMOTION YEAR GROUP.  THE NUMBER 
RECOMMENDED FOR SERAD SHALL NOT EXCEED 30 PERCENT OF ALL OFFICERS 
CONSIDERED IN THE SAME COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.  AN OFFICER WITH AN 
APPROVED RETIREMENT DATE OF 1 SEPTEMBER 2010 OR EARLIER AT THE TIME THE 
BOARD CONVENES WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED FOR SERAD.

5.  FTS COMMANDERS SELECTED FOR SERAD WILL BE FORMALLY NOTIFIED AS SOON 
AS BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED.
OFFICERS SELECTED FOR SERAD SHALL BE RELEASED FROM ACTIVE DUTY NO LATER 
THAN 1 SEPTEMBER 2010 OR THE FIRST DAY OF THE MONTH AFTER THE OFFICER 
ATTAINS 20 YEARS OF ACTIVE SERVICE, WHICHEVER IS LATER.  RELEASE FROM 
ACTIVE DUTY WILL BE DEFERRED, IF NECESSARY, TO ENABLE THE OFFICER TO 
BECOME ELIGIBLE FOR RETIREMENT WITH PAY.

6.  AS REQUIRED BY SECNAVINST 1920.6C, THE FTS CAPTAIN SELECTIVE 
RETENTION BOARD MUST MEET TO CONTINUE FTS CAPTAINS BEYOND THEIR 
RESPECTIVE "GATES."  THIS BOARD WILL CONSIDER "FIRST GATE" CAPTAINS WHO 
WERE PROMOTED DURING FY-08, AND "SECOND GATE" CAPTAINS WHO WERE 
PROMOTED DURING FY-05.

7.  "FIRST GATE" FTS CAPTAINS WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT NUMBERS TO 
MAINTAIN APPROPRIATE FTS CAPTAIN END STRENGTH.  "SECOND GATE" CAPTAINS 
WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT NUMBERS TO FILL SPECIFIC SENIOR 
MANAGEMENT BILLETS.

8.  FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION WILL BE FORMALLY NOTIFIED 
AS SOON AS BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED.  PER SECNAVINST 1920.6C, 
"FIRST GATE" FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED 
FROM ACTIVE DUTY AT THE END OF THREE YEARS TIME IN GRADE.  "SECOND 
GATE" FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED FROM 
ACTIVE DUTY NO LATER THAN 1 SEPTEMBER 2010.

9.  FOR ADDITIONAL DETAILS AND SPECIFIC INFORMATION ON HOW TO 
COMMUNICATE WITH THE PRESIDENT, FTS CDR SERAD BOARD #416, AND FTS CAPT 
SELECTIVE RETENTION BOARD #417, REFER TO THE FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY 
MANAGEMENT WEBPAGE FOUND AT: HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/ 
COMMUNITYMANAGERS/RESERVEBRANCH/FULLTIMESUPPORT/.
