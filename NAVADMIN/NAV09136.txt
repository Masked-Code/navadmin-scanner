DCOIC COMPLETED PRIOR TO ARRIVING IN ATHENS.  FAILURE TO COMPLETE THIS 
COURSE PRIOR TO THE INITIAL ANNUAL TRAINING WILL CAUSE THE SELECTEE TO 
LOSE THE SEAT AND ROLL TO A LATER CLASS. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION CAN BE
