RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE NAVY COUNSELORS ASSOCIATION 
(NCA) 24TH ANNUAL PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING SYMPOSIUM 
AT THE COOK CONVENTION CENTER IN MEMPHIS, TN, FROM 24-27 SEPTEMBER 
2012.  THIS YEARS THEME IS:  "READY TODAY THROUGH RELEVANT AND 
CAPABLE TRAINING."

2.  THE SYMPOSIUM PROVIDES AN OPPORTUNITY TO DISCUSS A BROAD RANGE 
OF CAREER ISSUES AND CONCERNS, ALLOWS MEMBERS TO ADDRESS AND WORK 
TOWARD INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS TO CURRENT AND FUTURE RETENTION, 
ATTRITION, AND CAREER DEVELOPMENT ISSUES.  COMMAND CAREER 
COUNSELORS, COMMAND MASTER CHIEFS, CAREER DEVELOPMENT TEAM 
MEMBERS, RECRUITERS, DETAILERS, AND OTHER MILITARY AND CIVILIAN 
PERSONNEL INVOLVED IN THE PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF SAILORS ARE 
ENCOURAGED TO ATTEND.

3.  TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY ORDERS ARE REQUIRED TO COVER 
EXPENSES, INCLUDING THE REGISTRATION FEE OF $150.00.  THERE IS NO 
GOVERNMENT MESSING AVAILABLE IN THE SYMPOSIUM AREA.  HOTEL 
RESERVATION INFORMATION IS AVAILABLE AT WWW.USNCA.ORG.

4.  EACH YEAR DURING THE SYMPOSIUM, THE NCA RECOGNIZES THREE CAREER 
COUNSELORS OF THE YEAR, ONE FROM SEA DUTY (TYPE 2, 3, OR 4), ONE 
FROM SHORE DUTY (TYPE 1 OR 6), AND ONE FROM THE RESERVE FORCE.  
COMMANDS MAY NOMINATE ASSIGNED CAREER COUNSELORS (E6 AND BELOW) 
USING THE FORMAT IN THE NCA BY-LAWS AVAILABLE AT WWW.USNCA.ORG.  
PACKAGE MUST INCLUDE A 5X7, FRONT AND SIDE COLOR PHOTOGRAPH.  
MEMBER WILL BE IN NAVY SERVICE UNIFORM (NSU).  NOMINATIONS MUST BE 
ENDORSED BY RESPECTIVE TYCOMS AND SHOULD ADDRESS SPECIFIC 
ACCOMPLISHMENTS AND CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE CAREER COUNSELOR COMMUNITY 
DURING THE PREVIOUS 12 MONTHS.  PACKAGES MUST BE RECEIVED AT THE 
BELOW ADDRESS NOT LATER THAN 1 AUGUST 2012:
       NCCM(AW) JERMAINE RAWLS
       PRESIDENT, NAVY COUNSELOR ASSOCIATION, INC.
       NAVY RECRUITING EAST
       5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE BLDG 750/ROOM 258
       MILLINGTON, TN 38054

5.  PER REF A, COMMANDING OFFICERS ARE AUTHORIZED TO SUPPORT 
ATTENDANCE, AND MAY ISSUE ORDERS FOR ATTENDANCE AT GOVERNMENT 
EXPENSE AFTER DETERMINING THERE IS A LEGITIMATE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT 
PURPOSE, AND THE OBJECTIVES TO BE ACHIEVED JUSTIFY THE EXPENSES 
(INCLUDING FEES, TRAVEL COSTS, AND OTHER COSTS ATTRIBUTABLE TO 
PARTICIPATION).  COMMANDING OFFICERS ARE DIRECTED TO FOLLOW THE 
GUIDANCE IN REF A WHEN MAKING THIS DETERMINATION.  PER JFTR AND 
JTR, TAD ORDERS MAY COVER EXPENSES INCIDENT TO ATTENDANCE AND 
PARTICIPATION IN THE CONFERENCE INCLUDING TRANSPORTATION, PER 
DIEM, AND REGISTRATION FEES.  IF FUNDS ARE NOT AVAILABLE, 
PERMISSIVE TAD ORDERS MAY BE ISSUED.  RETURN FLIGHTS SHOULD NOT 
BE SCHEDULED PRIOR TO 1700 ON 27 SEPTEMBER 2012.

6.  REGISTRATION, TRANSPORTATION, AND LODGING INFORMATION CAN 
BE FOUND ON THE NCA WEBSITE AT:  WWW.USNCA.ORG.  

7.  QUESTION REGARDING REGISTRATION CAN BE HANDLED VIA THE NCA 
CUSTOMER SERVICE LINE AT (571) 344-3663.

8.  PER REF B, SELECTED RESERVE PERSONNEL ARE AUTHORIZED TO 
PARTICIPATE AT THE NCA CONFERENCE IN A PAID DRILL STATUS.

9.  POINT OF CONTACT:  NCCM(AW) JERMAINE RAWLS AT 
(901) 874-9157/DSN 882, OR VIA E-MAIL AT JERMAINE.RAWLS(AT)NAVY.MIL.
