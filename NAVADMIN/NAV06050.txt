2.  MANY SUPERB APPLICATION PACKAGES WENT BEFORE THE BOARD, AND
COMPETITION WAS EXTRAORDINARILY KEEN.  THOSE SELECTED HAD THE
FOLLOWING COMMON CHARACTERISTICS:
    A.  SUSTAINED SUPERIOR PERFORMANCE IN ALL ASSIGNMENTS WITH
CONSISTENT TOP RANKINGS AND BREAK-OUTS IN FITNESS REPORTS.
    B.  STEADY UPWARD GROWTH.  SUCCESSFUL APPLICANTS TOOK ON DUTIES
AND RESPONSIBILITIES THAT GOT THEM OUT OF THEIR "COMFORT ZONES."
THEY EARNED AS MANY QUALIFICATIONS AS POSSIBLE, AND COMPLETED
CORRESPONDENCE COURSES IN OTHER RATINGS IN OTHER WORK CENTERS THEY
SUPERVISED.  THEY ALSO ACHIEVED CRITICAL QUALIFICATIONS.
        - DCTT, ETT, REPAIR LOCKER LEADER, ETC.
        - JOOD/OOD UNDERWAY.
        - OOD INPORT.
        - WARFARE QUALIFICATION.
        - MASTER TRAINING SPECIALIST.
        - SUCCESSFUL SERVICE IN CHALLENGING LEADERSHIP AND TECHNICAL
PROFICIENCY POSITIONS SUCH AS RECRUITERS, RECRUIT DIVISION
COMMANDERS, AND INSTRUCTORS.
        - CONTINUED EDUCATION AND PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL
DEVELOPMENT.
        - GOOD PATTERN OF SEA, SHORE, AND OVERSEAS DUTY.
INCREASINGLY CHALLENGING ASSIGNMENTS WERE KEY.

3.  TO ENSURE OUR TOP PERFORMERS STANDOUT, REPORTING SENIORS AND ALL
THOSE INVOLVED IN DRAFTING PERFORMANCE EVALUATIONS MUST KEEP IN MIND
THAT:
        - BREAKOUTS ARE IMPORTANT.  BE SURE THE NARRATIVE CLEARLY
IDENTIFIES TOP PERFORMERS, ESPECIALLY WHEN A NUMBER OF SAILORS ARE
RANKED IN THE SAME CATEGORY, E.G. EP.
        - ENSURE LEADERSHIP POSITIONS, DUTIES, AND ACCOMPLISHMENTS
ARE CLEARLY DESCRIBED.
        - TIMELY SUBMISSION OF REPORTS IS CRITICAL TO ENSURING
APPLICANTS' RECORDS ARE COMPLETE AT SELECTION BOARDS.

4.  COMMAND ENDORSEMENTS OF LDO/CWO APPLICATION PACKAGES SHOULD
CLEARLY STATE THE ENDORSERS RECOMMENDATION WITH REGARD TO
SELECTION.  IF A NUMBER OF SAILORS ARE APPLYING FROM THE SAME
COMMAND, RANK-ORDER THE TOP CANDIDATES TO HELP THE BOARD PICK THE
VERY BEST APPLICANTS.
