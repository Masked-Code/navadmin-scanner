AMPN/REF A IS MILPERSMAN ARTICLE 1001-020, FULL TIME SUPPORT OF THE 
NAVY RESERVE OFFICER PROGRAM.//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE ACCEPTANCE OF APPLICATION 
PACKAGES FROM QUALIFIED ACTIVE COMPONENT AND SELECTED RESERVE 
(SELRES) OFFICERS FOR THE FALL FY13 FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) OFFICER 
TRANSFER AND REDESIGNATION BOARD SCHEDULED TO CONVENE ON 
30 OCTOBER 2012.  PER REF A, THIS BOARD WILL ALSO ACCEPT APPLICATION 
PACKAGES FROM EXISTING FTS OFFICERS REQUESTING REDESIGNATION INTO 
ANOTHER FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY.

2.  COMPLETE APPLICATION PACKAGES MUST BE RECEIVED NO LATER THAN 1600 
CENTRAL STANDARD TIME (CST), 7 SEPTEMBER 2012.

3.  ELIGIBILITY.  THE FTS PROGRAM IS LOOKING FOR REGULAR ACTIVE 
COMPONENT AND SELRES OFFICERS TO TRANSITION INTO THE FOLLOWING FTS 
OFFICER COMMUNITIES TO MEET IMMEDIATE MANNING NEEDS IN THE YEAR 
GROUPS (YG) INDICATED:  
COMMUNITY/DESIGNATOR      AVAILABLE YG(S)
FTS SWO (1167/1117)       99-00, 03 AND JUNIOR
FTS SUB (1127)            NONE
FTS SPECWAR (1137)        97 AND JUNIOR
FTS EOD (1147)            NONE
FTS AVIATION (1317/1327)  00 AND JUNIOR
FTS HR (1207)             98-09
FTS AMDO (1527)           98 AND JUNIOR
FTS SUPPLY (3107)         01 AND JUNIOR
    A.  IN ADDITION TO THE ABOVE YG LIMITATIONS, ALL APPLICANTS MUST 
ALSO MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS DETAILED IN REF A AND THE FALL FY13 
FTS ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA DOCUMENT POSTED ON THE FTS REDESIGNATION 
BOARD WEBSITE (SEE PARA 7.A).
    B.  YG ELIGIBILITY MAY BE ADJUSTED BASED UPON COMMUNITY 
REQUIREMENTS UP UNTIL THE APPLICATION DUE DATE.  THE MOST UP-TO-DATE 
YG ELIGIBILITY MATRIX WILL BE POSTED ON THE FTS REDESIGNATION WEBSITE.
    C.  MEMBERS WHO DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS DETAILED IN 
REF A AND THE FALL FY13 FTS ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA DOCUMENT POSTED ON 
THE FTS REDESIGNATION BOARD WEBSITE, MAY SUBMIT A WAIVER REQUEST AS A 
PART OF THEIR APPLICATION.
    D.  WAIVER PACKAGES MUST INCLUDE MEMBER'S WAIVER REQUEST AND 
COMMANDING OFFICER'S ENDORSEMENT.  THIS ENDORSEMENT IS SEPARATE FROM 
ANY ENDORSEMENT CONTAINED IN THE MEMBER'S TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION 
APPLICATION AND MUST SPECIFICALLY ADDRESS THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENT 
REQUIRING A WAIVER.  AN EXAMPLE OF A WAIVER REQUEST CAN BE FOUND AT 
HTTP://WWW.PUBLIC.NAVY.MIL/BUPERS-NPC/OFFICER/COMMUNITYMANAGERS/
RESERVE/FTS/PAGES/FTSREDESIGNATIONBOARD.ASPX.
    E.  THE FTS SUBMARINE AND EXPLOSIVE ORDNANCE DISPOSAL (EOD) 
COMMUNITIES ARE NOT ACCEPTING APPLICATIONS FOR THE UPCOMING BOARD.
    F.  RESERVE DIRECT COMMISSION OFFICERS, LIMITED DUTY OFFICERS, AND 
CHIEF WARRANT OFFICERS (CWO) ARE ELIGIBLE FOR THE FTS PROGRAM.  ALL 
OFFICERS APPLYING TO THE FTS OFFICER PROGRAM MUST POSSESS A 4-YEAR 
BACCALUAREATE DEGREE.  CWOS ARE CONSIDERED WITHOUT REGARD TO YG IN 
THOSE COMMUNITIES ACCEPTING APPLICATIONS AND WHERE THEY ARE OTHERWISE 
ELIGIBLE BY REF A AND THE ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA POSTED ON THE FTS 
OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT (OCM) WEBSITE.
    G.  SELRES OFFICERS CURRENTLY SERVING ON ORDERS AS CANVASSER  
RECRUITERS OR RESERVE MANAGEMENT TEMPORARY RECALLS ARE ENCOURAGED TO 
APPLY IF THEY MEET ALL OTHER APPLICABLE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS.
    H.  SUBJECT TO OTHER CRITERIA, ACTIVE COMPONENT OFFICERS WITH A 
PENDING OR APPROVED RESIGNATION FROM THE ACTIVE COMPONENT ARE ELIGIBLE 
AND ARE STRONGLY ENCOURAGED TO APPLY. 
    I.  CURRENT ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (BOTH GENERAL AND SPECIFIC 
FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY REQUIREMENTS), YG RESTRICTIONS BY COMMUNITY, AND 
INFORMATION ON APPLICATION PROCEDURES ARE POSTED ON THE FTS 
REDESIGNATION BOARD WEBSITE (SEE PARA 7.A.).  APPLICATIONS FROM 
OFFICERS WHO DO NOT MEET ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA AND WHO DO NOT HAVE AN 
APPROVED WAIVER REQUEST WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.  THE FTS 
OCM WEBSITE CONTAINS VALUABLE INFORMATION ABOUT THE FTS PROGRAM AS 
WELL AS INFORMATION ABOUT THE VARIOUS FTS OFFICER COMMUNITIES (SEE 
PARA 7.B.). 

4.  APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS.  APPLICATION PACKAGES FOR THIS BOARD 
SHALL CONSIST OF TWO PARTS:  THE BUPERS ONLINE (BOL) E-APPLICATION AND 
SUPPORTING DOCUMENT.
    A.  THE BOL E-APPLICATION CAN BE ACCESSED IN THE APPLICATION MENU 
ONCE LOGGED IN AT WWW.BOL.NAVY.MIL.  YOU WILL RECEIVE AN E-MAIL 
CONFIRMING THAT YOUR E-APPLICATION INPUT HAS BEEN RECEIVED.  ALTHOUGH 
YOU MAY SUBSEQUENTLY ACCESS THE BOL E-APPLICATION, ONLY YOUR MOST 
RECENT DATA WILL BE SAVED AS PART OF YOUR APPLICATION PACKAGE.
    B.  IN ADDITION TO THE BOL E-APPLICATION, APPLICANTS MUST SUBMIT 
SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS.  AT A MINIMUM, SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS SUBMISSIONS 
MUST CONTAIN A COVER LETTER WITH COMMAND ENDORSEMENT AND EVIDENCE OF 
MEDICAL/PHYSICAL READINESS DEPENDING UPON THE COMMUNITY TO WHICH THE 
APPLICANT IS APPLYING.  SUCH DOCUMENTS WILL ONLY BE ACCEPTED DIRECTLY 
FROM THE APPLICANT (I.E. THIRD PARTY SUBMISSIONS WILL NOT BE 
CONSIDERED).  OPTIONAL SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS MAY INCLUDE:  LETTERS OF 
RECOMMENDATION, FITREPS/AWARDS/EVIDENCE OF GRADUATE EDUCATION OR 
UPDATED PHOTO NOT ALREADY CONTAINED IN THE MEMBER'S OFFICIAL MILITARY 
PERSONNEL FILE (OMPF).  SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS CAN BE SUBMITTED VIA ONE 
OF THE FOLLOWING METHODS:
        (1) SCAN AND E-MAIL (PREFFERRED) TO:  
CSCSELECTIONBOARD(AT)NAVY.MIL.  SUBJECT LINE OF E-MAIL SHOULD READ:  
FALL FY13 FTS OFFICER TRANSFER/REDESIG BOARD (#35) ICO (RANK FIRST 
NAME LAST NAME, DESIGNATOR).
        (2) STANDARD (US POSTAL SERVICE) MAIL (IF SCAN/E-MAIL 
SERVICES NOT AVAILABLE) TO:
            FALL FY13 FTS OFFICER TRANSFER AND REDESIGNATION BOARD
            (BOARD ID #35)
            NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND, CUSTOMER CALL CENTER
            5720 INTEGRITY DRIVE, BLD 768 ROOM E302
            MILLINGTON, TN 38055
        (3) OVERNIGHT/EXPRESS (FEDEX, UPS, ETC.) MAIL REQUIRING 
SIGNATURE TO:
            FALL FY13 FTS OFFICER TRANSFER AND REDESIGNATION BOARD
            (BOARD ID #35)
            NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND, CUSTOMER CALL CENTER
            5640 TICONDEROGA LOOP
            MILLINGTON, TN 38055-9200
    C.  TO PREVENT THE TRANSMISSION OF MULTIPLE COPIES OF APPLICATION 
DOCUMENTS, APPLICANTS SHALL ONLY SUBMIT THEIR SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS 
VIA A SINGLE METHOD AND CONFIRM RECEIPT WITH THE POINTS OF CONTACT 
LISTED IN PARA 7.F. BEFORE RESUBMITTING.  APPLICANTS ARE ENCOURAGED 
TO REVIEW THEIR SERVICE RECORD ON BOL VIA THE OMPF LINK AND TO SEND 
ONLY THAT SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION (FITREPS, AWARDS, ETC.) WHICH IS 
NOT CONTAINED THEREIN.

5.  APPLICANTS SHOULD HAVE A CURRENT PHOTO IN THEIR OMPF OR SUBMIT AN 
UPDATED PHOTO AS PART OF THEIR SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS.  SEE PARA 7.C. 
FOR OFFICIAL PHOTO SUBMISSION GUIDELINE LINK. 

6.  THE RESULTS OF THE BOARD WILL BE POSTED ON THE FTS OCM WEBSITE 
ONCE THE BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED.  APPLICANTS CAN EXPECT TO 
SEE THE RESULTS POSTED WITHIN 30 DAYS OF BOARD ADJOURNMENT.  BOARD 
SELECTS HAVE 14 CALENDAR DAYS FROM THE DATE RESULTS ARE RELEASED TO 
ACCEPT/DECLINE FTS TRANSFER/REDESIGNATION.  AFTER THIS TIME, THEY MAY 
FORFEIT THEIR SELECT STATUS.
