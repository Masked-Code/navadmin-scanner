SUBJ/ANNUAL RECERTIFICATION OF SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1. TO ENSURE THE NAVY ACCURATELY COMPENSATES SAILORS
FOR SERVING IN UNIQUELY CHALLENGING ASSIGNMENTS, COMMANDING OFFICERS
ARE REQUIRED TO ANNUALLY RECERTIFY SAILORS RECEIVING SPECIAL DUTY
ASSIGNMENT PAY (SDAP). THIS CERTIFICATION IS TO VERIFY EACH SAILOR
WHO IS ASSIGNED TO AN ELIGIBLE BILLET REMAINS QUALIFIED TO FILL THE
BILLET AND IS RECEIVING THE CORRECT SDAP. CERTIFICATION SHALL BE
COMPLETED NO LATER THAN 31 AUG 06. A COMPLETE LIST OF AUTHORIZED
SKILLS AND PAY AMOUNTS CAN BE FOUND IN NAVADMIN 004/06.
2. ELIGIBILITY. SAILORS MUST:
A. BE QUALIFIED FOR AND SERVING IN AN AUTHORIZED BILLET
DESIGNATED FOR SDAP AS PER DODI 1304.27 AND OPNAVINST 1160.6A.
B. NOT BE ASSIGNED IN EXCESS EXCEPT EXCESS NUCLEAR-TRAINED
PERSONNEL MEETING THE REQUIREMENTS OF OPNAVINST 1160.6A.
3. PROCEDURES.
A. ANY CHANGES TO SDAP RECIPIENTS MUST BE ANNOTATED ON SECTION
4 OR SECTION SP OF THE ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT
(EDVR). ANNOTATE THE REFERENCE BY WHICH CHANGES WERE MADE, SUCH AS
NAVY STANDARD INTEGRATED PERSONNEL SYSTEM (NSIPS) ENTRY, WHICH ADDED
OR DELETED MEMBER AS AN SDAP RECIPIENT.
B. COMMANDING OFFICER'S SIGNATURE TO CERTIFY THE REPORT MUST BE
ON THE EDVR COPY FORWARDED TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4811). BY
DIRECTION SIGNATURES ARE NOT AUTHORIZED. ENDORSEMENT MUST READ:
(QUOTE) I HAVE REVIEWED AND CERTIFY MEMBER'S ELIGIBILITY FOR SDAP.
(QUOTE)
