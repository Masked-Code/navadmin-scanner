SUBJ/ESTABLISHMENT OF THE NAVY DIVER RATING//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  AS PART OF THE NAVY'S STRATEGY FOR OUR PEOPLE,
THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE NAVY DIVER (ND) GENERAL RATING HAS BEEN
APPROVED.  THE NAVY DIVER RATING WILL IMPROVE NAVY DIVER FORCE
READINESS, SUPPORT OPTIMAL MANNING AND ALIGN ND ENLISTED PERSONNEL
WITH SEA WARRIOR.  BY ALLOWING SAILORS TO FOCUS ON RATING SPECIFIC
TECHNOLOGY AND TRAINING SYSTEMS, NAVY WILL REDUCE TRAINING
INFRASTRUCTURE COSTS, WHILE BROADENING THE PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT,
CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AND QUALITY OF SERVICE FOR OUR SAILORS.
2.  OFFICIAL CONVERSION FROM SOURCE RATINGS TO THE ND RATING FOR NEC
5311, 5341, 5342, 5343, AND 5346 DESIGNATED PERSONNEL WILL BE
AUTOMATIC.  CONVERSION FOR E6-E9 (ACTIVE DUTY AND SELECTED RESERVE)
PERSONNEL WILL OCCUR ON 1 JUN 06.  CONVERSION FOR E1-E5 (ACTIVE
DUTY) PERSONNEL WILL OCCUR ON 1 OCT 06.  CONVERSION FOR E1-E5
(SELECTED RESERVE) PERSONNEL WILL OCCUR ON 1 MAR 07.  NO INDIVIDUAL
ACTION BY THE MEMBER WILL BE REQUIRED.
3.  PERSONNEL WITH NEC 5311, 5341, 5342, 5343, AND 5346 WHO DO NOT
DESIRE TO CONVERT TO THE ND RATING MUST SUBMIT A NAVY ENLISTED
CLASSIFICATION CHANGE REQUEST (EPMAC 1221/2) TO REMOVE THE 5311,
5341, 5342, 5343, AND 5346 NEC AND AN ENLISTED PERSONNEL ACTION
REQUEST (NAVPERS 1306/7) TO REMAIN IN THEIR SOURCE RATING NOT LATER
THAN 1 APR 06.  PERSONNEL NOT APPROVED TO REMAIN IN THEIR SOURCE
RATING MUST REQUEST LATERAL CONVERSION TO A CREO 1 OR CREO 2 RATING
FOR WHICH THEY QUALIFY.  THESE CONVERSIONS MUST BE SUBMITTED
DIRECTLY TO COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-4811).
4.  THE ND RATING BADGE WILL BE THE CURRENT SECOND CLASS DIVER
INSIGNIA.
5.  DESIGNATED NEC 5311, 5341, 5342, 5343, AND 5346 PERSONNEL WHO
TRANSFER TO THE FLEET RESERVE OR THE RETIRED LIST BETWEEN 1 JUNE 06
AND 1 OCT 06 MAY CHOOSE TO RETIRE WITH THEIR NEW OR PREVIOUS RATING
BADGE.  THE ENLISTED MASTER FILE, HOWEVER, WILL REFLECT E6-E9 AND
E1-E5 PERSONNEL IN THE ND RATING EFFECTIVE 1 JUN 06 AND 1 OCT 06,
RESPECTIVELY.
