RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES THE CANCELLATION OF THE FY-13 FTS COMMANDER 
SERAD BOARD AND THE CONVENING OF THE FY-13 FTS CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD.

2.  BASED ON FTS COMMANDER INVENTORY PROJECTIONS, THE FY-13 FTS COMMANDER 
SERAD BOARD IS NOT REQUIRED AND HAS BEEN CANCELLED.

3.  THE FTS CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD IS DESIGNED TO BRING FTS CAPTAIN INVENTORY IN 
LINE WITH AUTHORIZED END STRENGTH, ALLOW FOR REASONABLE PROMOTION OPPORTUNITY, 
AND PERMIT SUFFICIENT JUNIOR OFFICER ACCESSIONS TO MEET FTS OFFICER 
OPERATIONAL REQUIREMENTS.

4.  AS REQUIRED BY REF A, THE FTS CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD WILL CONVENE ON 11 
SEPTEMBER 2012 TO CONSIDER FTS CAPTAINS FOR CONTINUATION BEYOND THEIR 
RESPECTIVE GATES.  THIS BOARD WILL CONSIDER FIRST GATE FTS CAPTAINS WHO WERE 
PROMOTED DURING FY-11, AND SECOND GATE FTS CAPTAINS WHO WERE PROMOTED DURING 
FY-08.

5.  FIRST GATE FTS CAPTAINS WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT NUMBERS TO MAINTAIN 
FTS CAPTAIN END STRENGTH.  SECOND GATE CAPTAINS WILL BE RETAINED IN SUFFICIENT 
NUMBERS TO FILL SPECIFIC SENIOR MANAGEMENT BILLETS.

6.  FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION WILL BE NOTIFIED AS SOON AS 
PRACTICABLE AFTER THE BOARD PROCEEDINGS ARE APPROVED.  PER REF A, FIRST GATE 
FTS CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED FROM ACTIVE DUTY NO 
LATER THAN THE DATE THEY ACHIEVE THREE YEARS TIME IN GRADE.  SECOND GATE FTS 
CAPTAINS NOT SELECTED FOR RETENTION SHALL BE RELEASED FROM ACTIVE DUTY NO 
LATER THAN 1 SEPTEMBER 2013.

7.  AMPLIFYING INFORMATION WILL BE POSTED ON THE FTS COMMUNITY MANAGER WEB 
SITES AS IT BECOMES AVAILABLE.  THE EXPECTED NUMBER OF CONTINUATIONS AND THE 
EFFECTIVE CONTINUATION RATE WILL BE POSTED NO LATER THAN 5 SEPTEMBER 2012.  
THE FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT WEBPAGE IS FOUND AT:
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/COMMUNITYMANAGERS/RESERVE/FTS/.

8.  COMMUNICATION TO THE CAPTAIN SELRET BOARD SHALL FOLLOW THE SAME PROCEDURES 
AND POLICIES AS THAT FOR STATUTORY PROMOTION BOARDS.
ADDRESS ALL CORRESPONDENCE TO THE PRESIDENT, FTS CAPT SELRET BOARD #416.  FOR 
ADDITIONAL GUIDANCE, REFER TO THE GENERAL BOARD INFORMATION PAGE ON THE NPC 
WEB SITE AT:
HTTP://WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/BOARDS/GENERALBOARDINFO/.

9.  POINT OF CONTACT IS LCDR KANAN OTT, FTS OFFICER COMMUNITY MANAGER, AT 
(901) 874-3162/ DSN 882 OR VIA E-MAIL AT KANAN.OTT(AT)NAVY.MIL.

10.  RELEASED BY VICE ADMIRAL S. R. VAN BUSKIRK, N1.//
