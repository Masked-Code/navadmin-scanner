SUBJ/FY-11 ENLISTED SELECTION BOARDS FOR NAVY RESERVE AND ACTIVE DUTY
MASTER AND SENIOR CHIEF PETTY OFFICERS CYCLE 205// 

RMKS/1.  THE FY-11 E8/E9 SELECTION BOARDS FOR NAVY RESERVE PERSONNEL 
WILL CONVENE ON 1 MARCH 2010 AND WILL CONSIDER PERSONNEL SERVING ON 
ACTIVE DUTY AS FULL TIME SUPPORT (FTS) IN THE ACTIVE/GUARD RESERVE 
AND SELECTED RESERVE (SELRES) PERSONNEL, TO INCLUDE THOSE SERVING ON 
VOLUNTARY AND INVOLUNTARY RECALL AND MOBILIZATION ORDERS.  INDIVIDUAL 
READY RESERVE (IRR) PERSONNEL WHO ARE MOBILIZED ARE CONSIDERED UNDER 
THE SELRES REQUIREMENTS FOR ADVANCEMENT.  THE FY-11 E9/E8 SELECTION 
BOARDS FOR ACTIVE DUTY PERSONNEL WILL CONVENE 29 MARCH 2010 AND 
19 APRIL 2010, RESPECTIVELY.  THIS NAVADMIN IS A SUPPLEMENT TO 
BUPERSINST 1430.16F.
2.  TIMELINE OF EVENTS
    A.  OCTOBER 2009:  COMMANDER, NAVY PERSONNEL COMMAND
 (COMNAVPERSCOM) (PERS-811/812) COMPILES AND SUBMITS INITIAL 
TIME-IN-RATE (TIR) ELIGIBILITY LIST OF CANDIDATES TO NAVAL EDUCATION 
AND TRAINING PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER (NETPDC) 
(N321).
    B.  NOVEMBER 2009:  NETPDTC (N321) FORWARDS TO COMMANDS/SERVICING 
PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS LISTINGS OF E7/E8 TIR ELIGIBLES VIA THE 
EXAMINATION STATUS VERIFICATION REPORT (ESVR).  COMMAND DESIGNATED 
PERSONNEL CAN ALSO ACCESS THEIR ESVR VIA INTERNET AT 
HTTPS://WWW2.NETC.NAVY.MIL/ ADVANCEMENT.
    C.  NOVEMBER 2009 - MARCH 2010:  COMMANDS MUST VERIFY ESVR AND 
TAKETHE FOLLOWING ACTIONS:
        (1) IF ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES ARE NOT LISTED - COMMANDS MUST 
MAIL A COMPLETED ANSWER SHEET (NETPDTC 1430/2) WITH LETTER OF 
TRANSMITTAL TO NETPDTC (N322) FOR ALL PERSONNEL NOT LISTED.  
REFER TO BUPERSINST 1430.16F, CHAPTER 6 FOR FURTHER GUIDANCE.
        (2) IF LISTED CANDIDATES ARE NOT ELIGIBLE OR NO LONGER 
RECOMMENDED FOR ADVANCEMENT - COMMANDS MUST SUBMIT A MESSAGE TO 
NETPDTC (N321), INFO PERS-811/812 REQUESTING INVALIDATION.  
WITHDRAWAL OFADVANCEMENT RECOMMENDATION MUST BE DOCUMENTED PER 
BUPERSINST 1430.16F, PARA 721B PRIOR TO MESSAGE TRANSMISSION.
        (3) CANDIDATES VALIDATED IN WRONG COMPETITIVE CATEGORY - 
COMMANDS MUST SUBMIT A MESSAGE TO PERS-811/812, INFO NETPDTC (N321) 
DETAILING DISCREPANCY.  PERS-811/812 WILL RESEARCH AND COMMUNICATE
RESOLUTION.
    D.  MARCH 2010 - APRIL 2010:  SELECTION BOARDS CONVENE.
3.  ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:  CANDIDATES MUST MEET THE ELIGIBILITY 
REQUIREMENTS OUTLINED IN BUPERSINST 1430.16F, CHAPTER 2 NO LATER THAN
1 NOVEMBER 2009.  A CANDIDATE WHO DOES NOT HAVE A 
RECOMMENDATION FOR ADVANCEMENT ON THEIR MOST RECENT EVALUATION SHALL 
NOT BE VALIDATED.
    A.  TIR:  TERMINAL ELIGIBILITY DATE FOR CYCLE 205 IS 
1 OCTOBER 2010.  PERSONNEL WITH A TIR DATE OF 1 OCTOBER 2007 OR
 EARLIER WILL BE TIR ELIGIBLE FOR THIS SELECTION BOARD CYCLE.  REFER 
TO BUPERSINST 1430.16F, PARA 216 TO COMPUTE TIR.
    B.  COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS:  CANDIDATES WHO HAVE BEEN SELECTED 
FOR SEAMAN TO ADMIRAL, MEDICAL ENLISTED COMMISSIONING, AND MEDICAL 
SERVICE CORPS IN-SERVICE PROCUREMENT PROGRAMS REMAIN ELIGIBLE FOR 
ADVANCEMENT CONSIDERATION TO SENIOR CHIEF AND MASTER CHIEF PETTY 
OFFICER.  SELECTION TO ANY OTHER OFFICER COMMISSIONING PROGRAMS 
RENDER CANDIDATES INELIGIBLE AND RECOMMENDATION FOR ADVANCEMENT MUST 
BE INVALIDATED BY PARENT COMMANDS.
    C.  HIGH YEAR TENURE (HYT):  CANDIDATES WHO REACH HYT FOR 
PRESENTPAYGRADE ON OR AFTER THE FIRST DAY OF THE ADVANCEMENT CYCLE, 
1 JULY 2010, OR HAVE RECEIVED A HYT WAIVER TO REMAIN ON ACTIVE 
DUTY (OR REMAIN IN SELRES STATUS) BEYOND 1 JULY 2010 ARE ELIGIBLE FOR 
ADVANCEMENT AND WILL BE CONSIDERED BY THE SELECTION BOARD.  SAILORS 
WITH A HYT DATE PRIOR TO 1 JULY 2010 ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO COMPETE FOR 
ADVANCEMENT UNLESS GRANTED A HYT WAIVER.  HYT WAIVERS MUST BE 
APPROVED AND CANDIDATES VALIDATED BY PARENT COMMANDS PRIOR TO 
CONVENING OF THE BOARD.  MOBILIZED SELRES PERSONNEL WHO REACH HYT 
WHILE MOBILIZED HAVE A COMNAVPERSCOM (PERS-913) APPROVED HYT WAIVER 
THROUGH EXPIRATION OF MOBILIZATION ORDERS.  PERSONNEL WHO EXCEED HYT 
LIMIT FOR THE NEXT PAYGRADE ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR ADVANCEMENT.
    D.  REQUESTS FOR TRANSFER TO THE FLEET RESERVE/RETIRED LIST: 
CANDIDATES SUBJECT TO HYT WHO SUBMIT INVOLUNTARY REQUESTS DUE TO HYT TO
TRANSFER AT OR BEYOND HYT LIMITS ARE ELIGIBLE FOR 
CONSIDERATION, AS LONG AS MEMBER IS PROJECTED TO REMAIN IN AN ELIGIBLE
STATUS AS OF 1 JULY 2010 OR LATER.  VOLUNTARY REQUESTS AS 
OUTLINED IN BUPERSINST 1430.16F, CHAPTER 2 RENDERS CANDIDATES 
INELIGIBLE FOR SELECTION BOARD CONSIDERATION ONCE THE REQUEST IS 
GENERATED AT THE COMMAND LEVEL.  COMMANDS MUST COUNSEL MEMBERS PER 
BUPERSINST 1430.16F, PARA 209.A.  AN APPROVED OR PENDING FLEET 
RESERVE/RETIRED LIST APPLICATION MUST BE CANCELLED NO LATER THAN
1 FEBRUARY 2010 TO REESTABLISH ELIGIBILITY FOR BOARD CONSIDERATION. 
UPON CANCELATION OF A VOLUNTARY FLEET RESERVE/RETIRED LIST 
APPLICATION, THE PARENT COMMAND MUST REVALIDATE THE CANDIDATE 
PER PARA 2.C (1) OF THIS NAVADMIN PRIOR TO BOARD CONVENING.
    E.  ENLISTED NAVY LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT:  NAVADMIN 272/08 REMOVED 
THE REQUIREMENT FOR ALL E7 PERSONNEL TO COMPLETE THE LEADING CHIEF 
PETTY OFFICER LEADERSHIP COURSE (LCPOLC) PRIOR TO CONSIDERATION BY THE 
E8 SELECTION BOARD.
    F.  BRANCH CLASS (BRCL) AND SPECIAL PROGRAM INDICATOR CODE:  
BRCL 32 WITH A BLANK SPECIAL PROGRAM INDICATOR (SPI) CODE IS AN 
INVALID NAVY ACCOUNTING STATUS WHICH MAY CAUSE SAILORS TO BE REFLECTED
IN THE INCORRECT ADVANCEMENT COMPETITIVE CATEGORY.  COMMANDS 
WITH SAILORS IN THIS INVALID ACCOUNTING STATUS MUST TAKE ACTION TO 
CORRECT THIS ERROR EITHER VIA NAVY STANDARD INTEGRATED PERSONNEL SYSTEM 
(NSIPS) CORRECTIVE ACTION OR THE SAILOR MUST SUBMIT A BOARD FOR 
CORRECTION OF NAVAL RECORDS (BCNR) PETITION.  THIS ACCOUNTING STATUS IS 
REFLECTED ON THE COMMAND'S ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT 
(EDVR).  SAILORS IN THIS INVALID ACCOUNTING STATUS WILL BE 
INVALIDATED.
