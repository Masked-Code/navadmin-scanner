1.  THIS ORDER TO ACCOUNT WILL COMMENCE AFTER STORM PASSAGE OR CLOSEST POINT 
OF APPROACH (CPA).  THIS ORDER TO ACCOUNT APPLIES TO ALL NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, 
RESERVES, DON CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES, NAF EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES, AND 
APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS WITHIN THE ESTABLISHED GEOGRAPHIC AREA OF INTEREST 
(GAOI) DESIGNATED IN PARAGRAPH 2. ECHELON II COMMANDERS AND ALL REGIONAL 
COMMANDERS SHALL ENSURE WIDEST DISSEMINATION TO ALL SUBORDINATE AND TENANT 
COMMANDS AND DETACHMENTS FOR IMMEDIATE ACTION UPON RECEIPT.

3.  ALL COMMANDS ARE EXPECTED TO: 
A.  COMMANDING OFFICERS (CO) AND/OR COMMANDING OFFICER REPRESENTATIVES (COR) 
WILL ENSURE THAT THEIR COMMAND'S NAVY FAMILY ACCOUNTABILITY AND ASSESSMENT 
SYSTEM (NFAAS) DATABASE IS CURRENT AND ACCURATE, TO INCLUDE PHONE NUMBERS AND 
ADDRESSES.  ENSURE ANY RECENT CHECK-INS TO YOUR COMMAND WHO HAVE NOT HAD 
THEIR CONTACT INFORMATION UPDATED IN NFAAS, COMMUNICATE RECALL INFORMATION TO 
THEIR RESPECTIVE CHAIN OF COMMAND. 
B.  MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR ALL NAVY ACTIVE DUTY, RESERVE, DON CIVILIAN 
EMPLOYEES, NAF EMPLOYEES, NEX EMPLOYEES AND APPLICABLE DEPENDENTS IN THE GAOI 
AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA. ALL CORS/SPONSORS IN THE AFFECTED GAOI WILL RECEIVE 
NOTIFICATION FROM NFAAS VIA TEXT/EMAIL TO MUSTER THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN 
NFAAS AFTER STORM PASSAGE/CPA. RCOS AND/OR CORS WILL ENSURE WIDEST 
DISSEMINATION FOR ACCURATE AND TIMELY ACCOUNTABILITY VIA NFAAS. 
C.  RCOS/CORS WILL DETERMINE ACCURATE STATUS AND WHEREABOUTS OF THEIR 
PERSONNEL AND MUSTER AND ACCOUNT FOR THEM AT HOME, WORK, TELEWORK, EVACUATED, 
OR DISPLACED LOCATION, OR NOT IN THE GAOI WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE 
(DEPLOYED, TDY/TAD/IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, UA/DESERTER, 
OTHER). 
D.  COMMANDS SHALL DIRECT RCOS/CORS/SPONSORS TO MUSTER IN NFAAS, AT 
HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL/CAS/LOGIN?SERVICE. IF NFAAS IS NOT AVAILABLE, 
COMMANDS/MEMBERS ARE TO REPORT THE STATUS OF PERSONNEL ACCOUNTABILITY (PA) TO 
THE AFFECTED REGION OPERATIONS CENTER (ROC) OR DIRECT CHAIN OF COMMAND. 
E.  DO NOT DELAY REPORTING ON PERSONNEL UNTIL ALL INFORMATION IS 
COMPLETE.  REPORT AND ACCOUNT MUSTERING INFORMATION WHEN OBTAINED, INCLUDING 
THE POSTING OF AMPLIFYING DATA IN THE NOTES AREA ON THE INDIVIDUAL'S 
ACCOUNTING LINE. 
 
4.  ALL CORS/SPONSORS ARE EXPECTED TO: 
A.  ACCOUNT FOR THEMSELVES AND DEPENDENTS IN NFAAS AT 
HTTPS://NAVYFAMILY.NAVY.MIL/CAS/LOGIN?SERVICE. 
B.  IF UNABLE TO ACCESS NFAAS, SPONSORS SHOULD CONTACT THEIR COR, COMMAND 
MUSTERING OFFICER, REGIONAL COMMANDING OFFICER REPRESENTATIVE (RCO) AND OR 
SUPERVISOR TO CHECK IN (MUSTER). 
C.  SPONSORS WILL PROVIDE COMMAND REPRESENTATIVES WITH CURRENT RESIDENCE, 
DISPLACED LOCATION, OR IF NOT IN THE GAOI, WITH SPECIFIED CIRCUMSTANCE 
(DEPLOYED, TDY/TAD /IA, ON LEAVE, SEPARATED/RETIRED, TRANSFERRED, 
UA/DESERTER, OTHER). 
 
5.  A NEEDS ASSESSMENT HAS BEEN INITIATED. 
 
6.  FOR NFAAS TECHNICAL SUPPORT, CONTACT NFAAS HELP DESK VIA EMAIL AT 
PAAS(AT)NAVY.MIL.
