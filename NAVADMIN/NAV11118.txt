SUBJ/COMMAND FITNESS LEADER CERTIFICATION COURSE UPDATE//

RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN ANNOUNCES IMPROVEMENTS TO THE FIVE-DAY 
COMMAND FITNESS LEADER (CFL) CERTIFICATION COURSE AND NEW RESOURCES 
AVAILABLE IN SUPPORT OF THE NAVY'S PHYSICAL READINESS PROGRAM.

2.  THE CENTER FOR PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT (CPPD), IN 
COLLABORATION WITH THE NAVY'S PHYSICAL READINESS PROGRAM OFFICE 
(OPNAV N135F), AND COMMANDER NAVY INSTALLATIONS COMMAND (CNIC), HAS 
COMPLETED A COMPREHENSIVE REVISION OF THE SUBJECT COURSE IN ORDER TO 
MEET NAVY EDUCATION AND TRAINING REQUIREMENTS FOR CERTIFIED NAVY 
CURRICULUM.  AN OFFICIAL COURSE IDENTIFICATION NUMBER, CIN S-562-
0612, HAS BEEN ISSUED, AND UPON SUCCESSFUL COURSE COMPLETION 
QUALIFICATIONS WILL BE DOCUMENTED IN THE MEMBER'S ELECTRONIC SERVICE 
RECORD.  EFFECTIVE 1 MAY 11, COURSE REGISTRATION WILL OCCUR AS 
FOLLOWS:
    A.  QUOTAS SHALL BE REQUESTED VIA CATALOG OF NAVY TRAINING 
COURSES (CANTRAC) AT 
HTTPS://CETARSJ2EEPD.CNET.NAVY.MIL/CANTRAC/VOL2.HTML (ALL LOWERCASE) 
AND ENTERPRISE NAVY TRAINING RESERVATION SYSTEM (ENTRS) AT 
HTTPS://CETARSJ2EEPD.CNET.NAVY.MIL/ENTRS/LOGIN.DO (ALL LOWERCASE 
EXCEPT FOR THE LETTERS "NTRS" IN "ENTRS")
    B.  COMPLETE APPLICATION PACKAGE (TO INCLUDE ALL REQUIRED 
SUPPORTING DOCUMENTATION) SHALL BE SUBMITTED VIA E-MAIL TO:  
CFL_TRAINING(AT)NAVY.MIL.
    C.  ONCE ELIGIBILITY HAS BEEN CONFIRMED, THE REQUESTING COMMAND 
WILL RECEIVE CONFIRMATION VIA E-MAIL.  
    D.  STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS FOR REGISTRATION CAN BE FOUND AT:  
WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/COMMANDSUPPORT/PHYSICALREADINESS.

3.  SEVERAL NEW PHYSICAL READINESS RESOURCES ARE AVAILABLE TO CFL'S.  
SPECIFICALLY:
    A.  NEW CFL LINK AT NAVY KNOWLEDGE ONLINE (NKO) UNDER THE 
PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT TAB.  RESOURCES AVAILABLE INCLUDE VARIOUS 
PHYSICAL READINESS INSTRUCTIONS, NAVADMINS, PRIMS TRAINING VIDEOS, 
BODY COMPOSITION ASSESSMENT TRAINING, AND CFL COURSE QUOTA REQUEST 
INFORMATION.
    B.  NEW CNIC PHYSICAL FITNESS WEBSITE, WWW.NAVYFITNESS.ORG WHICH 
PROVIDES MEMBERS WITH AN ARRAY OF FITNESS, NUTRITION, DEPLOYED 
FORCES SUPPORT, AND NAVY SPORTS PROGRAM INFORMATION - TO INCLUDE 
SEVERAL PHYSICAL READINESS MOBILE APPLICATIONS.  THIS SITE ALSO 
FEATURES THE NEW NAVY OPERATIONAL FITNESS AND FUELING SERIES (NOFFS) 
PROGRAM - A COMPREHENSIVE FITNESS AND NUTRITION RESOURCE DEVELOPED 
SPECIFICALLY FOR SAILORS.

    B.  CPPD N7:  QMC(SW/AW) JERROD MORGAN, CFL COURSE MANAGER,
