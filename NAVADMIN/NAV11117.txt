RMKS/1.  THE ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP SELECTION BOARD IS         
SCHEDULED TO CONVENE ON 22 SEPTEMBER 2011.  ACQUISITION CORPS        
MEMBERSHIP IS A PREREQUISITE FOR ASSIGNMENT TO ONE OF APPROXIMATELY  
700 DEPARTMENT OF THE NAVY (DON) CRITICAL ACQUISITION BILLETS        
THROUGHOUT THE FLEET.                                                

2.  POLICY AND GUIDANCE FOR ACQUISITION CORPS MEMBERSHIP PROCEDURES  
FOR DON OFFICERS MAY BE FOUND AT THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE:              
HTTPS://ACQUISITION.NAVY.MIL/RDA/HOME/CAREER_MANAGEMENT/ACQUISITION  
CORPS.  OFFICERS MUST BE A LCDR OR ABOVE TO BE A MEMBER OF THE       
ACQUISITION CORPS.                                                   

3.  APPLICATIONS (NO ENDORSEMENTS REQUIRED) MUST BE RECEIVED BY 27   
AUGUST 2011 TO BE CONSIDERED BY THE BOARD.  THE REQUIRED APPLICATION 
COVER LETTER AND DATA SHEET MAY BE FOUND AT THE FOLLOWING WEBSITE:   
FROM WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/OFFICER/ACQUISITION.  ALL APPLICANTS ARE       
REQUIRED TO PROVIDE INFORMATION AS DELINEATED IN THE SAMPLE DATA     
SHEET.  COPIES OF ORIGINAL COLLEGE TRANSCRIPTS ARE ACCEPTABLE TO     
DOCUMENT COMPLIANCE WITH THE BUSINESS ACADEMIC REQUIREMENT.          

4.  NAVY RESERVE COMMUNITIES INVOLVED IN NAVY ACQUISITION PROCESSES  
SHOULD PRE-SCREEN ANY RESERVE OFFICER APPLICANTS FOR CONSISTENCY IN  
ACCOUNTING FOR EXPERIENCE BEFORE FORWARDING THESE APPLICATIONS.      
THIS PRE-SCREENING DOCUMENTATION SHOULD BE INCLUDED WITH THEIR       
APPLICATION.  RESERVE EXPERIENCE CAN ONLY BE ACCRUED EITHER ON       
INDIVIDUAL RESERVE ASSIGNMENTS CONSISTING OF 120 DAYS OR LONGER OR   
FROM CIVILIAN ACQUISITION EXPERIENCE WHEN THE DUTIES AND             
RESPONSIBILITIES MATCH THE PRIMARY CAREER FIELD LISTED ON THE        
APPLICATION.
