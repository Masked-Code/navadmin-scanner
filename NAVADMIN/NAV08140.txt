SUBJ/FY-09 NATIONAL SECURITY MANAGEMENT PROGRAM (NSMP)// 
RMKS/1.  THIS NAVADMIN SOLICITS APPLICATIONS FOR THE NATIONAL SECURITY 
MANAGEMENT PROGRAM (NSMP).  APPLICATIONS ARE DUE NLT 26 SEP 08 FOR THE 
FY-09 SELECTION BOARD.
2.  NSMP IS A DOD-SPONSORED FOUR-WEEK COURSE OFFERED AT GEORGE 
WASHINGTON UNIVERSITY THAT FOCUSES ON VARIOUS ASPECTS OF NATIONAL 
SECURITY POLICY.  PARTICIPANTS WILL ATTEND THE SENIOR MANAGEMENT COURSE 
IN NATIONAL SECURITY.  THE COURSE CONVENING MONTH HAS NOT BEEN RELEASED 
AND WILL BE PROVIDED WHEN AVAILABLE. 
HISTORICALLY THE COURSE HAS BEEN OFFERED IN MAY OR JUNE.
3.  APPLICANTS MUST BE TOP-PERFORMING UNRESTRICTED LINE (URL) CAPTAINS 
OR OFFICERS SELECTED FOR CAPTAIN WHO HOLD A MASTER'S DEGREE OR PROVEN 
SUBSPECIALTY.  PARTICIPANTS WILL INCUR TWO YEARS OBLIGATED SERVICE UPON 
COMPLETION OF THE COURSE.
4.  REPORTING SENIORS SHOULD SUBMIT NOMINATIONS IN THE FOLLOWING FORMAT 
TO COMMANDING OFFICER, NAVAL EDUCATION AND TRAINING PROFESSIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER, ATTN:
CODE N2A2, 6490 SAUFLEY FIELD ROAD, PENSACOLA, FL 32509-5204:
    A.  RANK, NAME, SSN/DESIGNATOR OF NOMINEE
    B.  COMMAND ADDRESS, TELEPHONE (DSN AND COMM), AND E-MAIL ADDRESS
    C.  LEVEL OF EDUCATION ATTAINED
    D.  OPERATIONAL, TECHNICAL, AND MANAGERIAL SPECIALTIES AND 
SUBSPECIALTY CODES AND A LIST OF SIGNIFICANT ASSIGNMENTS AS COMMANDING 
OFFICER, PROJECT OFFICER, OR PROVEN SUBSPECIALIST.
    E.  FUTURE POTENTIAL, INCLUDING SUGGESTIONS FOR SPECIFIC CATEGORIES 
OF ASSIGNMENT.
    F.  STATEMENT FROM REPORTING SENIOR AFFIRMING NOMINEE'S 
AVAILABILITY TO ATTEND THE COURSE, IF SELECTED.
    G.  STATEMENT FROM NOMINEE INDICATING HOW PARTICIPATION WILL 
BENEFIT THE NAVY AND THE NOMINEE.
    H.  ONE-PAGE BIOGRAPHY
5.  SELECTEES WILL RECEIVE FULL TUITION, ROOM AND BOARD, AND TRAVEL 
FUNDING.
6.  POINT OF CONTACT:  MS. BILLIE COLONNA, NETPDTC N2A2, AT
(850) 452-1001, PRESS 3 IN THE MENU OPTION, THEN DIAL EXT 2219/DSN 922-
1807, OR E-MAIL AT BILLIE.COLONNA(AT)NAVY.MIL.
7.  RELEASED BY VADM M. E. FERGUSON III, N1.//
