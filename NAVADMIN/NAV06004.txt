SUBJ/SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY//
GENTEXT/REMARKS/1.  IN SUPPORT OF THE NAVY'S EVOLVING STRATEGY FOR
OUR PEOPLE, SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY PROVIDES A MONETARY
INCENTIVE TO SAILORS WITH SPECIFIC DESIGNATED SKILLS WHO HAVE CHOSEN
TO APPLY THEIR KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE TO UNIQUELY CHALLENGING
ASSIGNMENTS.
2.  THE NEW INCENTIVES AND INCREASES TO EXISTING INCENTIVES BECOME
EFFECTIVE 1 JAN 06 AND ARE SUMMARIZED IN PARAGRAPH 9 FOR EASE OF
REFERENCE.  REDUCTIONS OR TERMINATIONS OF PREVIOUS INCENTIVES ARE
EFFECTIVE 1 APR 06, AND ARE SUMMARIZED IN PARAGRAPH 10.  THIS
NAVADMIN SUPERCEDES NAVADMIN 084/05.
3.  TO RECEIVE SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY, PERSONNEL MUST BE
FILLING A SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY AUTHORIZED BILLET WITH THE
SPECIAL SKILL LISTED IN PARAGRAPH 8.  UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED,
PERSONNEL MAY NOT RECEIVE THIS PAY WHILE ASSIGNED IN EXCESS OF
AUTHORIZED BILLETS.  COMMANDS MUST REVIEW THEIR ACTIVITY MANNING
DOCUMENT AND ENLISTED DISTRIBUTION VERIFICATION REPORT TO ENSURE
THAT THE NUMBER OF PERSONNEL RECEIVING THIS SPECIAL PAY DOES NOT
EXCEED THE NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED BILLETS FOR THAT SKILL.
SERVICEMEMBERS ARE ONLY AUTHORIZED TO RECEIVE ONE SPECIAL DUTY
ASSIGNMENT PAY ENTITLEMENT.
4.  COMMANDS HOLDING SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY BILLETS ARE
REMINDED THAT ANNUAL RE-CERTIFICATION BEGINS 1 JUN 06 AS DIRECTED.
THE DEADLINE FOR THE ANNUAL RE-CERTIFICATION IS 31 AUG 06.
5.  OPNAVINST 1160.6A AND THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE FINANCIAL
MANAGEMENT REGULATION, VOLUME 7A, GOVERN THE POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
FOR THE ADMINISTRATION AND PAYMENT OF SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY.
COMMANDS AND SERVICING PERSONNEL SUPPORT DETACHMENTS ARE RESPONSIBLE
FOR ADMINISTERING ALL APPLICABLE CHANGES TO SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT
PAY PER THIS NAVADMIN AND THE ABOVE-LISTED DIRECTIVES.
6.  FOR MEMBERS IN A PATIENT STATUS ENTITLED TO SPECIAL DUTY
ASSIGNMENT PAY, THE FOLLOWING POLICY APPLIES:
    A.  WHEN A MEMBER RECEIVING THIS SPECIAL PAY IS IN A PATIENT
STATUS (INCLUDING CONVALESCENT LEAVE), TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY, OR
LIMITED DUTY, THE MEMBER IS ENTITLED TO CONTINUE TO RECEIVE THIS
SPECIAL PAY FOR 90 DAYS.  THERE ARE NO WAIVERS FOR PERIODS LONGER
THAN 90 DAYS.
    B.  WHEN A MEMBER RECEIVING SPECIAL DUTY ASSIGNMENT PAY IS IN A
PATIENT STATUS (INCLUDING CONVALESCENT LEAVE) DUE TO A MEDICAL
CONDITION RESULTING FROM INTEMPERATE USE OF ALCOHOL OR HABIT FORMING
DRUGS, ENTITLEMENT TO THE PAY STOPS WHEN THE MEMBER GOES INTO A
PATIENT STATUS (INCLUDING CONVALESCENT LEAVE), TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL
DUTY, OR LIMITED DUTY.
7.  SKILL SPECIFIC QUESTIONS MAY BE DIRECTED TO THE APPROPRIATE
ENLISTED COMMUNITY MANAGERS, AS LISTED IN PARAGRAPH 8.  TO LOCATE
THE PHONE NUMBER OF THE APPROPRIATE COMMUNITY MANAGER, REFER TO THE
PHONE LIST IN THE LINK-PERSPECTIVE MAGAZINE.  ADDITIONALLY, CONTACT
INFO MAY BE ACCESSED AT WWW.NPC.NAVY.MIL/ENLISTED/COMMUNITYMANAGERS.
YOU MUST LOG ONTO BUPERS ONLINE TO ACCESS THIS LISTING.
