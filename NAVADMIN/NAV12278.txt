(SSN 715)

FIVE OF FIVE COMMAND EXCELLENCE AWARDS, UP FROM ZERO OF FIVE IN 2010.  USS 
BUFFALO'S PERFORMANCE DURING 2011 RESULTED IN THE SUB WINNING THE BATTLE 
EFFICIENCY "E", THE GREEN COMMUNICATIONS "C", THE RED DAMAGE CONTROL "DC", THE 
BLUE SUPPLY "S", AND A MERITORIOUS UNIT COMMENDATION FOR OUTSTANDING 
OPERATIONAL PERFORMANCE AND FLEXIBILITY.

3.  CONGRATULATIONS TO USS BAINBRIDGE AND USS BUFFALO FOR YOUR CONTINUING
